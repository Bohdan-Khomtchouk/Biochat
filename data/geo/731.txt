TITLE
Leukotriene LTD4 effect on macrophage and endothelial cells

SUMMARY
Examination of gene expression in MonoMac6 macrophage and umbilical vein endothelial cells treated with 100 nM leukotriene LTD4 for 1 hour.  Results offer insight into autocrine action of LTD4 on macrophages and its paracrine action on endothelial cells.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
