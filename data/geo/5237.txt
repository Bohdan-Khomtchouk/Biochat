TITLE
20-hydroxyecdysone effect on skeletal muscle: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of triceps brachii muscles from C57BL/6 animals given a continuous infusion of 20-hydroxyecdysone (20E) for 5 or 15 days.  20E is an abundant phytoecdysteroid. Results provide insight into the molecular effect of 20E on skeletal muscles.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
