TITLE
Homeobox Dlx1/2 mutations effect on embryonic telencephalon

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of cortex and basal ganglia from embryonic telencephalon of homozygous and heterozygous Dlx1/2 mutants. Mutants examined at embryonic day 14.5. Dlx homeobox transcription factors are implicated in regulating the function of inhibitory neurons.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
