TITLE
Sex effect on chronic coxsackievirus B3-induced myocarditis: heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of heart from CVB3-infected males during chronic myocarditis/dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM) (day 90 postinfection). Myocarditis and DCM, often caused by viral infections, occur more frequently in men than in women. Results provided insight into molecular basis of DCM progression in males.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
