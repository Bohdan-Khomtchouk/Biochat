TITLE
TRPV1 activation effect on epithelial cell line

SUMMARY
Analyis of epi 4 gingival cells treated with capsaicin, the pungent compound present in red chilies.  Capsaicin is an activator of transient receptor potential cation channel subfamily V member 1 (TRPV1). Results provide insight into the function of TRPV1 in gingival cells.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
