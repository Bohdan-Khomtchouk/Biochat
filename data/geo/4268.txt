TITLE
Scleroderma-associated pulmonary hypertension: peripheral blood mononuclear cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of PBMCs from scleroderma patients with pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH). PAH is a common, potentially fatal complication of chronic systemic scleroderma. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying scleroderma-related PAH.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
