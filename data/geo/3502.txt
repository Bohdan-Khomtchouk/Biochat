TITLE
Schizophrenia: endothelial and neuronal cells from postmortem dorsolateral prefrontal cortex tissue

SUMMARY
Analysis of LCM-captured microvascular endothelial cells and neurons from post mortem brain tissue from schizophrenia patients, bipolar disorder patients, and healthy controls. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying the pathophysiology of schizophrenia.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
