TITLE
Circadian oscillations and cardiovascular function

SUMMARY
Examination of molecular basis of circadian oscillation system in aorta. After 2 week acclimation to 12/12 hour light/dark regimen, C57Bl6 mice subjected to 36 hours constant darkness. Aortae subsequently harvested at 4 hour intervals to 48 hours.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
