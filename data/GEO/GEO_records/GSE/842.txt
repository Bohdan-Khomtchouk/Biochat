TITLE
Naive, EAE placebo-treated and EAE 1,25(OH)2D3-treated groups

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
B10.PL mice with severe (stage 2.5-3) experimental autoimmune encephalomyelitis were treated with placebo or 200 ng 1,25(OH)2D3.  Six hours later, the spinal cords were harvested and mRNA was extracted for microarray analysis.  Naive mice serve as controls.  Individual samples were hybridized to individual microarrays.
