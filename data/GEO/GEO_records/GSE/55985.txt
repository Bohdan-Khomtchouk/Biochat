TITLE
Comparative analysis of chrysanthemum transcriptome in response to infection of three viruses: Cucumber mosaic virus, Tomato spotted wilt virus, and Potato virus X

ORGANISM
Chrysanthemum

SUMMARY
Gene expression analysis of chrysanthemum infected with three different viruses including Cucumber mosaic virus, Tomato spotted wilt virus, and Potato virus X have been performed using the chrysanthemum 135K microarray.

DESIGN
Mock and each virus infected chrysanthemum plants were subjected for microarray analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL18436 Chrysanthemum 135k microarray
