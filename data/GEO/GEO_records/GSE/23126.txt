TITLE
modENCODE_White Lab: genome-wide ChIP data of GFP from 10T-E16-24h on Illumina Genome Analyzer.

ORGANISM
modENCODE

SUMMARY
This is a dataset generated by the Drosophila Regulatory Elements modENCODE Project led by Kevin P. White at the University of Chicago. It contains genome-wide binding profile of the factor GFP from 10T-E16-24h generated by ChIP and analyzed on Illumina Genome Analyzer.
