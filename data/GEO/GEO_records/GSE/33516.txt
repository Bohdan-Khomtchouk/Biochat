TITLE
IL-5+ and IL-5- memory Th2

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We used microarray analysis to identify specific molecular mechanisms controlling IL-5 transcription in memory Th2 cells.

DESIGN
IL-5+ and IL-5- memory Th2 cells were selected for RNA extraction and hybridization on Affymetrix microarrays.
