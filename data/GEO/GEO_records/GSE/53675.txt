TITLE
MiRNA-expression based, individualized outcome prediction for the cardiovascular disease patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Individualized outcome prediction classifiers were successfully constructed through expression profiling of a total of 2,500 miRNAs in 13 cardiovascular disease patients and 5 healthy controls

DESIGN
In the study presented here, a well-defined cohort of 13 cardiovascular disease cases and 5 healthy controls was used to acquire expression profiles of a total of 2,500 unique genes.

PLATFORM
GPL18117 Exiqon Human miRCURY LNA Array (version 11.0) [miRBase 17]
