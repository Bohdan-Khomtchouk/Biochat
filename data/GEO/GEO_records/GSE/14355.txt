TITLE
Hydrogen peroxide Stress in Desulfovibrio vulgaris Hildenborough (REPLACED BY GSE21438)

ORGANISM
Desulfovibrio vulgaris str. Hildenborough

SUMMARY
Using data from microarray experiments, we investigated the effects of excess hydrogen peroxide on D. vulgaris. Keywords: stress response, time course

DESIGN
Comparison of wild type and deletion mutant cells treated with 1 mM H2O2 to untreated cells at times of 0 and 120 min. See also Series GSE4447 ( http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/projects/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE4447 )
