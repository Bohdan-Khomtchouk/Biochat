TITLE
Expresion data from primary retinal pigment  epithelium (RPE) and immortalized RPE

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We used microarrays to detail the global gene expression of primary RPE and immortalized RPE. We investigated highly expression genes in immortalized RPE.

DESIGN
We classified primary RPE (commercially available human fetal RPE, Lonza) and  immortalized RPE (ARPE-19, ARPE19/HPV-16 and h1RPE7)

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
