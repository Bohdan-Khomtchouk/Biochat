TITLE
RNA-seq analysis of human heart failure

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study is to compare the transcriptome of heart failure patients (with ischemic or dilated cardiomyopathy) undergoing heart transplantation compared with healthy controls.

DESIGN
We analyzed 36 human samples. 13 from ischemic and 13 from dilated human hearts compared with 10 healthy control donors.

PLATFORM
GPL16288 AB 5500xl Genetic Analyzer (Homo sapiens)
