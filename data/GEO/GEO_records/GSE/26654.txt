TITLE
use of aCGH in a large series of prenatal samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
we have conducted a comparative study of the different technologies available for the detection of chromosomal abnormalities in prenatal samples. Here we include results of the aCGH experiments

DESIGN
this series is composed of internal controls plus a year-long (2009-2010) consecutive series of pregnant women with indication for prenatal screening of chromosomal abnormalities a pdf and txt version of the table with 'sample name' plus relevant sample characteristics, including chromosomal abnormalities identified in our work is provided as a supplementary file on the Series record (below).
