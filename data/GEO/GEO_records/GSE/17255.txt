TITLE
Iron (Fe) deficiency in Citrus

ORGANISM
Citrus trifoliata

SUMMARY
Transcription profiling of citrus rootstock Poncirus trifoliata (L.) Raf. Keywords: Abiotic stress (Iron chlorosis)

DESIGN
Total RNA from four replicates for each sample category (Poncirus trifoliata (L.) Raf watered for 60 days with 18 uM Fe-EDDHA or without Fe-EDDHA) were generated and compared.
