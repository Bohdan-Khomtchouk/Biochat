TITLE
Critical role of the hydrogen peroxide-responsive nuclear kinase CK1-alpha-LS in vascular cell activation

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
CK1-alpha-LS was knocked down in human coronary artery smooth muscle cells.  Gene level and exon level changes in expression were assessed.

DESIGN
CK1-alpha-LS was knocked down in human coronary artery smooth muscle cells using retrovirus containing shRNA targeting the alternatively spliced L-insert.

PLATFORM
GPL5188 [HuEx-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Exon 1.0 ST Array [probe set (exon) version]
