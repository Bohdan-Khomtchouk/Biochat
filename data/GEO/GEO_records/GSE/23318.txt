TITLE
Expression array from mature leaves in the genus Arabidopsis

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana ; Arabidopsis halleri subsp. gemmifera ; Arabidopsis lyrata subsp. lyrata ; Arabidopsis thaliana x Arabidopsis lyrata ; Arabidopsis thaliana x Arabidopsis halleri subsp. gemmifera

SUMMARY
We compared the expression between inter-specific hybrids (A. thaliana-A. lyrata, A. thaliana-A. halleri) and mid parent values of their parental lines.

DESIGN
Mature leaves in A. thaliana (Col), A. lyrata ssp. lyrata, A. halleri ssp. gemmifera and interspecific hybrid between A. thaliana and A. lyrata and between A. thaliana and A. halleri

PLATFORM
GPL198 [ATH1-121501] Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array
