TITLE
Identification of iron regulated genes.

ORGANISM
Ustilago maydis

SUMMARY
Identification of transcriptional changes of Ustilago maydis wild type strain FB1 grown in axenic culture in complete medium containing glucose without or with the addition of 10 µM FeSO4. Keywords: gene induction

DESIGN
Strain FB1 (wt) was grown in CM glucose medium in the presence or absence of 10 µM FeSO4.
