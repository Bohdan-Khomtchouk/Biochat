TITLE
Epigenetic profiling at mouse imprinted gene clusters

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Epigenetic profiling of DNA methylation, histone H3 lysine 4 trimethylation and histone H3 lysine 9 trimethylation at imprinted gene clusters in the mouse.

DESIGN
Imprinted control regions were identified by comparing DNA and histone methylation patterns between sperm, heart and cerebellum.

GPL8714 Agilent 015961 Mouse Array
