TITLE
Transcriptional Response of Mycobacterium tuberculosis to Arabinomannan-specific Immune Sera

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis H37Rv

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Mycobacterium tuberculosis H37Rv exposed to immune sera from C57/BL6 mice immunized with a conjugate of Bacillus anthracis protective antigen and arabinomannan vs. protective antigen alone

DESIGN
Two condition experiment, arabinomannan-PA conjugate immune sera exposed vs. PA alone sera exposed. Biological replicates: 3 of each condition, independently exposed and harvested. One replicate per array, third array is a dye-flip.
