TITLE
The genomic effects of dieldrin in the hypothalamus of largemouth bass

ORGANISM
Micropterus salmoides

SUMMARY
This study examines the genomic effects of dieldrin in the hypothalamus of largemouth bass. Dieldrin is an insectide and organic pollutant.

DESIGN
Largemouth bass fed diedrin for two months once a day ad libitium; 4 biological replicates for each group
