TITLE
Identification of potential ABBV-075 responsive markers in mouse skin

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Determination of transcriptional alterations in skin samples from ABBV-075 treated mice

DESIGN
Mice were dosed with vehicle, or 1 mg/kg  ABBV-075,  skin was collected 6 hours and 24 hours post dosing and subjected to total RNA extraction and miroarray analysis.  Vehicle group contains 3 mice and ABBV-075 dosing group contains 4 mice
