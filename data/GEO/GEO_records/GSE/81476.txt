TITLE
PTEN-deficient synovial sarcoma

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptome comparisons by RNAseq of genetically engineered mouse models of synovial sarcoma, expressing SS18-SSX1 or SS18-SSX2 and having homozygous conditional genetic silencing of Pten or wildtype Pten.

DESIGN
6 primary tumor samples with wildtype Pten, 8 primary tumor and 5 metastatic site tumor samples with homozygous disruption of Pten

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
