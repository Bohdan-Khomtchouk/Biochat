TITLE
Dose response of carbon monoxide treatment of M. tuberculosis

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of M. tuberculosis growing in log phase treated with various concentrations of carbon monoxide versus untreated controls Keywords: Dose response

DESIGN
Two condition experiment, CO treated cells at 2000, 200 and 20 ppm versus untreated controls. Biological replicates: 2 replicates per dose, 1 array per replicate
