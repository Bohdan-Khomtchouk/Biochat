TITLE
“Like Sugar in Milk”: Reconstructing the genetic history of the Parsi population

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genomewide SNP data of Parsi population of India has been used to study their origin and migration.

DESIGN
19 samples were analysed with the Illumina platform Human660W-Quad v1.0 Genotyping BeadChip and are described herein.
