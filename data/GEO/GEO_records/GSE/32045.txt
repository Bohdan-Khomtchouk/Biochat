TITLE
Isw1 and Chd1 maintain chromatin organization during transcription [AcH4 data]

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
ChIP-chip assays to determine the occupancy of acetylated histone H4 in wildtype, isw1 and chd1 yeast.

DESIGN
Two color experiment. ChIP/Input. AcH4 data normalized to histone H3 and plotted as mutant over wildtype. Biological replicates=3.
