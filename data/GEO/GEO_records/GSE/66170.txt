TITLE
Using the Porcine SNP60k array to discover copy number variations in high and low fertility boars

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
The raw data from the Porcine SNP60k array was used to discover copy number variations in high and low fertile breeding boars. Analysis was done in SNP and Variation Suite ver. 7.7.8. (SVS, GoldenHelix Inc.) Results were published in Revay et al., BMC Genomics 2015

DESIGN
Arrays were performed on 13 Landrace, 3 Duroc and 20 Yorkshire boars of high and low fertility by the service provider DNA Landmarks.

PLATFORM
GPL11379 Illumina Porcine SNP60 BeadChip (WG-410)
