TITLE
Stability of the CpG island methylator phenotype during glioblastoma progression.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The Illumina HumanMethylation 450 BeadChip was used on 20 matched pairs of early and late secondary gliomas to assess the changes in DNA methylation during progression.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 40 samples were hybridised to the Illumina Infinium 450k Human Methylation Beadchip.

GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
