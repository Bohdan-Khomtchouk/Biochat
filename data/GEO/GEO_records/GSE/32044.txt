TITLE
Histone H3 K36me3 in wildtype, isw1 and chd1 yeast strains

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
ChIP-chip assays to measure the occupancy of histone H3 K36me3 over the yeast genome in  wildtype, isw1 and chd1 yeast strains. ChIPs were done with K36me3 antibody (Ab 9050) in G1 arrested yeast cells.

DESIGN
Two color experiment. Biological replicates=3 per IP per cell type.

PLATFORM
GPL13972 Agilent-031697 Yeast 8x60k Tiling Array
