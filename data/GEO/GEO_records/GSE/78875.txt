TITLE
Breast cancer subtype dictates DNA methylation and ALDH1A3-mediated expression of tumor suppressor RARRES1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of cancerous and normal breast cell lines, with and without decitabine. The Illumina HumanMethylation450 beadchip array was used.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 36 samples were hybridised to the Illumina Human Methylation 450 bead chip array

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
