TITLE
Zika virus infections of porcine fetal brain

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
Zika virus was inoculated at mid gestation (day 50) in to either intra cerebrally, or interperotoneally and intra amniotic. Controls were mock injected and subject to similar surgical proceedures. Fetal tissue were obtained. Sampled fetal brains are from littermates and are not the fetus directly infected.  After 28 days post infection.

DESIGN
examination of zika infection and route of infection

PLATFORM
GPL20983 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Sus scrofa)
