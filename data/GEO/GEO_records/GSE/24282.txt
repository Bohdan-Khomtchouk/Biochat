TITLE
CGH microarray analysis of human prostate adenocarcinoma and normal samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Prostate adenocarcinoma and matched adjacent normal samples were profiled for copy number with the Agilent 244A CGH Array to support a study of deep transcriptional sequencing on these samples.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment: Prostate adenocarcinoma versus matched normal from three separate patients.
