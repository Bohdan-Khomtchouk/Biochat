TITLE
Deptor transcriptionally regulates Endoplasmic Reticulum homeostasis in Multiple Myeloma cells.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed a RNA-seq analysis by using mRNA from KMS27 cells transfected with siRNA Deptor or siRNA negative control.

DESIGN
The raw FASTQ files are available under SRA project SRP072090, bioproject PRJNA315795. There are two samples: control and silenced for Deptor, loaded in triplicate.
