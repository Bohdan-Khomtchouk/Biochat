TITLE
Zebrafish ovarian follicle transcriptome

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
SAGE analysis of fully grown ovarian follicles has been carried out to supply a resource for identification of important molecules involved in vertebrate oogenesis and in early stages of embryonic development. Keywords: SAGE

DESIGN
About 250 full-grown ovarian follicles were obtained after gentle squeezing the abdomen of five mature zebrafish females.
