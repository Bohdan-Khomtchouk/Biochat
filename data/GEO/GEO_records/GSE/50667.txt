TITLE
The pattern of genomic instability during bladder cancer progression (part 2)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The Affymetrix SNP6.0 arrays were done for two metachronous tumors and the blood from three patients. It was used to define copy number abnormalities in order to support subclonal population analysis in this patient.

DESIGN
Nine samples were analysed concerning two metachronous tumor samples and a blood sample from the three different patients.

PLATFORM
GPL6801 [GenomeWideSNP_6] Affymetrix Genome-Wide Human SNP 6.0 Array
