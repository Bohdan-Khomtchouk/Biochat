TITLE
HT29 colon cancer cells: siRNA targeted gene knockdowns vs scrambled siRNA control or GFP siRNA control

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of HT29 colon cancer cells comparing siRNA-targeted knockdowns of glutaminase (GLUT), cytokeratin 20 (KER), DEAD/H box polypeptide 32 (DEAD), ribosomal protein L32 (RPL32) and transcription factor DP1 (TFDP1) versus scrambled siRNA control and GFP siRNA control Keywords: two-color spotted cDNA microarray, siRNA-targeted gene knockdowns
