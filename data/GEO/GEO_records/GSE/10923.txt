TITLE
NAP provides neuroprotection against kainic acid-induced cell death

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
NAP - neuroprotective peptide demonstrates increase in neuronal survival when injected into the hippocampus of rats in the model of epilepsy Microarray analysis was used to understand the expression of genes following KA treatment and the changes in gene expression following KA+NAP treatment Keywords: stress response
