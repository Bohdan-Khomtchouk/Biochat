TITLE
Comparison of the effects of GMCSF, IL-4 and 15kDa Granulysin on elutriated monocytes: a 48 hour time point analysis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling of elutriated monocytes cultured for 48 hours in RPMI-1640, 10% AB plamsa and one of the following: GMCSF (56IU/ml), IL-4 (2000 IU/ml), 15 kDa Granulysin (10 nM) Sources tested: monocytes form 3 donors tested at time 0, 4 hours, 12 hours, 24 hours, 48 hours

DESIGN
5 time points were compared: day 0 (cells before start the culture), 4, 12, 24, 48 hours

PLATFORM
GPL6480 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Probe Name version)
