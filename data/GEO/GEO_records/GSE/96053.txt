TITLE
Dlk response to optic nerve injury

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression of whole retina after optic nerve crush. The " SAMPLE_ID " sample characteristic is a sample identifier internal to Genentech. The ID of this project in Genentech's ExpressionPlot database is PRJ0004847

DESIGN
Microarray analysis of whole retina after optic nerve crush in tamoxifen-treated adult DLK conditional knockout mice with and without CreER transgene using uninjured eye as control.
