TITLE
Expression of the human Cathepsin L inhibitor hurpin in mice: skin alterations and increased carcinogenesis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Aim of this study was to identify gene expression differences in the skin of hurpin (=headpin=serpinB13) transgenic mice in comparison to wildtype mice. Keywords: genetic modification

DESIGN
Comparison of 4 transgenic mice with 3 wildtype mice. One wildtye sample was used two times. Age: 8 month. Gender: mixed (male and female)

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
