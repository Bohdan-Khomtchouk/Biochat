TITLE
Anopheles gambiae G3 small RNA sequence: Adult Female Body

ORGANISM
Anopheles gambiae

SUMMARY
We provide a broad overview of sequence diversity in An. gambiae mature microRNAs, including annotation of novel microRNAs identified in this study.

DESIGN
Non-coding RNA profiling by high throughput sequencing, in duplicate, using Illumina GAIIx.
