TITLE
Expression of Cell Cycle related genes in developing mouse cerebellum

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The goal of the study was to analize expression of cell cycle related genes during postnatal development of Ts65Dn (Down syndrome mouse model) and control mice cerebellum. Keywords: Gene expression study in mouse model of disease

DESIGN
RNA from 3 controls and 3 Ts65Dn postnatal day 2 cerebellum were analized on separate arrays.
