TITLE
Porcine IVM GDNF

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
Treatment of oocytes derived from large (4-6 mm; LG) or small ( > 3 mm; SM) follicles with glial cell line-derived neurotrophic factor (GDNF) during in vitro maturation.

DESIGN
Four-condition experiment, SM and LG oocytes, each with and without GDNF. Biological replicates: 9 per condition, independently collected. Pools of three replicates per array.

PLATFORM
GPL14925 Agilent-031068 EmbryoGene Porcine Array Version 1 (EMPV1)
