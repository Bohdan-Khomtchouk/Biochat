TITLE
Temporal patterns of gene expression associated with tuberous root formation and development in sweetpotato (Ipomoea batatas)

ORGANISM
Ipomoea batatas

SUMMARY
Investigation of whole transcriptome gene expression level during tuberous root formation and development in sweetpotato (Ipomoea batatas) cv. Guangshu 87 Identification of transcription factors (TFs) during tuberous root formation and development in sweetpotato (Ipomoea batatas) cv. Guangshu 87

DESIGN
A total of 7 samples were analyzed using RNA isolated from sweetpotato roots at 10, 15, 20, 30, 60, 90, 120days after transplanting. Each sample had two biological replicates.
