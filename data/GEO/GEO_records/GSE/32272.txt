TITLE
Expression data from chick cochlea and utricle

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
Inner ear auditory and vestibular tissues differ in their responses to mechanical stimuli.

DESIGN
Chick cochlea and utricle sensory epithelia were microdissected at E20-E21.  RNA was extracted and cRNA hybridized to Affymetrix microarrays.

PLATFORM
GPL3213 [Chicken] Affymetrix Chicken Genome Array
