TITLE
FSTL1 promotes keratinocyte migration.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study identifies FSTL1 as key component of intiating keratinocyte migration in skin

DESIGN
Total RNA from N/TERT-1 keratinocytes transfected with a non-targeting siRNA or Smartpool siRNA against FSTL1  were subjected to microarray analysis
