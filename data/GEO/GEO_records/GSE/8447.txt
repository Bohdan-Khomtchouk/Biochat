TITLE
c-Myc binding in HL60 cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of technical variance of ChIP-on-Chip studies by characterization of Myc-binding in HL60 cells. Keywords: Chip-on-chip

DESIGN
Characterization of c-Myc binding in HL60 cells.  Thirteen biologically independent replicates of growing HL60 cells were subjected to ChIP with an N-terminal c-Myc antibody and hybridized to CpG island microarrays

PLATFORM
GPL2040 UHN Human CpG 12K Array (HCGI12K)
