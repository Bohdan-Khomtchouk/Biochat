TITLE
Thermotoga maritima wild type minimal media expression analysis

ORGANISM
Thermotoga maritima MSB8

SUMMARY
An eight chip study using total RNA recovered from separate wild-type cultures of Thermotoga maritima at mid-log with 3 different minimal sugar media.

DESIGN
A eight-chip study using total RNA recovered from separate wild-type cultures of Thermotoga maritima at mid-log with 3 different minimal sugar media. 4 biological replicates maltose, 2 biological replicates L-arabinose, 2 biological replicates cellobiose.
