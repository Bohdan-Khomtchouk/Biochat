TITLE
NaCl Stress in Desulfovibrio vulgaris Hildenborough (Series 2)

ORGANISM
Desulfovibrio vulgaris str. Hildenborough

SUMMARY
Increased salinity constitutes an important and frequent fluctuation faced by D. vulgaris in its natural habitat. Using data from microarray experiments, we investigated the effects of excess NaCl on D. vulgaris. Keywords: stress response, time course

DESIGN
Comparison of cells treated with 250mM NaCl to untreated cells at times of 0 and 120 min.  See also Series GSE4447: http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/projects/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE4447
