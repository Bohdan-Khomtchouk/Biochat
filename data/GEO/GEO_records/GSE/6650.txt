TITLE
Comparison of normal and heat shock trophozoites

ORGANISM
Entamoeba histolytica

SUMMARY
We examined the stress response in Entamoeba histolytica trophozoites by comparing untreated log-phase HM-1:IMSS trophozoites to those subjected to heat shock at 42C for 1 hour. Keywords: stress reponse

DESIGN
We compared two arrays from normal trophozoites to two arrays from trophozoites subjected to heat shock.
