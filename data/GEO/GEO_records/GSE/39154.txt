TITLE
Ogt chromatin recruitment is mediated by TET proteins in mouse ES cells [ChIP-Seq]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
These data include the genome wide location analysis of Ogt by ChIP of Ogt in mouse ES cells.

DESIGN
Precipitation of formaldehyde cross-linked chromatin prepared from mouse ES cell using specific antibody against OGT and IgG as control.
