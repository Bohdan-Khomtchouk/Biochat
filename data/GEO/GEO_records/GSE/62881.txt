TITLE
Influence Of Insulin On Oocyte Maturation In Invitro Produced Bovine Embryos

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of transcriptome bovine blastocysts Day 8, deriving from oocytes matured under different insulin concentrations as model for metabolic imbalance

DESIGN
Three conditions experiment: control, Low Insulin (INS0.1), High Insulin (INS10) treatments.  Biological replicates: 4 for each treatment. One replicate per array.
