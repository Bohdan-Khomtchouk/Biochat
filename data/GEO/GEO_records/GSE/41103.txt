TITLE
Rice Submergence Stress Response

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
Two rice genotypes, M202 (intolerant) and M202(Sub1), a tolerant genotype, were submerged for 1 day and 6 day and sampled for identifying differentially expressed genes.

DESIGN
4 samples. M202- 1 day and 6 day; M202(Sub1)- 1 day and 6 day

PLATFORM
GPL2025 [Rice] Affymetrix Rice Genome Array
