TITLE
Gene expression analysis of patient (HN137) primary and metastatic tumor derived cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparison of copy number variations and gene expression profiles of cell lines obtained from the primary and lymph node metastatic tumors from a single patient.

DESIGN
Gene expression analysis of patient (HN137) primary and metastatic tumor derived cell lines. Total RNA obtained from HN137-Pri and HN137-Met cell lines for microarray analysis.
