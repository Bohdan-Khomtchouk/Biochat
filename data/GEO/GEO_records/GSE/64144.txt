TITLE
Evaluation of methyl-binding domain based enrichment approaches revisited

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
A comparison of methylMiner and MethylCap, two methylation enrichment kits

DESIGN
The main analysis include two individuals (mice) in technical duplicates from various lab/technical conditions. The results are compared with previous studies of two human samples from a number of lab/technical conditions (human data available at dbGaP: record number phs000608.v1.p1).  In addition a DNA from a third mouse is used for investigations of specific alterations of the protocol.

PLATFORM
GPL18061 AB 5500xl-W Genetic Analysis System (Mus musculus)
