TITLE
Nicotinana tabacum cv. K326 long day diurnal timecourse: Root, Shoot, Shoot Apex

ORGANISM
Nicotiana tabacum

SUMMARY
Measurement of gene expression over a diurnal timecourse in different tissues of N.tabacum. The primary aim of the collection of this data was to validate predicted gene models in the associated publication

DESIGN
36 samples covering 3 tissues and 4 timepoints. Each in triplicate

PLATFORM
GPL13760 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Nicotiana tabacum)
