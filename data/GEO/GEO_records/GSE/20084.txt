TITLE
Temporal gene expression by Ephedra herba in brain, heart and liver of mouse

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
A microarray analysis was conducted using the brain, heart, and liver tissues of mouse orally administrated aqueous extracts of Ephedra herba, in which the expression level of many genes were altered depending on the duration of treatment time.

DESIGN
Aqueous extracts of Ephedra herba were administered orally for 1 hr, 6 hrs, 12 hrs, 24 hrs, 3 days, 5 days and 10 days on mice.
