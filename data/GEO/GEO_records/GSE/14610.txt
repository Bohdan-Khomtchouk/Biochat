TITLE
AURATUS goldfish environmental genomics projects: quinpirole in the hypothalamus

ORGANISM
Carassius auratus

SUMMARY
Sexually mature female goldfish were administered 2 i.p. injections: 1) 0.1% DMSO in 0.6% saline, and; 2) 0.6% saline OR quinpirole (2 ug/g) dissolved in 0.6% saline.

DESIGN
A common reference design was used: 4 independent groups of hypothalami from sexually mature female goldfish treated with 40 ug/g SKF 38393 hybridized against a common vehicle control. Slides 5, 11, 14, 26.

GPL3735 AURATUS: The goldfish environmental genomics project v1.0
