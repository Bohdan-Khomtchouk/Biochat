TITLE
AltitudeOmics microRNA expression data

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Acclimatization to high altitude over 21 days, and upon re-ascent 7 and 21 days post-descent. See Subudhi et al. (2014) PLoS One 9(3):e92191, PMCID PMC3962396, PMID 24658407 for experiment details.

DESIGN
98 total samples representing 21 subjects at 6 time points (not all subjects represented at all time points) were taken. Significance was determined by change vs. baseline (sea level).

PLATFORM
GPL16384 [miRNA-3] Affymetrix Multispecies miRNA-3 Array
