TITLE
Human Breast Cancer Cell MCF-7: Control vs. Gli1 Transfected

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of breast cancer cell line MCF-7 stably transfected with  pcDNA3.1-Gli1 comparing control ones transfected with CON

DESIGN
Total RNA of cells transfected with con or pcDNA3.1-GLI1 was extracted using the TRIZOL Reagent according to the manufacturer's instructions. Microarray analysis of Gli1-MCF-7 vs. pcDNA3.1-MCF-7 cells in dye-swap experiment
