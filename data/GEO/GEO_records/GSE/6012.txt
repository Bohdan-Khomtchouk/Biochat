TITLE
Skin biopsies from atopic eczema and healthy controls

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The aim of this study was to find disease-associated genes in atopic eczema. Keywords: Skin biopsy, DNA microarray, atopic eczema

DESIGN
Skin biopsies were analyzed from ten patients with active atopic eczema and ten healthy controls.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
