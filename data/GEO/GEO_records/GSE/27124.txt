TITLE
Comparison of pan-inhibitor of HDACS (TSA) to specific HDAC8 inhibitor (HDAC Inhibitor XIX, Compound 2) in human myometrium

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To compare gene expression in human myometrium after TSA treatment to gene expression after treatment with a specific HDAC8 inhibitor

DESIGN
Four Control, four TSA and four Compound 2 samples (2 patients, replicated) derived from non-pregnant myometrial strips

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
