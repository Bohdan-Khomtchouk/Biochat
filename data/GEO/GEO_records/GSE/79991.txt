TITLE
Characterization of melanoma cells exposed to melanoma-specific CD8+ T cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human low passage melanoma cell lines were compared after 24h culture alone or in presence of melanoma-specific CD8+ T cell clones

DESIGN
Three melanoma cell lines where either left untreated or co-cultured with melanoma-specific CD8+ T cells for 24h

PLATFORM
GPL17077 Agilent-039494 SurePrint G3 Human GE v2 8x60K Microarray 039381 (Probe Name version)

CITATIONS
