TITLE
Molecular characterization of Georgia cohort of primary breast cancer FFPE tissues on custom breast cancer DASL panel

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of 143 formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded (FFPE) primary breast tumors using a Custom Breast Cancer Panel and Human Cancer Panel for the DASL platform. Molecular markers between the pathology defined subtypes of breast cancer were assessed to hypothesize potential therapeutic targets specific to the subtypes
