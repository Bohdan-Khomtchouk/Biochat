TITLE
NeuroD2 ChIP-SEQ from postnatal cortex

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
NeuroD2 targets were identified from postnatal day 0 cerebral cortex tissue.

DESIGN
The cerebral cortex (dorsal telencelphalon) from P0 mouse pup was dissected and ChIP-SEQ was performed using three separate antibodies against NeuroD2.
