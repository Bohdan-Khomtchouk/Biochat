TITLE
Role of NPR1 in SA mediated transcription

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Affymetrix expression analysis was used to validate the role of NPR1 in SA mediated transcription.

DESIGN
The RNA of col-0 and npr1-1 mutant were isolated after 24hr water and SA treatment.The ATH1 gene chip was used for expression analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL198 [ATH1-121501] Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array
