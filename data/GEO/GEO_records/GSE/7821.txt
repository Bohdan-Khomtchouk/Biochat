TITLE
Expression data from human intestinal biopsies

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Whole genome expression profiling of 40 healthy human twins (20 monozygotic, 20 dizygotic) Keywords: heritability test

DESIGN
Sampling was controlled for sampling time, fasting conditions, sampling conditions, sampling storage and processing. 10 twinpairs (20 individuals) were dizygotic and 10 twinpairs (20 individuals) were monozygotic twins.
