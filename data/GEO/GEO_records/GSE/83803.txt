TITLE
RNA-seq in heads from Monomorium pharaonis nurses and foragers

ORGANISM
Monomorium pharaonis

SUMMARY
Replicate pools of heads from 10 individual Monomorium pharaonis workers, collected in the act of nursing larvae or foraging for food, each from a separate replicate colony

DESIGN
Replicate colonies contained ~300-400 workers, ~300-400 brood, and 10 queens; nurse and forager workers were collected in the act of nursing larvae or foraging for food
