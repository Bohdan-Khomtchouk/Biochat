TITLE
Poplar tissue types 2

ORGANISM
Populus trichocarpa

SUMMARY
Global gene expression pattern of phloem and xylem tissue were determined using a Nimblegen microarray based on JGI v1.1 gene models. Xylem tissue from both Populus trichocarpa Nisqually-1 and Populus tremula X Populus alba hybrid were used in the study.
