TITLE
PSII_DCMU_vs_PSII

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Series of 6 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (PSII_DCMU) and control (PSII) each. Comparison of plants grown under PSII-specific light and treated with the electron transport inhibitor DCMU versus plants grown under PSII-specific light without DCMU treatment. T. Pfannschmidt, unpublished Keywords: repeat sample
