TITLE
Gene expression profiling of human cardiac macrophages sorted from failing human hearts

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling of human cardiac macrophages sorted from failing human hearts

DESIGN
Human macropages were sorted from tissue explants obtained from patients with dilated and ischemic cardiomyopathy using flow cytometry. Cells were sorted directly into lysis buffer. Sunstes examined included CCR2+ monocytes, CCR2+ macrophages, and CCR2- macrophages.
