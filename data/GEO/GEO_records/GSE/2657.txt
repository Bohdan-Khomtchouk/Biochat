TITLE
Classical Hodgkin’s Lymphoma: Molecular evidence for specific alterations of circulating T lymphocytes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles were performed from CD3+ T cells isolated from peripheral blood samples of untreated patients with cHL versus 2 control groups consisting of healthy donors and patients with sarcoidosis.
