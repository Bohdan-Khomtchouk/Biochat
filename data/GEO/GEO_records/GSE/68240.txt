TITLE
Gene expression profiling for human iPS-derived motor neurons from sporadic ALS patients reveals a strong association between mitochondrial functions and neurodegeneration

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Differentiated motor neurons from hiPSC derived from peripheral nerve fibroblasts of sporadic ALS patients and evaluated the gene expression profile by means microarray-linked to specific analysis tools.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, ALS patients motor neurons vs. controls. Biological replicates: 3 ALS replicates, 3 control replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL14550 Agilent-028004 SurePrint G3 Human GE 8x60K Microarray (Probe Name Version)
