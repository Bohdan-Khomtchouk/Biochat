TITLE
AURATUS goldfish environmental genomics projects: SCH 23390 in the telencephalon

ORGANISM
Carassius auratus

SUMMARY
Sexually regressing female goldfish were administered 2 i.p. injections: 1) 40 ug/g SCH 23390 in 0.1% DMSO and 0.6% saline, and; 2) 0.6% saline Tissues were harvested 5h post-injection.
