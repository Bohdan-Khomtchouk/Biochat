TITLE
Genomic profile of fatigued men receiving localized radiation therapy

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Microarray gene expression of peripheral blood of the prostate cancer patients receiving localized external beam radiation therapy (EBRT) Assay processed at EBRT time points, baseline, midpoint (days 19-21) and endpoint (days 38-42)

DESIGN
Explore the effect of fatigue on gene expression at the three EBRT time points Citation: Biol. Res. Nurs. (2015 Accepted)

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
