TITLE
HCT116 colon cancer CRC in high and low glucose

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
HCT116 Kras WT and Kras G13D isogenic pair cell lines were cultured in either 25 mM or 1 mM for one week.

DESIGN
Two independent replicate samples for each cell line and for each treatment were profiled using Affymetrix HG U133 plus 2.0 arrays

GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
