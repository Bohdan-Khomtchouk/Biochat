TITLE
Shigella Apoptosis Arrays

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Array data from HeLa cells that remained Uninfected or were infected with wildtype S. flexneri serotype 2a strain 2457T.  These groupings are further divided such that Uninfected or Infected cells remained untreated or were treated with the apopotosis inducer staurosporine (STS).  The design of the experiment was a time course and samples were harvested at the indicated time points (hr).  All hybridizations were conducted against a common reference sample.
