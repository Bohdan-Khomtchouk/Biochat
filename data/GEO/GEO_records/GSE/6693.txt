TITLE
Time-dependent response of hippocampal CA1 and CA3 to oxidative stress

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Mechanistic study on the differential responses of the two hippocampal adjoining regions, i.e., CA1 and CA3, to elevated oxidative stress. Keywords: Time course stress response study

DESIGN
Time course study. Involved four time points and comparison of two brain regions.
