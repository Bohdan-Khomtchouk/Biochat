TITLE
Transcriptome Analysis Of Bovine Expanded Blastocysts Produced In Vivo Vs In Vitro Sof Opu

ORGANISM

SUMMARY
Comparison of gene expression from expanded bovine blastocysts collected 7 days after fertilization and produced in vivo vs in vitro-SOF-OPU

DESIGN
