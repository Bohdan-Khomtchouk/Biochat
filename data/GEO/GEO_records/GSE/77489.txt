TITLE
Foot and mouth disease virus (FMDV) infection in Indian cattle (Bos indicus): Profiling of serum microRNAs

ORGANISM
Bos indicus

SUMMARY
Profiling of miRNA in serum of FMDV infected cattle, to understand disease pathogenesis and to identify FMD specific miRNA biomarkers to aid early pre-clinical diagnosis

DESIGN
Agilent  one-color miRNA experiment,Organism: Bos indicus,  Bovine miRNA 8x60K Microarray (AMADID: 072447)

PLATFORM
GPL21397 Agilent Bovine miRNA 8x60K Microarray (AMADID: 072447)
