TITLE
Comparative transcriptome analysis of geographically distinct virulent and attenuated Babesia bovis

ORGANISM
Babesia bovis

SUMMARY
Geographical distinct virulent Babesia bovis strains have similar gene expression changes as they go through attenuation.

DESIGN
Pair end RNA-sequencing reads on three biological replicate sample pairs of virulent parent and attenuated derivative Babesia bovis strain isolated in Argentina.

PLATFORM
GPL17815 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Babesia bovis)
