TITLE
Universal corneal epithelial-like cells derived from human pluripotent stem cells in xeno-free and albumin-free condition

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Time course experiment of the CEC differentiation. Samples collected from differentiation 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 weeks.

DESIGN
A total RNA was harvested from the differentiating cells every two weeks, starting from the undifferentiated H9 to week 8 differentiated cells.
