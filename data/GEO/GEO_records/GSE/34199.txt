TITLE
The NIH Human Pluripotent Stem Cell Database (Agilent, miRNA)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
MicroRNA profiling was performed on RNA samples matched to those included in the NIH Human Pluripotent Stem Cell Database (Series GSE32923). Twenty undifferentiated human embryonic stem cell lines and 4 human tissues were analyzed. Expanded descriptions of methods used are available at:

DESIGN
49 samples: 38 human ESC (UNDIFFerentiated), 1 human Brain, 1 human Heart, 1 human Liver, 1 human Ovary and 7 processing controls (UniRef).
