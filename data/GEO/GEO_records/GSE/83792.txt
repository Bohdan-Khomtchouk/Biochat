TITLE
Effect of post-ruminal butyrate infusion on gene expression in the duodenum of growing sheep

ORGANISM
Ovis aries

SUMMARY
Objectives were to determine the effect of supplying butyrate post-ruminally on gene expression in the duodenum of growing polled Dorset lambs.

DESIGN
The transcriptome of the duodenum from 9 lambs were examined for expression differences in response to butyrate infusion into the abomasum.

PLATFORM
GPL19480 [OviGene-1_1-st] Ovine Gene 1.1 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
