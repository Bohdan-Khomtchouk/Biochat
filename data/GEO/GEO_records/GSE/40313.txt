TITLE
Genomic analysis of the combined effects of H-NS and co-regulators on E. coli gene expression

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli str. K-12 substr. MG1655

SUMMARY
This entry refers to transcriptome analysis of E. coli with single deletions of hns, stpA, hha and ydgT and double deletions of hns with each of the other three regulators mentioned.
