TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of fasted mouse livers

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report application of RNA-seq to quantify gene expression changes in fasted mouse livers compared to re-fed controls.

DESIGN
RNA-seq from livers of re-fed and 48h fasted mice.

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
