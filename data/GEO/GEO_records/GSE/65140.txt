TITLE
Gene expression profiling of SHSY5Y cells exposed to Endosulfan

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
SHSY5Y cells grown in MEM:F12 media (1:1) were treated with Endosulfan and total RNA was isolated from cells after 24 hour exposure Three replicate were used for the experiment

DESIGN
Cells grown in  75mm2 flask. three replicates for each sample
