TITLE
Porphyromonas gingivalis W50 Heme-excess vs Heme-limitation

ORGANISM
Porphyromonas gingivalis

SUMMARY
Transcription profiling of P. gingivalis W50 grown in continuous culture under conditions of heme-excess and heme-limitation.

DESIGN
Reference design (using Cy5 labelled genomic DNA as the reference) to compare two conditions: heme-excess vs heme-limitation.  Three samples for each condition, independently grown.
