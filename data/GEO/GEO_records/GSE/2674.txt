TITLE
Gene expression in white adipose tissue stimulated by beta 3 adrenergic receptor agonist

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Experiment designed to identify differences in gene expression profile in white adipose tissue upon stimulation by beta 3 adrenergic receptor agonist. This series compares 2 groups of C57BL/6J acutely treated with CL-316,243 or Saline for 3 hours. Keywords: parallel sample

PLATFORM
