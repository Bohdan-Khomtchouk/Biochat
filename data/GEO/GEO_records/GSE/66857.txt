TITLE
Microarray analysis of gene expression in aging in mouse sabmandibular gland.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression pattern of young (8 weeks old) and old (50 weeks old) mice sabmandibular gland was analyzed for aging dependent gene expression in sabmandibular gland.

DESIGN
Total RNA  obtained from isolated mouse sabmandibular gland subjected to 50 weeks old mice compared to  8 weeks old mice.

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
