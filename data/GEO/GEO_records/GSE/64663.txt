TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of male flowers corresponding to anther developmental stages in Cryptomeria japonica

ORGANISM
Cryptomeria japonica

SUMMARY
Investigation of gene expression changes among anther development in wild type and male sterile mutant.

DESIGN
Male strobili of wild-type were collected at 10 stages with three biological repeats (total 30 samples) and male strobili of mutant were collected at stages 3 to 6 with three biological repeats (total 12 samples).
