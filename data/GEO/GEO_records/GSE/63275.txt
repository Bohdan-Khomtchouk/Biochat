TITLE
Expression analysis of kairomone responsive genes in Daphnia pulex

ORGANISM
Daphnia pulex

SUMMARY
Investigation of gene expression level changes in Daphnia pulex MFP strain between in the presence or absence of Chaoborus kairomone.

DESIGN
12 samples (4 conditions x 3 replicates) in one 12-plex NimbleGen array GPL11278
