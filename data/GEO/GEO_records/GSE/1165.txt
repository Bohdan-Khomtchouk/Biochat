TITLE
ML4c_rec_24h_vs_ML_4C

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Series of 6 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (ML4c_rec_24h) and control (ML_4C) each. Comparison of plants recovered for 24 h in medium light at 20 °C after a period of 24 h of exposure to photoinhibitory conditions in medium light at 4 °C  versus plants exposed to photoinhibitory conditions of medium light at 4 °C for 24 h. A. Haldrup, unpublished Keywords: repeat sample
