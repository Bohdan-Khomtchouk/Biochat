TITLE
Ovarian cancer cell line RMG1: ALDEFLUOR positive vs. negative

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of ALDH1 high cancer stem like cells of ovarian cancer cell line, sorted out using ALDEFLUOR assay.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, ALDEFLUOR positive vs. negative cells. Biological replicates: 1 ALDEFLUOR positive replicate, 1 negative replicate.
