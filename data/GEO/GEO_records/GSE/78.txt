TITLE
Congenital heart disease in Csx/ Nkx2.5 mutant embryos

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We have developed gene-targeted mice with deletion of Nkx2.5.  Embryos were isolated at embryonic day 9.5 and the middle third containing the heart was run on Affymetrix  Mu11kA and Mu11kB arrays.  For more information about this model see Keywords = Congenital heart disease Keywords: other
