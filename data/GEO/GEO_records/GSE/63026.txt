TITLE
Gene expression analysis of Neoechinulin B (NeoB)-treated Huh7.5.1 cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Huh-7.5.1 cells were treated with 0.2% DMSO, 20 microM NeoB for 24 h.  Treatment with 0.2% DMSO for 24h was prepared as non-treated Huh7.5.1 cells. Huh7.5.1 cells were kindly provided by Prof. Francis Chisari at The Scripps Research Institute.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from NeoB-treated and un-treated Huh7.5.1 cells

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
