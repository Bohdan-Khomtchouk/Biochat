TITLE
Expression data for LMF

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study compared the expression signatures of HL60 cells +/- tretinoin.  This data was then used for another study showing a method for high-throughput gene expression signature analysis. Keywords: expression analysis

DESIGN
The experiment consists of 3 HL60 samples treated with DMSO and 3 HL60 samples treated with tretinoin.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
