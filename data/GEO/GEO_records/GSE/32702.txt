ORGANISM
Equus caballus

SUMMARY
Custom exon aCGH analysis of copy number across the genomes of 16 horse breeds

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, All breed samples were compared to a single Thoroughbred reference, Reference was then compared to Twilight (DNA from horse used for reference genome assembly)
