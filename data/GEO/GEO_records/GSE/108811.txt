TITLE
Endoplasmic reticulum stress engenders immune-resistant, latent pancreatic cancer metastases

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Latent pancreatic cancer metastases are created when T cells select disseminated cancer cells in which immune resistance and quiescence have been imposed by endoplasmic stress

DESIGN
Comparison by single-cell RNA sequencing of two subpopulation of in vitro pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma cells

PLATFORM
GPL19057 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Mus musculus)
