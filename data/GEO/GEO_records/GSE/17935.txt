TITLE
Gene expression in mouse embryonic heart

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study was conducted to examine normal gene expression in the heart during mouse embryonic development.

DESIGN
Wild type embryo hearts (n=5) were collected at stage E12.5 and total RNA was isolated for analysis by Affymetrix mouse genome 430A GeneChip.

PLATFORM
GPL339 [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
