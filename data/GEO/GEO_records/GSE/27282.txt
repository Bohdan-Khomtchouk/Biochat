TITLE
Comparison of B6/D2 Whole Brain Alternative Splicing

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This dataset consists of whole brain samples from 10 B6 and 12 D2 mice, in order to assess the amount of strain-specific alternative splicing.

DESIGN
This design consists of whole brain total RNA samples from 10 B6 and 12 D2 mice
