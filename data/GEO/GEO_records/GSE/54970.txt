TITLE
Expression data from dendritic cells treated with IFNβ for 2.5 hours and control

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We used microarray to characterize interferon stimulated genes in dendritic cells

DESIGN
Monocyte-derived DCs were obtained from healthy human blood donors and were treated with IFNβ for 2.5 hours. Naive DCs served as negative control.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
