TITLE
EHMT1 and EHMT2 inhibition induce fetal hemoglobin expression [ChIP-seq]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Using UNC0638 and genetic assays to inhibit EHMT1/2 and derepress fetal hemoglobin in adult hematopoietic cells.

DESIGN
ChIP-seq for 2 histone modifications and total H3 (for quantitative normalization) in biological triplicates of cord blood, adult bone marrow, and UNC0638-treated adult bone marrow.

GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens) GPL18573 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Homo sapiens)
