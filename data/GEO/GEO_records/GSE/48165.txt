TITLE
Transcripyome analysis of Brucella melitensis 16M and otpR mutant using Solexa/Illumina platform

ORGANISM
Brucella melitensis

SUMMARY
We sequenced the mRNA of Brucella melitensis 16M and otpR mutant under acid stress to identify genes that were regulated by regulator OtpR.

DESIGN
Examination of mRNA levels in individual sample under acid stress.
