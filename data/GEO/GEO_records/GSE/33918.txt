TITLE
The small molecule compound P2 in HCT116p53-/- and HCT116p53+/+ cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Global gene expression in HCT116p53-/- and HCT116p53+/+ cells

DESIGN
HCT116p53-/- and p53+/+ were untreated and treated with P2 small molecule compound at 25 microM, total RNA was isolated and gene expression was analyzed using Illumina chips

PLATFORM
GPL6883 Illumina HumanRef-8 v3.0 expression beadchip
