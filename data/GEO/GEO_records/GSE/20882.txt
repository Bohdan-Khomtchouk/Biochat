TITLE
mRNA-Seq of head tissue from Drosophila mojavensis

ORGANISM
Drosophila mojavensis

SUMMARY
We sequenced mRNA from head tissue of females and male of Drosophila mojavensis to identify genes differentially expressed between the sexes and sex-specific alternative splicing events. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODEDataReleasePolicyFinal2008.pdf

DESIGN
Comparison of expresssion profiles in female and male head tissue from D. mojavensis

GPL10180 Illumina Genome Analyzer (Drosophila mojavensis) GPL10181 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Drosophila mojavensis)
