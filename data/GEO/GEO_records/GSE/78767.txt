TITLE
Natural DNA methylation variation in Lotus japonicus

ORGANISM
Lotus japonicus

SUMMARY
Natural DNA methylation variation was examined in a population of wild Lotus japonicus accessions from Japan

DESIGN
Whole genome DNA methylation data for 18 wild Lotus japonicus accessions (two with replicates) grown at 20C

PLATFORM
GPL20925 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Lotus japonicus)
