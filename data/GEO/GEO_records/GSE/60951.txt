TITLE
A unique chromatin complex occupies young alpha-satellite arrays of human centromeres

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Using high-resolution chromatin immunoprecipitation (ChIP) of centromere components and clustering of sequence data we find that specific dimeric alpha-satellite units shared by multiple individuals dominate functional human centromeres.

DESIGN
We have analyzed the chromatin landscape of the human genome using paired-end MNase-seq
