TITLE
Genome-wide maps of chromatin state in EndoC-βH1 Cells 

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We report the actively transcribed chromatin regions in EndoC-βH1 Cells that are associated with histone H3 lysine 27 acetylation mark.

DESIGN
Examination the histone modification H3K27Ac in EndoC-βH1 Cells by deep sequencing, in triplicate, using Illumina Hiseq 2500.

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
