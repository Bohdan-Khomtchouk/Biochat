TITLE
System Analysis: Respiratory Syncytial Virus induces a decrease expression of HLA-DR at both the cell surface and transcriptional level in Infants with Severe Bronchiolitis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The objective of this study was to better understand the immune response to RSV at the port of entry, in the upper respiratory tract mucosa of children naturally infected with RSV.

DESIGN
For the present study, we collected Nasal wash samples from a total of 76 naturally infected RSV infants within 48 hours of hospital admission and 10 healthy controls. After sample collected, samples were process and RNA was extracted for further microarray hybridization.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
