TITLE
Alpha-factor block-release

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
These data are from a time series, where yeast were arrested in alpha-factor, then the alpha-factor was washed out, and the cells were release into fresh medium. Samples were taken every 7 minutes as the cells went through the cell cycle. This study is described in more detail in Spellman PT et al.(1998) Mol Biol Cell 9:3273-97 Keywords: time-course
