TITLE
Gene profile of colon cancer cells treated with Y15 (FAK inhibitor) and 5-fluorouracil

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The gene expression profiles were identified in colon cancer cell lines treated either with Y15 or with 5-FU

DESIGN
SW620 and Lovo-1 colon cancer cell lines were treated with 10 microM Y15 for 24 hours or with 5-FU 200 microM for 24 hours and compared treated to untreated

GPL13369 Illumina Human Whole-Genome DASL HT
