TITLE
Color-flu: multi-spectral fluorescent reporter influenza viruses as powerful tools for in vivo studies

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We sorted macrophages (known to be infected by influenza viruses from the lungs of mice infected with MA-Venus-PR8 on the basis of their fluorescent protein expression and performed microarray analysis. Macrophages isolated from the lungs of mice inoculated with PBS (naive macrophages) served as controls.

DESIGN
15 samples
