TITLE
SNP data from 45 adrenocortical carcinomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
SNP array data from 45 adrenocortical carcinomas were used to detect recurrent copy number alterations.

DESIGN
7 tumors were analyzed with Illumina Human610-Quad v1.0 BeadChip. 38 tumors were analyzed with Illumina HumanOmniExpress BeadChip.
