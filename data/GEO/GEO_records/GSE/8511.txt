TITLE
Profiling of prostate tissues in prostate cancer progression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Samples of benign prostate tissue, localized prostate cancer tissue, and metastatic prostate cancer tissue are profiled to study expression changes in diagnosis and progression of prostate cancer. Each tissue sample is also profiled for metabolomics data Keywords: Cancer Progression

DESIGN
41 samples were analyzed (16 benign prostate tissue, 12 local prostate cancer tissue, 13 metastatic prostate cancer tissue)

PLATFORM
GPL1708 Agilent-012391 Whole Human Genome Oligo Microarray G4112A (Feature Number version)
