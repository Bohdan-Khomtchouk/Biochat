TITLE
Microarray expression of B-cell Non-Hodgkins Lymphoma cases

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of major types of B-cell NHL clinical samples. While comparing all the types together, their expression was compared against reactive lymph nodes, except SMZL whose expression was compared against normal splenic cells. Keywords: disease state analysis

DESIGN
Clinical samples; 8 reactive lymph nodes, 5 splenic controls Vs 187 cases (38 MCL, 38 CLL, 9 BL, 27 SMZL, 36 DLBCL, 33 FL , 6 nMZL)

PLATFORM
GPL6011 CNIO Human Oncochip 1.0 , 1.2 and 2.0
