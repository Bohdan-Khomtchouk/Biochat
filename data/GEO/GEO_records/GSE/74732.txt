TITLE
Microarray analysis of WT, SKO, PKO and DKO liver samples

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of WT (Pten2fl/fl:Shp2fl/fl:Alb-Cre-), SKO (Shp2hep-/-, or Shp2fl/fl:Alb-Cre+), PKO (Ptenhep-/-, or Pten2fl/fl:Alb-Cre+) and DKO (Ptenfl/fl:Shp2fl/fl:Alb-Cre+) liver samples to gain global molecular insights how shp2 and pten is involved in liver tumorigenesis.

DESIGN
Two liver samples from WT, SKO, PKO and DKO were subjected to microarray using illumina system

PLATFORM
GPL6885 Illumina MouseRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
