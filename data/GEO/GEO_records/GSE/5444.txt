TITLE
Gene expression profile in individuals affected by Mucolipidosis type IV

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We compared gene expression profiling of fibroblasts from individuals affected by Mucolipidosis type IV (MLIV) and healthy ones (AB). Keywords: Disease state analysis

DESIGN
We directly compared MLIV indivisuals versus normal controls. A total of four technical replicate hybridizations were performed.

PLATFORM
GPL2136 Micro-CRIBI Human Oligo Array (Operon V2.0)
