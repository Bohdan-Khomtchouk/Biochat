TITLE
RNA-seq of Arabidopsis thaliana Col-0 siliques

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Paired-end Illumina RNA sequencing of Arabidopsis thaliana Col-0 siliques.

DESIGN
mRNA profiles of Arabidopsis Col-0 WT siliques were generated by paired-end deep sequencing, in replicate using Illumina HiSeq 2000.
