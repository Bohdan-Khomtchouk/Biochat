TITLE
Ikaros mutant thymic tumors

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The experiment was to compare leukemic T cells from thymic lymphomas from homozygote mice for the IkL/L hypomorphic mutation and non-transformed thymocytes, either of WT or IkL/L genotype. The aim was to identify a gene expression signature specific to the IkL/L tumors. Keywords = thymic lymphoma Ikaros Keywords: other
