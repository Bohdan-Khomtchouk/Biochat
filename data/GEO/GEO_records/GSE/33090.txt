TITLE
Dramatic effects of social behavior on gene regulation in rhesus macaques [Individual_expression]

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression differences in relationship to dominance rank in female rhesus macaques.

DESIGN
RNA obtained from isolated peripheral blood mononuclear cells from 49 adult female rhesus macaques of dominance ranks 1 (high) to 5 (low) across 10 social groups. Total 100 samples = (47 individuals X 2 replicates) + (2 individuals X 3 replicates)

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
