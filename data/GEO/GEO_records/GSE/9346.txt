TITLE
The function of CERK1 receptor-like kinase in the chitin elicitor signaling in Arabidopsis

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
To see the function of CERK1 receptor-like kinase in the chitin elicitor signaling in Arabidopsis, we compared the gene expression profiles in the chitin oligosaccharide treated seedlings of wild type A. thaliana and CERK1 knock-out mutant. Keywords: Defense response

DESIGN
1,Chitin oligosaccharide treatment (wild type), 2,Chitin oligosaccharide treatment (wild type) color swap, 3,Chitin oligosaccharide treatment (cerk1), 4,Chitin oligosaccharide treatment (cerk1) color swap
