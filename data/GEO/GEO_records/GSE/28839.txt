TITLE
Genome-wide nucleosome position data for 3 yeast species

ORGANISM
Schizosaccharomyces pombe ; Saccharomyces cerevisiae ; Candida albicans

SUMMARY
Genome-wide nucleosome position data for wildtype and mutant strains in S. cerevisiae, C. albicans, and S. pombe

DESIGN
Illumina sequencing of mononucleosomal DNA isolated from mid-log cultures grown in rich medium (abbreviated CM, in house recipe). S. pombe samples were grown in YES medium at permissive temperature (30C) and restrictive temperature (35C)

PLATFORM
GPL9133 Illumina Genome Analyzer (Candida albicans) GPL9134 Illumina Genome Analyzer (Saccharomyces cerevisiae) GPL9854 Illumina Genome Analyzer (Schizosaccharomyces pombe)
