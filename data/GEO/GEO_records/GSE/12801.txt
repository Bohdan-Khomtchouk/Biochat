TITLE
FOXA1 ChIP-chip in DLD1 cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
FOXA1 recruitment sites within chromosomes 8, 11 and 12 were analyzed by ChIP-chip in DLD1 cells

DESIGN
DLD1 colon cancer cells were grown in full media, cross-linked and processed as in (Carroll et al. 2005). DNA was hybridized to Human Affymetrix tiled array F. Duplicates of FOXA1 and input DNA.

PLATFORM
GPL4915 [Hs35b_P06R] Affymetrix Human Tiling 2.0R Set, Array 6
