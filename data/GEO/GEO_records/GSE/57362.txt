TITLE
A DNA methylation atlas of the human eye and its diseases

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-scale DNA methylation profiling using the Infinium DNA methylation BeadChip platform and samples from normal human eye and five ocular- related diseases

DESIGN
DNA methylation analysis of eye samples from patient suffering ocular diseases (retinal detachment, diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, uveal melanoma and retinoblastoma) using the Infinium DNA methylation BeadChip platform .

GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
