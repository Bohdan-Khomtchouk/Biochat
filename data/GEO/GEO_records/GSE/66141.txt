TITLE
Mouse Middle Ear Infection with NTHi

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
NTHi bacteria or saline were inoculated into the middle ears of mice.  Mice were sacrificed at various times to monitor the course of infection. We used Mouse430_2 arrays to profile gene expression in response to bacterial infection

DESIGN
RNA extracted at sacrifice

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
