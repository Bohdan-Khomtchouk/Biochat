TITLE
White adipose tissues: amphiregulin overexpression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of transgenic mice specifically expressing amphiregulin in white adipose tissues. The objective of this study is to explore gene expression profiles of adipose tissues in response to amphiregulin overexpression.
