TITLE
Transcriptomic definition of curcumin treated microglia

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Curcumin is a potent modulator of the inflammatory transcriptome in microglia

DESIGN
We have performed global gene expression analysis of BV-2 microglial cells under the following conditions: untreated, 20 µM Curcumin-treated, 100ng/ml LPS-treated, or 20 µM Curcumin-treated + 100ng/ml LPS-treated
