TITLE
Effect of erythropoietin on cerebral ischemia-induced changes in gene expression in rats

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Analysis of the gene expression profile in the cerebral cortex of rats 2h or 6h after middle cerebral artery occlusion with or without a single erythropoietin treatment

DESIGN
6 conditions with each 3 replicates, hybridized with 1 color.
