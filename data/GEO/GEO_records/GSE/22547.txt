TITLE
aCGH hybridization of breast cancer cell line T47D

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
An array CGH hybridization of breast cancer cell line T47D compared against a normal human female reference.

DESIGN
One hybridization of T47D genomic DNA against a normal human female reference was performed. T47D was labeled with Cy5 and reference with Cy3.
