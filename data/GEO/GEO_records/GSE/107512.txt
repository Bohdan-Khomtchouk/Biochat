TITLE
Maternal Stress and Genomic Variation

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Custom Affymetrix SNP used to assay 25 mothers for desired variants. Note that sample m21 was removed as it was found to be contaminated. Data processed using the Affymetrix SNP Genotyping Console (Version 4.2, Affymetrix Inc., Santa Clara, CA, USA).

DESIGN
25 samples analyzed with one subsequently removed due to contamination.The associated DNA methylation data is GSE54399.

PLATFORM
GPL24321 [Axiom_550458] Affymetrix Axiom Human Custom Genotyping Array
