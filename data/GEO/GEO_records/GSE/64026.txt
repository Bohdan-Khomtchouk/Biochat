TITLE
SRD: a Staphylococcus Regulatory RNA Database

ORGANISM
Staphylococcus aureus

SUMMARY
We report the use of Next Generation RNA Sequencing for confirming or improving initial identification of small regulatory RNA in Staphylococcus aureus to prodive a list of the most probable RNA molecules transcribed as independent units.

DESIGN
Extraction of RNAs in exponential phase of growth in Newman and N315 strains

PLATFORM
GPL19531 Illumina HiSeq 1500 (Staphylococcus aureus)
