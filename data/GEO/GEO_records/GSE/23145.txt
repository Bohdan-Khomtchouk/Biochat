TITLE
modENCODE_White Lab: genome-wide ChIP data of 3dFemale from 7T on Illumina Genome Analyzer.

ORGANISM
modENCODE

SUMMARY
This is a dataset generated by the Drosophila Regulatory Elements modENCODE Project led by Kevin P. White at the University of Chicago. It contains genome-wide binding profile of the factor 3dFemale from 7T generated by ChIP and analyzed on Illumina Genome Analyzer.
