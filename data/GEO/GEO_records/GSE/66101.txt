TITLE
The Transcriptome of Basilar Papilla Hair Cell Regeneration in the Avian Inner Ear

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
A comprehensive transcriptome profile of chicken basilar papilla hair cell across a 7 days regenerative time course.

DESIGN
Compare gene expression changes in chicken basilar papilla treated with streptomycin to the control. A total of 60 samples were collected from 8 different time points. Each time point contains 2 biological samples.

PLATFORM
GPL16133 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Gallus gallus)
