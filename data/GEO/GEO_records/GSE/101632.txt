TITLE
Foxp1 binding profiling in mouse embryonic neural stem cells (NSCs)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
By chromatin-immunoprecipitating (ChIP) Foxp1 in NSCs we identified genome-wide binding sites for Foxp1

DESIGN
Foxp1 ChIP-sequencing of mouse embryonic neural stem cells derived from E14.5 CD-1 mice
