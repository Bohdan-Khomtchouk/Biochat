TITLE
LNCaP cells treated with celastrol or vehicle for 6h

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Celastrol is a natural product that affects LNCaP gene expression by 6h We used microarrays to detail the global programme of gene expression affected by celastrol treatment at 6h Keywords: drug treatment

DESIGN
LNCaP cells were grown to 50% confluency and treated with celastrol for 6h prior to direct Trizol lysis and RNA isolation
