TITLE
Global Analysis of the Expression of Salmonella RyhB regulon

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium str. 14028S

SUMMARY
Analysis of the Expression Change of RyhB Regulon in RyhB-deleted Mutant Strains.

DESIGN
Wild-type Salmonella 14028 vs. three mutant strains (∆ryhB1, ∆ryhB2, and ∆ryhB1 & ∆ryhB2); One condition experiment, Iron-depleted condition by adding 200 µM of 2,2’-dipyridyl (dip).
