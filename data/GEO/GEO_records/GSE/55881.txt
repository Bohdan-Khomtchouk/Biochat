TITLE
Expression Profiles of Primary Mouse Hepatocytes treated with Cyclosporin A and solvent control [RNA]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The transcriptomics changes induced in Primary Mouse Hepatocytes by Cyclosporin A after treatment for 24h and 48h

DESIGN
The study investigated differential gene expression in Primary Mouse Hepatocytes mRNA following 24 and 48 hours of exposure to Cyclosporin A and solvent. Three biological replicates per compound/solvent. In total 24 arrays .

GPL15967 [Mouse4302_Mm_ENTREZG] Affymetrix GeneChip Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array [Brainarray Version 15.1.0]
