TITLE
IL7R: WT vs  mutant in vitro and in vivo.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The screening of putative candidate genes downstream of IL7R-INS in vitro KSL/CLP and in vivo (depicted as B-neoplasm, Notch-T-ALL, or myeloid disorder) in comparison to WT.

DESIGN
Five-condition experiment: WT-KSL vs. INS-KSL cells; WT-CLP vs.INS-CLP; WT/ICN vs. INS/ICN; WT-CMP vs. INS-CMP. Biological replicate: each 1, One replicate per array.
