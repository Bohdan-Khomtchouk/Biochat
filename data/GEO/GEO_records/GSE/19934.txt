TITLE
Effect of histone deacetylase inhibitors on miRNA expression in thyroid cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
BCPAP papillary thyroid carcinoma cells were cultured in the presence or the absence of TSA (330nM) for 16h in fresh complete medium. Three independent experiments were performed and total RNA was extracted.

DESIGN
Three independent experiments performed in dye-swap (TSA versus control).
