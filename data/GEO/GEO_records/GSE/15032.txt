TITLE
The expression profiles of PSPTO_2222 and PSPTO_2222-2223 mutants in MM or KB

ORGANISM
[Pseudomonas syringae] pv. tomato str. DC3000

SUMMARY
PSPTO_2222 and PSPTO_2222-2223 deletions mutants were grown in MM or KB, and their expression profiles were analyzed.

DESIGN
2222MM: PSPTO_2222 mutants cultured in MM. 3/2 MM: PSPTO_2222-2223 deletion mutants cultured in MM. 3-2 kb: PSPTO_2222-2223 deletion mutants cultured in KB. P2KB:  PSPTO_2222 mutants cultured in KB.
