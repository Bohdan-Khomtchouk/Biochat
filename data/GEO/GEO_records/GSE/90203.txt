TITLE
polyA mRNA RNA-seq from placenta (ENCSR000BZP)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from adult 8 week placental tissue from staged pregnant females in mouse strain C57BL/6J. They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Poly-A+ RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
