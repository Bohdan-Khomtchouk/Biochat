TITLE
miRNA profiling of celiac disease

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
microRNAs were profiled in healthy controls, classic celiac patients (CD), CD patients with anemia and GFD treated CD with normalization of duodenal mucosa

DESIGN
all CD conditions were related to controls. For each group, five patients were pooled. One replicate per experiment

PLATFORM
GPL11434 miRCURY LNA microRNA Array, 6th generation - hsa, mmu & rno
