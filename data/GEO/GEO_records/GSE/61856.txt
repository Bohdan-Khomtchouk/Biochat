TITLE
small RNA analysis in human platelets stored in blood bank

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human platelets collected from healthy donors according to standard international protocols were pooled and stored at 4ºC during 7 days. The small RNA population changes across the days were evaluated by NGS aproach.

DESIGN
The smallRNA population was evaluated at fresh platelets and after 1 day, 2 days, 3 days, 4 days, 5 days and 7 days of storage at 4ºC.
