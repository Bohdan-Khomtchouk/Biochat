TITLE
Pseudomonas putida KT2440 : Control vs. indole or ampicillin or indole plus ampicillin treated

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas putida KT2440

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of P. putida KT2440 cells comparing untreated cells with 1 mM indole or 50 μg/ml ampicillin or 1 mM indole plus 50 μg/ml ampicillin treated cells

DESIGN
Four-condition experiment, Control vs. 1 mM indole treatment,  Control vs. 50 μg/ml ampicillin treatment, Control vs. 1 mM indole and 50 μg/ml ampicillin treatment
