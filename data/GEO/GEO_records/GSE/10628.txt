TITLE
Foxj3 KO myoblast transcriptome data

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Myoblasts harvested from a postnatal day 2 WT and Foxj3 KO litter. We used Affymetrix microarrays to identify dysregulated transcripts in Foxj3 mutant myoblasts. Keywords: mutant analysis, skeletal myoblasts

DESIGN
Myoblasts were passaged 3 times prior to harvest

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
