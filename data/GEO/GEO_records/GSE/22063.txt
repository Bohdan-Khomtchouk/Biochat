TITLE
Transcriptional responses of Rhaodobacter sphaeroides to Tungsten

ORGANISM
Rhodobacter sphaeroides 2.4.1

SUMMARY
Cultures were grown photosynthetically in the media supplemented with either Molibdenium (control), Tungsten instead of Molibdenium or conbination of the both metal ions.

DESIGN
photosynthetic cultures were grown in sealed bottles at 10 W/m2 illumination.
