TITLE
Strains of increasing genetic divergence to the reference strain

ORGANISM
Burkholderia cenocepacia J2315 ; Paraburkholderia xenovorans LB400 ; Burkholderia vietnamiensis G4 ; Burkholderia cenocepacia AU 1054 ; Burkholderia cenocepacia HI2424 ; Burkholderia ambifaria AMMD

SUMMARY
comparative genomic hybridization between Burkholderia cepacia sequenced strains to determine what are the limits to strain relativity in order to still provide accurate estimations of gene content

DESIGN
Two color experiment, Burkholderia cenocepacia J2315 (reference), B. cenocepacia AU1054 (tester), HI2424 (tester), B. ambifaria AMMD (tester), B. vietnamiensis G4 (tester), B. xenovorans LB400 (tester): At least two replicates including a single dye swap for each reference-tester comparison
