TITLE
Glycolytic Enzymes Bind mRNA and Coalesce in G-bodies Essential for Cell Proliferation Under Hypoxic Conditions

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
This experiment aimed to identify the direct RNA targets of G-body components Pfk2, Eno1, and Fba1 in budding yeast.

DESIGN
PAR-CLIP-seq was performed on RNAs crosslinked to Pfk2, Eno1, or Fba1.
