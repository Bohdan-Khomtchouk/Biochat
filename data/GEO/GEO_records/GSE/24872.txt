TITLE
The transcriptome of Aedes aegypti (Liverpool strain) females kept on a sugar diet versus those blood-fed

ORGANISM
Aedes aegypti

SUMMARY
We report the RNA-seq based analyses of the transcriptional changes in the Aedes aegypti transcriptome 5 hours after blood feeding.

DESIGN
Comparison of the transcriptome of Aedes aegypti females at two physiological conditions and one time point.
