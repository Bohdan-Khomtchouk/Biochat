TITLE
Gene profile of aged Wt, miR-155-/-, miR-146a-/- and DKO CD4+ T cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Differential gene expression profile of CD4+ T cells from 10 months old Wt, miR-155-/-, miR-146a-/- and DKO mice spleens.

DESIGN
Wt, miR-155-/-, miR-146a-/- and DKO mice were aged 10 months, CD4+ T cells were sorted from mice spleens for analyses.

GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
