TITLE
Macaca fascicularis GeneChip: Four tissues

ORGANISM
Macaca fascicularis

SUMMARY
RNA samples from brain, cerebellum, liver, and testis of 3-year-old make Macaca fascicularis was hybridized to the M.fascicularis GeneChip, which was designed by the Laboratory of Genetic Resources, National Institute of Biomedical Innovation. Keywords: Control study

DESIGN
The oligo probes were designed using the cDNA sequences of Macaca fascicularis.
