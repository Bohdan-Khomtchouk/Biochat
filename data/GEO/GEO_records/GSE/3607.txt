TITLE
Identification of Xenotropic MuLV-related virus

ORGANISM
Viruses

SUMMARY
This series includes 20 microarrays used to identify Xenotropic MuLV-related virus (XMRV) in a set of human prostate tumors associated with R462Q RNASEL variant (Urisman, et al. 2006). Keywords: viral detection

DESIGN
The series includes 19 prostate tumor samples (VP) and a cell line control (HeLa).
