TITLE
Diet induced changes in mouse liver

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Examination of gene expression profiles from liver of C57BL/6 mice and LDL receptor deficient mice fed on either a low fat diet or a high fat Western-style diet for 12 weeks.  Three replicates of each condition analyzed. Keywords = LDL receptor deficiency, high fat diet, atherosclerosis, liver Keywords: repeat sample
