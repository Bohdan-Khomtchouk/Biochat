TITLE
Virus Mimicry in the Tumor Microenvironment Activates RIG-I Through Unshielding of Endogenous RNA in Exosomes [patients RNA-Seq]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study is to investigate if endogenous RNA in exosomes activates RIG-I through unshielding.

DESIGN
transcription profiling of exosomal RNA isolated from breast cancer patients before, during and after radiation therapy.
