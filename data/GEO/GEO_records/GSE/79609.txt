TITLE
Toxoplasma gondii remodels the cis-regulatory landscape of infected human host cells [ATAC-seq]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To identify the open chromatin cis-regulatory elements present in uninfected and T. gondii (Type I-RH) infected HFFs.

DESIGN
Three bioloigcal replicates of uninfected HFFs and three biological replicates of T. gondii (Type I-RH) infected HFFs.
