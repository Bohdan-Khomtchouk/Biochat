TITLE
Halothane/Isoflurane Repetitive Exposures

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Rats were exposed to 90 minutes of 0.8% halothane or 1.0% isoflurane twice a day for a total of 5 or 10 exposures. Animals did not require intubation.  All exposures and hybridizations were performed at the Univ. of Pennsylvania. Keywords = anesthetics Keywords: other
