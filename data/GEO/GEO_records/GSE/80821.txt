TITLE
Changes in miRNA profile induced by mutant IDH1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Pilot study aimed at comparing miRNA profiles of human glioma cells with and without the R132H IDH1 mutation.

DESIGN
Three cultures of U87MG cells expressing GFP-vector, and three cultures of U87MG cells expressing GFP-R132H IDH1

PLATFORM
GPL21799 NanoString nCounter Human miRNA assay v2.1
