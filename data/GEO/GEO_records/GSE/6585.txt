TITLE
MSX2 enhances the malignant phenotype and associates with Twist 1 expression in human pancreatic cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Twist 1 expression was significantly associated with MSX2 expression in human pancreatic carcinomas and was down-regulated in MSX2 pancreatic cancer cells. Keywords: cell type comparison

DESIGN
Cell type comparison. Overexpressoin of MSX2 in human pancreatic cancer cell line BxPC3. B3-EV,	human pancreatic cancer cell line BxPC3 with empty vector. B7, MSX2-overexpressing BxPC3 with expression vectors.
