TITLE
RNA-seq analysis of Lactobacillus at acidic stress

ORGANISM
Lactobacillus rhamnosus GG

SUMMARY
To understand transcriptional regulation of probiotic bacteria under acidic condition, RNAseq analysis was carried out over different growth conditions

DESIGN
Comparison of acidic (pH4) and neutral (pH7) conditions by differentially expressed genes
