TITLE
E. coli cells: Control vs. P. aeruginosa spent-media treated

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of E. coli cells comparing control untreated cells with cells treated with P. aeruginosa spent media. The goal was to identify the effects of P. aeruginosa spent media on E. coli cells.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment. 2 biological replicates
