TITLE
Genome-wide Expression Profiling in Irritable Bowel Syndrome

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Differential gene expression profiling in peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) was performed using Human Transcriptome Array 2 (HTA2)

DESIGN
Expression profiles of peripheral blood mononuclear cell (PBMCs) from 35 IBS samples and 32 healthy control was assessed.
