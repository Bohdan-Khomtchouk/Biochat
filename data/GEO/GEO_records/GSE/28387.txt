TITLE
Fetal blood transcriptome in maternal anti-fetal rejection

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Both the fetus and the mother who are involved in maternal anti-fetal rejection during pregnancy show distinct alterations in the peripheral blood transcriptome

DESIGN
Total RNA isolated from umbilical cord blood and maternal blood was compared between cases without (Normal) and with maternal anti-fetal rejection  (FIRS2) using whole genome DASL assay.

PLATFORM
GPL13369 Illumina Human Whole-Genome DASL HT
