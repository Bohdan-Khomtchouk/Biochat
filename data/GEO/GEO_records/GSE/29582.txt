TITLE
Expression Data from Toxoplasma gondii Infected Murine Bone Marrow-Derived Macrophages

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We wanted to determine how  type I ROP16 affect host gene expression We infected BMDM's from BALB/c with type I (RH) and type I (RH) Î”ROP16

DESIGN
BMDM's were plated, then infected with parasites for 18 hours. Then the RNA was harvested from the cells by Trizol.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
