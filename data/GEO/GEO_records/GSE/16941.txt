SUMMARY
Gene copy number comparison between similar wine yeast strains of Saccharomyces cerevisiae from different geographic origins.

DESIGN
Microarray-CGH of wine strains EC1118, L-957 and LV CB compared to a common reference. Reference DNA was from laboratory strain S288C.
