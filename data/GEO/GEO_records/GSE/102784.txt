TITLE
Structural basis for the recognition of GC-motifs by Smad4 transcription factor

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
ChIP-Seq analysis of Smad4 in differentiated embryonic stem cells revealed the enrichment of a GC-rich motif.

DESIGN
In this dataset, results of Smad4 ChIP-Seq performed with two different antibodies are presented.

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
