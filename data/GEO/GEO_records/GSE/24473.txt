TITLE
Ras-Association Domain Family 1C Protein Promotes Breast Cancer Cell Migration

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RASSF1C, unlike RASSF1A, is not a tumor suppressor, but instead may play a role in stimulating metastasis and survival in breast cancer cells RASSF1C over-expression enhances T47D cell invasion/migration in vitro

DESIGN
Three RASSF1C over-expression samples vs three controls
