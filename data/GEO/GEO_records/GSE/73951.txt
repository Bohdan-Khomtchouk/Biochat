TITLE
Genome-wide analysis in ESET+/+ and ESET-/- thymocytes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of ESET deficient thymocytes at gene expression level. Each thymocyte developmental stage (preselected DP, postselected DP and CD4SP) was analysed.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from isolated subpopulations of thymocytes from ESET+/+ and ESET-/- mice were analysed.

PLATFORM
GPL6885 Illumina MouseRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
