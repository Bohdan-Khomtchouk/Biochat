TITLE
TMPyP4 promotes cancer cell migration at low doses, but induces cell death at high doses

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The RNA-seq results reveal TMPyP4 and TPyP4 could promote cancer cell migration.

DESIGN
Examination of the gene expression of the following cells:A549-untreated and TMPyP4-treated or TPyP4-treated.
