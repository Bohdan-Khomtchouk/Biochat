TITLE
iPSC RNA-seq upon CRISPRn or CRISPRi manipulation

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Assess the on- and off-target effects of dox-inducible CRISPR/Cas9 and CRISPRi constructs in a human iPS cell line.

DESIGN
Transcript quantification of 3 cell lines, each plus or minus doxycycline and with or without specific single guide RNAs (sgRNAs), with 2 biological replicates each.
