TITLE
Gene expression analysis following JQ1 treatments in colon cancer lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNAseq is performed (50bp single end reads) on SW480, HT-29, HCT-15, HCT-116, COLO 205, and COLO 320 cell lines after DMSO or JQ1 treatment

DESIGN
Examination of transcriptomic changes after JQ1 treatment
