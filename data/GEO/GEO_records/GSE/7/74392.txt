TITLE
Affymetrix SNP array data for Genetic Structure of the Newfoundland Population samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
the data was used to investigate genetic structure of the Newfoundland population.

DESIGN
Affymetrix SNP arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's directions on DNA extracted fromperipheral blood samples. Customized Axiom Array was used to genotype 442 samples collected from Newfoundland and Labrador
