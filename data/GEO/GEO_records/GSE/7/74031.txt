TITLE
Genes regulated by dimethyl-2-ketoglutarate (DKG) in breast cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We report the gene expression profile of DKG-treated MDA-MB-231 cells.

DESIGN
2 replicates of parental MDA-MB-231 cells and MDA-MB-231 cells treated with DKG (10 mM, 4-days) were subjected to RNA-seq
