TITLE
short RNA-Seq of Pseudomonas aeruginosa outer membrane vesicles (OMVs) and whole cells

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa PA14

SUMMARY
Characterization of the sRNA content of P. aeruginosa OMVs compared to whole cells. Result: OMVs contain differentially packaged sRNAs.

DESIGN
Whole cell PA14 and OMVs from 3 separate preparations.
