SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from independent growths of cell line K562.  They are stranded SE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA < 200 nucleotides in size.  These data were collected as part of the RNA Evaluation Study.
