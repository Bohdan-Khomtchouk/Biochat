TITLE
Orbital Cavernous Hemangioma Tissues: Control vs. Benign Lymphoepithelial Lesions of the Lacrimal Gland tissues

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Aim to investigate the pathogenesis of BLEL of the lacrimal gland, which is a kind of IgG4 related disease.

DESIGN
A prospective study. Orbital Cavernous Hemangioma Tissues (nine continuous cases) vs. Benign Lymphoepithelial Lesions of the Lacrimal Gland tissues (nine continuous cases). Duplicate chips were used for each RNA sample.
