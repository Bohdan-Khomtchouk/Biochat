TITLE
Wobbler modifier

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The vacuolar-vesicular protein sorting (vps) factors are involved in vesicular trafficking in eukaryotic cells. The wobbler mouse is an animal model of amyotrophic lateral sclerosis. Motoneuron survival and spermiogenesis are severely compromised in the wobbler mouse. The effect of the wobbler modifier in Chr. 10 on gene expression levels in testis.
