TITLE
IL-13 Overexpression in IL4Ra^Flox Alb^Cre mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to determine the response of albumin positive cells to IL-13 overexpression.

DESIGN
14 samples were analyzed. 4 Wildtype eGFP samples, 4 Alb-Cre eGFP samples, 3 IL-13 WT samples, and 3 Alb-Cre IL-13 samples.
