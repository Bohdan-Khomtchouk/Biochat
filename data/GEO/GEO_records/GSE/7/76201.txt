TITLE
Analyses of ILC3s from the mesenteric lymph nodes (LN)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analyses of ILC3s in Rorc floxed control and Id2iΔRORγt mice following daily treatments of tamoxifen for two weeks. Cells were sort purified as lineage negative, CD127+ CD90.2+ CCR6+ ST2-

DESIGN
CCR6+ ILCs were sorted from the mesenteric LN of Rorc floxed control and Id2iΔRORγt mice following daily treatments of tamoxifen for two weeks.
