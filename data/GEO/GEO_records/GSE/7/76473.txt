TITLE
Inactivation of KLF4 in T cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia promotes the expansion of leukemia cells by activating the Map2k7/Jnk pathway. [ChIP-Seq]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Chip-Seq analysis of bone marrow cells from control and Klf4 deficient T-ALL mice

DESIGN
GFP+CD4+CD8+bone marrow cells were sorted from control and Klf4 deficient T-ALL mice induced by NOTCH1 at 4 weeks after transplantation.
