TITLE
Toxoplasma gondii  remodels the cis-regulatory landscape of infected human host cells [HELP-GT]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To study the host DNA hydroxymethylome of human fibroblasts after infection with T. gondii (Type I-RH).

DESIGN
Three bioloigcal replicates of uninfected HFFs and three biological replicates of T. gondii (Type I-RH) infected HFFs were sequenced using HELP-tagging.
