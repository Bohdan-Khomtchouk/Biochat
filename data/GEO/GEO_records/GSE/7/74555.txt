TITLE
Affymetrix SNP array data for HNSCC samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Affymetrix SNP arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's directions on DNA extracted from cryopreserved HNSCC samples.

DESIGN
Copy number analysis of Affymetrix 500K SNP arrays was performed for 11 HNSCC samples, which were used as references for copy number inference.
