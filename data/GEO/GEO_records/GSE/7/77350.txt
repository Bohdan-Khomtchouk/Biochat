TITLE
Systematic Evaluation Of Genes And Genetic Variants Associated With Type 1 Diabetes Susceptibility

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Application of Systems Genetics analysis for systematic evaluation of candidate causal genes associated with risk of Type 1 Diabetes along with follow-up bioinformatics pathway analysis.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from whole blood (for EBV-B cells) and Peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) (for T-cells)
