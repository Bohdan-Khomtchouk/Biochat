TITLE
Clear cell renal cell carcinoma (ccRCC) samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To select signatures of ccRCC, 265 ccRCC samples were obtained from the Van Andel Research Institute. Gene expression profiles of  265 samples were determined using the HG-U133_Plus_2 platform.

DESIGN
For this study, Total RNA from 265 ccRCC tumor samples were hybridised to the Affymetrix HG-U133_Plus_2  microarrays.
