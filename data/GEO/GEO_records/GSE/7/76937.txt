TITLE
RUNX2 and CBFB genome-wide binding in osteosarcoma SAOS2 cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal is to profile RUNX2 and CBFB binding sites in an osteosarcoma cell line, SAOS2.

DESIGN
Fifty million of SAOS2 cells were fixed and subjected to ChIP-seq assay using RUNX2 or CBFB specific antibody.
