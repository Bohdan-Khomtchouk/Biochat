TITLE
Porphyromonas gingivalis transcriptome in presence or absence of pABA

ORGANISM
Porphyromonas gingivalis

SUMMARY
We report the results of high throughput sequencing on P. gingivalis cells in the presence or absence of 4 aminobenzoate/para-amino benzoic acid (pABA)

DESIGN
Examination of Pg incubated for 18 hours with pABA or vehicle control
