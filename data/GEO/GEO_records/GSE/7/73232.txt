TITLE
Interleukin-21 limits inflammation and the viral reservoir in ART-treated, SIV-infected rhesus macaques

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

SUMMARY
Interleukin-21 treatment during ART was able to decrease residual immune activation and viral burden in SIV-infected rhesus macaques

DESIGN
total PBMCs at pre-ART and during ART treatment were used to generate RNA-seq data
