TITLE
Affymetrix SNP array data for Chinese Lung Adenocarcinomas (ADCs)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Chinese lung adenocarcinomas genomic copy number data

DESIGN
Affymetrix SNP arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's directions on DNA extracted from tumor samples. Copy number analysis of Affymetrix SNP 6.0 arrays was performed for all tumor samples.
