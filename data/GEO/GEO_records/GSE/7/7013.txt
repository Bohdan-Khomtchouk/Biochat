TITLE
Gnotobiotic mouse ileum; Listeria infection series

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Ileal profiles from gnotobiotic mice mono-associated with Listeria species or B. thetaiotaomicron.   Samples were derived from 72h colonizations of Fabpi-hEcad transgenic B6  mice fed a standard-chow polysaccharide rich (PR) diet. Keywords: Germ-free vs. Mono-associations
