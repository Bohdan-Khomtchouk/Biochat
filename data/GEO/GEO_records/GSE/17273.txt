SUMMARY
Mouse embryonic stem cells were used to study RNA Polymerase 2 phosphorylation of Ser 5 IP.

DESIGN
Duplicate whole genome mouse arrays of embryonic stem cells. RNA Polymerase II phosphoCTD (Serine 5) antibody was used in the experiment.
