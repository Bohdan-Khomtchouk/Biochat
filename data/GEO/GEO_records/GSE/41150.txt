TITLE
Genes up and down regulated in LNCaP cells overexpressing MED1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To identify MED1 target genes involved in prostate tumorigenesis.

DESIGN
LNCap cells were transiently transfected with a MED1 expression vector or and empty vector control for 24 hours. Total RNA was extracted and gene microarray carried out. Experiment was performed in duplicate. Four samples total.

PLATFORM
GPL571 [HG-U133A_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A 2.0 Array
