TITLE
microRNA expression and opioid regulation in the zebrafish

ORGANISM
Danio rerio ; Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We examined morphine responsing miRNAs in Zebrafish embroys. Microarray studies revealed morphine can change microRNA expression in Zebrafish embroys.

DESIGN
18 arrays: 9 untreated, 9 morphine, 3 time points

PLATFORM
GPL9517 Multi-species 1.1K miRNA array
