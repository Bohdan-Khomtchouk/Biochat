TITLE
Differential gene expression in peripheral blood CD2+ leukocytes from PATH particpants

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling was carried out on peripheral blood CD2+ leukocytes from 29 children with asthma.  The primary research question is whether gene expression differs in individuals from high socioeconomic status environments vs low socioeconomic status environments. Keywords: Risk prediction
