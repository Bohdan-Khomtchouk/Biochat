TITLE
Expression data from MCF7/BUS cell culture exposed to xenoestrogens

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
MCF7 cells were exposed to xenoestrogens (vehicle = 0.1% ethanol, 30 pM 17beta-estradiol, 3 micromolar genistein, 10 micromolar genistein, 1 micomolar daidzein, 10 nM bisphenol A, 10 nM para-nonylphenol, 3 nM PPT) for 48 hours. Keywords: xenoestrogen response
