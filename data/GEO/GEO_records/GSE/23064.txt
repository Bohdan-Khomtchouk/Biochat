TITLE
Murine hamatopoietic stem cell (HSC): wild type and Skp2 knockout

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of mouse HSC comparing wild type (WT) and Skp2 knockout

DESIGN
Collected bone marrow (BM) cells from WT and Skp2 knockout mice, stained with HSC surface markers for FACS. Collected HSC and extracted total RNA then sent out for microarray analysis.
