TITLE
Heat stress response in Gillichthys mirabilis

ORGANISM
Gillichthys mirabilis

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to measure the effect of heat stress on the transcriptome of a temperate fish species - Gillichthys mirabilis. Keywords: Stress response

DESIGN
Three control fish are compared to three heat-shocked fish.

PLATFORM
GPL5573 Gillichthys mirabilis cDNA microarray
