TITLE
iTreg cells compared to WT Total Treg

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
iTreg cells from Tbmc mLN mice treated with one week of 1% Oral Ova were compared to Total Treg from WT mice.

DESIGN
Tbmc mice were treated with 1% Oral Ova and Foxp3 GFP+ CD4+ cells were sorted from mLN. CD4+ Foxp3+ cells were also sorted from WT Foxp3 GFP Balb/c mice.
