TITLE
Gene expression based analysis of CroV infection of Cafeteria roenbergensis

ORGANISM
Cafeteria roenbergensis virus BV-PW1

SUMMARY
In this study, total RNA was extracted from cultures of Cafeteria infected with the Cafeteria roenbergensis virus BV-PW1 (CroV) and hybridised to a CroV microarray in order to determine the temporal expression profile during infection.
