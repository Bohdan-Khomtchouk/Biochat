TITLE
Identification of Copy Number Variants in Dogs

ORGANISM
Canis lupus ; Canis lupus familiaris

SUMMARY
Custom exome aCGH analysis of copy number across the genomes of 16 canine breeds

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, All breed samples were compared to a single Boxer reference, Seizure samples were compared to breed and sex matched controls, and German Shepherds were compared to the Boxer reference
