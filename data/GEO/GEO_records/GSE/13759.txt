TITLE
Poorly metastatic HCT116 vs metastatic E1 colon cancer cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparison of gene expression between poorly metastatic HCT116 colon cancer cell line and the in-vivo derived metastatic E1 cell line, which has EMT (epithelial to mesenchymal transition) features.

DESIGN
Each cell line has triplicate chips.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
