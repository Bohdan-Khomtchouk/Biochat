TITLE
Multicomponent Signatures for Predicting Epigenomic Treatment Efficacy

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of breast cancer cell lines and mouse xenograft, treated with HDAC Inhibitors.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 48 samples were hybridised to the Illumina Infinium 450K Human Methylation Beadchip
