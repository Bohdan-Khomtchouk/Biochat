TITLE
Transcriptomic analysis of Alishewanella species utilizing pectin as a sole carbon source

ORGANISM
Alishewanella aestuarii

SUMMARY
Comparative transcriptomic analysis of three Alishewanella species utilizing pectin as a sole carbon source

DESIGN
Gene expression level of three Alishewanella species grown on pectin was investigated and compared with A. jeotgali grown on succinate
