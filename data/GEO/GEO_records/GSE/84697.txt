TITLE
Tumor immune microenvironment characterization in clear cell renal cell carcinoma identifies prognostic and immunotherapeutically relevant mRNA signatures

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Tumor-infiltrating macrophages, NK cells, CD8+ and CD4+ T cells sorted from clear cell renal cell carcinoma patient specimens.

DESIGN
4 macrophage samples (CD45+CD3-CD56-CD14+), 2 CD16+ NK cell samples (CD45+CD3-CD56+CD16+), 5 CD8+ T cell samples (CD45+CD3+CD8+), 3 CD4+ T cell samples (CD45+CD3+CD4+), and one non-immune cell sample (CD45-).
