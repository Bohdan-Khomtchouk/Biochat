TITLE
BORG analysis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
A long intergenic non-protein-coding RNA , BORG, is almost exclusively expressed in neurons and is induced during the differentiation of human and mouse pluripotent cells and neuronal progenitors into neurons.

DESIGN
shRNA knockdown was used to examine the effects of BORG expression

PLATFORM
GPL13185 [mjay] Custom Affymetrix Mouse Exon Junction Array
