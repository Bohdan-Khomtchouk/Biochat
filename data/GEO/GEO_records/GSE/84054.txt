TITLE
Transcriptome profiling of ER+ breast cancer primary tumor and its tumorsphere derivative

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We profiled RNA expression in the ER+ primary tumor and the matching tumorspheres. The objective was to find genes differentially expressed between the tumorspheres and bulk tumor.

DESIGN
12 pairs of matching ER+ bulk tumor and its tumorspheres

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
