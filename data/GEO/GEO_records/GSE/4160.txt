TITLE
Fur mutant analysis

ORGANISM
Helicobacter pylori

SUMMARY
Wildtype G27 and it's Fur mutant derivative were exposed to pH 5.0 for the indicated length of time.  Bacterial cells in the exponential phase of growth (denoted log) and stationary phase of growth were used.  fur1 denotes a single fur mutant while fur2 denotes an additional fur mutant. An all pairs experiment design type is where all labeled extracts are compared to every other labeled extract. Keywords: all_pairs
