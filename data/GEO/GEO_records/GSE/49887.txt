TITLE
Small Cell Carcinoma of the Ovary (SCCO) vs. Normal Ovary

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of 4 SCCO tumors compared to age-matched normal ovary to investigate underlying tumor biology and potential therapeutic targets.

DESIGN
RNA from 4 SCCO fresh-frozen tumors were individually hybridized to a pool of 2 commercial normal ovary RNA on Agilent 44K v2 human gene expression microarrays
