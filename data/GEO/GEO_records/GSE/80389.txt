TITLE
Gastric cancer targeted RNA-seq

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
targeted gene sequencong of 157 preselected genes in upper GI biopsies and resected stomack tissues

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from clinical specimens. Samples with RIN above 3 were included for library construction using Illumina Truseq targeted RNA expression kits.
