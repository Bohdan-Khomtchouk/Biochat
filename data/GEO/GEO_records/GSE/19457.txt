TITLE
Mental Retardation Patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Profiling the genomic profiles of mental retardation patients. 13 mental retardation patients were selected for detection of genomic aberrations.

DESIGN
Patient's DNA were hybridized against Promega control on Agilent G4426B arrays and scanned with the Agilent G2505B scanner.

PLATFORM
GPL9796 IRCL Human Genome CGH Microarray 4x44K (G4426B) (probe version)
