TITLE
The effect of CLA diet on liver gene expression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
apoE-deficient mice were fed a Western-type diet enriched with linoleic acid (control), cis-9, trans-11-CLA or trans-10, cis-12-CLA (1.0 % wt/wt) for 12 weeks and their hepatic gene expression was analysed using DNA microarrays Keywords: influence of diet
