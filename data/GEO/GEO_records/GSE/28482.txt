TITLE
Effects of 20 months Dietary Methionine Restriction on Hepatic Gene Expression in Fischer 344 Rats

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Dietary methionine restriction (MR)  has been shown to increase lifespan and decrease adiposity in rodents. This study was designed to examine the transcriptional effects of MR in metabolically relevant tissues. This experiment contains data from the liver.
