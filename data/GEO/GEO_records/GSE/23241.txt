TITLE
Uterine Gene Profile of Estriol, a Weak Estrogen

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Females were ovariectomized and injected with saline estradiol or estriol. Uterine tissue was collected after 2 or 24 hours. RNA was analyzed by microarray compare ealry and late responses to a potent and a weak estrogen agaonist.

DESIGN
3 uteri per group were analyzed individually on one-color Agilent arrays.
