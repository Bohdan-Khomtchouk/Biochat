TITLE
Comparison of SND2-overexpressing and wild type Arabidopsis thaliana Col-0 inflorescence stems

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Homozygous T4 SND2-overexpressing Arabidopsis plants were grown alongside the wild type and the lower 100 mm of primary inflorescence stems sampled at 4 and 8 weeks of age

DESIGN
A direct comparison was used (box plot). Three biological replicates and two technical replicates (dye swaps) were included for each experiment
