TITLE
Gene expression change by miR-17-92 in the dorsal root ganglion

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Gene expression profile of L5 DRG was examined to analyze a role of miR-17-92 in the DRG.

DESIGN
Gene expression in the L5 DRG was measured in rats 7 days after injection of control or miR-17-92-expressing AAV vector (n = 4).
