TITLE
R. sphaeroides Δirr vs. R. sphaeroides 2.4.1 under microaerobic conditions

ORGANISM
Rhodobacter sphaeroides 2.4.1

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of R. sphaeroides Δirr compared to control R. sphaeroides 2.4.1 under microaerobic conditions.

DESIGN
Two strain experiment under microarobic conditions (30 µM). 6 Biological replicas, independetly grown and harvested at OD660=0,4; 1-3 pooled in replicate 1, 4-6 pooled in replicate 2
