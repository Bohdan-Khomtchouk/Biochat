TITLE
Mutation accumulation and the yeast transcriptome

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Spontaneous mutations were accumulated in haploid strains of yeast. Expression profiling was performed on four MA lines and the ancestor in a complete loop design. Keywords: Gene expression profiling

DESIGN
Every line is compared to every other line, for a total of four replicates per line.
