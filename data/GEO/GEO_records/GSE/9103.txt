TITLE
Skeletal Muscle Transcript Profiles in Trained or Sedentary Young and Old Subjects

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Aging  is associated with mitochondrial dysfunction and insulin resistance. We conducted a study to determine the role of long-term vigorous endurance exercise on age-related changes in insulin sensitivity and various indices of mitochondrial functions. Keywords: The effect of excises in young and old human subjects by transcription profiling
