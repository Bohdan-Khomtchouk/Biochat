TITLE
Gene expression after knocking down of c-Met in breast cancer brain metastatic cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study is to identify the target genes of c-Met signaling in breast cancer brain metastasis.

DESIGN
231BrM/Dox/shMet and SKBrM3/Dox/shMet cells were treated with or without doxycycline  for 48hrs.
