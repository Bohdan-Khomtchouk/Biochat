TITLE
SBRI_AC Host response in primary mouse tracheal epitelial cells (mTEC)  to influenza infection with PR8, VN or X31 at MOI of 3 at 12 hours

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Array analysis of total RNA from mTECs 12 h post infection with influenza. Strains used were Mock, PR8, X31, VN6+2 (MOI 3)

DESIGN
Time Point: 12 h post infection (Mock, PR8, X31, VN) MOI 3.0; in biological triplicates. Total samples: 12.

PLATFORM
GPL13712 [MoEx-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Exon 1.0 ST Array [Alternative CDF: BrainArray version 14: MoEx10stv1_Mm_ENTREZG]
