TITLE
E. coli BW25113 ompA mutant vs wild type biofilm and E. coli BW25113 ompA/pCA24N_ompA vs ompA/pCA24N suspension cells

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
This Series involves two studies: 1) The gene expression of E. coli K-12 BW25113 ompA mutant strain vs. wild type strain glasswool biofilm cells and E. coli K-12 BW25113 ompA mutant vs. wild type polystyrene biofilm cells. 2) The gene expression of E. coli BW25113 ompA/pCA24N_ompA vs. ompA/pCA24N suspension cells.
