TITLE
Control vs. 35S:AtRALF1-1

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
This series (two repeats) compares the transcriptional changes caused by the overexpression of the AtRALF1-1 cDNA.  AtRALF1-1 is one of the nine Arabidopsis isoforms of the polypeptide hormone RALF (PNAS 98, 12843). Keywords: other
