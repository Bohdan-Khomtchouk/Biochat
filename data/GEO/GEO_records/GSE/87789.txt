TITLE
R. sphaeroides pBBBRUpsMx2 vs. R. sphaeroides pBBR under singlet oxygen stress

ORGANISM
Rhodobacter sphaeroides 2.4.1

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of R. sphaeroides pBBRUpsMx2 compared to control R. sphaeroides pBBR under high oxygen and singlet oxygen conditions.

DESIGN
RNA samples collected from R. sphaeroides cells were analyzed by two-color microarrays
