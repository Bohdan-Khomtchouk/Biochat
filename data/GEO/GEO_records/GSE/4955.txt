TITLE
Profiling of WSSV infection in hepatopancreas of shrimp

ORGANISM
Litopenaeus vannamei

SUMMARY
Profiles of gene expression in hepatopancreas isolated from shrimp experimentally infected with White Spot Syndrome Virus were compared to those of un-infected controls Keywords: response to viral disease

DESIGN
Two groups of eight shrimp were compared in terms of hepatopancreas gene expression, 40 hours after challenge with White Spot Syndrome Virus
