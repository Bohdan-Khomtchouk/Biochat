TITLE
Effects of voluntary exercise in rat aorta

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Effects of voluntary exercise in rat aorta. Spontaneously hypertensive rats (SHR) performed 5 weeks of voluntary exercise (wheel-cage running). Aortic tissue was collected and samples were pooled (3 aortae/chip). Aortae from running rats were compared to aortae from non-running rats. Keywords: parallel sample

PLATFORM
