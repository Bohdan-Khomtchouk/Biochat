TITLE
Recurrent activating mutation in PRKACA in cortisol-producing adrenal tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed mRNA-seq of a PRKACA-mutant adrenal tumor and demonstrated that the mutation is expressed at the mRNA level.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from a cortisol-producing adrenal tumor with a PRKACA p.Leu206Arg mutation.

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
