TITLE
RNAseq analysis of chemotherapy and radiation therapy-naïve breast tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Assessment of chemo- and radiation therapy-naïve biopsy-confirmed invasive human breast tumors by RNAseq.

DESIGN
103 total samples from 63 unique patients. Clinical details were provided only for the 50 samples in current publication. However, all 103 samples were analyzed together.

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
