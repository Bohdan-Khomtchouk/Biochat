TITLE
11-Point Time Course Study of Differentiating J1 Embryoid Bodies

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
An 11-point time course study on differentiating embryoid bodies from a murine J1 embryonic stem cell line. The time course includes 0 hr, 6 hr, 12 hr, 18 hr, 24 hr, 36 hr, 48 hr, 4 days, 7 days, 9 days and 14 days. Keywords: Time course

DESIGN
this experiment include 11 samples and 66 replicates
