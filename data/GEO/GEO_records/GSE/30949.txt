TITLE
E. faecalis transcriptional responses to copper stress

ORGANISM
Enterococcus faecalis V583

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Enterococcus faecalis V583 comparing the transcriptome of cells grown in M17 + glucose 0.5% (GM17) with the transcriptome of cells grown in M17 + glucose 0.5% + 0.05 mM CuSO4
