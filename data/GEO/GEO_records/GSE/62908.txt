TITLE
ChIP-seq mapping of histone modifications in CD14++ CD16- monocytes from septic disease patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Chromatin immunoprecipitation with antibodies specific for histone modifications H3K4me3, H3K9ac and H3K27me3 and subsequent high-throughput sequencing were performed on fixed chromatin from two septic disease patients.

DESIGN
ChIP-seq

PLATFORM
