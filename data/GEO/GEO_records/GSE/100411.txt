TITLE
Signaling strength determines proapoptotic functions of STING

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
T cells exhibit an intensified STING response, which leads to the expression of a distinct set of genes and results in the induction of apoptosis

DESIGN
CD4+ T cells were stimulated either with DMSO or 10-carboxymethyl-9-acridanone (CMA) for 16 hours. RNA was isolated for analysis.
