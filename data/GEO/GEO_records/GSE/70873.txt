TITLE
neonatal and adult mice lung

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We performed miRNA array analysis from 4 groups (neonatal lung control, neonatal lung after hyperoxia, adult lung control, adult lung after hyperoxia).   We used pools of every 100ng of total RNA of three samples for each groups.

DESIGN
Examination of miRNA levels during lung development and in hyperoxia

PLATFORM
GPL20687 Applied Biosystems Rodent MicroRNA Cards (A+B Cards)
