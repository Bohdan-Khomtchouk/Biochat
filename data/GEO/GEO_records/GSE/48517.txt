TITLE
PAR-CLIP for JARID2 in mouse embryonic stem cells

Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This experiment was designed to identify RNAs making direct contact with JARID2 in mouse embryonic stem cells

DESIGN
E14 cells were pulsed with 4-SU, irradiated with UV, and subjected to JARID2 immunoprecipitation.
