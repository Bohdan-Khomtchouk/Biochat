TITLE
Genome-Wide Small RNA Profiling of a Soybean Seed Compartment

ORGANISM
Glycine max

SUMMARY
We report the genome-wide small RNA of soybean early maturation seed coat parenchyma compartment soybean early maturation seeds using Illumina high-throughput sequencing technology.

DESIGN
Illumina sequencing of small RNA from early maturation seed coat parenchyma compartment and early-maturation stage whole seeds
