TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of gene expression profiles in the Rotterdam Study III

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles were compared to investigate age-marking differential gene expression.

DESIGN
We obtained Whole blood RNA of 881 participants of the Rotterdam Study III.
