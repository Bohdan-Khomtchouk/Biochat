TITLE
MCF-7 Fractionated RNA

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNA from MCF-7 cells was fractionated by sucrose density gradient centrifugation to separate RNA associated with membrane-bound polysomes from RNA associated with free polysomes.  These two populations were hybridized in triplicate to U133A microarrays. Keywords: repeat sample

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
