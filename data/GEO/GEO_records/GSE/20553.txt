TITLE
Methylated DNA Immunoprecipitation (MeDIP) using a custom type 2 diabetes and related phenotypes array

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Custom array designed to tile Linkage Disequilibrium Blocks of T2D GWAS SNPs, monogenic candidates for T2D and Obesity, and all plausible imprinted loci from human and mouse data.

DESIGN
Case Control comparison of MeDIP for Type 2 Diabetes. MeDIP versus Input fraction.

PLATFORM
GPL10113 NimbleGen/UCL human 388K tiling array
