TITLE
Gene expression changes in anti-diabetic treated Zucker diabetic rats

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of insulin-sensitive tissues comparing control untreated ZDF rat with ZDF rat treated with three different anti-diabetic drugs and ZLC rat.

DESIGN
Four-condition experiment, ZDF vs. anti-diabetic drug treated ZDF and ZLC rat. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 treated, One replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL15372 Operon Rat V3.0 OpArray
