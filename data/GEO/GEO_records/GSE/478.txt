TITLE
Alveoli loss during caloric restriction time course

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Pulmonary alveoli are complex architectural units thought to undergo endogenous or pharmacologically induced programs of regeneration and degeneration. To study the molecular mechanism of alveoli loss mice were calorie restricted at different timepoints. Lungs were harvested and processed for RNA extraction. Keywords: other
