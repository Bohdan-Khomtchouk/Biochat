TITLE
Comparing effects of rapamycin and caffeine on gene expression in YPD media

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
To identify genes influenced by rapamycin and caffeine in strains W303a, S288c, and Sigma 2000 Keywords: Drug dose response

DESIGN
Arrays compare W303a, S288c, and Sigma 2000 cells grown in YPD and either untreated or treated with different concentrations of rapamycin or caffeine

PLATFORM
GPL2763 S. cerevisiae cDNA spotted microarray
