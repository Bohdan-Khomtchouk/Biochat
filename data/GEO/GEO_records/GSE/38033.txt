TITLE
Zur regulon of Neisseria meningitidis

ORGANISM
Neisseria meningitidis

SUMMARY
The zur regulon in Neisseria meningitidis was elucidated in the strain MC58 using a zur knockout strain and conditions which activate Zur ( zinc supplementation in the medium)

DESIGN
Common reference design, zur knock out strain was used as the common reference and the samples wild type strain grown in RPMI and in RPMI with Zinc supplementation were compared to the common reference.
