TITLE
DNMT inhibition in the Neonatal Preoptic Area

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
PN2 male and female rat pups were treated with vehicle or zebularine.  POA RNA was extracted and sequenced to deterime the effects of DNMT inhibition in POA gene expression.

DESIGN
Males and Females were treated with either vehicle (0.1% DNMSO in saline) or Zebularine
