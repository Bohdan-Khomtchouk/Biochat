TITLE
Profiling of in vitro differentiated activated T helper cells [mRNA]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Combined analysis of mRNA and miRNA transcriptoms revealed a complex network regulating major immune regulatory signaling pathways

DESIGN
We performed mRNA and small RNA expression microarray analysis on three T-helper populations: Th0 (control), Th2, and Th17

PLATFORM
GPL16570 [MoGene-2_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 2.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
