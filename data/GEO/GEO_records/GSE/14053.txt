TITLE
Expression data from trichomes and pavement cells

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Comparison of cell sap from trichomes and pavement cells.  We performed a detailed transcriptome analysis of cell sap from leaf epidermal cell types using the Affymetrix Ath1 chip.

DESIGN
Trichome and pavement cell sap was collected from leaves of 5 week old plants.

PLATFORM
GPL198 [ATH1-121501] Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array
