TITLE
MiRNA and lung cancer (INCA project) - 40 miRNAs microarrays

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
MicroRNA expression pattern in 20 pairs of primary lung cancers and their corresponding non cancerous lung tissues. These specimens were obtained from the Nice Hospital Tumor Bio Bank, France.

DESIGN
Dye swap-experiment comparing cancerous tissue versus adjacent normal lung tissue.
