TITLE
Olfactory epithelium of SR-A mutant mice following bilateral olfactory bulbectomy

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Sham and bilateral OBX were performed on wild-type and SR-A mutant mice. Tissue collected 2, 8, 16, and 48 hours following OBX. Expression profiles were determined using Affynetrix MG U74Av2 chips. Keywords: repeat
