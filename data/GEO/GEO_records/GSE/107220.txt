TITLE
High-resolution transcriptome analysis of mouse subventricular zone reveals features of stem cell lineage, anatomy, and aging

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Single cell RNA sequencing of mouse subventricular zone (SVZ) harboring a CGD1 transgene expressing GFP.

DESIGN
Whole SVZ dissected, FACS sorted for GFPhi, Low, negative, and Unsorted Viable (6600 cells) or Aged SVZ samples, 2 weeks, 2 months, 6 months, 12 months, FACS sorted for viable cells, undergone Drop-seq single cell RNA sequencing.

PLATFORM
GPL19057 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Mus musculus)
