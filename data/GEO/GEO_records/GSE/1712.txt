TITLE
Glycoconjugate expression profiling in mouse postnatal cerebellar development

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We used a custom Affymetrix GeneChip, the " GLYCOv2 " array, to measure differences in gene expression patterns from adult and postnatal day 7 (P7) mouse cerebellum RNA.
