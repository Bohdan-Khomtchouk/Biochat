TITLE
MicroRNA expression in neonatal and adult rat cardiomyocytes

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Expression profiles of microRNAs in neonatal (isolated from day0 newborn rats) and adult rat cardiomyocytes (isolated from 2month old rats)

DESIGN
Two condition experiment; Biological replicates: 7 samples of cardiomyocytes from neonatal rats (from independent isolations); 6 samples of cardiomyocytes isolated from adult animals (from independent isolations)

PLATFORM
GPL11434 miRCURY LNA microRNA Array, 6th generation - hsa, mmu & rno
