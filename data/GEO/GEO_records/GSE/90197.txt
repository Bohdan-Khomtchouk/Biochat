TITLE
polyA mRNA RNA-seq from brain (ENCSR000BZJ)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from embryonic day 14.5 (E.14.5) whole brain tissue from mixed sex littermates in mouse strain C57BL/6J. They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Poly-A+ RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
