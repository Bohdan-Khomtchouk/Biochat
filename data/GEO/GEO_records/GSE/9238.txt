TITLE
SWCoV1 series; viral detection in Beluga whale liver

ORGANISM
Delphinapterus leucas

SUMMARY
This series includes 3 microarrays used to detect SWCoV1, a novel group III coronavirus in Delphinapterus leucas (Beluga whale) liver. Keywords: viral detection

DESIGN
The series includes 2 control whale livers and 1 whale liver containing SWCoV1.
