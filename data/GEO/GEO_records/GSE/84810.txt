TITLE
Improving the Positive Predictive Value of Non-invasive Prenatal Screening (NIPS)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Assessment of maternal blood to detect maternal microduplication causing false positive trisomy result in fetus

DESIGN
Array evidence including 2 maternal gDNA samples which were positive for a maternal duplication on either chromosome 18 or 21 Affymetrix SNP arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's directions on DNA extracted from peripheral blood samples.
