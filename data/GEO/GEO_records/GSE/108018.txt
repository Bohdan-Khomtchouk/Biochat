TITLE
RNAseq comparison of fadR deletion mutant and the wild type strain

ORGANISM
Sulfolobus acidocaldarius DSM 639

SUMMARY
To unravel the regulon of FadR (Saci_1107), comparative transcriptomic analysis was performed for the fadR deletion mutant versus the isogenic WT strain using RNA-seq approach.

DESIGN
Examination of gene expression between knock out mutant and wild type strain

PLATFORM
GPL24383 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Sulfolobus acidocaldarius DSM 639)
