TITLE
Identification of a Myometrial Molecular Profile for Dystocic Labor

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study identifies a transciptomic myometrial profile associated with dystocia in spontanous nulliparous term labour We used microarrays to compare myometrial biopsies obtained at cesarean section from women in spontaneous term labour

DESIGN
Women in spontaneous labour undergoing cesarean section for dystocia (slow progressing labour) compared to women who had progressed in the second stage

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
