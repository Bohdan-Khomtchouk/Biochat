TITLE

Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Expression profile of colon mucosa of F344 rats treated with 5% DSS during five days vs colon mucosa of F344 rats pre-treated for 20 days with resveratrol 1mg/kg/day and during the five days of 5% DSS administration Keywords: Inflammation experiment
