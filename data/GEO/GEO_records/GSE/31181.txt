TITLE
ChIP-Seq of HA tagged ZFP57 and KAP1 in mouse ES cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We have performed ChIP seq analysis to obtain the positions of KAP1 and ZFP57 binding sites in mouse ES cells.  By comparing the two lists, we were able to find bona fide sites.

DESIGN
ChIP-Seq of HA tagged ZFP57 and KAP1 in mouse ES cells
