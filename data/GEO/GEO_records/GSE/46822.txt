TITLE
Genome wide map of H3K4me3 histone modification in C6 rat glioma cells [SOLiD]

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
We report a map of H3K4me3 - an activiting expression histone modification in C6 rat glioma cells. The data was obtained using whole genome high throughput technology. The sequencing was performed on Solid 5500xl platform.

DESIGN
Examination of  H3K4me3 histone modification in C6 rat glioma cell line
