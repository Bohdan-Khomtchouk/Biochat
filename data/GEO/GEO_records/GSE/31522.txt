TITLE
Comparison of Thermoanaerobacter pseudethanolicus 39E grown on different xylose concentrations

ORGANISM
Thermoanaerobacter pseudethanolicus ATCC 33223

SUMMARY
Thermoanaerobacter pseudethanolicus 39E was grown on 10, 50 and 200 mM xylose in order to identify pathways and transporters responsible for xylose transport and conversion to ethanol.
