TITLE
Genome-wide transcriptome sequencing at different stages of Drosophila development, RNA-seq

ORGANISM
modENCODE

SUMMARY
This is a dataset generated by the Drosophila Regulatory Elements modENCODE Project led by Kevin P. White at the University of Chicago. It contains total RNA sequencing data generated on Solexa Genome Analyzer for whole Drosophila animals at 12 different time-points of development.
