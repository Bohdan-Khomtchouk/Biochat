TITLE
miR-NC or miR-331-3p treatment of LNCaP prostate cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
LNCaP prostate cancer cells were treated for 24 hours with either a miRNA negative control (miR-NC) or miRNA 331-3p (miR-331-3p)

DESIGN
There were 6 samples in total. 3 x miRNA negative control and 3 x miR-331-3p treated cells. Cells were treated for 24 hours and RNA was harvested at this time point.

PLATFORM
GPL6884 Illumina HumanWG-6 v3.0 expression beadchip
