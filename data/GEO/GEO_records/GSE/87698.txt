TITLE
Effect of copper (II) chloride treatment on rice leaves

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
Analysis of leaves of wild-type and jasmonate (JA)-deficeint mutant cpm2 treated with CuCl2. Results provide the role of JA on defence responses after copper chloride treatment.

DESIGN
Expression profiling in wild-type and cpm2 leaves treated with or without 1 mM copper (II) chloride was analyzed using one-color method with three biological replicates.
