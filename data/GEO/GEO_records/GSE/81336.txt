TITLE
Next generation sequencing analysis of soy glyceollins and 17-β estradiol: Effects on transcript abundance in the female mouse brain

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Purpose: The goal of this study was to compare and contrast the next generation sequencing data to data obtained from a whole brain microarray study

DESIGN
Examination of the effects of Glyceollin alone, 17β Estradiol alone or in combination on gene expression in the adult female mouse brain
