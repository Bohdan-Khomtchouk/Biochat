TITLE
BRC77 Agilent Gene Expression Arrays

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Agilent 44K whole genome studies were used to correlate gene expression values with mutations found in massively parallel sequencing experiments.

DESIGN
77 patients were profiled using Agilent's two-color microarray platform.  Tumor(cy5) was co-hybridized to a reference(cy3).
