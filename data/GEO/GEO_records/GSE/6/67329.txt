TITLE
microRNA Induction of Fatal Differentiation Programs is a Context-selective Vulnerability in Ovarian Cancer Cells [miRNA]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of microRNA (miRNA) expression in ovarian cancer cell lines and human ovarian surface epithelium (HOSE) in order to identify differentially expressed miRNAs between cancer lines and HOSE

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated from cells 72 hours after plating
