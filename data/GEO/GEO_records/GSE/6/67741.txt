TITLE
Ribosome profiling with translation inhibitors reveals pervasive translation in murine ES cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Ribosome profiling with translation inhibitors reveals pervasive translation in murine ES cells.

DESIGN
Ribosome profiling or stranded RNAseq (ribominus) with murine embryonic stem cells treated with either DMD-pateamineA, Puromycin, Harringtonine or vehicle (no drug control). Two replicates per condition.
