TITLE
Comparison of transcriptional profiles between sni1 and wild type

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Microarray experiment was performed using 4-week old plants to compare transcriptional profiles between sni1 (suppressor of npr1) and wild type (Col-0).  Three biological replicates were included.
