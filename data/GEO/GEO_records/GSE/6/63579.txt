TITLE
Enhancement of ER function by PI3K inhibition limits the antitumor effects of PI3K inhibitors in breast cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression data of breast cancer samples before and after PI3K inhibition

DESIGN
In the study presented here, we evaluated the expression profiles of a total of 105 unique genes in 6 breast cancer samples before and after PI3K-inhibitor therapy
