TITLE
RNA-seq. and DNA-seq. of Pseudomonas aeruginosa PAO1 derived membrane vesicles

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa PAO1

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study is to identify RNA and DNA associated with outer membrane vesicle and compare it with the cells

DESIGN
