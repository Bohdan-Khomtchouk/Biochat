TITLE
Gene expression in plep-1 mutant young adult hermaphrodites

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
a plep-1 mutant (MosI insertion line) was compared to the reference strain N2

DESIGN
N2 and plep-1 samples were competitively hybridised against a pooled reference sample, with 3 biological and 4 technical replicates each
