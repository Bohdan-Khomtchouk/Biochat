TITLE
Combinatorial effects of estrogen with progestogens or androgen in the breast cancer cell line ZR-75-1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Cells were cotreated with dihydrotestosterone, progesterone or medroxyprogesterone acetate and estrdiol to assess the combinatorial effects of hormone exposure in breast cancer cells

DESIGN
Cells were plated in hormone stripped media for 56h, followed by treatment for 16h with 10nM of the nominated hormone(s)
