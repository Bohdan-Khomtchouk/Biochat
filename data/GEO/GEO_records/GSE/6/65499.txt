TITLE
CaSki cells: adherent cultured cell vs. sphere cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human cervical squamous cell carcinoma cell line CaSki comparing adherent cultured cells with spheres cultured in serum free culture medium.  Goal was to identify candidate antigens for immunotherapy targeting cancer stem cells.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, CaSki vs. CaSki-sphere cells.
