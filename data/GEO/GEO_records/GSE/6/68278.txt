TITLE
Alcohol-induced anxiolysis is mediated through inhibition of microRNA-494 expression in the amygdala

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
MicroRNA profiling of adult rat amygdala following acute ethanol (1g/kg IP) exposure

DESIGN
Two experimental groups, Control vs. Acute Ethanol. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 acute ethanol (Each RNA sample was pooled from two rats)
