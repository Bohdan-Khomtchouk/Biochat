TITLE
Transcriptional response to diets varying in phosphor content in pigs (low-intake; medium intake; high-intake)

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
in vivo microarray study of transcriptional changes in porcine peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) due to dietary intake of phosphor

DESIGN
per dietary group 6-7 pigs were collected for expression profiling at 2 time points
