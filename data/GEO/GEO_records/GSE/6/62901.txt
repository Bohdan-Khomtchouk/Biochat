TITLE
Expression analysis of motile strain of Sphingomonas sp. A1

ORGANISM
Sphingomonas sp. A1

SUMMARY
Investigation of whole genome gene expression level in motile strain of Sphingomonas. sp A1 All flagellar genes in motile strain of Sphingomonas. sp A1 are highly transcribed.

DESIGN
A two chip study using total RNA recovered from wild-type and motile strains of Sphingomonas. sp A1 grown in 0.5% alginate medium.
