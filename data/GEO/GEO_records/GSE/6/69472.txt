TITLE
RNA Proximity Ligation to Resolve Intramolecular RNA Structures in situ

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Proof-of-concept of a new method involving the limited digestion and subsequent ligation of intramolecular RNA structures in situ followed by deep sequencing

DESIGN
Proof-of-concept of RPL in S. cerevisiae and H. sapiens tissue culture
