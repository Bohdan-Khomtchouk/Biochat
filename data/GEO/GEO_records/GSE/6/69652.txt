TITLE
Dichloroacetate (DCA) prevents cisplatin-induced nephrotoxicity without compromising its anti-cancer properties.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The aim of this study was to compare the transcriptional changes that occur in the kidneys of mice administered cisplatin or DCA, or both, by RNA-seq.

DESIGN
generation of RNA-seq datasets from mice subjected to 4 different treatments. 3 biological replicates per treatment. Saline treatment is control.
