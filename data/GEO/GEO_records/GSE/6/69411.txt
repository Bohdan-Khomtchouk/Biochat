TITLE
Arabidopsis Forever Young Flower (FYF): 35S::FYF+GR

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of  35S::FYF+GR transgenic plant's floral buds(DEX treat) compared to  35S::FYF+GR transgenic plant's floral buds(mock treat). DEX : Dexamethasone

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment: 35S::FYF+GR(DEX treat) vs 35S::FYF+GR(mock treat)  floral buds, Biological replicates : 1 DEX treatment 1  mock treatment
