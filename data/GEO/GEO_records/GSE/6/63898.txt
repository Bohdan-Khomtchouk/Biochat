TITLE
DNA methylation-based prognosis and epidrivers in hepatocellular carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide expression analysis of 228 hepatocellular carcinoma and 168 cirrhotic samples as part of a integrated study of gene expression and DNA-methylation de-regulation in patients with hepatocellular carcinoma Analysis of whole-genome transcriptome changes in human samples from hepatocellular carcinoma patients

DESIGN
Fresh-frozen tissue (primary hepatocellular carcinoma and non-tumor cirrhosis) from patients treated with surgical resection.
