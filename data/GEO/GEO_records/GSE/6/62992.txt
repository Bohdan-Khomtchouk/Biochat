TITLE
Predicting genome-wide DNA methylation

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of normal whole blood samples. The data consist of 100 samples with Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip data.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 100 samples were hybridized to the Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip
