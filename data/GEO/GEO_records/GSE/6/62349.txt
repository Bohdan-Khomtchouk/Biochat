TITLE
Transcriptome profiling of male gametophyte development in Nicotiana tabacum

ORGANISM
Nicotiana tabacum

SUMMARY
Transcriptome profiling of three developmental stages of immature male gametophyte intobacco (Nicotiana tabacum)

DESIGN
Total RNA isolated from tobacco microspores and early and late bicellular pollen was hybridised on Agilent Tobacco Gene Expression Microarray 4x44K in two biological replicates per sample
