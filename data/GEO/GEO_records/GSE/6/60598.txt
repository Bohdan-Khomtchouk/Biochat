TITLE
Early life Lead exposure causes distinct gender specific changes in the DNA methylation profile of DNA extracted from dried blood spots

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Study of influence of gender and Lead(Pb) exposure on DNA methylation for whole blood measured in dried blood spots for a Detroit cohort of child between the age of 3 months to 5years

DESIGN
DNA methylation profiling of dried blood spots for a Detroit cohort of child between the ages of 3 months to 5 years Differential methylation study
