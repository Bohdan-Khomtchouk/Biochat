TITLE
N17 Modifies Mutant Huntingtin Nuclear Pathogenesis and Severity of Disease in HD BAC Transgenic Mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Longitudinal microarray data from BACHD-ΔN17 mice with wildtype littermate controls

DESIGN
Total RNA from cortex and striatum of wildtype and BACHD-ΔN17 mice collected at 2mo, 7mo, and 11mo was used for microarray on the Illumina MouseRef8v.2 platform.
