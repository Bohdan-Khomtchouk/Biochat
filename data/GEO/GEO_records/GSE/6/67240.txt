TITLE
Study the effect of SUZ12 on the responsiveness of IFNg target genes in multiple cancer and non cancer cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We studied the effect of knocking down SUZ12 on the responsiveness of IFNg target genes

DESIGN
12 cell lines were transfected with siRNA against SUZ12 (siSUZ12) or siCtrl and left untreated or treated with IFNg for 6 hours
