TITLE
Hemin and Iron transcriptome of Porphyromonas gingivalis (part 2)

ORGANISM
Porphyromonas gingivalis

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of P. gingivalis cells comparing cells grown under iron replete conditions to that grown under iron depleted conditions

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, iron replete vs. iron deplete grown cells. Biological replicates: 4 independently grown and harvested bacterial cells. Two technical replicates (four replicates per array).
