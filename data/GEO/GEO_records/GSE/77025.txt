TITLE
RNA sequencing of adult zebrafish spinal cord

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
The goal of this study is to determine gene expression changes in the adult zebrafish spinal cord at 2 weeks after complete transection.

DESIGN
2 samples were analyzed in duplicates:  sham injured spinal cord and transected spinal cord at 2 weeks post-injury

PLATFORM
GPL14875 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Danio rerio)
