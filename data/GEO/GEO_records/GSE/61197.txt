TITLE
ASCL1 is a lineage oncogene providing therapeutic targets for high-grade neuroendocrine lung cancers

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
ChIP-Seq analysis performed on 5 ASCL1(+) cell lines and 2 ASCL1 (-) cell lines in order to understand the transcriptome of ASCL1 as it pertains to high-grade neuroendocrine lung cancers

DESIGN
5 ASCL1(+) lung cancer cell lines and 2 ASCL1(-) lung cancer cell lines were compared using ChIP-Seq analysis
