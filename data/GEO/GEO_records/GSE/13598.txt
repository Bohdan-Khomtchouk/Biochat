TITLE
mRNA expression of 131 cancer cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
131 human cancer cell lines' mRNA expression profiles have been characterized. Keywords: Cell Line Comparison

DESIGN
mRNA samples obtained from 131 human cancer cell lines were hybridized to Agilent Human 3.0 A1 arrays and gene expression (mlratio) was measured relative to a common reference RNA pool (Human Universal Reference RNA, Stratagene, La Jolla, CA).
