TITLE
Small RNA sequencing analysis for male germ cells during spermatogenesis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Small RNAs in FACS-purified spermatogonia, spermatocytes (L/Z and P/D) and round spermatids were sequenced to study piRNA populations.

DESIGN
Small RNAs in FACS-purified spermatogonia, spermatocytes (L/Z and P/D) and round spermatids were sequenced to study piRNA populations.
