TITLE
Human Rheumatoid Arthritis Synovial Fibroblasts: Control vs. Tenascin-C Stimulated

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human rheumatoid arthritis synovial fibroblasts comparing control cells treated with BSA with cells treated with Tenascin-C.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, BSA vs. Tenascin-C stimulated cells. One replicate per array.
