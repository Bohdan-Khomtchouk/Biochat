TITLE
mmu-miR-15a-3p activity in mouse cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We intended to investigate effects of mmu-miR-15a-3p on gene expression in mice

DESIGN
We used microarrays to compare gene expression in mouse B/CMBA.Ov cell lines transfected with mmu-miR-15a-3p and negative control mimic
