TITLE
Transcriptional effects of TNF-α and TGF-β1 on human umbilical vein endothelial cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human umbilical vein endothelial cells following stimulation with tumour necrosis factor alpha and transforming growth factor beta singly or combined for 8 hr

DESIGN
All stimulations were for 8 hr - TNF-α vs no cytokine; TGF-β1 vs no cytokine; TNF-α & TGF-β1 vs TNF-α alone; TNF-α & TGF-β1 vs TGF-β1 alone

PLATFORM
GPL10289 Adelaide Microarray Facility CompuGen Human 19K Oligo Array
