TITLE
Evaluation of the Affymetrix Brassica Exon 1.0 ST array

ORGANISM
Brassica

SUMMARY
The aim of the experiment was to evaluate the performance of the Affymetrix Brassica Exon 1.0 ST array. Root and leaf samples from Brassica rapa line R-O-18 were compared. The same RNA samples were hybridised to the Agilent Brassica array, to compare the performance of the two arrays.
