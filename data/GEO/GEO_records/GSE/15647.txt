TITLE
U937 AML1-ETO inducible samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
U937 AML cells that express an inducible AML1-ETO construct under the control of the tetracycline promoter. Microarrays used to discover an AML1-ETO signature for a GE-HTS screen to identify AML1-ETO modulators.

DESIGN
U937 AML cells that express an inducible AML1-ETO construct under the control of the tetracycline promoter at 0, 12, 24, 48, and 72 hours and corresponding controls (2 replicates each).

PLATFORM
GPL91 [HG_U95A] Affymetrix Human Genome U95A Array
