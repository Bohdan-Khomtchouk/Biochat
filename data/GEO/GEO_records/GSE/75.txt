TITLE
FVB Benchmark Set for Cardiac Development, Maturation, and Aging

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The Affymetrix GeneChip MgU74Av1 was used to analyze expression profiles of mice at different developmental stages to monitor changes in cardiac gene expression over time.  More information about this model may be obtained at http://cardiogenomics.med.harvard.edu/groups/proj1/pages/fvb_home.html Keywords = Cardiac development Keywords: time-course

PLATFORM
GPL32 [MG_U74A] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Array
