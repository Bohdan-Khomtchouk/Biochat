TITLE
Coevolution of hormone metabolism and signaling networks expands plant adaptive plasticity

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Differential transcriptomic analysis of 10-day-old Arabidopsis seedlings treated with 50 μM ABA, PA, or DPA.

DESIGN
10-day-old Arabidopsis seedlings grown on MS medium was treated with 50 μM ABA, PA, or DPA. Total RNA was extracted from biological duplicates, Illumina RNAseq was performed on each sample.
