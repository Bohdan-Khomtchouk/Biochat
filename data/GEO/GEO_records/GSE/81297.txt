TITLE
Transcriptome response of avocado roots subjected to flooding and infection by the oomycete Phytophthora cinnamomi

ORGANISM
Persea americana

SUMMARY
Transcriptome response of avocado roots subjected to flooding, infection by the oomycete Phytophthora cinnamomi or a combination of both. Analysis was carried out at two time-points. Aim was to identify important genes in response to these stresses.

DESIGN
Four-condition experiment: Flooded,Infected, Control, Flooded and Infected. Reference design. Biological Replicates: Three replicates for each treatment at each time-point.
