TITLE
Deep sequencing the circadian transcriptome of Drosophila brain

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
RNAseq transcriptional profiling of Drosophila brains from wildtype, and period loss-of-function animals with time points taken over two days.

DESIGN
2 days of brain collection, time points at ZT0, ZT6, ZT12, and ZT18; wildtype and per0 flies.  10-12 brains per time point.

PLATFORM
GPL11203 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Drosophila melanogaster)
