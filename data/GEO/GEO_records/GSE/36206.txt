TITLE
Translatome of NIH3T3 cells: control vs. stress treatment

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Translation efficiencies of mouse mRNAs in control and in cells treated with thapsigargin 10microM for 3h.

DESIGN
Two-conditions experiments. Biological replicates: 1 from three pooled control experiments, 1 from three pooled stress experiments
