TITLE
Drought-responsive changes in poplar roots

ORGANISM
Populus nigra

SUMMARY
Drought-treated and corresponding control root tissue of poplar was subjected to array analyses

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, control (K) vs. Drought-stressed (S) leaves. Biological replicates: 3 control (1-3), drought-exposed (1-3), independently grown and harvested. One swap replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL6036 PICME Poplar 28K 4_1/4_3
