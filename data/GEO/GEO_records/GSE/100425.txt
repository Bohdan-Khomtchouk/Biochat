TITLE
Heterogeneous responses of hematopoietic stem cells to inflammatory stimuli are altered with age - bulk

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This includes bulk RNA-seq samples for sorted LT-HSCs, ST-HSCs, and MPPs stimulated (or not) with LPS+PAM. Samples taken at various time points.

DESIGN
sorted LT-HSCs, ST-HSCs, and MPPs stimulated (or not) with LPS+PAM at various time points

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)

CITATIONS
Mati Mann, Arnav Mehta, Carl de Boer, Monika S. Kowalczyk, Kevin Lee, Daneyal Farouq, Aviv Regev, and David Baltimore. Heterogeneous Responses of Hematopoietic Stem Cells to Inflammatory Stimuli are Altered with Age. bioRxiv 2017. doi:10.1101/163402
