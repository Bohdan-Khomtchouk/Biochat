NCI60 Expression Profiling using the Agilent Whole Human Genome Oligo Microarray

ORGANISM

SUMMARY
We present here a new expression profiling study of the 60 cell lines of the National Cancer Institute (NCI) Developmental Therapeutics program (DTP) drug screen (NCI-60) using the 41,000-probe Agilent Whole Human Genome Oligo Microarray. The expression levels of âˆ¼21,000 genes  were measured.

DESIGN
The profiling study includes quadruplicate technical replicates for six cell lines and duplicates for the remaining cell lines. The resulting data sets are freely available and searchable online in the CellMiner database ( http://discover.nci.nih.gov ).
