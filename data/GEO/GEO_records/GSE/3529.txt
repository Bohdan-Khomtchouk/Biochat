TITLE
Profiling of three different ER+ breast cancer cell lines grown in the presence and absence of estrogen.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Three human ER+ breast cancer cell lines--MCF-7, T47-D, BT-474--grown with or without estradiol (E2). Keywords: Cell Line Comparison

DESIGN
We analyzed biological duplicates of each cell line grown in the presence or absence of estrogen, 12 arrays in all.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
