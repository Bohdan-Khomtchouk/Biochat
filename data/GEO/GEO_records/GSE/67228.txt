TITLE
Expression data from tomato roots and leaves treated with or without Cd.

ORGANISM
Solanum lycopersicum

SUMMARY
We used microarrays to detail the global gene expression in tissues from tomato roots and leaves treated with or without Cd.

DESIGN
Tissues from tomato roots and leaves treated with or without Cd were for RNA extraction and hybridization on Affymetrix microarrays.
