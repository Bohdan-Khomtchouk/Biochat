TITLE
Array CGH with DNA extracted from Candida albicans strains and its derivatives, which is monosomic for chromosome 5

ORGANISM
Candida albicans

SUMMARY
Goal of this experiment was to confirm the proposed monosomy condition of chromsome 5 genes in the derivatives

DESIGN
Pair-wise comparison of genomic content between parental strains and their derivatives.

GPL17313 NimbleGen HX3 array [091120_Candida19_CSU_ASU]
