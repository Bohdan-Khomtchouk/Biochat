TITLE
Transgenic overexpression of Pla2g10

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray gene profiling of thymus from PLA2G10-Tg mice in comparison with that from control mice.

DESIGN
Pla2g10-Tg/+ mice and littermate controls (C57BL/6 background); 6-months old; thymus; pooled from 4 mice for each genotype.
