TITLE
Copy number profiling of PEO1 ovarian cancer cell line

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
PEO1 is part of the PEO/PEA series of ovarian cancer cell lines established in 1988 from progressive samples from three separate cases of ovarian cancer (Langdon et al., 1988).

DESIGN
Copy number profiling of PEO1 ovarian cancer cell line on Illumina HumanOmni1-Quad BeadChip
