TITLE
Halobacterium NRC-1 Growth curve and GTF perturbation

ORGANISM
Halobacterium salinarum NRC-1

SUMMARY
Total RNA was collected at 6 or 8 points throughout the growth curve of Halobacterium NRC1 for wild-type, non-native expression of general transcription factors and knock-out strains of general transcription factors.  This RNA was hybridized against our standard reference RNA (wild-type OD600 0.5-0.6). Keywords: growth genetic modification
