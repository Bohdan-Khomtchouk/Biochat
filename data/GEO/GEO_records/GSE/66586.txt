TITLE
Neuroblastoma cell line gene expression profiling

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles in 5 MYCN-amplified, 3 non-MYCN-amplified, and 2 non-malignant cell lines were analyzed.

DESIGN
5 MYCN-amplified, 3 non-MYCN-amplified, and 2 non-malignant cell lines were profiled without any pre-treatment.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
