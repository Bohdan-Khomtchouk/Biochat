TITLE
Relative spatial heterogeneity revealed by transcriptional profiling of multi-region high-grade glioma samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Affymetrix GeneChip® Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array profiling of 20 primary, multi-region high-grade glioma samples.

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from primary high-grade glioma samples and hybridized to GeneChip® Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array according to the manufacturer's instructions.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
