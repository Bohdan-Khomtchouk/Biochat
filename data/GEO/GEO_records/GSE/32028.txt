TITLE
Mouse Placenta:Maternal experimental UTI vs Sham UTI infected

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling to map the changes in placental function subsequent to maternal experimental UTI that result in intrauterine growth restriction.

DESIGN
One condition experiment: 2 biological replicates of placenta from mice with maternal experimental UTI, 2 biological replicates of placenta from mice with sham UTI.
