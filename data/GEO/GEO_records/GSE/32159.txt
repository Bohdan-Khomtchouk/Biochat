TITLE
Community network analysis of complex microbial biofilms reveals characteristic microbial assemblages in health and disease

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study compared the subgingival microbiota of subjects with periodontitis  to those with periodontal health using the Human Oral Microbe Identification Microarray (HOMIM).

DESIGN
The microbial profiles of subgingival plaque from healthy and periodontitis subjects was compared using community network analysis.
