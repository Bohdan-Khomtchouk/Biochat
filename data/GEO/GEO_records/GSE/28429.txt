TITLE
Candida albicans Microarray studies: CAP2 (Wild type) vs. cap2D (Homozygous null mutant)

ORGANISM
Candida albicans

SUMMARY
Transcriptome profiling to identify Cap2/Hap43 regulons in the human fungal pathogen Candida albicans: Wild type vs. cap2D grown in iron-depleted medium

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, WT vs. cap2D cells. Biological replicates: Wild-type and cap2D, independently grown in iron-depleted medium and harvested. One replicate per array.  Hybridization was repeated (technical replicate) for each biological replicate.
