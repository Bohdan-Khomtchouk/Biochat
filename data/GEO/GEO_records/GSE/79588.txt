TITLE
Genome-wide maps of TRIM25 binding sites in triple negative breast cancer cell

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We report high-throughput profiling of TRIM25 binding sites in triple negative breast cancer cells.

DESIGN
ChIP-seq was performed using a TRIM25 antibody on 2 triple negative breast cancer cell lines

PLATFORM
