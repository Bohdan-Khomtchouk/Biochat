TITLE
Transcriptomic profiling of normal human cardiac fibroblast treated with halofuginone

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Normal human cardiac fibroblast were treated with 200 nM halofuginone for 24 hours and the transcriptomic profile was measured by RNAseq.

DESIGN
Total of 6 samples analyzed, 3 treated with vehicle and 3 treated with halofuginone.
