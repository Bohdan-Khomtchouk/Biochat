TITLE
Ribosome profiling and RNA sequencing of MCF10A cells treated with TGFb

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
MCF10A were either untreated or treated with TGFb. RNA sequencing and ribosome profiling was performed.

DESIGN
Ribosome profiling on 3 biological replicates and matching RNA sequencing on one of the replicates.
