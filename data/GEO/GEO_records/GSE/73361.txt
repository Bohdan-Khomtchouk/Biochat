TITLE
Expression analysis of 24 endometrial stroma sarcoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The whole-gene expression profile of 24 endometrial stroma sarcol was used to identify genes that were deregulated in the tumors genome.

DESIGN
The analysis was performed comparing the high-grade to the low-grade tumors.
