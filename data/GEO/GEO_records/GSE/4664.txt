TITLE
24 hour time course: regulation of uterine genes by estradiol in ovariectomized mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Using microarray technology, we compared the global expression pattern of uterine RNA from ovariectomized control mice to those of ovariectomized mice treated with estradiol for various intervals between 30 minutes and 24 hours. Keywords: estrogen, uterus, genomic, mouse
