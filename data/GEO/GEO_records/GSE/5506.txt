TITLE
LNCaP cells treated with gedunin or vehicle for 6h

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gedunin is a natural product that affects LNCaP gene expression by 6h We used microarrays to detail the global programme of gene expression affected by gedunin treatment at 6h Keywords: drug treatment

DESIGN
LNCaP cells were grown to 50% confluency and treated with gedunin for 6h prior to direct Trizol lysis and RNA isolation
