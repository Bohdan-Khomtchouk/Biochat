TITLE
Hybridization of four Euphorbs on Euphorbiaceae arrays

ORGANISM
Manihot esculenta ; Ricinus communis ; Euphorbia esula ; Euphorbia pulcherrima

SUMMARY
These experiments were designed to determine the usefulness of the spurge/cassava euphorbiaceae arrays for investigating gene expression in other euphorbs. Keywords: heterologous hybridization euphorbia

DESIGN
two color dye swap design

PLATFORM
GPL4655 High density Euphorbia esula and Manihot esculenta array
