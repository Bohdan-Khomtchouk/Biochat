TITLE
polyA depleted RNA-seq from GM12878 (ENCSR000CQF)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from the nuclear fraction of independent growths of cell line GM12878. They are stranded PE76 Illumina GAIIx RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted and DSN normalized Poly-A- RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
