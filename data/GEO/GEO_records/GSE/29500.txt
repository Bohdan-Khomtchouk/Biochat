TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of atrial left-right differences in two different mouse strains

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Two wild-type mouse strains (Swiss Agouti and MF1) were investigated regarding the mRNA expression patterns of their left and right atria at 12 months of age.

DESIGN
Eight samples of each strain, four with left atrium and four with right atrium examined.

PLATFORM
GPL6885 Illumina MouseRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
