TITLE
Expression data in Human rectal mucosa before and after an dietary intervention with casein, soy protein or maltodextrin

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The objective of the sudy is to evalute the effects of quantity and quality of protein intake on large intestine mucosa We used microarrays to investigate gene expression in rectal biopsies before and after the diet

DESIGN
Rectal biopsies were collected before and after a dietary intervention with casein, soy protein or maltodextrin (placebo),

PLATFORM
GPL20103 [HuGene-2_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 2.0 ST Array [CDF: hugene20st_Hs_ENTREZG.cdf, Brainarray version 19.0.0]
