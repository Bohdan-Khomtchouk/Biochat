TITLE
Expression data from human colonic biopsy samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Age-related transcriptome changes can contribute to better understanding of gastrointestinal disorders such as colorectal cancer in older population

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from colonic biopsy samples from histologically normal children and the samples were hybridized on Affymetrix HGU133 Plus 2.0 microarrays
