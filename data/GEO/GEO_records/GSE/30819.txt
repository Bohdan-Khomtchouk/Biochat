TITLE
Extraction of high quality total RNA from liquid nitrogen stocks

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Microarray expression arrays on THP-1 acute myeloid leukemia cell line samples, contrasting expression profiles of freshly harvested cell pellets, cells replated into fresh media for 24 hours and cells directly pelleted from liquid nitrogen stocks.

DESIGN
Nine samples were analysed, composed of 3 biological replicates for each state (fresh, replated, frozen)

PLATFORM
GPL6884 Illumina HumanWG-6 v3.0 expression beadchip
