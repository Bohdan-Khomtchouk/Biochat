TITLE
Bag101 overexpression in S. pombe

ORGANISM
Schizosaccharomyces pombe

SUMMARY
Bag101 overexpression causes growth defects. The purpose of the experiment was to quantify gene expression differences caused by bag101 overexpression.

DESIGN
5 independent repeats of control (no bag101 overexpression) and 5 independent repeats of bag101 overexpression.
