TITLE
Methylation profiling in Ulcerative colitis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The series was designed to identify the different methylated single CpGs involved in the pathophysiology of ulcerative colitis.

DESIGN
A cohort of n=20 monozygotic twins, discordant for ulcerative colitis was selected. Illumina and Nimblegen platforms were used.

PLATFORM
GPL8490 Illumina HumanMethylation27 BeadChip (HumanMethylation27_270596_v.1.2) GPL13269 Nimblegen Human high-density promoter array CpG island methylation array
