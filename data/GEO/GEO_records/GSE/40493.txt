TITLE
Bcl6-deficient regulatory T cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
gene expression data from wild-type and Bcl6-/- regulatory T cells in order to find genes regulated by Bcl6 in Treg cells

DESIGN
FoxP3+ Tregs were sorted from wild-type (WT) and Bcl6-/- (KO) mice-- 8 samples, 2 from each type of Treg, 2 WT and 2 KO
