TITLE
Genome-scale methylome profiling of adrenocortical carcinomas (ACC) and adenomas (ACA)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-scale DNA methylation was analyzed in a cohort of ACC and ACA to identify DNA methylation changes.

DESIGN
Bisulfite converted DNA from 51 fresh frozen ACC and 30 ACA samples were hybridized to Illumina HumanMethylation27 BeadChips.

PLATFORM
GPL8490 Illumina HumanMethylation27 BeadChip (HumanMethylation27_270596_v.1.2)
