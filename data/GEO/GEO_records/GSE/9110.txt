TITLE
SNP array data for 56 chronic myeloid leukemia samples.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Copy number and LOH analysis was performed for 56 chronic myeloid leukemia cases obtained from 23 CML cases obtained atchronic phase, accelerated phase, blast crisis, or remission. All caseswere genotyped with Affymetrix 250k Sty and Nsp arrays. Keywords: Acute leukemia, BCR-ABL1, chronic myeloid leukemia, copy number analysis, loss-of-heterozygosity, genomics *** Due to privacy concerns, the primary SNP array data is no longer available with unrestricted access.
