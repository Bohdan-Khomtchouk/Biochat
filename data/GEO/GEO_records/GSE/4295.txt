TITLE
Sugar stress during fermentation (baker's yeast)

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Functional genomic analysis using different types of baker's yeast. Keywords: fermentation

DESIGN
Fermentation at different concentrations of sucrose. PerChip normalization using only positive (exclude pombe) genes and PerGene normalization were applied using GeneSpring (v7.0).

PLATFORM
GPL2529 [Yeast_2] Affymetrix Yeast Genome 2.0 Array
