TITLE
Barrett's esophagus

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Barrett's esophagus transcriptome was analysed and compared with Barrett's esophagus primary cell culture and esophageal adenocarcinoma. Keywords: SAGE analysis to compare tissues

DESIGN
Barrett's esophagus biopsy was taken from 1 male metaplastic Barrett's esophagus patient. Barrett's esophagus primary cell culture was cultures from a biopsy taken from a Barrett's esophagus patient and cultured for about 4 to 5 weeks. Esophageal adenocarcinoma was taken from a patient known to have cancer and previously Barrett's esophagus
