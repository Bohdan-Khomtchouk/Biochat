TITLE
Suz12, EZH2, EED1, H3me3K27, H3me3K9 and RNA Pol II target promoters in mouse F9 and mES cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
For each sample a matched total was prepared in parallel.  For each biological replicate the cells were crosslinked on different days.  Amplicons were prepared from each sample and hybridized on mouse 1.5 kb promoter arrays from Nimblegen. Keywords: ChIP-chip
