TITLE
Single-Cell RNAseq analysis of diffuse neoplastic infiltrating cells at the migrating front of human glioblastoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We used single-cell RNAseq to investigate the heterogeneity of glioblastoma tumors and assess differential expression between cells within and in proximity of the tumor.

DESIGN
Examination of cell types in human primary glioblastoma samples.
