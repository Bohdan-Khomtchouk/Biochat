TITLE
Drosophila melanogaster short read RNA

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Drosophila melanogaster RNA sequencing with Illumina Genome Analyzer. High-throughput sequencing of Drosophila melanogaster RNAs. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODEDataReleasePolicyFinal2008.pdf

DESIGN
5 Samples: Single Stranded cDNA, Oligo-DT Primed, run using a 1, 2 and 4 p-mol concentration, no replicates. Double Stranded cDNA, Random Primed, run using a 2 p-mol concentration, 2 replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL9058 Illumina Genome Analyzer (Drosophila melanogaster)
