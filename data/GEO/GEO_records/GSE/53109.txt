TITLE
Chromatin state in thymic epithelial cells [ChIP-seq]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The study set out to assess the genome-wide distribution of the chromatin modifications H3K4me3 and H3K27me3 in mature thymic epithelial cells.

DESIGN
ChIP and input DNA was prepared from biological replicated FACS sorted mature mTEC samples and sequenced.

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
