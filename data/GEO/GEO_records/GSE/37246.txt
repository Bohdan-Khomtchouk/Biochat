TITLE
SBRI_AB Host response in primary mouse tracheal epitelial cells (mTEC)  to influenza infection with PR8, VN or X31 at MOI of 0.015 at 12 hours

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Array analysis of total RNA from mTECs 12 h post infection with influenza. Strains used were Mock, PR8, X31, VN6+2 (MOI 0.015)

DESIGN
Time Point: 12 h post infection (Mock, PR8, X31, VN) MOI 0.015; in biological triplicates. Total samples: 12.
