TITLE
RNA-seq of YB5 cells treated with Proscillaridin A

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNA-seq was performed after YB5 cells were treated with DAC, Proscillaridin A, or the combinatino of the two

DESIGN
Biological triplicates were performed for a total of 12 samples. Fold change of each gene was calculated by comparing change in expression after inhibitor treatment to expression in the control samples

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
