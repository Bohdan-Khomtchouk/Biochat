TITLE
Systemic Lupus Erythematosus patient blood with controls

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The experiment consists of 31 Systemic Lupus Erythematosus patient blood samples and 29 healthy donor blood samples.

DESIGN
Whole blood was collected in PaxGene tubes from 31 SLE and 29 healthy donors.
