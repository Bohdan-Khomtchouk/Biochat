TITLE
Transcription profiling of horned and polled and scurs cattle

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Transciprional profiling of horn forming region of Bos taurus comparing horned, polled and scurred cattle.

DESIGN
3 condition experiment, Polled vs Horned, Polled vs Scurred, Scurred vs Horned, male and female samples, including dye swaps. 5 chips. No confounding between sex and chip. No confounding between indivdual and chip.

PLATFORM
GPL7053 Agilent-015354 Bovine Oligo Microarray (4x44K) (Feature Number version)
