TITLE
P. aeruginosa PA14-WT vs pyrF mutant vs pyrF mutant w/uracil

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa

SUMMARY
Comparative transcriptome analyses of P. aeruginosa PA14 pyrF mutant with PA14 wild type, and with PA14 pyrF mutant with 1 mM uracil supplement and PA14 wild type with 10 mM uracil in biofilm cells. All samples were cultured in LB with glass wool at 37C for 7h. Keywords: Pseudomonas aeruginosa biofilm pyrF uracil

DESIGN
Strains: P. aeruginosa PA14 wild type, PA14 pyrF mutant Medium: LB for PA14 wild type and PA14 pyrF mutant, LB with 1 mM uracil for PA14 pyrF mutant, LB with 10 mM uracil for PA14 wild type Cell type: Biofilm cells grown on glass wool Time: 7 h Temperature: 37C

PLATFORM
GPL84 [Pae_G1a] Affymetrix Pseudomonas aeruginosa Array

CITATIONS
Akihiro Ueda, Can Attila, Marvin Whiteley, Thomas K. Wood. Uracil influences quorum sensing and biofilm formation in Pseudomonas aeruginosa and fluorouracil is an antagonist; Volume 2 Issue 1, Pages 62 - 74. DOI: 10.1111/j.1751-7915.2008.00060.x
