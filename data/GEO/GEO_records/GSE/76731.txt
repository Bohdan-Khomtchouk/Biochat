TITLE
Microarray of Bordetella-infected bone marrow-derived dendritic cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Bone marrow-derived dendritic cells were infected with live or heat-killed Bordetella and cells were analyzed on day 4 post-infection cells were > 90% CD11c-positive

DESIGN
3 replicates were performed

PLATFORM
