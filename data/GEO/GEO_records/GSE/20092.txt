TITLE
Nitrate Stress in Desulfovibrio vulgaris Hildenborough (Fur knockout : JW707)

ORGANISM
Desulfovibrio vulgaris str. Hildenborough

SUMMARY
Using data from microarray experiments, we investigated the effects of excess sodium nitrate on D. vulgaris. Keywords: stress response, time course

DESIGN
Comparison of cells treated with 105 mM sodium nitrate to untreated cells at times of 0, 30, 60, 120, and 240 min.
