TITLE
Transcriptional profiling of a breast cancer cell line panel using RNAseq technology

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
56 breast cancer cell lines were profiled to identify patterns of gene expression associated with subtype and response to therapeutic compounds.

DESIGN
Cell lines were profiled in their baseline, unperturbed state.
