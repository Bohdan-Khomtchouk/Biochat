TITLE
RNA-seq for gastric cancer and normal tissues/cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed RNA-seq experiments to identify differentially expressed intergenic transcripts between gastric cancer and normal tissues/cells.

DESIGN
Three primary cell culture samples from gastric cancer tissues, three gastric cancer cell lines and two normal tissue samples were used for the experiments.

PLATFORM
GPL9115 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Homo sapiens)
