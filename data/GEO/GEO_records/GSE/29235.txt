TITLE
Transcriptional response of Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 to Zinc

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pneumoniae D39

SUMMARY
Transcriptome comparison of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type grown in CDM Plus 0mM Zn2+ to grown in CDM plus 0.2 mM Zn2+.

DESIGN
Two condition design comparison of Wild-type strain including a dye swap

PLATFORM
GPL11484 Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 Multiplatform 1909k array

CITATIONS
