TITLE
Baseline skeletal muscle gene expression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Muscle biopsy samples were obtained from two groups of male subjects prior to endurance training. The samples were used to predict training responses. Baseline gene expression involving 30 probe sets was able to classify subjects into high and low responders.

DESIGN
Resting skeletal muscle sample after an overnight fast.

PLATFORM
GPL9454 Affymetrix GeneChip Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array [HGU133Plus2_Hs_ENST (v10) CDF]
