TITLE
Phase II exploratory pharmacogenomics study of cetuximab monotherapy in patients with advanced metastatic CRC

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Patients with metastatic colorectal cancer were enrolled for treatment with cetuximab monotherapy.  Transcriptional profiling was conducted on RNA from pre-treatment metastatic site biopsies to identify genes whose expression correlates with best clinical responses. Keywords: comparison of disease control group versus non-responder group of patients

DESIGN
single pre-treatment metastatic biopsy from each patient analyzed

PLATFORM
GPL571 [HG-U133A_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A 2.0 Array
