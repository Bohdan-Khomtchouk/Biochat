TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of mouse embryonic stem cells upon PARP-1 knockout.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA-seq was performed in both wild type and parp1-/- ES cells to check the effect of PARP-1 depletion on gene expression profile in mESC.

DESIGN
Two biological replicates were performed in both wild type and Parp-1-/- conditions.
