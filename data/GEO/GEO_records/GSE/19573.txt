TITLE
Ceratitis capitata gene expression related to mating status in mature females (mated vs virgin)

ORGANISM
Ceratitis capitata

SUMMARY
This analysis compares gene expression between 4 day old mated and 4 day old virgin medfly, Ceratitis capitata females

DESIGN
This analysis compares gene expression between 4 day old mated and 4 day old virgin medfly, Ceratitis capitata females. Samples where made from the heads of the females.
