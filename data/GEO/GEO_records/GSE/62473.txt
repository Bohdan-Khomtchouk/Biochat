TITLE
Transriptome analysis of VCAP and CWR22-Pc cell lines after concomittant treatment with androgen with bicalutamide and enzalutamide

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transriptome analysis of VCAP and CWR22-Pc cell lines after concomittant treatment with androgen with bicalutamide and enzalutamide

DESIGN
VCAP and CWR22-Pc cells growing in log phase were treated for 24 hours with vehicle, the androgen DHT, and DHT plus either bicalutamide or enzalutamide for 24 hours.
