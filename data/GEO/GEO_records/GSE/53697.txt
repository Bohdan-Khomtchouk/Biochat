TITLE
RNAseq in Alzheimer's Disease patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human brain samples from control and advanced Alzheimer's Diseased patients were subjected to RNAseq analysis to monitor RNA level changes during AD progression

DESIGN
Human brain samples were obtained from the Mount Sinai Brain Bank; RNA was Trizol extracted, Ribominus selected and submitted for high-throughput sequencing.

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
