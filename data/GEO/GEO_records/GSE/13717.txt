TITLE
Transcriptional profile of Candida parapsilosis in SD media

ORGANISM
Candida parapsilosis

SUMMARY
Partial genome microarray  and plankton cells

DESIGN
RNA was isolated from biofilms and from planktonic cells grown in SD media. Samples were labeled with either Cy5 or Cy3.  Four independent biological replicates were compared, including dye swaps.
