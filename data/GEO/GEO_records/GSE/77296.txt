TITLE
Gene expression-based, etiology analysis for Hirschsprung's Disease

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Several microRNAs which assosiate with Hirschsprung's disease were screened from 271 differentially expressed microRNAs between pathological changed colon segments of Hirschsprung's Disease and normal colon.These identified miRNAs could be used as diagnostic markers of HSCR segments.
