TITLE
Murine bone marrow cells: Wild-type vs. PU.1 knockdown

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Genome-wide screen for aberrant DNA methylation in bone marrow of PU.1 knockdown mice compared to control wildtype animals in three stages

DESIGN
7 age and gender matched mice pairs in the preleukemic stage, 5 pairs in the early leukemic stage and 7 pairs in the late leukemic stage

PLATFORM
GPL10324 Agilent-015279 Mouse CpG Island ChIP-on-Chip Microarray 2x105K  (Probe Name version)
