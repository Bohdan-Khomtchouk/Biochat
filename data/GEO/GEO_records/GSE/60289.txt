TITLE
Expression profile in response to sphingolipid

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
In order the obtain information which genes are differentially expressed in response to glucosylceramide

DESIGN
GC of hen of the woods and soybean were added at 40 ug/ml to yeast cells, incubated for 17 h and total RNA was extracted.
