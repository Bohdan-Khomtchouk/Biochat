TITLE
Impaired Insulin-/IGF1-Signaling Extends Life Span by Promoting Mitochondrial L-Proline Catabolism to Induce a Transient ROS-Signal

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans ; Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptome profiling of three models with impaired insulin/IGF1 signaling. 1. Deep sequencing of endogenous mRNA from Caenorhabditis elegans N2 var. Bristol (wildtype) and daf-2(e1370) mutant; 2. Deep sequencing  of endogenous mRNA from murine embryonic fibroblasts (MEF)  wildtype and irs1-/- knockout; 3. Deep sequencing of endogenous mRNA from murine embryoinic fibroblast (MEF) insr+/- -lox and insr+/- knockout Jena Centre for Systems Biology of Ageing - JenAge (www.jenage.de)

DESIGN
14 samples examined: C. elegans N2 var. Bristol (wildtype) vs. daf-2(e1370) mutant; MEF wildtype vs. irs1-/- knockout; MEF insr+/- -lox vs. insr +/- knockout
