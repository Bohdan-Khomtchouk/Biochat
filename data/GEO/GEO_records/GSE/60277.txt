TITLE
Transcript profiling of seven tissues in P. glauca

ORGANISM
Picea glauca

SUMMARY
The goal is to identify tissue specific patterns of expression in Picea glauca seedlings

DESIGN
One-color comparison of seven tissues of white spruce. Four biological repetitions per tissue (non-related trees), repeated in two channels as technical replicates, for a total of 56 slides.
