TITLE
Heme-based respiration of Leuconostoc gasicomitatum

ORGANISM
Leuconostoc gelidum subsp. gasicomitatum LMG 18811

SUMMARY
Transcriptonal profiling of Leuconostoc gasicomitatum LMG18811T (wild type)  grown in MRS medium with or without heme. Mutant LMG18811T::pSIP1333A (mutating cydB gene which is essential in the respiratory chain) grown in MRS with or without heme. Comparing mutant and wildtype with and without heme.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiments with wild type and cydB mutant. 1) wild type with heme vs wild type without heme (HVV), 2) wild type with heme vs mutant with heme (HMHV) 3) mutant with heme vs mutant without heme (MHM) 4) wildtype without heme vs mutant without heme (MV).
