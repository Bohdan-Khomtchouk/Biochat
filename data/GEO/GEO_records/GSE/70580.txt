TITLE
Single cell RNA-sequencing of human tonsil Innate lymphoid cells (ILCs)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Single cell RNA-sequencing of human tonsil Innate lymphoid cells (ILCs) from three independent tonsil donors.

DESIGN
Sequencing libraries were prepared from FACS sorted individual ILCs with the Smart-Seq2 protocol  (Picelli et al. Nature Methods 2013)
