TITLE
The Australian Mammographic Density Twins and Sisters Study - DNA methylation data

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA methylation data for 479 women from 130 families including 66 monozygotic twin pairs, 66 dizygotic twin pairs and 215 sisters of twins.

DESIGN
Genomic DNA was extracted from dried blood spots. DNA was sodium bisulfite converted using the EZ DNA Methylation-Gold protocol. Epigenome-wide methylation was assessed using the Infinium HumanMethylation450 BeadChip arrays

GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
