TITLE
RNA-Seq of Bacillus subtilis and its phage AR9 during the infection

ORGANISM
Bacillus subtilis ; Bacillus phage AR9

SUMMARY
Time course of after infection (5 min, 20 min and 40 min) of Bacillus subtilis by phage AR9

DESIGN
Examination of changes of gene expression at early (5 min), middle (20 min) and late (40 min) stages of infection of Bacillus subtilis by phage AR9. Mapping of transcription start sites of phage AR9.
