TITLE
Genome-scale analysis of DNA methylation in rheumatic heart disease

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The aim of the current study is to identify DNA methylation markers associated with rheumatic heart disease with secondary pulmonary arterial hypertension (RHD-PAH).

DESIGN
Genome-wide DNA methylation study is performed among 6 RHD-PAH patients and 6 healthy controls using Illumina HumanMethylation 450K array.
