TITLE
miRNA in adult T-cell leukemia/lymphoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
miRNA expression was investigated in ATLL samples and in normal CD4 T-cells (resting and in vitro-stimulated).

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated from ATLL samples and normal T-cell controls. miRNA expression was measured using Agilent miRNA arrays.

PLATFORM
GPL9081 Agilent-016436 Human miRNA Microarray 1.0 G4472A (miRNA ID version)
