TITLE
Transcriptomic Profiling of Salmonella enterica Enteritidis and Typhimurium under Chlorine Based Oxidation

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium str. LT2

SUMMARY
Investigation of whole genome gene expression level changes in Salmonella enterica serova Enteritidis and Typhimurium under chlorine treatment

DESIGN
An eighteen chip study using total RNA isolated from three separate cultures of (1) S. Enteritidis in BHI broth (2) S. Typhimurium in BHI broth (3) S. Enteritidis in BHI broth w/ 130 ppm chlorine (4) S. Typhimurium in BHI w/ 130 ppm chlorine (5) S. Enteritidis in BHI broth w/ 390 ppm (6) S. Typhimurium in BHI broth w/ 390 ppm. Each chip measures the expression level of 5,027 ORFs covering the whole genome of S. Enteritidis and S. Typhimurium.
