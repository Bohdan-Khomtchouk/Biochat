TITLE
Pervasive effects of aging on gene expression in wild wolves

ORGANISM
Canis lupus

SUMMARY
We sequenced total RNA from whole blood samples of 27 wild gray wolves from Yellowstone National Park.

DESIGN
Gene expression level analysis of both male and female wolves, ranging from ages 0.8-8.8 years.
