TITLE
Gene Expression profiling in healthy population samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
734 whole-blood RNA samples from Estonian Biobank were profiled to find molecular mechanisms behind human complex diseases deidentified clinical and serum metabolite data requested but not provided

DESIGN
734 whole-blood RNA samples were profiled with Illumina HT12v3 array. Same samples are genotyped and profiled for serum metabolites.
