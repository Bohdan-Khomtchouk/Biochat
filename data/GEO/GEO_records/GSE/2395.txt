SUMMARY
Summary: CF patients homozygous for the DF08 DF08 genotype present a full range of phenotypic manifestations that exist within the pulmonary system. This project aims to identify candidate genes that influence the severity of pulmonary disease Hypothesis: The goal is to find genes predictive of progression in CF.
