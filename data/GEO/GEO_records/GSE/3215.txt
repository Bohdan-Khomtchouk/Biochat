TITLE
Gene Expression for fetal kidneys of hyper insulinemia induced intrauterine growth restriction  rat model

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Inrauterine growth restriction was induced by chronic hyper insulinemia in pregnant rats and differential gene expression was studied using affymetrix rat genome RAE230A.Data was analysed using SAM. Keywords: disease state analysis

DESIGN
rna was isolated from 5 kidneys of growth restricted fetus and compared with 5 normal controls

PLATFORM
GPL341 [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
