SUMMARY
A genome-scale assessment of peripheral blood B-cell molecular homeostasis in patients with rheumatoid arthritis. Keywords: Rheumatoid Arthritis, B-cells

DESIGN
RNA from B-cells was seperately pooled from 8 RA patients and 8 controls, hybridized to long oligonucleotide microarrays in order to identify disease related differentially expressed genes.
