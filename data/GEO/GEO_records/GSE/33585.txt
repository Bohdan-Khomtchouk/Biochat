TITLE
Expression data from monocytic cell lines (THP)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The experiment aims to identify transcriptional effects of Infliximab (an anti-TNF antibody) and CDP870 on human cell lines Results were generated using 3 timepoints: 0h, 6h, and 24h with each stimuli.

DESIGN
3 biological replicates in each of the 5 groups: oh control; 6h IFX; 24h IFX; 6h CDP870; 24h CDP870

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
