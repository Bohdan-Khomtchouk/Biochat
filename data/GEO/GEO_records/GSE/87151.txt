TITLE
Expression data from fetal sheep lung tissue (right lower lobe)

ORGANISM
Ovis aries

SUMMARY
Study investigating responses of lung to sub-clinical antenatal steroid (betamethasone phosphate) exposure Microarray was used to determine the inflammatory transcriptome in homogenised lung tissue samples

DESIGN
Fetal sheep at 115d GA were catheterised prior to 2 maternal IV infusions of betametahasone phosphate
