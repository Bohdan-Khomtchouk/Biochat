TITLE
Profiling and comparison of miRNA populations in male and female tissues from Drosophila melanogaster at various stages

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
By surveying miRNA populations in each sex, we identified sets of miRNAs differentially expressed in male and female tissues across various stages of development.

DESIGN
Small RNAs cloning of dissected male and female tissues from Drosophila melanogaster at various stages

PLATFORM
GPL11203 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Drosophila melanogaster)
