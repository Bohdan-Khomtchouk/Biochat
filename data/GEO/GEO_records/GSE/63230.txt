TITLE
EZH2-bound RNAs associated with progression of prostate cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Through high-throughput RNA immunoprecipitation sequencing (RIP-seq), we discovered that EZH2 protein associates with messenger RNAs of a number of genes.

DESIGN
Examination of EZH2-bound RNAs in 2 cell types (LNCaP, C4-2).
