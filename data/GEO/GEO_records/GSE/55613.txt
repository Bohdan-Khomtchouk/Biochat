TITLE
Effects of SGI-110 on A2780 ovarian cancer xenografts

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide DNA methylation profiles of SGI-110-treated ovarian cancer xenografts were obtained using next generation Illumina Infinium 450k assay which includes over 450,000 GpG sites.

DESIGN
DNA from 6 samples were hybridized to the Illumina's Infinium HumanMethylation 450 BeadChip

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
