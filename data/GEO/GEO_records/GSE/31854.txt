TITLE
Liver protective effects of white button mushroom (Agaricus Bisporus)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To study the mechanism of protective effect by White Button Mushroom (WBM) for Nonalcoholic Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD) in ovariectomized mice (model for postmenopausal women).

DESIGN
The ovariectomized mice were fed WBM diet for 3 month, sacrificed to harvest liver. 4 mice for control diet and 4 mice for WBM diet.
