TITLE
WT vs. ACE2 knockout (grown in hyphal form)

ORGANISM
Candida albicans

SUMMARY
We have used DNA microarray technology to compare the expression of over 6000 genes between C. albicans WT and ACE2 knockout strains in the hyphal form. Keywords: Gene expression comparison

DESIGN
We analyzed 5 arrays for the hyphal-form comparison experiment. No dye-swaps were performed.
