TITLE
Bcl6 mediates innate immune response

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Using microarrays, we compared the changes in levels of gene expression between wildtype and Bcl6 KO macrophages in the absence or presence of LPS.

DESIGN
Total RNA was obtained from WT and Bcl6 KO unstimulated and LPS-stimulated primary bone marrow-derived macrophages
