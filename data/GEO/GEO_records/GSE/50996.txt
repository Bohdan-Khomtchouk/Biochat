TITLE
Identification of RNAs associated with S. cerevisiae or N. crassa Puf3

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Affinity purification of S. cerevisiae or N. crassa Puf3 from S. cerevisiae cells and identification of associated RNAs by microarray

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, RNAs associated with purified Puf3 vs depleted supernant. Biological replicates: 3 x N. crassa Puf3, 3 x S. cerevisiae Puf3, and 3 x mock. One replicate per array.
