TITLE
Cell type-specific ribosome profiling in vivo.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens ; Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report cell-type specific ribosome profiling in a mouse glioma model.

DESIGN
We report a strategy for cell-type specific ribosome profiling in vivo. Our strategy was applied to the characterization of a mouse glioma model.
