TITLE
Gene expression in the olfactory epithelia of parasitic and reproductive adult sea lampreys

ORGANISM
Petromyzon marinus

SUMMARY
To determine gene expression differences in the olfactory epithelium of sea lamprey between sequential yet behaviorally distinct adult life history stages

DESIGN
2 samples: parasitic adults removed from fish in northern Lake Huron and Lake Michigan in February and March, and reproductive adults collected from Lake Huron and Lake Michigan tributaries in June

PLATFORM
GPL11029 454 GS 20 (Petromyzon marinus)
