TITLE
Hepatic Gene Expression in LIRKO Mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Control and Liver Insulin Receptor KO mice (LIRKO) were sacrificed in the non-fasted state. RNA was prepared from liver samples and subjected to expression microarray analysis Each array was hybridized with sample derived from 2-3 mice of the same genotype.

DESIGN
Three arrays per group were used.

PLATFORM
GPL81 [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
