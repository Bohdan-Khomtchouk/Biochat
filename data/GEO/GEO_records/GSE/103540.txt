TITLE
Transcriptome data of Pichia caribbica induced by H2O2

ORGANISM
Meyerozyma caribbica

SUMMARY
Pichia caribbica, which was isolated by our laboratory, was cultured at 4 °C on nutrient yeast dextrose agar (NYDA).A loop of the culture was inoculated in nutrient yeast dextrose broth and incubated on a rotary shaker at 180 rpm at 28 °C for 20 h.

DESIGN
Pichia caribbica cultured at NYDB (control, non H2O2) and NYDB ( induced by H2O2)

PLATFORM
GPL23987 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Meyerozyma caribbica)
