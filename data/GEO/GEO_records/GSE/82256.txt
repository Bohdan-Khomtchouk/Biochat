TITLE
Shewanella piezotolerans WP3 wild-type strain cold shock for 30 min

ORGANISM
Shewanella piezotolerans WP3

SUMMARY
The WP3 strains was cultured at 20°C and cold shocked at 0°C for 30 min. The transcriptional profiles after and before cold shock were compared.

DESIGN
Three biological replicate samples and 6 technical replicates for each experiment

PLATFORM
GPL16568 Shewanella piezotolerans WP3 whole genome microarray
