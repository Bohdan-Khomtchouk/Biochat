TITLE
Gene expression profiling of retinoblastoma Y79 cell line grown with scaffold compared to cells without scaffold

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Retinoblastoma Y79 cell line was grown on 3D scaffolds and compared its gene expression profile with Y79 cells grown without scaffold.

DESIGN
Agilent one-color experiment, Organism: Human, Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F, Labeling kit: Agilent Quick-Amp labeling Kit (p/n5190-0442)

PLATFORM
GPL6480 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Probe Name version)
