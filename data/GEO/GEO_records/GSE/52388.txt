TITLE
IgMhigh vs IgMlow B cells from bovine leukemia virus-infected cattle

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of blood B cells from bovine leukemia virus-infected cattle comparing IgMhigh B cells with IgMlow B cells. Goal was to estimate the difference of cellular function in both subset.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, IgMhigh B cells vs. IgMlow B cells from three bovine leukemia virus-infected cattle.
