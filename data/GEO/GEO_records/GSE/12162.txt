TITLE
Murine Colon26 cells: Primary vs. liver metastasis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of mouse Colon26 cancer cells comparing control primary tumours in the colon with their liver metastases. Keywords: Metastatic variant

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Colon26 primary tumours vs. their liver metastases. Biological replicates: 2 primary tumours, 2 liver metastases, independently grown and harvested. One replicate per array.
