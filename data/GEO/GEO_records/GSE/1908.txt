TITLE
NIAID Mouse Lymphoma Gene Expression Profiling

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This series contains microarrays for ten types of mouse lymphomas, nude mouse purified resting B cells, and nude mouse purified B cells stimulated with LPS.  Microarray chips containing 70-mer oligonucleotides representing approximately 6,800 genes were prepared by the Microarray Center of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases, Bethesda, MD.
