TITLE
Expression analysis of prostate tissue on locus 8q24 part 3

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Investigation on expression levels of normal tissue from prostate cancer patients on locus 8q24.

DESIGN
3 chips with 3 arrays each study, using 3 pairs of normal vs. tumor tissue and 3 replicates of the same sample. Each chip contained one pair of normal vs. tumor and one copy of the repeated sample.
