TITLE
Transcription Factor-Mediated Control of Anthocyanin Biosynthesis in Vegetative Tissues of Tomato

ORGANISM
Solanum lycopersicum

SUMMARY
DEX inducible expression system for Rosea1 and Delila transcription factors was introduced in tomato. Seedlings and callus tissues were induced by DEX. RNA of induced and uniniduced roots and calli was isolated after 3 and 24h.

DESIGN
