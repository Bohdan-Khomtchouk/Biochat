TITLE
Mi2ß knockdown in ß-estradiol-treated (24 h) G1E-ER-GATA-1 cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Objective: To determine the extent to which GATA-1 utilizes Mi2ß to regulate gene transcription in the context of G1E-ER-GATA-1 cells.

DESIGN
3 Mi2ß knockdown samples were compared to 3 control samples.
