TITLE
RNAseq of wild type and maternal-zygotic Nanog mutant (MZnanog) zebrafish embryos

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Goal of this study is differential gene expression between wild type and MZnanog mutant during early zebrafish embryogenesis

DESIGN
Three timepoints - 2 hours post fertilization (hpf), 4 hpf, and 6.5 hpf; two replicates of wild type at each time point, one replicate for MZnanog at each time point

PLATFORM
GPL18413 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Danio rerio)
