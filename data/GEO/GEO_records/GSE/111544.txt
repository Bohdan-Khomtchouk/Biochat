TITLE
Whole human genome expression analysis on left ventricular free wall heart tissue samples (Dilated Cardiomyopathy)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human left ventricular free wall heart tissue was obtained from end-stage heart failure patients at the moment of heart transplantation

DESIGN
RNA Extraction was done on frozen tissues without stimulation. Whole genome expression analysis was conducted on 14 dilated cardimyopathy (DCM) samples.
