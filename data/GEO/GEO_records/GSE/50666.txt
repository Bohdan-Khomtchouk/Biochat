TITLE
The pattern of genomic instability during bladder cancer progression (part 1)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The Illumina SNP bead arrays were done for two metachronous tumors and the blood from a given patient. It was used to define copy number abnormalities in order to support subclonal population analysis in this patient.

DESIGN
Three samples were analysed concerning two metachronous tumor samples and a blood sample from the same patient.

PLATFORM
GPL8882 Illumina HumanOmni1-Quad BeadChip
