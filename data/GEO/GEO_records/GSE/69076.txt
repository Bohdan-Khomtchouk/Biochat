TITLE
Identification of LPS-responsive genes in bone marrow-derived dendritic cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of in vitro generated murine dendritic cells after stimulation with LPS for various timepoints.

DESIGN
4 condition experiment - LPS stimulation at 0, 1, 6, and 24 hours.  3 independent biological replicates for each condition.
