TITLE
HUVEC gene profile after Sox7/Sox17 siRNA silencing

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To find out the target of transcription factor Sox7 and Sox17, DNA microarray analysis was performed with RNA from Sox7 and Sox17 knockdown HUVECs.

DESIGN
Control / Sox7 knockdown / Sox17 knckdown / Sox7,Sox17 double knockdown. 2 independent experiments.
