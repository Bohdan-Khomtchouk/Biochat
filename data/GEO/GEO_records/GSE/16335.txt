TITLE
Escherichia coli K-12: luxS mutant LW12 vs. wild type W3110 under H2O2 stress

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Escherichia coli K-12 comparing luxS mutant LW12 with wild type W3110 exposure to 10mM or 30mM hydrogen peroxide.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, luxS mutant LW12 vs. wild type W3110, treatment with 10mM hydrogen peroxide for 30min or treatment with 30mM hydrogen peroxide for 30min. Two biological replicates.
