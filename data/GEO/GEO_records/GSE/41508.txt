TITLE
Expression data from YAP-S127D DLD1 Xenografts

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We overexpressed YAP-S127D in DLD1 colorectal cancer cells for 21 days after sub-cutaneous (SubQ) injection into the flanks of nude mice.

DESIGN
Mice were given dox for 21 days, sacrificed, and tumors were isolated for RNA extraction and analyzed by Affymetrix microarrays.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
