TITLE
PLZF

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Cultured THY1+ spermatogonia were used to reveal the genomic targets of PLZF and SALL4 in undifferentiated spermatogonia.

DESIGN
Three replicate preparations of DBA/2 mouse THY1+ spermatogonia were used for independent ChIP of PLZF and SALL4, NGS and MACS-based peak calling.
