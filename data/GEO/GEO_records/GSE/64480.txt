TITLE
Treatment of human monocytes with TLR7 or TLR8 agonists

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Goal was to detect differences in response to TLR7 versus TLR8 agonists in human monocytes from healthy donors

DESIGN
3 deidentified donors from the Red Cross, monocytes from each donor incubated overnight with either vehicle, TLR7 agonist or TLR8 agonist

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
