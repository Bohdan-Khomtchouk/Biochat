TITLE
Molecular and Structural Bases of Hypothalamic  Puberty

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

SUMMARY
To compare the global profile of hypothalamic gene expression in agonadal male Rhesus Monkeys before and after reactivation of the pulsatile GnRH release during the pubertal phase of development.

DESIGN
20 Samples looking at Cortex and Mediobasal hypothalamus in 3 stages of Rhesus Monkeys (early pubertal, late juvenile, and late pubertal).

PLATFORM
GPL3535 [Rhesus] Affymetrix Rhesus Macaque Genome Array
