TITLE
Shewanella piezotolerans WP3 wild-type strain heat shock for 60 min

ORGANISM
Shewanella piezotolerans WP3

SUMMARY
The WP3 strains was cultured at 20°C and heat shocked at 40°C for 60 min. The transcriptional profiles after and before heat shock were compared.

DESIGN
Three biological replicates sample and 6 technical replicates for each experiment
