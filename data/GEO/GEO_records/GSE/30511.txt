TITLE
Allele specific mRNA-seq expression profiling in Arabidopsis thaliana Col and Ler reciprocal F1 hybrid embryo and endosperm

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
High throughput Illumina sequencing of poly-A selected RNA from Arabidopsis Col and Ler reciprocal F1 hybrid embryo and endosperm tissue isolated at 6-7 days after pollination to identify imprinted genes.

DESIGN
Examination of parent-of-origin specific and total gene expression in seed tissues.

PLATFORM
GPL9302 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Arabidopsis thaliana)
