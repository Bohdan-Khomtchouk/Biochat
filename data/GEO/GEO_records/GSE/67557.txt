TITLE
Expression data from human HT29, HDFa cell lines after normal and tumor tissue derived DNA treatment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profile based on human HT29, HDFa cell lines after normal and tumor tissue derived DNA treatment

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from human HT29, HDFa cell lines after normal and tumor tissue derived DNA treatment were hybridized on Affymetrix HGU133 Plus 2.0 microarrays
