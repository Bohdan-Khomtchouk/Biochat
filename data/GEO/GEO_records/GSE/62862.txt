TITLE
Search for microRNAs under the control of Grainyhead like-2

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Compare microRNA expression profile of HPPL (bipotential liver progenitor cell line) overexpressing Grhl2 with that of the control

DESIGN
Grhl2 was introduced to HPPL by retrovirus vector and infected cells were selected by G418. Total RNA including microRNA was extracted from cells by using Qiagen miRNeasy kit.
