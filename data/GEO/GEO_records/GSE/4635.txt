TITLE
Proteomic and Genomic Profiling of Bronchial Epithelial Cells in Never and Current Smokers

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparison of gene and protein expression in the large airway epithelium of never and current smokers. Keywords: gene expression array-based (RNA / in situ oligonucleotide)

DESIGN
Large airway bronchial epithelial cells were obtained from never and current smokers by brushing the right mainstem bronchus during fiberoptic bronchoscopy.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
