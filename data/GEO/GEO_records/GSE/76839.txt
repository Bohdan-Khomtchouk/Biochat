TITLE
microRNA expression in Tim-3 knockdown RAW264.7 cell

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RAW264.7 cell was stably transfected with Tim-3 siRNA(SI)  or negative control(NC) and were analyzed for microRNA expression Differerently expressed microRNA in Tim-3 knockdown RAW264.7 cells were summarized and were used for further analysis

DESIGN
RAW264.7 cells stably transfected with Tim-3 siRNA(SI)  or negative control(NC) and were used to collect microRNA without any other treatment

PLATFORM
GPL16384 [miRNA-3] Affymetrix Multispecies miRNA-3 Array
