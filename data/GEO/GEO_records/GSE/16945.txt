TITLE
Comparative transcription profiling of peripheral blood mononuclear cells from vasculitis patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) from 13 vasculitis patients expressed varying gene profiles.

DESIGN
A total of 13 sets of two color arrays were performed and each individual array included a target sample and a control sample.

PLATFORM
GPL4133 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Feature Number version)
