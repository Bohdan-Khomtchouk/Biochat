TITLE
Transcriptome of DTAC-resistant Salmonella Enteritidis

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Enteritidis

SUMMARY
Screen for differences in gene expression between a parental Salmonella enterica serovar Enteritidis strain (ATCC4931) and an adapted strain with increased resistance to the widely used antimicrobial sanitizer dodecyltrimethylammonium chloride (DTAC)

DESIGN
Time course of comparative gene expression changes between log phase parental and adapted Enteritidis strains after 0, 10, 30 and 150 min of exposure to 50% of the respective MIC of DTAC.
