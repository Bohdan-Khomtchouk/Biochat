SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from independent isolates of epithelial cell of alveolus of lung primary of cell type (HPAE EpiC). They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RAMPAGE libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA > 200 nucleotides in size.
