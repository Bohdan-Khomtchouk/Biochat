TITLE
miRNA Signatures indicate Acute Liver Failure in hepatitis E infected pregnant females

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Whole genome gene expression profiling and miRNA profiling of Hepatitis E infected pregnant females

DESIGN
Illumina based genome wide expression profiling and miRNA profiling was done to identify key genes and miRNAs that could be used as biomarkers for prognosis
