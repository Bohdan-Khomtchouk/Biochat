TITLE
The integrative analysis of microRNA and mRNA expression in mouse uterus under delayed implantation and activation

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study was to elucidate the molecular mechanisms on delayed implantation in a global perspective through the combination of global microRNAs (miRNAs) and mRNA expression analysis in mouse uterus through Solexa sequencing.

DESIGN
Examination of m/miRNA expression in mouse uterus.

PLATFORM
