TITLE
caArray_liu-00258: Gene expression profiles of BRCA1-linked, BRCA2-linked, and sporadic ovarian cancers

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We used complementary DNA (cDNA) microarrays to compare gene expression patterns in ovarian cancers associated with BRCA1 or BRCA2 mutations with gene expression patterns in sporadic epithelial ovarian cancers and to identify patterns common to both hereditary and sporadic tumors.
