TITLE
miRNA expression in developmental spinal cord

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study compared the microRNA expression in mouse fetal spinal cord at day 13.5 and newborn mouse spinal cord.

DESIGN
Three biological replications of the two samples were pooled. For each pooled sample, two technical replications were performed.

PLATFORM
GPL18894 Custom human/mouse/rat miRNA array (miRBase 7.1)
