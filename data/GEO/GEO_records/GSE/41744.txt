TITLE
Ficolin-1 is upregulated in leukocytes and glomeruli from microscopic polyangiitis patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To search for new markers of active lesions that might help better understand the molecular basis of MPA and aid in its diagnosis, DNA microarray analysis was performed with peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs).

DESIGN
A total of 12 sets of two color arrays were performed and each individual array included a target sample and a control sample.

PLATFORM
GPL4133 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Feature Number version)
