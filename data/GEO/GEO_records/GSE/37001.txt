TITLE
METTL3 KD in HepG2 cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To gain insight into possible processes that require m6A for their function, METTL3 was knocked down (KD) in HepG2 cells by siRNA transfections

DESIGN
Differential expression analysis of METTL3 KD versus mock-transfected HepG2 cells, in 2 biological replicates
