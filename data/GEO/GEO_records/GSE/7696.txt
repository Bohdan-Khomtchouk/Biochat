TITLE
Glioblastoma from a homogenous cohort of patients treated within clinical trial

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of 80 glioblastoma specimen of patients treated within clinical trials and 4 samples of " normal " brain tissue (non-tumoral). The data was used to identify factors of resistance to a chemoradiation therapy  protocol of radiotherapy and concomitant and adjuvant temozolomide (alkylating agent). Keywords: Disease state comparison

DESIGN
80 glioblastoma specimen and 4 non-tumoral brain samples

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
