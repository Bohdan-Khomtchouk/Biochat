TITLE
MSCs rescue lethally irradiated mice by differentiation-independent mechanisms

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Overall survival after irradiation was compared between HSC after single transplantation of MSC. Transplanted MSC did not differentiate into HSC, but salvaged endogeneous hematopoiesis.

DESIGN
BM, HSC and MSC were isolated from bone marrow 7 months after transplantation with MSCs.
