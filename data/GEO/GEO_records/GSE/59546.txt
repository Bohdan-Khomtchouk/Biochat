TITLE
The Genetic Prehistory of the New World Arctic

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To carry out population genetics analyses of the Arctic gregion we carried out Illumina Bead-Array-based enotyping on 18 samples from Greenland.

DESIGN
19 samples were analysed with the Illumina platform Human660W-Quad v1.0 Genotyping BeadChip and are described herein.
