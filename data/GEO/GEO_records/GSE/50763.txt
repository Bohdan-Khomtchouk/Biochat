TITLE
Thrombin inhibition with dabigatran etexilate in C57BL/6 mice fed a high fat diet

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Purpose: Determine the impact of thrombin inhibition with dabigatran etexilate on high fat diet-induced gene expression in livers of mice fed a high fat diet

DESIGN
Examination of hepatic gene expression in 3 C57BL/6 mice fed a high fat diet and in 3 C57BL/6 mice fed a high fat diet containing 10 mg/g dabigatran etexilate

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
