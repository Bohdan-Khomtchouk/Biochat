TITLE
Array CGH analysis in gastric gastrointestinal stromal tumors (GISTs)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To explore mechanisms underlying wild-type GISTs, array CGH was performed on 32 gastric GISTs (4 risk groups stratified by tumor size and mitotic counts comprised 8 cases each).

DESIGN
27 KIT-mutant, 1 PDGFRA-mutant and 4 wild-type GISTs

PLATFORM
GPL9128 Agilent-014693 Human Genome CGH Microarray 244A (Probe name version)
