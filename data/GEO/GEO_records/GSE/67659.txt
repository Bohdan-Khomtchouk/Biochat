TITLE
Comparative transcriptomics of endometrial stromal fibroblasts

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens ; Bos taurus ; Oryctolagus cuniculus ; Rattus norvegicus ; Monodelphis domestica ; Neovison vison

SUMMARY
We sequenced mRNA of endometrial stromal fibroblasts from six mammalian species.

DESIGN
