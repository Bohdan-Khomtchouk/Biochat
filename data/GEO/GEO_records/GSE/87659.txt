TITLE
Expression analysis of Danio Rerio strain AB liver over 2 diurnal cycle

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Investigation of diurnal gene expression in liver over the course of 2 days at 4hr interval.

DESIGN
A single slide 12-array design for 3 pooled liver samples from 3-6 months old male Zebrafish starting from 7AM (LD24) (light ON) until 3AM (LD68) 44 hours later (light OFF). Light/dark cycle 14:10
