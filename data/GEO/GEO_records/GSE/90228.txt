TITLE
polyA mRNA RNA-seq from HepG2 (ENCSR000CPC)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from the nuclear fraction of independent growths of cell line HepG2. They are stranded PE76 Illumina GAIIx RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Poly-A+ RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
