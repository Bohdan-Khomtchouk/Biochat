TITLE
HNF4 small intestine knockout

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To define changes in gene expression resulting from loss of hnf4 in the small intestine. Keywords: genetic modification

DESIGN
hnf4 null intestines were generated by breeding hnf4loxp mice to vill-cre mice.  Animals appeared to be healthy.  Gene arrays were performed on two null and two control intestines.

PLATFORM
GPL339 [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
