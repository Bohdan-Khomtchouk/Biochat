TITLE
Age-associated genomic methylation in the TOPS Family Study of Epigenetics Cohort

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide age-associated methylation signatures of 485,000 CpG sites were profiled in 192 individuals, age 6-85, of Northern European ancestry were profiled using the Illumina HM450 array.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 192 samples were hybridized to the Illumina Infinium 450k Human Methylation Beadchip
