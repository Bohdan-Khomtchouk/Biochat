TITLE
Barley host response to the direct application of the trichothecene mycotoxin deoxynivalenol

ORGANISM
Hordeum vulgare

SUMMARY
Barley florets (cv. Morex) were treated with 2.0 microgram deoxynivalenol per floret via a 10 microliter solution or mock inoculated with water. Samples were collected at 1, 12, 24, and 48 hours after inoculation.   ****[PLEXdb( ) has submitted this series at GEO on behalf of the original contributor,  Stephanie A. Gardiner.
