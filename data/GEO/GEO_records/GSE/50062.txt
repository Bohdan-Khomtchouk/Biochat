TITLE
Which role for genomics in environmental risk assessment? A case study in Venice Lagoon.

ORGANISM
Ruditapes philippinarum

SUMMARY
DNA microarray analyses of Ruditapes philippinarum sampled in Venice lagoon areas subjected to different anthropogenic impact.

DESIGN
A comparative analysis of gene expression was conducted between Manila clam from lowly-polluted Chioggia and Colmata area and polluted Marghera site.
