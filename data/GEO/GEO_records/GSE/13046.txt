TITLE
Microarray analysis of Huh7 cells treated with IFNa2, OSM or IFNa2 combined with OSM

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
OSM increases the antiviral effect of IFNα in Huh7 cells infected with hepatitis A virus (HAV) or HCV replicon and synergizes with IFNα in the induction of antiviral genes

DESIGN
16 samples. Four replicates for each experimental condition. 72 hours of treatment in DMEM supplemented with 2% FBS.

PLATFORM
GPL571 [HG-U133A_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
