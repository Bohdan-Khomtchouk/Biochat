TITLE
RNA-seq (strand-specific library) of barley shoots and roots from control and heat conditions.

ORGANISM
Hordeum vulgare

SUMMARY
Samples were collected after 1 h of the heat treatment. Samples were collected for control and heat stressed plants.

DESIGN
RNA-seq was performed for shoot (in triplicate for each condition) and root (in duplicate for each condition)

PLATFORM
GPL21973 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Hordeum vulgare)
