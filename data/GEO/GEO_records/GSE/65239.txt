TITLE
Transcriptomics of circulating human eosinophils

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Global expression pattern in eosinophils in various conditions such as eosinophilic asthma, hypereosinophilic dermatological diseases, parasitosis and pulmonary aspergillosis.

DESIGN
Eosinophil were isolated from peripheral blood by FACS from 4 hypereosinophilic conditions: eosinophilic asthma (4 replicates), hypereosinophilic dermatological diseases (3 replicates), parasitosis (3 replicates) and pulmonary aspergillosis (4 replicates).
