TITLE
Clinically non-involved skin of patients with psoriasis, lichen planus, and healthy individuals

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To investigate differences in the kinetics of allergic contact dermatitis reactions in psoriasis patients, molecular changes in clinically non-involved skin of psoriasis patients was investigated

DESIGN
whole genome expression arrays of clinically non-involved skin of psoriasis (n=8), lichen planus (n=3), and healthy individuals (n=7) were performed
