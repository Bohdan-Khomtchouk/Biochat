TITLE
Control vs. AngII Infusion

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The genetic background of mice used in this study is mainly C57BL/6J with a small contribution from 129/Sv and DBA/2J strains. Male littermates were received continuous administration of AngII for 7 days at 0.9ug/hour. On day 7 of AngII infusion, the blood pressure of infused mice was (mmHg, mean ± SD) 156 ± 1, while the control littermates remained normotensive (106 ± 9). Keywords: other
