TITLE
Analysis of the Aloe vera transcriptome using the Affymetrix ATH1 GenChip

ORGANISM
Aloe vera

SUMMARY
The Aloe vera transcriptome was analysed by hybridising triplicate samples of root and leaf tissue to the Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 array.

DESIGN
In total, 7 samples were hybridised to the array. Samples consisted of 1 genomic DNA, and triplicate samples of leaf and root RNA.

PLATFORM
GPL198 [ATH1-121501] Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array
