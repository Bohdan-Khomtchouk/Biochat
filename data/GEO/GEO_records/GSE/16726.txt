TITLE
Effect of deletion of cra on E. coli biofilm formation

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli str. K-12 substr. MG1655

SUMMARY
Transcriptional analysis of biofilm wild-type and cra mutant E. coli cells was carried out to determine the effect of cra on biofilm formation.

DESIGN
Samples were prepared in duplicates, with one of the replicates being dye swapped.
