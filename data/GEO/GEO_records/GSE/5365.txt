TITLE
Micromolar fluoride alters ameloblast lineage cells in vitro

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Previously characterized ameloblast-lineage cells were used in this study to determine whether micromolar levels of fluoride could be directly affect these cells. Keywords: substance response

DESIGN
Six samples altogether were analyzed in this study. Every two of them were from a same biological souce and subject to with or without the treatment of 10uM NaF (the only difference between the two samples.) The experiment was repeated 3 times.
