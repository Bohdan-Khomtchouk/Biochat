TITLE
Gene expression profiling of Imatinib and PD166326 resistant cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A comparison between parental K562 cells (CML) and two clones derived from this cell line : ImaR and PDR which are resistant against Imatinib mesylate and PD166326 respectively , two inhibitors of BCR-ABL. Keywords: gene expression, comparison
