TITLE
Analysis of Gene Expression in Ginger Tissues at Different Developmental Stages

ORGANISM
Zingiber officinale

SUMMARY
Rhizome, root and leaf of Yellow ginger were compared in this study. Rhizomes from 2, 3, 4, 6, and 7 month old plants (after planting) were compared to each other and to root from 2 and 7 month plants and leaves from 2 and 7 month old plants.  Plants were grown under controlled conditions in the greenhouse.
