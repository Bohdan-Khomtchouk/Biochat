TITLE
HCT116 cells treated with the p97 inhibitior CB-5083

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to analyze differential gene expression 8 hr after treatment of CB-5083, a small molecule inhibitor of p97 (VCP), in HCT116 cells grown in culture.

DESIGN
DMSO treated cells were compared to cells treated with 1uM CB-5083 after 8 hr.
