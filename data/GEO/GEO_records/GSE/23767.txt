TITLE
Tracking the molecular patterns of a common response across tissues in murine sepsis to refine treatment strategies

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional effects in liver, lung, spleen and blood samples from mice challenged to Sham and Sepsis by Peritoneal Contamination and Infection (PCI) were monitored after 6 and 24 hours

DESIGN
4 Tissues x 2 Time Resolved Treatment Groups x 4 Replicates, 4 Tissues x Sham Control x 3 Replicates

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
