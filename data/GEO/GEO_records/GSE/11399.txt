TITLE
Transcriptional Response of Candida albicans Cells Following Phagocytosis by Mouse Macrophages

ORGANISM
Candida albicans

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Candida albicans that had been phagocytosed for 1, 2, or 4 hours by either bone marrow-derived mouse macrophaged (BMDM) or immortalized RAW264.7 cells Keywords: Time course

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, phagocytosed cells vs cells maintained in D-10 media. Time course: 1, 2, and 4 hours. Biological replicates: 4 independent experiments at each cell-time combinations.

PLATFORM
GPL6822 BRI-NRC C. albicans expression microarray
