SUMMARY
Analysis of mid-term response to hydroxyurea in a yeast wild-type strain (W303a). Cells were grown in YPD rich medium containing 200 mM hydroxyurea (HU) for 2 hours. Gene expression changes were analysed compared to the same strain grown in the same medium without HU. Keywords: repeat sample
