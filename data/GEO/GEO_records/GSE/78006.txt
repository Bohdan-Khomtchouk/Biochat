TITLE
Ettlia oleoabundans under varying nitrate, NaCl, light and cell density conditions

ORGANISM
Ettlia oleoabundans

SUMMARY
Ettlia oleoabundans is a triacylglyceride-producing chlorophyte.  We investigated gene expression and lipid changes after 6 days under varying concentrations of nitrate and NaCl.

DESIGN
E. oleoabundans was grown under phototrophic growth conditions, under varying conditions.

GPL21465 Illumina HiSeq 1000 (Ettlia oleoabundans)
