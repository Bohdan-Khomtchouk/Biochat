TITLE
Innate host defense requires TFEB-mediated transcription of cytoprotective and antimicrobial genes

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
We report the profiling of induced mRNA transcripts in two C. elegan replicate populations -- WT (N2) and mutant strain with deficient HLH30. Both strains were fed either OP50 strain of e-coli (normal feed) or S. aureus

DESIGN
Examination of infected versus uninfected wildtype and mutant lawns of animals

PLATFORM
GPL13657 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Caenorhabditis elegans)
