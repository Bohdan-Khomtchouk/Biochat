TITLE
Response in the NGF withdrawal model

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA was purified from DRG neurons grown for 4 days in culture. The " SAMPLE_ID " sample characteristic is a sample identifier internal to Genentech. The ID of this project in Genentech's ExpressionPlot database is PRJ0011148

DESIGN
RNA sequencing from primary DRG neurons grown for 4 days in vitro and deprived of NGF for 4.5 h in the presence of DMSO, DLKi 400 μM or ISRIB 400 μM. NGF with DMSO was used as control.
