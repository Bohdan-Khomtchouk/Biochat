TITLE
Gene expression profiling of Akt3 over-expressing breast cancer cells after estrogen and tamoxifen treatment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The aim of this study is to identify the differentially expressed genes in upon Akt3 over-expression after estrogen and tamoxifen treatment.

DESIGN
RNA were extracted from three independently treated respective breast cancer cell lines and processed for further microarray experiments.

GPL4133 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Feature Number version)
