TITLE
Transcriptional profiling of 293T cells treated with 2µM camptothecin compared to untreated cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Total RNA from untreated 293T cells (2 samples) and 293T cells treated with 2µM camptothecin for 48h (2 samples) was isolated using RNeasy Midi Kit (QIAGEN, Valencia) followed by gene expression profiling on Affymetrix human U133A arrays and analysis by MAS 5.1.
