TITLE
polyA mRNA RNA-seq from A549 (ENCSR000CON)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from the whole cell fraction of independent growths of cell line A549. They are stranded PE76 Illumina GAIIx RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Poly-A+ RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
