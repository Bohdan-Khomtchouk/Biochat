TITLE
Transcriptional response of Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 to Maltose

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pneumoniae D39

SUMMARY
Transcriptome comparison of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type grown in MM17 (0.5 % (w/v) Maltose + M17) to grown in GM17 (0.5 % (w/v) Glucose + M17).

DESIGN
Two conditions design comparison of one strain including a dye swap.
