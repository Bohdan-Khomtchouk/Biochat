TITLE
Genome-wide baseline microRNA expression quantification in HapMap phase I CEU and YRI samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Baseline microRNA (miRNA) expression was evaluated in 107 HapMap lymphoblastoid cell lines (LCLs; 53 CEU and 54 YRI) using Exiqon miRCURY LNA arrays v10.0 (Exiqon array).

DESIGN
Total RNA from each of the 107 HapMap sample was labeled and hybridized onto an Exiqon array

PLATFORM
GPL14999 Exiqon miRCURYTM LNA array v.10.0 (miRBase 11.0)
