TITLE
Cytogenetic aberrations in hepatocellular carcinoma [I]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Array CGH (44K) of liver tumors and unaffected liver controls from mice conditionally deficient for Mad2 and p53 in hepatocytes

DESIGN
aCGH of sample DNA was hybridized with sex mismatched control DNA from a different mouse. Dye swaps were performed for each sample
