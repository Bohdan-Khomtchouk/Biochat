TITLE
Toxoplasma gondii remodels the cis-regulatory landscape of infected human host cells [HELP-tagging]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To study the host DNA methylome of human fibroblasts after infection with T. gondii (Type I-RH).

DESIGN
Three biological replicates of uninfected HFFs and three biological replicates of T. gondii (Type I-RH) infected HFFs were sequenced using HELP-tagging.

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
