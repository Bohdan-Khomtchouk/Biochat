TITLE
Neisseria gonorrhoeae FA1090 transcriptional response to iron availability

ORGANISM
Neisseria gonorrhoeae

SUMMARY
4 pan-Neisseria arrays examining the gonococcal response to iron availability. Dual channel (Cy3/Cy5) arrays, using random nonamers for cDNA generation. Arrays scanned with ScanArray ExpressHT microarray scanner.  Data analyzed using ArrayVision 7.0 and compiled in GeneSpring. Keywords: other
