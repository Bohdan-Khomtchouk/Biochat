TITLE
Expression profiling of control vs. ndr1 overexpression zebrafish blastulas

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Identification of genes downstream of ndr1 in zebrafish blastulas, and identification of direct targets by reference with Smad2 and Eomesa ChIP-seq data

DESIGN
Triplicate control injected samples and triplicate samples of embryos injected with 50pg ndr1 mRNA per embryo.  Total RNA prepared from ~200 sphere stage embryos per sample.
