TITLE
SADE analysis of microdissected kidney

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens ; Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Libraries generated from microdissected kidney samples from the healthy pole of cancerous kidneys. Donors were from either sex. Libraries were  generated using Sau3A I as anchoring enzyme. Data are provided after removal of linker derived sequences.
