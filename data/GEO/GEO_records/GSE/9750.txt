TITLE
Identification of gene expression profiles in cervical cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study is aimed in identification of gene expression profiles in  cervical cancer and the role of specific genes in cervical carcinogenesis. Keywords: Gene expression in cervical cancer

DESIGN
A total of 66 samples were included in this study, which include 33 primary tumors, 9 cell lines, and 24 normal cervical epithelium.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
