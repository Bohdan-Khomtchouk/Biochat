TITLE
Transcriptomic expression analysis on hippocampus samples

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Hippocampus transcriptome analysis of Ms5Yah carrying a 7.7Mb deletion on chromosome 16 and wild-type littermates.

DESIGN
We analyzed hippocampi from 8 wild-type and 9 Ms5Yah male mice using the Affymetrix Mouse Exon 1.0 ST platform. Array data was processed by Affymetrix Exon Array Computational Tool.

PLATFORM
GPL6096 [MoEx-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Exon 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
