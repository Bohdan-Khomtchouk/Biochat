TITLE
Aedes aegypti sex specific gene expression

ORGANISM
Aedes aegypti

SUMMARY
This analysis compare gene expression between 4 day old sugar fed female and male Aedes aegypti mosquitoes. Keywords: Aedes aegypti sex specific expression

DESIGN
This analysis compare gene expression between 4 day old sugar fed female and male Aedes aegypti mosquitoes. Samples were made from whole 4 day old sugar fed female and male mosquitoes.
