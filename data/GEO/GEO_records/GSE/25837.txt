TITLE
Differential gene expression in circulating leukocytes from chronically lonely individuals

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling was carried out on peripheral blood mononuclear cell mRNA samples collected from 93 older adults participating in the Chicago Health Aging and Social Relations Study (CHASRS).  The primary research question is whether gene expression differs in participants who are chronically lonely. Keywords: Risk prediction
