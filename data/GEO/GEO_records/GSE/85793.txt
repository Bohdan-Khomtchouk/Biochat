TITLE
RNAseq liver Glucagon+T3

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
21 samples of liver RNA coming from mice treated with glucagon (Gluc), thyroid hormone (T3), both, or the conjugated compound (Cp). Each sample comes from a single mice.

DESIGN
Examination of mRNA levels in livers extracted from individual male mice maintained on a western diet following daily treatment with glucagon, T3, their combination, or a single molecule hybrid of glucagon and T3 for 16 days.

PLATFORM
GPL16790 AB 5500 Genetic Analyzer (Mus musculus)
