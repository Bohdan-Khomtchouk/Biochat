TITLE
modENCODE Drosophila RNA Binding Protein RNAi RNA-Seq Studies

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Deep Sequencing of mRNA from the Drosophila melanogaster S2-DRSC cells that have been RNAi depleted of mRNAs encoding RNA binding proteins. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODEDataReleasePolicyFinal2008.pdf

DESIGN
Analysis of Poly(A)+ RNA from S2-DRSC Cells depleted of mRNAs encoding RNA binding proteins
