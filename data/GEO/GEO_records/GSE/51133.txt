TITLE
Analysis of cord blood CD4T cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Purified CD4 T cells isolated from the cord blood were divided in subsets according to IL2R, IL7R and chemokine receptors (CCR6, CXCR3 and CCR4) and compared by whole genome microarray analysis.

DESIGN
CD4 T cell memory subsets and Naive T cell collected from 3 donors (#480, #487, #488)
