TITLE
Transcriptome analysis by RNA-seq of ovarian cancer derived from the Cancer Institute Hospital of Japanese Foundation for Cancer Research.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed the RNA-seq experiments for mRNA of ovarian cancer tissue. Raw data not provided. Institutional Review Board (IRB) does not allow submitters to disclose raw data to the public

DESIGN
Messenger RNA were extracted from primary tumor of 28 ovarian cancer patients, prepared with Ilumina TruSeq Stranded mRNA  Library Prep Kit and sequenced  on the Illumina HiSeq

PLATFORM
GPL22100 Illumina Hiseq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
