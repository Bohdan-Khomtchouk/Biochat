TITLE
AML genome hybridization to HG44A

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
CGH analysis was conducted for AML blasts with Agilent HG44A arrays. Keywords: disease state analysis

DESIGN
A total of 16 AML patients were analyzed. CD34-positive fractions were used as leukemic fractions, while CD34-negative fractions were used as control ones.

PLATFORM
GPL2873 Agilent-012750 Human Genome CGH Microarray 44A (Feature number version)
