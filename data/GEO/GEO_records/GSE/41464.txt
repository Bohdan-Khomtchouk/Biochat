TITLE
Sense exon profiling across human, mouse, and rat

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcription profiling of sense transcripts of 10 tissues each from human, mouse, and rat.

DESIGN
We profiled the sense transcription level of 10 tissues each from human, mouse, and rat. Only Affymetrix core probesets were used. Two technical replicates per sample.
