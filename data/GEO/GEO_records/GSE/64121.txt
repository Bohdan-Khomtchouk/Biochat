TITLE
Detection of copy number variation in patients with Pelizaeus-Merzbacher disease (PMD) - Affymetrix

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The objective of the study was to investigate the extent of duplicated, triplicated, and quadruplicated regions in PMD patients so that junction analysis could be performed.

DESIGN
4 samples were analyzed. Replicates are not included.
