TITLE
Rat Kidney Methylprednisolone

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Summary: To identify distinct temporal patterns of mRNA expression in the kidney of rats following a bolus dose of the corticosteroid methylprednisolone. Hypothesis: That prednisone pharmacodynamics can be derived from expression profiling data. Keywords: other
