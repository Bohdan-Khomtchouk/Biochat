TITLE
HEK293 Heat-shock experiment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
HEK293 cells were heatshocked and differentially expressed transcripts were identified

DESIGN
Transcriptomes of heatshocked HEK293 cells were compared to control cells. Heatshock and control samples were treated and sequenced in triplicate.
