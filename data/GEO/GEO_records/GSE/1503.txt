TITLE
Male/Female BALB/c Mouse Submandibular/Sublingual/Parotid/Lacrimal/Meibomian  Glands

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Series includes pooled (n = 5 mice per biological replicate) samples from submandibular, sublingual, parotid, lacrimal, and meibomian glands of BALB/c mice. Both male and female samples were analyzed on CodeLink Mouse Uniset I Microarrays. Keywords: repeat sample
