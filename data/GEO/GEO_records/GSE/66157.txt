TITLE
The Genetic Legacy of the Expansion of Turkic-Speaking Nomads Across Eurasia

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We genotyped 322 new samples from 38 Eurasian populations and combined it with previously published data to characterize the population structure of Turkic-speaking populations in the context of their geographic neighbors across Eurasia

DESIGN
322 samples were analysed with the Illumina Human610-Quad, Human660W-Quad, and HumanOmni1-Quad Genotyping BeadChips and are described herein. Please note that the '1M_Matrix_processed_2.txt' was provided on Jul 17, 2015 to replace the original file (with more missing values).
