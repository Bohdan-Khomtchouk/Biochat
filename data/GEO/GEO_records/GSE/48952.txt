TITLE
Microarray experment to identify genes differentially expressed in the DDT resistant 91R strain of Drosophila

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of mature adult 91R males compared to 91C males.

DESIGN
RNA was extracted from mature adult 91R or 91C strain of Drosophila melanogaster and subjected to micraoarray analysis.
