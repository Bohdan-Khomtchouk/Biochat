TITLE
Temperature adaptation effects on BAT metabolism

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We are reporting here the effects of adaptation to different ambient temperatures in the whole genome gene expression of interscapular BAT

DESIGN
B6/J mice were adapted to three different ambient temperatures (30ºC, 22ºC and 6ºC) for a period of 4 weeks.

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
