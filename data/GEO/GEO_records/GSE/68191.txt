TITLE
Differential gene expression between infected and non-infected dendritic cells with HIV-1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The whole genome microarray shows differences in ISGs and chemokine gene expression

DESIGN
Productive infection of dendritic cells was carried out with Vpx+ virus. Control of infection was carried out with defective RT HIV-1
