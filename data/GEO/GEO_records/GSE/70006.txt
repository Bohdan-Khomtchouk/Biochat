TITLE
rat PFC: Sham vs. SNI

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
DNA methylation profiles of prefrontal cortec (PFC) comparing 9 months post Spared Nerve Injury (SNI) rats with Sham rats.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Sham vs. SNI rat PFC. Biological replicates: 8 Sham replicates, 7 SNI replicates.
