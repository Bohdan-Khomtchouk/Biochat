TITLE
Maintenance of Leukemic Cell Identity by the Activity of the Polycomb Complex PRC1 (ChIP-seq_2)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
These data include the genome wide occupancy of H2AUbq and H3K27me3 by ChIP sequencing in  Ring1a-/-;Ring1bf/f Cdkn2a-/- MLL-AF9 leukemic cells treated with OHT or EtOH

DESIGN
Immuno-precipitation of formaldehyde cross-linked chromatin prepared from  Ring1a-/-;Ring1bf/f Cdkn2a-/- MLL-AF9 leukemic cells treated with OHT or EtOH using H2AK119Ubq and H3K27me3 antibody.

PLATFORM
