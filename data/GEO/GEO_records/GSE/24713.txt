TITLE
Expression data collected using a custom Affymetrix array (Adenoma Biomarker Gene Chip) designed to differentiate neoplastic colorectal from non-neoplastic

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This array was designed to verify a number of previously identified markers for colo-rectal adenoma. It contains probesets from the Affymetrix Hu133, HuGene and other novel probesets not included on other arrays.

DESIGN
68 samples of colon tissues were analysed, covering the classes normal, adenoma and carcinoma.

PLATFORM
GPL11060 [CG_AGPa520460F] Affymetrix Adenoma Biomarker 46K Gene Chip v1.0
