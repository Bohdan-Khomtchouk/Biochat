TITLE
S. cerevisiae L3262: Control vs. SPT15 multiple point mutant strains, Ets2 and Ets3

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of ethanol tolerant strains Ets2 and Ets3 comparing control Saccharomyces cerevisiae L3262 with ethanol tolerant strains Ets2 and Ets3, through screening a mutant library of SPT15 of Saccharomyces cerevisiae L3262.

DESIGN
Four-condition experiment, L3262 vs. Ets2 or Ets3 strains. Biological replicates: 4 control, each 2 transfected(Ets2, Ets3), independently grown and harvested. One replicate per array.
