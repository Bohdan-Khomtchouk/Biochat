TITLE
Montastraea franksi Control vs.  50 ppb Copper

ORGANISM
Orbicella franksi

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling of corals exposed to control (ambient seawater) or 50 ppb copper for 24 hours

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Control vs 50ppb. Biological replicates: 5 genotypes paired in control vs 50ppb, each genotype collected from different location on reef. One replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL16198 Montastraea faveolata 1.5K v1
