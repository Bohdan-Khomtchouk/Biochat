TITLE
Transcriptome in control, Fzd3 and Celsr3 mutant embryonic mouse cortex

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We compared the transcriptome in E16.5 embryonic mouse cortex isolated by microdissection, in control (+/+) mice and in mice with mutation in frizzled3 (Fzd3) and Celsr3.

DESIGN
Triplicate RNA samples prepared for each genotype

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
