TITLE
Estrogen-like activity and dual roles in cell signaling of an Agaricus blazei Murrill mycelia-dikaryon extract

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparing the expression profiles of the genes in response to 17b-estradiol (E2) and Agaricus blazei extract (ABE)

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, E2- or ABE-treated vs. control cells. 2 to 3 biological replicates, independently grown and harvested. Two to three replicates per array.
