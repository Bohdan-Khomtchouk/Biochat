TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of E. coli in M9 minimal media supplemented with propylene glycol or glycerol

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli str. K-12 substr. MG1655

SUMMARY
The aim of this study is to investigate the changes of global gene expression in E. coli during a carbon source shift. Expression profiling of an evolved strain of E. coli K12 MG1655 grown in M9 minimal media supplemented with propylene glycol or glycerol.

DESIGN
A six chip study under two separate culture conditions

PLATFORM
GPL3154 [E_coli_2] Affymetrix E. coli Genome 2.0 Array
