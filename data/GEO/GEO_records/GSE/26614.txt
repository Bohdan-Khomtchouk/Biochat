TITLE
Pig Longissimus muscle: comparison between Basque and Large White breeds

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of pig skeletal muscle comparing two divergent breeds: Large white (LW, conventional) and Basque (B, local, indigeneous).

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, LW vs. B muscles. Biological replicates: 20 B and 20 LW. One replicate per array.
