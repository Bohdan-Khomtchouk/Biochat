TITLE
Early response of Coxiella burnetii to temperature stress

ORGANISM
Coxiella burnetii

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Coxiella burnetii phase I (RSA 493) submitting either to Cold and Heat shock comparing to control untreated Coxiella burnetii phase I (RSA 493) grown at 35°C.

DESIGN
Four experiments : Cold shock 30 min Vs 35°C; Cold shock 60 min Vs 35°C; Heat shock 30 min Vs 35°C; Heat shock 60 min Vs 35°C 3 biological replicates, independently grown and harvested. Four replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL6675 IPMC - Coxiella burnetti 2k
