TITLE
Identification of genes that are linked with optineurin expression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study aimed to identify genes that are linked with optineurin expression using a combined siRNA-microarray approach Keywords: siRNA treatment study

DESIGN
Samples a01, a02 and a03 were treated with siRNA specific for optineurin mRNA Samples b01, b02 and b03 were treated with water (mock experiment)
