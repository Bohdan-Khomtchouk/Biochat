TITLE
Expression changes in L4 DRG initiated by sciatic nerve injury

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression of L4 DRG after sciatic nerve cut compared to contralateral uninjured DRGs. The " SAMPLE_ID " sample characteristic is a sample identifier internal to Genentech. The ID of this project in Genentech's ExpressionPlot database is PRJ0003322 Keywords: Expression profiling by array

DESIGN
Microarray analysis of L4 DRGs 3 days after sciatic nerve transection in adult mice using uninjured DRGs as control.
