TITLE
Gene expression analysis of normal gingiva

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
There were no studies about gene expression of normal gingiva before. We performed this study to compare with gene expression of gingival fibromatosis

DESIGN
Normal gingival tissue was collected from nine healthy subjects. Gene expression profiles were conducted using cDNA microarray analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
