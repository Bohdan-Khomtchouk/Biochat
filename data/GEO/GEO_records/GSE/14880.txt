TITLE
Differentation in neuroblastomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression profiling according to differentiation was analysed in 34 neuroblastomas

DESIGN
Unsupervised clusterings were performed to confirm the link between phenotype of differentiation and expression profile Different groups were compared with SAM-t tests.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
