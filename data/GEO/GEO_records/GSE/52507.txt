TITLE
Oncorhynchus mykiss liver and muscle cells: Small vs. Large neomale offspring

ORGANISM
Oncorhynchus mykiss

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of rainbow trout liver and muscle cells comparing small fish with large fish within a population of neomale offspring.

DESIGN
Small vs. large-fish liver and muscle cells from neomale offspring. Biological replicates: 4 small replicates, 4 large replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL7303 Agilent-020938 S. salar (Salmon) Oligo Microarray (Probe Name version)
