SUMMARY
Proto-oncogene c-Fos is a leucine-zipper containing transcription factor which has a DNA binding domain and a transactivation domain. Fos dimerizes with another transcription factor Jun to form AP1 transcription factor. The AP-1 complex has been implicated in transformation and progression of cancer.
