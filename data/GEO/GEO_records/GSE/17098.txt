TITLE
Tricellular pollen and pollen mother cells (11447)

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
We isolated tricellular pollen (TCP) and pollen mother cells (PMC) of rice using laser microdissection, and did microarray analysis with Agilent 44k rice array.

DESIGN
2 biological rep of TCP and 2 biological rep of PMC were label by Cy3, comparing to 4 rep of seedling sample labled by Cy5

PLATFORM
GPL6864 Agilent-015241 Rice Gene Expression 4x44K Microarray (Feature Number version)
