TITLE
Transcriptional Profile Analysis of the Lateral Geniculate Nucleus in Monocular Deprived Macaque Monkeys

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

SUMMARY
Laser capture microdissection was used to obtain individual LGN layers for DNA microarray in macaque monkeys that underwent monocular eyelid suture at birth. Keywords: parallel sample
