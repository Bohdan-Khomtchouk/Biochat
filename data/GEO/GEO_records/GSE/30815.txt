TITLE
CDK8 knockdown in HT-29 human colon cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of HT-29 human colon cancer cells transfected with non-targeting control (NTC) siRNA and two different siRNA sequences against CDK8 (siCDK8-1 and siCDK8-2).

DESIGN
Three condition experiment comparing siNTC versus siCDK8-1 and siCDK8-2 after 3 days (3 biological replicates each); in total 9 samples profiled.
