TITLE
HuMiChip analysis of 86 samples from different body sites

ORGANISM
human metagenome

SUMMARY
HuMiChip was used to analyze human oral and gut microbiomes, showing significantly different functional gene profiles between oral and gut microbiome.

DESIGN
The results were used to demonstarte the usefulness of applying HuMiChip to human microbiome studies.
