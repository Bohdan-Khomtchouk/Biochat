TITLE
PAR-CLIP for HA-tagged EZH2 in mouse embryonic stem cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This experiment was designed to indentify RNAs making direct contact with  EZH2 in mouse embryonic stem cells

DESIGN
E14 with an integrated transgene encoding HA-EZH2 were pulsed with 4-SU, irradiated with UV, and subjected to HA immunoprecipitation.

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
