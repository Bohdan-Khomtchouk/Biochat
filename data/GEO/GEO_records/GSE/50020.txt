TITLE
Sequences tags  expressed  in ovules of Arabidopsis thaliana using Massively Parallel Signature Sequencing (MPSS)

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
We report the use of MPSS data to compare transcripts expressed in wild-type and sporocyteless mutant ovules

DESIGN
All MPSS libraries were performed as described by Meyers et al (Nature Biotechnol. 2004 Aug;22(8):1006-11. Epub 2004 Jul 11.)

GPL17594 Arabidopsis thaliana MPSS
