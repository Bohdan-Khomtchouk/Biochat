TITLE
Fusion discovery in breast cancer cell line

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
SnowShoes-FTD, a fusion transcript discovery tool, was used to identify fusions in breast cancer cell lines using the RNA-Seq data

DESIGN
Total RNA extracted from cell lines. The total RNA was used for construction of RNA-Seq library for RNA-Sequencing.

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
