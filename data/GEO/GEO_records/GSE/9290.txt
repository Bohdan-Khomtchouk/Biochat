TITLE
Irradiated mouse: Irradiated mouse vs. non-irradiated mouse

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Adopting boron neutron capture, we separately irradiated Kupffer cells and endothelial cells in mouse liver in vivo and the changes in gene transcriptions were analyzed by an oligonucleotide microarray. Keywords: Gene expression

DESIGN
control(non-irradiated mouse liver) vs. Endothelial(alpha-particle), Kupffer(alpha-particle), neutron or Gamma irradiated mouse liver
