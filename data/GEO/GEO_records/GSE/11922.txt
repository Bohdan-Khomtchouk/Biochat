TITLE
High temporal resolution profiling of NIH3T3 cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
High-temporal resolution profiling was performed on NIH3T3 fibroblasts to detect rhythmic transcripts Keywords: time course

DESIGN
Samples were collected every hour for 48 hours from forskolin-synchronized NIH3T3 cells.  Samples were analyzed using Affymetrix arrays.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
