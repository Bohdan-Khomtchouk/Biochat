TITLE
Transcriptomic analysis in murine mammary carcinoma tissue after treatment with control vehicle or abemaciclib

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We measured changes in expression of 3,826 genes in MMTV-rtTA/tetO-HER2 mammary carcinoma tissue after treatment with control vehicle or abemaciclib

DESIGN
Examination of changes in gene expression in 23 tumors (11 control, 12 abemaciclib)
