TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of gene expression in the cervical spinal cord of a mouse model of atopic dermatitis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Genome-wide analysis of gene expression in the cervical spinal cord of atopic dermatitis model mice was performed. Results provide important information for understanding of molecular mechanisms of the symptoms of atopic dermatitis.

DESIGN
Total RNA was odtained from the cervical spinal cord of normal or atopic dermatitis model mice.

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
