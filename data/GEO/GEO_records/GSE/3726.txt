TITLE
Prognostic gene signatures can be measured with samples stored in RNAlater

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A number of breast or colon specific genes predictive of the relapse status were used in comparing the outcome from matched fresh frozen and stored in RNAlater preservative. Keywords: disease state analysis

DESIGN
31 pairs of matched breast and 21 pairs of matched colon primary tumors either fresh frozen or stored in RNAlater preservative

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
