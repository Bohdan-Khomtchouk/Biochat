TITLE
Gene expression profiles of human memory B cells stimulated with BCR/CD40L/IFN-γ/IL21

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of the effect of BCR/CD40/IFN-γ/IL21 on human B cells at gene expression level.

DESIGN
Total RNA samples were obtained from human memory B cells subjected to 24 hours of in vitro culture with or without BCR, CD40, IFN-γ, IL21.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
