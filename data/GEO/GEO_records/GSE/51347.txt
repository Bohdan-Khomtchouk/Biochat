TITLE
Effect of multiple longitudinal biopsy sampling in individual mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Small scale study to asses the effects of taking multiple skinbiopts from individual mice in time. Results showed no influence on expression levels in time from older samplings on newer samplings.

DESIGN
skin biopts from 4 individual mice in taken at 6 points in time

PLATFORM
GPL10192 NimbleGen Mus musculus MM9 Expression Array (12x135k)
