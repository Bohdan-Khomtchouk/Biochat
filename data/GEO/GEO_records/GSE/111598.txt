TITLE
Single cell-RNA-seq for airway epithelial cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Outputs from single-cell RNA-seq sequenced reads from isolated mouse epithelial cells from homeostatic submucosal glands and Naphthalene-injured surface epithelium of adult mouse airway (P70)

DESIGN
Isolated single cells were processed and sequenced using 10X genomics Chromium single-cell RNA-seq protocol and Illumina NextSeq 500

PLATFORM
GPL19057 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Mus musculus)
