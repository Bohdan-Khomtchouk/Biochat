TITLE
Frontal cortex transcriptomic analysis of a TDP-43 Q331K knock-in mouse [20month]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Here we analyse transcriptome profiles within the frontal cortex between wild type, heterozygous and homozygous TDP-43 Q331K knockin mice at 20 months of age (C57BL/6)

DESIGN
Transcriptome (RNA-Seq) from 20 month old frontal cortex tissue

PLATFORM
GPL15103 Illumina HiSeq 1000 (Mus musculus)
