TITLE
The effects of YB1 knockdown on global gene expression in tumour cell lines.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Y-box binding factor 1 (YB1) has been associated with prognosis in many tumor types. Reduction of YB-1 inhibits tumor cell growth in vitro and in vivo

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated from A549, MCF7 and HCT116 tumor cell lines following 48hr treatment with 5nM Yb1 siRNA

PLATFORM
GPL6104 Illumina humanRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
