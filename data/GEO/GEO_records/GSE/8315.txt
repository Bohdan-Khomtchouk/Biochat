TITLE
Immediate-Early and Delayed Primary Response Genes are Distinct in Function and Genomic Architecture

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling time course of serum deprived quiescent human T98G cells follwing platelet derived growth factor (PDGF) stimulation in the absence and presence of the translation inhibitor, cycloheximide.  RNA was harvested at 0.5, 2, and 4 hr following PDGF treatment. Keywords: time course

DESIGN
Two-color microarrays with dye-swap; 3 biological replicates per condition, independently grown and harvested. One replicate per array.
