TITLE
Aberrant expression of microRNAs is associated to the progression of liver fibrosis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Establish the miRNA expression profile between advanced liver fibrosis and liver without fibrosis in mice

DESIGN
Administrate CCL4 or olive oil in mouse twice a week first 4 weeks per peritoneum and then administrated once a week next four weeks. Mice were sacrificed on 4, 6, and 8 weeks.

GPL8824 Agilent-019119 Mouse miRNA Microarray 1.0 G4472A (miRNA ID version)
