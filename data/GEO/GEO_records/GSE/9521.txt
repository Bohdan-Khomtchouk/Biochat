TITLE
Thy1+ 2dpp gonocytes RA regulation

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
to investigate the RA regulated genes in 2 dpp thy1+ gonocytes Keywords: RA effects on gonocytes

DESIGN
gonocytes were isolated from 2 dpp mouse testes by THY1+ MACS sorting and cultured without serum, growth factors and feeder cells for 8hr, with either vehicle ethanol or RA (100 nM). Two control and two RA treated samples were included.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
