TITLE
Biobank of Karolinska Endarterectomy (BiKE)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A biobank collection of carotid plaque samples taken from patients undergoing endarterectomy operations.

DESIGN
Samples from carotid plaques and peripheral blood mononuclear cells in a setup with clinical registry data collection. See PMID 22371308 for further details.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
