TITLE
Expression profile of Ewing sarcoma and osteosarcoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The expression profile of primary pediatric Ewing sarcoma (ES) and osteosarcoma (OS) were analyzed.

DESIGN
8 Ewing sarcoma patient samples (TUM-ES0XXX) and 10 osteosarcoma patient samples (TUM-OS0XXX) were analyzed.
