TITLE
Time-course in rice plants of the day length shift experiment

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
Time-course in rice plants of the day length shift experiment in a growth chamber.

DESIGN
Rice plants (cv. Norin 8) were collected at every 2h from dusk to noon on the day 0, 1, and 5 from the day length shift (a long-day conditon to a short-day condition).
