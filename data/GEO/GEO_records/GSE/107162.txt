TITLE
Application of the transcriptomic biomarker, TGx-DDI, in genotoxicity screening

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Cell-based application of a transcriptomic biomarker, TGx-DDI, in addressing potentially irrelevant positive findings in chromosome damage assays

DESIGN
This experiment examined the whole genome transcriptional response of TK6 cells exposed to 45 compounds of the responsive concentration for 4 hours.
