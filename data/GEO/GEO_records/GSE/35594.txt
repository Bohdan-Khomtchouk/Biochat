TITLE
Microarray analysis of wildtype and Klf1-/- E13.5 fetal liver

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The aim of this experiment was to investigate the role of KLF1 in the fetal liver Affymetrix microarrays were performed on fetal liver cells from E13.5 wildtype and Klf1-/- mice.

DESIGN
Three wildtype replicates and three Klf1-/- replicates, all from E13.5 fetal liver.
