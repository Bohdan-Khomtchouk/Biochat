TITLE
High-temporal resolution profiling of mouse liver

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
High-temporal resolution profiling was performed on mouse liver to detect rhythmic transcripts Keywords: time course

DESIGN
Samples were collected every hour for 48 hours from 3-5 mice per time point from liver.  Samples were pooled and analyzed using Affymetrix arrays.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
