TITLE
Novel Regions of Variable DNA Methylation in Human Placenta associated with Newborn Neurobehavioral Traits

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide profiling of placental DNA methylation in relation to neurobehavioral development. The Illumina 450k methylation array was used to profile 335 samples. These samples have been collected from the Rhode Island Child Health Study (RICHS).

DESIGN
Illumina analysis performed on 335 human placentas

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
