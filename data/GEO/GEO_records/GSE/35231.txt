TITLE
Mycobacterium tuberculosis CDC1551 WT/sigI mutant

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis CDC1551

SUMMARY
Transcription profile of WT compare to sigma factor sigI deletion mutant at OD1 and OD2.

DESIGN
This microarray is simple comparison of wild type strain of M. tuberculosis CDC1551 and its sigma factor gene deletion mutant (sigI) at OD1 and OD2, respectively.

PLATFORM
GPL15148 JCVI PFGRC Mycobacterium tuberculosis 22K v4 array designed primarily based on strain H37Rv [Probe-level version]
