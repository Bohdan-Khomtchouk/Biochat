TITLE
Viral Profiles in Sjogren's Syndrome

ORGANISM
Viruses

SUMMARY
A study was designed to further define the viral landscape within Sjogren's Syndrome (pSS) affected salivary gland tissue to identify potential viral-mediated triggers in the pathogenesis of this autoimmune disease.
