TITLE
RNA profiling in Drosophila virilis and Drosophila yakuba

ORGANISM
Drosophila virilis ; Drosophila yakuba

SUMMARY
Gene annoation and determination of gene expression levels in Drosophila virilis and Drosophila yakuba by deep sequencing.

DESIGN
Total RNA-seq data from heads of 2-5 day old mated D virilis and D yakuba females, 1 sample from each species.

PLATFORM
GPL13307 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Drosophila yakuba) GPL13313 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Drosophila virilis)
