TITLE
Transriptome analysis of Hi-Myc prostate tumors after castration and antiandrogen treatment

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transriptome analysis of prostate tumors from Hi-Myc mouse prostates 3 days after castration or treatment with bicalutamide or enzalutamide

DESIGN
12 month old Hi-Myc mice were surgically castrated or treated with vehicle or bicalutamide or enzalutamide for 3 days. Mice tissue was analyzed
