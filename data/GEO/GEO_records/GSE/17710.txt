TITLE
Human lung squamous cell carcinoma expression profiling

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Lung squamous cell carcinoma gene expression (LSCC) is highly variable.  This study discovered and validated LSCC gene expression subtypes.

DESIGN
RNA from tumors and a common reference were hybridized to Agilent two color microarrays
