TITLE
RNAseq in IMR-32 neuroblastoma cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
IMR-32 cells were subjected to lentiviral YRNA infection or nELAVL RNAi and/or UV stress followed by RNAseq analysis to monitor RNA level changes

DESIGN
RNA from IMR-32 cells was Trizol extracted, Ribominus selected and submitted for high-throughput sequencing.

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
