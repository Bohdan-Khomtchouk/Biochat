TITLE
Murine colon: IL-10-/- versus BALB/c

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
MicroRNA profiling of mouse colonic tissue comparing severe colon pathology with mild and no pathology.

DESIGN
Three condition experiment: BALB/c (No Pathology Control), IL-10-/- with Pathology = 1, IL-10-/- with Pathology > 3.  Biological replicates were pooled RNA samples of 3 separate mice per condition.

PLATFORM
GPL7723 miRCURY LNA microRNA Array, v.11.0 - hsa, mmu & rno
