TITLE
Smad1/5/8 mutant granulosa cell tumor gene expression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The objective of this study was to understand the gene expression changes during granulosa cell tumor development in Smad1/5/8 mutant ovaries. Keywords: Microarray analysis, gene expression

DESIGN
Three independent mouse mutant granulosa cell tumors were compared to three wild type granulosa cells samples.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
