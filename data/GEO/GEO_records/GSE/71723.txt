TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of alternative splicing during development and drought in Zea mays

ORGANISM
Zea mays

SUMMARY
Analysis of the maize alternative splicing landscape, including transcript discovery and quantification during development and drought

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated from well-watered and drought stressed leaves, ears and tassels of maize plants and used to construct RNA seq libraries
