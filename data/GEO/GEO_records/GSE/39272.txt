TITLE
Dexamethasone regulation of mRNAs in astrocytes in vitro

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of mRNA gene expression changes mediated by synthetic GR-agonist dexamethasone in mouse cortical primary astrocyte cultures

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from astrocyte cultures subjected to 0.25, 1, 2, 4, 6, 12, 18, or 24 hours of ~100nM dexamethasone exposure compared to vehicle control astrocyte cultures

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
