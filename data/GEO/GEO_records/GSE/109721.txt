TITLE
ChIP-seq for H3K4me3, H3K27me3, H3K79me2 in MCF10A cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed ChIP-Seq against histones H3K4me3, H3K27me3, and H3K79me2 in MCF10A cells to identify transcription factors with bivalent and pseudo-bivalent epigenetic modifications.

DESIGN
Performing ChIP-Seq on MCF10A cells
