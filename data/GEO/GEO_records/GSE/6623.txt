TITLE
FoxO are critical mediators of hematopoietic stem cell resistance to physiologic oxidative stress

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To investigate the role of FoxO transcription factors as mediators of hematopoietic stem cell resistance to oxidative stress. Keywords: Hematopoietic stem cells, myeloid progenitors, oxidative stress, ROS, Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0

DESIGN
Study the effect of the deficiency of FoxO1, FoxO3, and FoxO4 in murine bone marrow hematopoietic stem cells and myeloid progenitors on expression of genes involved in reactive oxygen species (ROS) metabolism.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
