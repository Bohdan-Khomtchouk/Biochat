TITLE
Drosophila Embryos: Engrailed GooseberryNeuro double heterozygous vs Control Wild Type

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of drosophila embryos (0-14h collection) comparing embryos double heterozygous for Engrailed and GooseberryNeuro with control wild type embryos

DESIGN
Two conditions experiment, Double heterezygous Engrailed GooseberryNeuro vs Wild Type. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 mutant conditions, independently selected. Triplicate per array
