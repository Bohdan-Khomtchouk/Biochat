TITLE
Array-CGH screening of pediatric low-grade astrocytomas (n=66)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA copy-number profiling of 66 astrocytomas of WHO °I or °II Keywords: Genetic modification

DESIGN
Comparison of tumor DNA (tumor) versus control DNA (pool of lymphocyte DNA from 10 healthy donors). Whole-genome screening for DNA copy-number aberrations using array-based comparative genomic hybridization (aCGH). Tumor DNA labeled in Cy3 and control DNA labeled in Cy5.

PLATFORM
GPL5713 DKFZ Homo sapiens 8k BAC array
