TITLE
Primary effusion lymphoma cell lines BC-1 and BC-3

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of primary effusion lymphoma cell lines BC-1 and BC-3

DESIGN
Total RNA from BC-1 and BC-3 cell lines was processed for analysis in one replicate on Human Gene 1.0 ST arrays to obtain data on whether genes are expressed and to compare to existing microarray data.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
