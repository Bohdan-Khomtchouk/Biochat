TITLE
Salmonella Typhimurium: control vs. cortisol treatment

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of log and stationary phases of S. Typhimurium, comparing untreated controls with cortisol-treated samples.

DESIGN
Each array used labelled cDNA against a common genomic DNA reference. Triplicate arrays were carried out for each of the 4 conditions: untreated log phase, untreated stationary phase, cortisol treated log phase and cortisol treated stationary phase.
