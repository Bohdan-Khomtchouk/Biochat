TITLE
HLrec_2d_vs_0h

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Series of 6 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (HLrec_2d) and control (0h) each. Comparison of plants recovered for 48 h in normal light at 80 µE after 1 h of exposure to light stress at 1000 µE versus plants grown under normal light conditions of 80 µE. E. Richly et al., EMBO Rep. 4 (2003), pp. 491–498 Keywords: repeat sample
