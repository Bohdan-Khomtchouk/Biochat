TITLE
Extraocular, masticatory, and hindlimb muscles, comparison of putative muscle allotypes (Porter lab)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Extraocular Muscle is Defined by a Fundamentally Distinct Gene Expression Profile.  Adult mouse extraocular, masticatory, and hindlimb (gastrocnemius/soleus) muscles of adult mice were compared using Affymetrix microarrays.  Data form part of publication: Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences USA 98:12062-12067, 2001. Keywords: parallel sample

PLATFORM
GPL32 [MG_U74A] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Array
