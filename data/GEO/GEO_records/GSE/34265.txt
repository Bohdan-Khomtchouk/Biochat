TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of rice infected with Rice ragged stunt virus

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa Japonica Group

SUMMARY
Rice ragged stunt virus (RRSV) is the causal agent of rice ragged stunt disease which causes severe loss of rice yield in Asia countries. In this study, we have analyzed the relationship between symptom and host gene responses by RRSV infection.

DESIGN
Comparison between RRSV and mock infected rice. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 infected, independently grown and harvested. 1 samples derived from 5 plants grown under same conditons

PLATFORM
GPL7252 Custom GER rice oligoarray
