TITLE
Cystatin C modulates the release of inflammatory cytokines in Lipopolysaccharide-activated monocytes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Cystation modulates expression of inflammatory cytkines in LPS activated monocytes

DESIGN
Gene expression in monocytes treated LPS+Cystatin C is compared to monocytes treated with LPS only. The dataset also includes a number of control treatments.
