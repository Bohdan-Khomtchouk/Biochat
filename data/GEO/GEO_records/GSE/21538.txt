TITLE
Initial analysis of copy number variations in cattle selected for resistance or susceptibility to intestinal nematodes

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Here we describe the initial analysis of copy number variations in cattle selected for resistance or susceptibility to intestinal nematodes

DESIGN
The custom aCGH chips that interrogated the whole genome CNVs were build for 3 pairs of contemporary parasite-susceptible and resistant Angus.
