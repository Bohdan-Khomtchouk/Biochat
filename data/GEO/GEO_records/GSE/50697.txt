TITLE
Gene expression of SUM159 breast cancer cell line expressing microRNA--203

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Determine the effect of miR-203 expression on the global mRNA expression in mesenchymal breast cancer cell line.

DESIGN
Generate control (pBabe puro) and miR-203 (pBabe puro miR-203) cells using retroviral transduction and puromycin selections.  Extract total RNA using Trizol.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
