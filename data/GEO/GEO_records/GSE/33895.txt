TITLE
Enterohemorrhagic Escherichia coli (EHEC)8624 double deletions of qseC and qseE grown in DMEM

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli O157:H7

SUMMARY
Analysis of effect of deletion of the two adrenergic kinases QseC and QseE on gene expression of EHEC 8624 in the absence and presence of epinephrine

DESIGN
A double mutant of qseC and qseE in the EHEC 8624 was grown in the absence or presence of epinephrine to OD600=1.0 in DMEM then processed according to manufacturer's specifications: http//www.affymetrix.com/support/technical/manual/expression_manual.affx

PLATFORM
GPL3154 [E_coli_2] Affymetrix E. coli Genome 2.0 Array
