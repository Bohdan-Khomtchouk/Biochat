TITLE
Expression data from bone marrow-derived cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We used microarrays to identify genes in the migrated bone marrow-derived cells by G-CSF

DESIGN
Bone marrow-derived cells migrating to liver tissue were collected from mice exposed to carbon tetrachloride and G-CSF
