TITLE
Differential gene expression between left and right chicken HH14 sinus horns

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
Microarrays experiments were performed in order to assess which genes are differentially expressed between the embryonic chicken sinus horns at HH14.

DESIGN
Three, technical, dye swapped comparisons between Left and Right sinus horns. In total six arrays were used in this study
