TITLE
Loss of BMPR2 Signaling in Smooth Muscle in Mouse Lung

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Mice expressing a doxycycline-inducible dominant negative BMPR2 transgene expressed only in smooth muscle are activated for one or eight weeks, and compared to transactivator-only mice also fed doxycycline. All mice are 12 weeks of old at sacrifice. Keywords: time course with SM22-rtTA x tetO7-BMPR2 transgene
