TITLE
Characterization of EZH2-deficient human embryonic stem cells [ChIP-seq and bulk RNA-seq]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Here we analyze histone modifications and transcriptome profiles of EZH2-deficient human embroynic stem cells

DESIGN
Histone modifications (H3K27me3, H3K4me3, H3K27ac and H3K4me1) and transcriptome (mRNA-Seq) from EZH2-/- (Null), Ezh2-/- +EZH2 (Null + EZH2), Ezh2 -/+ (Het) and EZH2 +/+ (WT) human ESC
