TITLE
Phages DT1 and 2972 gene expression study

ORGANISM
Streptococcus virus DT1 ; Streptococcus virus 2972

SUMMARY
This series represents the gene expression study of phages DT1 and 2972 during the whole process of infection. Gene expression was measured at nine time intervals (0, 2, 7, 12, 17, 22, 27, 32, 37 minutes) during phage infection. Keywords: time-course
