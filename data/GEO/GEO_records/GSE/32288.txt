TITLE
Gene expression profile in Yaf2 KD and/or RYBP KO ES cells.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Gene expression change by Yaf2 KD in wild type or RYBP KO ES cells.

DESIGN
Total RNAs were extracted from the respective ES cells, and were subjected to microarray analysis using Affymetrix GeneChip Mouse Genome 430A 2.0 arrays
