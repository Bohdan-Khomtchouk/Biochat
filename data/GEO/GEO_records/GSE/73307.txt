TITLE
A transcriptomic study of maternal thyroid adaptation to pregnancy in rats

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
To identify potential regulators of late pregnancy-dependent thyroid weight increase, thyroid gene expression profiles of non-pregnant (NP) and late pregnant (LP, day 18) rats was analyzed using the RNA-seq approach.

DESIGN
Identifying global gene changes in the maternal thyroid in rats

PLATFORM
GPL18694 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Rattus norvegicus)
