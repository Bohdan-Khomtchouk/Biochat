TITLE
eQTL Mapping of the Drosophila Female Head Transcriptome

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
A dataset of ~600 RIL crosses using RILs from the Drosophila Synthetic Population Resource to be used for genomewide eQTL mapping. All samples are from female heads.

DESIGN
54 arrays with 12 samples per array resulting in data for 596 RIL crosses.

PLATFORM
GPL17887 NimbleGen D. melanogaster Gene Expression 12x135K Array [090828_Dmel_exp]
