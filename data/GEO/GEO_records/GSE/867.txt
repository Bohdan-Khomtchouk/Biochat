TITLE
Nrf2 Knockout vs. Wildtype mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
In situ synthesized oligo arrays, U74Av2, from Affymetrix were used to measure differential gene expression in RNA samples generated from the liver (GSE864) and spleen (GSE865) of Nrf2 knockout and wildtype mice at 5 months of age. This SuperSeries is composed of the SubSeries listed below.
