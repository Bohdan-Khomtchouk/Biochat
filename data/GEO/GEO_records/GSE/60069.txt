TITLE
Characteristics of Long Non-coding RNAs in the Brown Norway Rat and Alterations in the Dahl Salt-Sensitive Rat

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Using poly(A)-independent and strand-specific RNA-seq, we identified approximately 1,500 to 1,800 lncRNAs expressed in each of the following tissues of Brown Norway rats: the renal cortex, renal outer medulla, liver, cardiac left ventricle, adrenal gland, and hypothalamus.

DESIGN
Examination of several tissues of BN rats

PLATFORM
GPL14844 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Rattus norvegicus)
