TITLE
Role of FGFR1 in neuronal devlopment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Purpose: The goal was to study the effects of nFGFR1 on neuronal progenitor cells, by analyzing different effects of FGFR1 mutants through RNA-seq.

DESIGN
NPCs were transfected with either Beta-Gal, FGFR1(SP-/NLS+) or FGFR1(SP-/NLS+)(TK-)

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
