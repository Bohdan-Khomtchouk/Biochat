TITLE
Disomic yeast response to heat shock

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
These experiments were done to compare the transcriptional response to high temperature of disomic yeast to that of WT yeast.

DESIGN
Total RNA from strains and timepoints specified was labeled by Cy3- this was compared to WT total RNA in all cases.
