TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of WT and CD2AP KO TFH cells in LCMV-c13 infection

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report CD2AP KO TFH cells in LCMVc13 infection have enhanced cytokine production and cell cycling characteristics compared to WT cells

DESIGN
