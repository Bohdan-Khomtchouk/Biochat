TITLE
Crosstalk between Estrogen and TNFalpha in MCF-7 Breast Cancer Cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Study was carried out to examine how E2 and TNFa together influence gene expression in breast cancer cells compared to either factor alone. Keywords: drug response

DESIGN
MCF-7 cells were treated with 10 nM E2, 10 ng/ml TNFa, or both for 2h. Experiment was carried out in triplicate.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
