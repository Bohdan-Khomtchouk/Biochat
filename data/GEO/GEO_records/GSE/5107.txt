TITLE
Distinct Transcription Profiles of Primary and Secondary Glioblastomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression data of primary and secondary glioblastoma subgroups. Keywords: glioblastoma, transcription profiles

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from eighty-three glioblastoma biopsies in total. Probes were synthesized using the commercial Affymetrix kits and hybridized onto U133A Arrays.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
