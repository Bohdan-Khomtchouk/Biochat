TITLE
Mycobacterium avium subsp. paratuberculosis K-10

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium avium subsp. paratuberculosis K-10

SUMMARY
To compare the transcriptional profile of K-10 and phoPR in the log phase, and to find out the effects of phoPR on MAP gene expression.

DESIGN
There are three replicates from K-10 as the control samples, and three replicates from phoPR used to examine the differences.
