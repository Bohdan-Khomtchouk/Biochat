TITLE
Leucegene: AML sequencing (part 5)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNA sequencing of human leukemia

DESIGN
The goals of this project are to obtain a comprehensive study of mutations and gene expression in human acute myeloid leukemia (AML). Methods: AML cells were thawed. DNA and RNA (polyA) was extracted and sequences were obtained with an illumina HiSeq 2000 sequencer. Results are pending.
