TITLE
Half-Half experiments

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Various cell lines including clones of MDAH-041 cells were karyotypically defined by SKY. Two replicates of each line was harvested from one flask of cells.

DESIGN
Replicate from 1/2 of a flask was compared to the replicate from the other half of the flask

PLATFORM
GPL6102 Illumina human-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
