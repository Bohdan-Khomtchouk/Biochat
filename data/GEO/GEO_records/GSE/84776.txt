TITLE
Compared performance of Affymetrix HTA arrays and Illumina RNAseq for the analysis of tumours [RNA-seq]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Here we compared the performance of Affymetrix HTA 2.0 microarray and Illumina 2000 RNA-sequencing techniques on the clinical samples collected from patients with lung squamous cell carcinoma.

DESIGN
Comparison of transcriptome (9 tumor and 9 adjacent tissue samples) in RNA-seq and microarray.

GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
