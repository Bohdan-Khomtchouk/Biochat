TITLE
Human perirenal adipose tissue

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of perirenal adipose tissue from healthy kidney donors (age 44±9 years, BMI 25.8±3.3 kg/m2, mean±SD). The samples were taken bylaparoscopic technique beneath the incision plane that was created in the renal fascia (Greotas fascia) close to the renal vein.

DESIGN
Ten samples of human perirenal adipose tissue
