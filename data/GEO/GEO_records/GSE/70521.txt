TITLE
A comparison of whole genome gene expression profiles of GFP/CD19-Cre and LMP2A/CD19-Cre mouse splenic B220+ B cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression profiling change by expression of Epstein-Barr virus latent membrane protein 2A (LMP2A) in B cells

DESIGN
A Dye-swapped experiment was performed by hybridizing complimentary RNA (cRNA) labeled with either Cyanine (Cy) -3 or Cy-5 onto 4X44K ver.2 Agilent Whole Mouse Genome Oligo Microarray (G4846A).
