TITLE
Genome-wide maps of EGR2 transcription factor binding sites in T cell anergy

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report the application of sequencing technology for mapping the EGR2 transcriptional program in T cell anergy

DESIGN
Examination of Egr2 genomic binding sites in T cells following overnight treatment with immobilized anti-CD3 mAb (1ug/ml)
