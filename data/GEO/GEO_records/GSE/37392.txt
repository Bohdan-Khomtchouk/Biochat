TITLE
Pellicle Stimulon in Mycobacterium tuberculosis

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis

SUMMARY
Triplicate 10mL cultures of M. tuberculosis grown in Sautons media without detergent in planktonic (exponential growth phase at OD600 0.2) compared to pellicle (3 weeks growth without shaking in 5% CO2) conditions.

DESIGN
comparison of pellicle growth transcriptome to planktonic growth transcriptome, 3 biological replicates, third is dye-flip

PLATFORM
GPL5774 JCVI PFGRC Mycobacterium tuberculosis 22K v4 array designed primarily based on strain H37Rv
