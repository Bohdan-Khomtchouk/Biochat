TITLE
Serum microRNA analysis of primary ovarian cancer patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To validate prediction marker of ovarian cancer patients, comprehensive analysis of serum microRNA of ovarian cancer patients were performed.

DESIGN
Sera of seventeen primary ovarian cancer and five normal ovary or benign ovarian tumor patients were enrolled this study. Among these, sera of eleven patients were obtained at before and after operation.  Total RNA obtained from thirty-four sera were analyzed with SurePrint G3 Human miRNA.
