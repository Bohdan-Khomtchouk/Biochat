TITLE
Non-coding RNA expression in colorectal cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression was measured in 113 colorectal cancer samples using a costumized array.

DESIGN
Expression was measured in 113 human colorectal cancer samples using a custom-designed array containing 26,910 non-coding RNA features and a set of 6,856 protein-coding genes.
