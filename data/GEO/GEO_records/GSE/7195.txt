TITLE
Smooth Muscle Cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This set contains 68 arrays of two series of experiments. 60 arrays are used to profile the heterogeneity of SMCs from different anatomic locations. 8 arrays are used to profile the change of gene expression of vascular smooth muscle cells upon serum exposure. An all pairs experiment design type is where all labeled extracts are compared to every other labeled extract. Keywords: all_pairs

DESIGN
Using regression correlation
