TITLE
Frequent and Focal FGFR1 Amplification Associates With Therapeutically Tractable FGFR1 Dependency in Squamous-cell Lung Cancer.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We analyzed 155 squamous- cell lung cancer and 77 adenocarcinoma of the lung samples using 6.0 SNP-arrays all normal tissues that were used to infer copy number are provided

DESIGN
DNA was extracted from fresh frozen tissues and copy number data was generated for all samples
