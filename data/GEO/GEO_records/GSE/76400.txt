TITLE
Control of Fiber Color in Naturally Colored Cotton by Flavonoid Biosynthesis

ORGANISM
Gossypium hirsutum

SUMMARY
Compare the expression profiles of natural brown and green cotton fibers, and white color fiber at 0 and 12 days post-anthesis (DPA) to identify genes involved in pigment synthesis

DESIGN
Examination of 3 different color cotton Fibers in 2  time points (0 day and 12 day).

PLATFORM
GPL16485 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Gossypium hirsutum)
