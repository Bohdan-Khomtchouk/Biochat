TITLE
Elutriation time course

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Small G1 daughter yeast cells were isolated by centrifugal elutriation. They were then released into YEP ethanol, and followed through one cell cycle, with samples being taken every 30 minutes. This study is described in more detail in Spellman PT et al.(1998) Mol Biol Cell 9:3273-97 Keywords: time-course
