TITLE
Mouse Embryo Sox1 cre-dependent knockout of EED

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Floxed EED homozygote mice are knocked-out by Sox1-driven Cre to remove H3K27me3 during neurogenesis

DESIGN
Control mice are compared to EED-cKO mice in bulk RNAseq, in dissected forebrain and spinal cord
