TITLE
Mouse Wild Type Tissue Survey

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Survey of gene expression in 9 types of C57/Bl mouse wild type tissue.

DESIGN
RNA was extracted from homogenized whole organs/tissues and include brain, spleen, liver, thymus, kidney, lung, testes, bone marrow, and lymph node. 8 week old male littermate C57/Bl mice
