TITLE
Compilation of expression profiles distinct thyroid neoplasias

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A cohort of distinct thyroid neoplasias was hybridized onto the Affymetrix U95 GeneChip

DESIGN
We profiled the gene expression of a cohort of 9 Hurthle cell adenomas, 17 follicular adenomas, 9 follicular thyroid carcinomas, 13 follicular variants of papillary thyroid carcinomas, 6 Hashimoto throiditis, 8 thyroid hyperplasias and 9 papillary thyroid carcinomas.

PLATFORM
GPL8300 [HG_U95Av2] Affymetrix Human Genome U95 Version 2 Array
