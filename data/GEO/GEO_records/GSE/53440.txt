TITLE
CGH array of opportunistic strains

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Genomic comparation of 3 opportunistic strains (60, 102 and D14) and one laboratory strain (W303) with the type strain S288C

DESIGN
Comparison of each strain with the type strain. Biological replicates:2-3. One or two non-dye swap and one dye swap replicates were merged. One replicate per array
