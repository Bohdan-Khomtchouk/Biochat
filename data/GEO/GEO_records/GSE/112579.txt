TITLE
Transcriptome of tumor biopsy sample of patient with multiple metachronous ovarian cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We profiled gene expression in formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded (FFPE) tumor biopsy sample of patient with multiple metachronous ovarian cancer.

DESIGN
Microrray analysis was perrformed for single biopsy sample. Sample was evaluated by a pathologist to confirm the diagnosis and estimate the tumor cell numbers (sample should contain at least 80% of tumor cells).

PLATFORM
GPL22166 CustomArray Signaling Pathway platform version 2
