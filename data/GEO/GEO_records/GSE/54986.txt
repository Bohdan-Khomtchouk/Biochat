TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of colorectal cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Cancerous and paracancerous tissues of 6 cases having colorectal cancer were examined, such as  up- or down-regulated specific  cellular functions.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from isolated cancer tissues, Illumina HumanHT-12 v4 Expression BeadChip being used. Data processing is performed with GenomeStudio 1.0.2.20706,Gene Expression Module 1.0.6.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
