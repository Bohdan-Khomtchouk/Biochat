TITLE
Global Gene Expression of Human Hepatoma Cells After Amino Acid Limitation

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
1507 known genes have been identified differentially regulated during HisOH treatment by more than 2 fold. This includes 250 down-regulated genes and 1257 up-regulated genes.

DESIGN
Two conditions were used, 8 arrays, 4 for each condition, M-basal condition, H-HisOH treatment.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
