TITLE
MacroH2A1 ChIP-chip

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
MacroH2A1 ChIP-chip was performed on custom Nimblegen genomic tiling arrays, to understand the genomic binding patterns of this histone variant and its relationship to gene expression Keywords: ChIP-chip

DESIGN
Two macroH2A biological replicates are included. An H3 ChIP-chip sample is included as a control

PLATFORM
GPL6023 Custom Tiling array of 920 regions (HG18)
