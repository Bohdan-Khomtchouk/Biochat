TITLE
Differentiation of fiber initials on cotton ovules

ORGANISM
Gossypium hirsutum

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of cotton ovule cells.  Comparison of expression of genes in cultured ovules (-1 DPA) treated with or without phytohormones (5um IAA and 1um GA) and over a time-course from 0 to 12 hours.

DESIGN
The phytohormone incubation periods for unfertilized ovules were 0, 1, 3, 6, and 12 hours (H) with two biological replicates at each time-point.

PLATFORM
GPL8062 Cotton Array Version 2
