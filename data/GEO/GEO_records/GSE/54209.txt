TITLE
Serum Response Factor binding in Dstn-corn1 mutant and WT murine cornea

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report the application of chromatin immunoprecipitation sequencing to identify binding sites of the transcription factor serum response factor (SRF) in the cornea of WT and Dstn-corn1 mutant mice

DESIGN
Examination of SRF binding sites in WT and Dstn-corn1 mutant cornea

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
