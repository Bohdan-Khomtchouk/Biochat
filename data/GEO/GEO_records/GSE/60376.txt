TITLE
Response of M. tuberculosis to 8 hour exposure to vitamin C

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis H37Rv

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of M.tuberculosis to 10 mM vitamin C at 8 h. This was compared to gene expression profile of untreated M. tuberculosis culture.

DESIGN
Organism: Mycobacterium tuberculosis H37Rv, Genotypic Technology designed Custom Mycobacterium tuberculosis H37Rv Whole Genome 8x15k GE Microarray (AMADID-020181)
