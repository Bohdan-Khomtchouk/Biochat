TITLE
Analysis of differentially expressed genes in HepG2 after transfection with miR-192,miR-204 and siHOTTIP

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study aimed to identify differential expressed genes before and after transfection with miR-192,miR-204 and siHOTTIP, using the HepG2 liver cancer cell line as a model.

DESIGN
There were a total of 4 samples examined.
