TITLE
Comparison of WT Vs NaS1-/- mouse ileum

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Pairwise comparison of ileum gene expression in pools (n=14) of wildtype 4 week old male Vs NaS1-/- knockout mice. Keywords: Genetic modification

DESIGN
pairwise comparison of pooled WT Vs pooled NaS1-/- ileum tissues. Samples were hybridized in quadruplicate and 2 sets of samples were dye swapped.
