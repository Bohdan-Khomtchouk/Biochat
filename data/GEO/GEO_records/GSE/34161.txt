TITLE
Pig adipose tissue: comparison between Basque and Large White breeds

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of pig adipose tissue comparing two divergent breeds: Large white (LW, conventional) and Basque (B, local, indigeneous).

DESIGN
Four-condition experiment, LW vs. B muscles at 35 kg and 145 kg live weight. Biological replicates: at 145 kg, 10 B and 10 LW; at 35 kg 5 B and 5 LW. One replicate per array.
