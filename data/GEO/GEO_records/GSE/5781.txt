TITLE
Array CGH in TAR syndrome

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The series represents the analysis data of four TAR syndrome patients by submegabase resolution BAC array. Keywords: array CGH

DESIGN
This study aimed to detect submicroscopic rearrangements in patients with TAR syndrome by using a submegabase resolution BAC array.
