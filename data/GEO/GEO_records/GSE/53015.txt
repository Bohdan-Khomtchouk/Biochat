TITLE
Small RNA sequencing of control and Su(var)3-7 mutant Drosophila ovary

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Comparison of small RNA fractions derived from piRNA clusters and transposon sequences in control and Su(var)3-7 null mutant.

DESIGN
Small RNA profile of 3-day old control and Su(var)3-7 mutant ovaries were generated by high-throughput sequencing on Illumina HiSeq 2000
