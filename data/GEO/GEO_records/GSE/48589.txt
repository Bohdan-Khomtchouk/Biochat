TITLE
Runx3 function in CD4+ splenic dendritic cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
CD4+ dendritic cells are part of the innate immunity essential for priming and activating of CD4+ T cells

DESIGN
To identify Runx3 responsive genes CD4+ dendritic cells were sorted from freshly isolated macs enriched splenic DCs taken from 6 weeks old mice. Six samples from six mice were sorted and analyzed where in each littermates pair consisted of a  control and Runx3 KO.
