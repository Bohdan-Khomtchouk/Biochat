TITLE
porcine T cells : colostral T cells vs blood T cells

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
CD8 single positive T cells and CD4/CD8 double positive T cells were sorted from porcine colosrum and blood by using a cell sorter. Transcriptional profiles was compared between colostral and blood T cells.

DESIGN
Two comparisons: colostral CD8 single postive T cells vs blood CD8 single positive T cells, and colostral double positive T cells vs blood double T cells. Biological replicates;  colostrum and blood from two sows
