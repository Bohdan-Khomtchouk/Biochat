TITLE
miRNA profiling of adult and pediatric renal tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Profiling of adult and pediatric renal tumors reveals that genome wide miRNA expression patterns are unique to each tumor subtypes. Keywords: Disease state analysis

DESIGN
Samples from Clear Cell RCC, Papillary RCC, Chromophobe RCC, Oncocytoma, and Wilms Tumor compared to pooled normal kidney samples from these same patients.  No technical replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL6668 MRA-1001 Human miRNA Array versions 7, 9, and 10.1_071219
