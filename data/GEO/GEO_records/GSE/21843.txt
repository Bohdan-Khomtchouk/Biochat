TITLE
Three Major salivary glands

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
The aim of this study is characterize the gene expression of rat parotid, submandibular and sublingual glands, providing basic information for the salivary marker proteins.

DESIGN
Three Wistar rats were used to compare gene expression of the parotid, submandibular and sublingual glands.
