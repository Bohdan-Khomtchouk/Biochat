TITLE
WS1 response to high dose of UV-C

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The transcriptional response to 50 J/m2 of UV-light (254 nm) was assessed with HGU95AV2 Affymetrix probe arrays at 6, 12, 18 and 24 hours after exposure. Mock-treated samples were assessed at 6 and 24 hours. Keywords: time-course
