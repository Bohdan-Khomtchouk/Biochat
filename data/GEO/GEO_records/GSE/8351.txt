TITLE
Array-CGH of human hepatocellular carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparison of normal versus tumor DNA (hepatocellular carcinoma). Whole-genome screening of DNA-copy number changes by array-based or matrix comparative genomic hybridization (aCGH). Tumor DNA labeled in Cy3 and pooled DNA of lymphocytes from healthy donors labeled in Cy5. Keywords: Genetic modification
