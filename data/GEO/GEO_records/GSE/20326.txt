TITLE
Cerium_Oxide_Nanoparticles_e_coli

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
The effects of cerium oxide nanoparticles on the gene expression of Gram-negative Escherichia coli is tested.

DESIGN
8 cultures of E. coli were treated with either cerium nanoparticles or cerium chloride solution at 50 mgs/ml. 4 cultures were untreated.
