TITLE
RNA-Seq of head tissue from Drosophila melanogaster Wild Type and AdarEA mutant

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
We profiled gene expression in flies with and without A-to-I RNA editing (AdarEA mutant Drosophila melanogaster with a Canton-S background).

DESIGN
mRNA profiles of heads of 3 day old male AdarEA mutant (y, sc, AdarE374A) and wild type (white Canton-S) flies

PLATFORM
GPL19132 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Drosophila melanogaster)
