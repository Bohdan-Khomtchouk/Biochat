TITLE
Enterohemorrhagic Escherichia coli: impact of short chain fatty acid treatment on virulence gene expression

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
transcriptome analysis of enterohemorrhagic E. coli treated with either one of two different concentrations of short chain fatty acid mixes or the corresponding sodium chloride osmolarity control

DESIGN
four conditions: 30mM SCFA mix; 30mM NaCl control; 172mM SCFA mix: 172 mM NaCl control. Biological replicates: 4 per group

PLATFORM
GPL13359 Agilent-020097 E. coli Gene Expression Microarray (Probe Name version)
