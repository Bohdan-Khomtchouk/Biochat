TITLE
Mouse Primary Cultured Hepatocytes:  Control vs SQ1 or Pravastatin treatment.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of mouse primary cultured hepatocytes comparing control (untreated), SQ1, and Pravastatin treated hepatocytes.

DESIGN
Control (untreated) versus SQ1 or Pravastatin treated primary hepatocytes. Biological replicates: 8 control replicates, 8 treated replicates. 4 each using dye swap design.

GPL11202 Agilent-026655 Whole Mouse Genome Microarray 4x44K v2 (Probe Name version)
