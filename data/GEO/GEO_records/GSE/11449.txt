TITLE
RAW264.7 cells were treated with KDO, IFN beta and 8Br

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RAW 264.7 cells were stimulated with KDO (100 ng/ml), IFN-β (300 pM) and/or 8-Br 100 μM. These ligands were applied individually or in combination with KDO for 60 min and 120 min. Total RNA was extracted using TriPure (Roche) following its protocol. Keywords: designed
