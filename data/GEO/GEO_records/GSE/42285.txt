TITLE
Host response in the lung to influenza infection with PR8, VN or X31 strains at 12, 16 and 24 hours

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Array analysis of total lung RNA obtained from mice 12,16,24 h post infection with influenza. Strains used were Mock, PR8, X31, VN62 (1x10^5pfu)

DESIGN
Time Point: 12, 16, 24 h post infection (Mock, PR8, X31, VN62); Mock group has three biological replicates, virally infected groups have nine replicates.
