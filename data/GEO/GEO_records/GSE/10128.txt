TITLE
Genomic copy number alterations as predictive markers of systemic recurrence in breast cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Array CGH containing 4,044 human bacterial artificial chromosome clones was used to assess copy number changes in 31 pairs of clinicopathologically well matched recurred / nonrecurred breast cancer tissues. Keywords: array CGH
