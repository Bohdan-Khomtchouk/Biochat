TITLE
Affymetrix SNP array data for colon cancer samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA from resected colon cancer primary tumour tissue was analyzed for association between tumour dissemination and copy number alterations.

DESIGN
Affymetrix SNP arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's directions on DNA extracted from cryopreserved tissue samples.

PLATFORM
GPL6801 [GenomeWideSNP_6] Affymetrix Genome-Wide Human SNP 6.0 Array
