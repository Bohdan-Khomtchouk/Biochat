TITLE
Expression Profiling of Circulating Red Blood Cells and Non Red Blood Cells in the Chicken Embryo (E4 and E6)

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
Blood was extracted from embryonic hearts at E4 and E6 and non-red blood was separated by density gradient centrifugation Keywords: Cell type comparison

DESIGN
There are a total of 9 samples which have one biological replicate each, except negative control E6 embryonic heart (used to screen heart contamination in our blood samples - which were drawn from the heart) which has only one sample.

GPL3213 [Chicken] Affymetrix Chicken Genome Array
