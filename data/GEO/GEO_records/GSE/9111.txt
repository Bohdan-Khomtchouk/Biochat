SNP array data for 50 leukemia remission samples

ORGANISM

SUMMARY
250k Sty, 250k Nsp, 250k Hind and 250k Xba Affymetrix SNP arrays for 50 leukemia remission samples used as controls for copy number analysis for GSE9109 and GSE9112.
