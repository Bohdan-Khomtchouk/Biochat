TITLE
Galanthamine hydrobromide 72h

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of 72h 1mM Galanthamine hydrobromide treated embryos. At this concentration GAL causes myopathology without causing morbidity.

DESIGN
Microarray analysis were performed, with 72h 1mM Galanthamine hydrobromide treated embryos compared with untreated control embryos of the same clutch.
