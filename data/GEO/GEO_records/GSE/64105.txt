TITLE
Transcriptional response of Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 to ascorbic acid

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pneumoniae D39

SUMMARY
Transcriptome comparison of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type grown in M17 medium to grown in M17 medium + 10mM ascorbic acid (AM17).

DESIGN
Two conditions design comparision of one strain including a dye swap.
