TITLE
Human T cells isolated from healthy European and African American blood donors: Control vs. Interferon alpha 2b-treated

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human T cells analyzing the impact of race on the responsiveness to IFNa in healthy blood donors. Control and IFNa-treated samples derived from healthy Caucasian American vs. African American blood donors are compared.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Control vs. IFNa-treated. Paired design; untreated and treated samples for each donor.
