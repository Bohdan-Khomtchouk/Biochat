TITLE
Exercised Induced Hypertrophy

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The Affymetrix GeneChip MgU74Av2 was used to analyze expression profiles of mice that had cardiac hypertrophy induced by swimming training.  More information of this model can be obtained at http://cardiogenomics.med.harvard.edu/groups/proj1/pages/swim_home.html Keywords = exercise induced hypertrophy, cardiac Keywords: time-course

PLATFORM
