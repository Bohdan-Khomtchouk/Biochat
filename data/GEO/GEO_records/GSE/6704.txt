TITLE
Gene expression changes in BVDV2-infected BL3 cells

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to study the gene expression changes that occurred in BL3 cells that were infected with type 2 bovine viral diarrhea virus (BVDV2).  What happens within the cell and how the cell responds to the virus is completely unknown. Keywords: gene expression changes, BVDV2-infected cells, acute infection
