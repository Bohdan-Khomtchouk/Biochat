TITLE
Human bronchial epithelial cell differentiation time course

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis was performed to identify transcriptional changes that occur during mucociliary differentiation of human primary bronchial epithelial cells cultured at an air-liquid interface (ALI). Keywords: time course

DESIGN
Cells from three different donors were cultured and collected at 11 different time points from day 0 to day 28 of ALI culture.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
