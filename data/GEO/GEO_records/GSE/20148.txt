TITLE
C. elegans microarrays: daf-2(-) vs. daf-2(-) xbp-1(-)

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of day 1 animals comparing untreated daf-2(-) animals and daf-2(-) xbp-1(-) animals. Goal was to identify genes whose expression in daf-2 mutants is dependent (directly or indirectly) on xbp-1.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, daf-2(-) animals and daf-2(-) xbp-1(-) animals. 4 Biological replicates in daf-2(e1370) background: 4 sets of daf-2(e1370) animals and their matching daf-2(e1370) xbp-1(zc12) animals. 4 Biological replicates in daf-2(e1368) background: 4 sets of daf-2(e1368) animals and their matching daf-2(e1368) xbp-1(zc12) animals.
