TITLE
DNA methylomes of Newborns and Nonagenarians

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA methylation differences between Newborns and Nonagenarians

DESIGN
The study aimed to compare the DNA methylation differences between newborns and nonagenarians using methylation array technology (450K, Illumina). The identified differently methylated CpG were further analyzed for their presence in diseases related to the aging phenotype (Werner and Progeria syndrome).
