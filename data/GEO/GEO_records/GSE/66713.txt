TITLE
The premammillary nucleus (PMM) of the mediobasal hypothalamus: Photosensitive vs. Photorefractory

ORGANISM
Meleagris gallopavo

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling of the premammillary nucleus comparing short day photosensitive, long day photosensitive, and long day photorefractory turkey hens

DESIGN
Three-condition experiment, short day photosensitive vs. long day photosensitive vs. long day photorefractory. Loop design with 4 biological replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL19870 Arizona Gallus gallus 20.7K Oligo Array v1.0
