TITLE
RNA-Seq analysis of KLF3 knockout E14.5 TER119+ (erythroid) murine fetal liver cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The aim of this study was to analyze the transcriptome of TER119+ fetal liver cells in the absence of the transcription factor KLF3 at murine embryonic day E14.5

DESIGN
Three wildtype (WT; Klf3+/+) and three knockout (KO; Klf3-/-) samples

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
