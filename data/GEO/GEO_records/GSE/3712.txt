TITLE
Bhh signaling in pronephric development

ORGANISM
Xenopus laevis

SUMMARY
Pronephric explants were dissected in the presence or absence of hedgehog signaling.  Targets from pooled biological samples were examined in triplicate and the expression profiles were determined using Affymetrix Xenopus Genechip 1 arrays. Keywords: repeat

DESIGN
3 bhh treated pooled explants and 3 untreated pooled explants were analyzed

PLATFORM
GPL1318 [Xenopus_laevis] Affymetrix Xenopus laevis Genome Array
