TITLE
The expression profiling of mouse kidneys during early developmental stages

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Comparative analysis of gene expression patterns between prenatal (embryonic e16d) and postnatal (p1d and p7d) mouse kidney

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated of e16 kidney embryos, and kidneys from mice at posnatal day 1 and day 7

PLATFORM
GPL6103 Illumina mouseRef-8 v1.1 expression beadchip
