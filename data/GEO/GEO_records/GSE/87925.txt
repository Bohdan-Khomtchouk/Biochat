TITLE
RAMPAGE from diencephalon (ENCSR000AGL)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from human fetal diencephalon tissue from independent donors. They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RAMPAGE libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
