TITLE
Differential response of gun mutants to norflurazon

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Analysis of the genome wide response of wild type and two mutant arabidopsis thaliana seedlings to norflurazon Keywords: Genome wide response to inhibition of chloroplast development

DESIGN
5 day old seedlings of wild type (Col-0) and two mutants (gun1-9 and gun5). Three biological replicates for untreated or treated seedlings.
