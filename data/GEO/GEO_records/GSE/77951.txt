TITLE
Mouse embryonic lung FOXF1 ChIP-seq analysis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
FOXF1 targets in embryonic day 18.5 mouse lungs were determined using FOXF1 immunoprecipitation followed by sequencing.

DESIGN
FOXF1 binding sites in two biological replicates of pooled E18.5 wildtype mouse lungs (n=3) were to a 2% input control sample.

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
