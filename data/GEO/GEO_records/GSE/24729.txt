TITLE
Effect of mitochondria on nuclear gene expression in Drosophila melanogaster

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Five different mitochondrial strains were introgressed in male and female fruit flies with identical (w1118) nuclear genetic background.

DESIGN
40 samples: Five mitochondrial strains, two independent introgressions, two sexes, two biological replicates.

PLATFORM
