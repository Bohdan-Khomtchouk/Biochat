TITLE
Expression data in PBMCs from SLE patients and controls

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Peripheral blood mononuclear cells were collected from SLE patients in an observational study performed at the University of Michigan Blood microarray expression data were used to confirm the presence of an Interferon signature and identify additional surrogate genes

DESIGN
RNA from PBMCs of SLE patients and normal donor controls were obtained for one time point and were profiled on Affymetrix arrays

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
