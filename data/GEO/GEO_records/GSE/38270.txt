TITLE
450k Analysis of 24 HNSCC cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The methylation analysis of HPV+ HNSCC cell lines and HPV- HNSCC cell lines as well as clones from an infected HPV- cell line by Infinium HumanMethylation450 BeadChip

DESIGN
Analysis of 24 450k methylation profiles, comprising 3 HPV+ HNSCC cell lines and 3 HPV- HNSCC cell lines (in duplicate each) and 12 infected HPV- HNSCC cell line clones
