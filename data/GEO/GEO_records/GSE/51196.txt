TITLE
Analysis of epigenetic changes in F-36P leukemic cells by arsenic disulfide treatment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of F-36P leukemic cells treated by arsenic disulfide,  The Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip was used to obtain DNA methylation profiles

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 3 samples were hybridised to the Illumina  HumanMethylation450 BeadChip

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
