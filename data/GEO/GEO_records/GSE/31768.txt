TITLE
Expression & polysome association in pluripotent human embryonic stem cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal was to measure the expression and polysome association of genes with polysomes in human embryonic stem cells

DESIGN
H9 human embryonic stem cells were kept in pluripotent conditions. Total RNA and polysome extracts were isolated.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
