TITLE
The effects of Lactobacillus plantarum and E. coli on Caco2 human colonocytes genes expression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
In this study, we examined Caco-2 cell gene expression after infection with E. coli (Ec), Lactobacillus plantarum (Lp) and the combination of the two (mix) Keywords: Lactobacillus plantarum and E. coli influences on Caco2 cells gene expression
