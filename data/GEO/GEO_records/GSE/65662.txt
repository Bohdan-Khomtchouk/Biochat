TITLE
Comparative genomic and transcriptomic analyses of the Fuzhuan brick tea-fermentation fungus Aspergillus cristatus

ORGANISM
Aspergillus cristatus

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study is to find the differently expressed genes in Aspergillus cristatus at different osmotic pressure.

DESIGN
Examination of 3 transcriptomes in different osmotic pressure to find their different gene expression style.

PLATFORM
GPL19744 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Aspergillus cristatus)
