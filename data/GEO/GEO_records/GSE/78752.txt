TITLE
Transcriptome of liver tissue in 5 week old Stabilin-1 knock-out mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Stabilin-1/CLEVER-1 is a multidomain protein present in lymphatic and vascular endothelial cells and in M2 immunosuppressive macrophages. Stabilin-1 functions in scavenging, endocytosis and leukocyte adhesion to and transmigration through the endothelial cells.

DESIGN
The transcriptome of liver tissue in 5wk old Stab1 knock-out mice was compared to that of corresponding wild type mice
