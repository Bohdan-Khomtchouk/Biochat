TITLE
Analysis of differential gene expression in the Streptococcus sanguinis brpT mutant

ORGANISM
Streptococcus sanguinis

SUMMARY
Gene expression profile of the Streptococcus sanguinis TetR family transcriptional regulator, brpT, deletion mutant compared to the wild-type strain SK36

DESIGN
Experiment: brpT mutant vs. wild-type SK36. Biological replicates (1-5) independently grown and prepared for each sample.
