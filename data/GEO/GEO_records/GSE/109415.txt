TITLE
Transcriptomic analysis of Dinoroseobacter shibae DFL12T in response to phage infection

ORGANISM
Dinoroseobacter shibae

SUMMARY
Dinoroseobacter shibae DFL12T was cultured with/without phage R2C, and gene expression was analyzed at 60 min and 140 min during the incubation

DESIGN
Infected and uninfected Dinoroseobacter shibae DFL12T were sampled at two time points and two biological replicates sample for each experiment
