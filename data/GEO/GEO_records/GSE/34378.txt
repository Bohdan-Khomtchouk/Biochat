TITLE
Aging Experiment

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Lifelong murine gene expression profiles in relation to chronological and biological aging in multiple organs

DESIGN
Five tissues: Kidney, Liver, Lung, Spleen, Brain. Six timepoints: 13wks, 26 wks, 52wks, 78wks, 104wks, 130wks. One Genotype: C57Bl/6, 3 replicates
