TITLE
TGF-beta stimulation in ovarian cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide DNA methylation profiles of TGF-beta ovarian cancer cells were obtained using next generation Illumina Infinium 450k assay which includes over 450,000 GpG sites.

DESIGN
DNA from 12 samples were hybridized to the Illumina's Infinium HumanMethylation 450 BeadChip

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
