TITLE
Murine postpartum testis developmental time course (0 to 35 day)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Murine testis developmental time course created from tissue samples collected from birth through adulthood and hybridized to M430_2 chips in duplicate. Keywords: time-course

DESIGN
Time course of gene expression in the murine postpartum testis development (duplicates in day 0, 3, 6, 8, 10, 14, 18, 20, 30, 35). Total 20 samples.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
