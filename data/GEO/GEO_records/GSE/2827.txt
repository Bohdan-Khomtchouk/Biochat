TITLE
The response of E. coli to exposure to the biocide polyhexamethylene biguanide

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
The global response of E. coli tot he broad spectrum biocide polyhexamethyelene biguanide (PHMB) was investigated using transcriptional profiling. Keywords: dose response

DESIGN
E. coli was grown until early exponential phase and (t=2.5 hrs) then exposed to PHMB (7.5 mg/l). RNA was extracted (3.75 hrs) from triplicate exposed cultures and triplicate unexposed cultures.
