TITLE
Woodchuck and human liver comparison

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens ; Marmota monax

SUMMARY
Total RNA from adult human and woodchuck liver was analyzed on human membrane arrays. Two human and two woodchuck liver samples were analyzed in duplicate. The samples were numbered H1a, H1b, H2a, H2b, WC1a, WC1b, WC2a, and WC2b. H, represents human liver; WC represents woodchuck liver. The numbers 1 and 2 were the two individual samples. The a and b represent replicates of the same RNA sample. Keywords = liver, adult, normal, human, woodchuck Keywords: other

PLATFORM
