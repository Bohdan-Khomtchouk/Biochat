TITLE
Sphere-Derived Multipotent Progenitor Cells Obtained From Human Oral Mucosa Are Enriched in Neural Crest Cells.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study was to isolate NCSCs from oral mucosa using the neurosphere technique.

DESIGN
Total RNA from human oral mucosa stromal cells and sphere-formig oral mucosa stromal cells was collected and compared at their gene expression level. Samples from 3 patients were analysed.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
