TITLE
Ultra high resolution array painting

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Ultra high resolution breakpoint mapping using custom oligonucleotide arrays and array painting Keywords: Breakpoint mapping

DESIGN
The two derivative chromosomes from 4 patients with balanced translocations were flow sorted, differentially labelled and hybridised to custom oligonucleotide arrays designed to cover the potential breakpoint region at high resolution.  Single hybridisations with no replicates.
