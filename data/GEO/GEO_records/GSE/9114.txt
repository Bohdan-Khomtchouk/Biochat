TITLE
CGH profiles of sporadic breast tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genomic DNA from sporadic breast tumours was isolated and analysed using array CGH. The NKI 1MB BAC/PAC micro array was used to identify chromosomal aberrations of all tumours. Keywords: sporadic breast tumour, CGH

DESIGN
Locate chromosomal aberrations in sporadic breast tumours

PLATFORM
GPL4560 Netherlands Cancer Institute human 3.5k BAC array
