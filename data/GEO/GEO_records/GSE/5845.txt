SUMMARY
40 bladder cancer cell lines were profiled with their genome-wide gene expression patterns using Affymetrix HG-U133A chips. Keywords: bladder cancer cell line expression profiling

DESIGN
Total RNA sample of each of the 40 cell lines was obtained before the treatment of any anticancer compound.
