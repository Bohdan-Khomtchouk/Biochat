TITLE
Transcriptome in yeast by exposure of 7 transition metals

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
7 transition metals (silver, arsenic, cadmium, chromium, copper, mercury and zinc) were treated (2hr) on diploid yeast BY4743. Keywords: Response to metals

DESIGN
Condition for RNA preparation 7 metals 3 independent isolates two concentrations (EC10 and EC50) every metal has 6 total RNA samples * 2 technical replicates (dye-swap) Labeling and hybridization (Paradigm Array Labs) total RNA Low-input labeling method (Agilent) 10,807 60-mer oligo microarray representing 6,256 ORF (Agilent)

PLATFORM
GPL6068 Agilent-011447 Yeast Oligo Microarray (G4140A) - alternative version
