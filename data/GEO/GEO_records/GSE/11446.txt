TITLE
CD8 T cells stimulated with IL-2 complex in vivo

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
IL-2 signals into CD8 T cells have a programming and regulatory role in driving cells to full effector and memory differentiation. This study was designed to look for IL-2 target genes that affect CD8 T cell responses. Keywords: Time-course, cytokine treatment Keywords: Expression profiling by array
