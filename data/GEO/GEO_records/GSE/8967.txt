TITLE
Human colon cancer cell lines: NT(untreated) vs EGF, Cetuximab and Gefitinib treatments

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene Expression Profiling of HT-29 and Caco-2 colon cancer cell lines untreated compared with EGF, Cetuximab, Gefitinib,EGF plus Cetuximab and EGF plus gefitinib treatments. Keywords: Gene Expression Profiling

DESIGN
Five experimental conditions: NTvsEGF, NTvsCetuximab, NTvsGefitinib, NTvsEGFplusGefitinib, NTvsEGFplusCetuximab, for two human colon cancer cell lines (HT-29 and Caco-2).
