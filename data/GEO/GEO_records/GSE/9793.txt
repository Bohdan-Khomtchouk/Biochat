TITLE
Expression comparison between MGAS2221 and isogenic spyCEP mutant

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pyogenes

SUMMARY
Expression analysis was performed to confirm that spyCEP is the only gene differentially regulated between parental strain MGAS2221 and isogenic spyCEP mutant 2221DELTACEP. Keywords: expression comparison

DESIGN
4 cultures of each strain were analyzed.
