TITLE
ChIP-seq of ER, PR, p300 in two ER+ breast cancer cell lines treated with estrogen and with and without progestins

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Exploring effect of progesterone/progestin treatment on ER and PR binding.

DESIGN
Two cell lines, three conditions (Full Media with E2, E2+ Progesterone, Full Media + R5020 Progestin), three factors (ER, PR, p300), all with three replicates, each with a matched Input control.
