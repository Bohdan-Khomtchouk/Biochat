TITLE
Rat Primary Cultured Hepatocytes:  Control vs SQ1 or Pravastatin treatment.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of rat primary cultured hepatocytes comparing control (untreated), SQ1, and Pravastatin treated hepatocytes.

DESIGN
Control (untreated) versus SQ1 or Pravastatin treated primary hepatocytes. Biological replicates: 8 control replicates, 8 treated replicates. 4 each using dye swap design.
