TITLE
Bis-(2-chloroethyl) sulfide exposure time course and dose response in the rat lung

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Male Sprague-dawley rats were exposed to saline, isopropyl alcohol, 1mg/kg, 3mg/kg or 6 mg/kg sulfur mustard for 30 min, 1 hr, 3 hr, 6 hr, or 24 hr before analysis of lung tissue by oligonucleotide array analysis.
