TITLE
Characterization of miRNomes in acute and chronic myeloid leukemia cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
An in-depth analysis of miRNomes in 3 human myeloid leukemia cell lines was carried out to comprehensively identify miRNAs that distinguish acute and chronic myeloid leukemias and relate to myeloid cell differentiation.

DESIGN
Characterization the miRNomes in 3 myeloid leukemia cell lines.
