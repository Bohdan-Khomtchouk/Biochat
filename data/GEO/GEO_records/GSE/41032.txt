TITLE
miRNA profiles of brain glioma stem cells and normal neural stem cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A human microarray comprising 1658 human genome probes was used to evaluate the specific expression of miRNA  between brian glioma stem cells (GSC) and normal neural stem cells (NSC).

DESIGN
2 total RNA samples are analyzed, brian glioma stem cells (GSC) and normal neural stem cells (NSC). cell type: brain glioma stem cells: GSC_1, GSC_2, GSC_3 ; cell line: normal neural stem cells: NSC_1, NSC_2, NSC_3 biological replicate: NSC_1, NSC_2, NSC_3; biological replicate: GSC_1, GSC_2, GSC_3
