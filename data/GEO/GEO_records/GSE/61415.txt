TITLE
Streptococcus pyogenes MGAS5005: control vs heme exposure

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pyogenes

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Streptococcus pyogenes MGAS5005 cells comparing control untreated GAS cells with GAS cells exposed to 4uM heme for 1.5 h

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, untreated vs. heme-treated MGAS5005 cells. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 Heme-treated, independantly grown and harvested. One replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL19152 M1, M3, M18 GAS Array
