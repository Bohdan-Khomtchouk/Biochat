TITLE
Characterization of CD1- and CD1+ porcine blood dendritic cells confirm them as orthologues of the two major mammalian conventional subsets

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
Porcine CD1- and CD1+ dendritic cells represent the orthologues of human and mouse cDC1 and cDC2 cellssubsets respectively

DESIGN
Porcine CD1- and CD1+ dendritic cells
