TITLE
Sequential loss of plasticity during trophectoderm and inner cell mass lineage segregation in the mouse embryo

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report the whole transcriptome data of single-cells derived from the early 16-cell stage to the 64-cell stage in the mouse embryo.

DESIGN
RNA from 262 cells from 36 mouse embryos (16- to 64-cell stage)
