TITLE
Differentially expressed genes between non-treated and salt-treated WT plants

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
The experiment was designed to identify differentially expressed genes between control and NaCl-treated WT seedlings

DESIGN
Arabidopsis Columbia WT seedlings (14-day-old, short-day condition), 300 mM NaCl treatment for 0h, 2h, 5h, 2 biological replicates
