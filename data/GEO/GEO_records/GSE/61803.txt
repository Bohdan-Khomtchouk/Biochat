TITLE
Identify genes modulated by a 5 uM 30-minute pulse dose of gemcitabine

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Identified genes that were up- or down-regulated in HT-29 cells in response to gemcitabine

DESIGN
HT-29 cells were treated with a 30-minute pulse dose of 5 uM gemcitabine. Cells were harvested 24 hours later

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
