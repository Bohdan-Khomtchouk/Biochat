TITLE
Mycoplasma gallisepticum: Rlow vs. F

ORGANISM
Mycoplasma gallisepticum

SUMMARY
Mycoplasma gallisepticum transcriptome comparison between in vitro grown cultures of strains Rlow and F utilizing oligo DNA microarrays.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Rlow vs. F strain cells.  Biological replicates: 3.  1 technical replicate per biological replicate which includes a dye swap.

PLATFORM
GPL4729 Geary Mycoplasma gallisepticum oligo array
