TITLE
Transcriptome profiling of untreated and doxycycline-treated HeLa/TO-SmN

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Purpose is to examine the effect of SmN expression to gene expression in HeLa

DESIGN
Compare the differences between the transcriptome of untreated and doxycycline-treated HeLa/TO-SmN in which expression of SmN is under doxycycline control
