TITLE
Angiopoietin-like 4 enhances the ability of tendon fibroblasts to undergo proliferation and migration

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This microarray study provides novel insights into the role of Angiopoietin-like 4 in tendon biology with implications for injury repair and exercise physiology.

DESIGN
To gain an insight into Angiopoietin-like 4 -induced modifications in tendon fibroblasts by global transcriptional analysis
