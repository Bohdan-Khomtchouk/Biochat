TITLE
Expression data for xenograft tumor treated with IL-6 or siCUL4A.

ORGANISM
synthetic construct

SUMMARY
The miRNAs expression was altered with the treatment with IL-6 or siCUL4A in xenograft tumor of human colon adenocarcinoma cell line HCT116 cells. And various miRNAs induced by IL-6 also may contribute to tumor growth .
