TITLE
Glioblastoma stem cell (GSC) clones survived 500uM Temozolomide (TMZ) treatment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparison of parental GSC (GSC-parental) with treatment resistant GSC clones survived 500uM TMZ treatment (GSC-500uM TMZ) We used microarrays to identify defense profiles of GSC-500uM TMZ

DESIGN
We used 500uM TMZ to selecte treatment resistant GSC clones (GSC-500uM TMZ)

GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
