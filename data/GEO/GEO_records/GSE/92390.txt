TITLE
HiC of selected non-small cell lung carcinoma cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Utilizing HiC to characterize the three-dimensional architecture of two NSCLC lines: NCIH460A, NCIH460B.

DESIGN
