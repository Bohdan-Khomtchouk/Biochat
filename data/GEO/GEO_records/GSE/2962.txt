TITLE
Continuous GH treatment of old rat liver

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Effect of continuous GH treatment on old rat liver.  Male rats, 2-year-old, were treated with vehicle or human GH (0.34 microgram/gram body weight) for 3 weeks. Keywords: response of old rat liver to growth hormone

DESIGN
Comparison of treated and untreated rats.  Individual RNA were used for 4 microarray hybridization. Dye swapped.
