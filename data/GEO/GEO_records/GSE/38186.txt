TITLE
Escherichia coli K-12: 0%, 10% and 30% hydrogen peroxide (H2O2) treatments, in wild type and luxS mutants

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
Transcripitonal profiling of Escherichia coli K-12 W3110 comparing wild type and luxS mutants without or with 10%, 30% H2O2 treatments, two biological replicates for each condition

DESIGN
two-variables experiments: samples without or with treatment of 10% or 30% H2O2 for 30min; wild type and luxS mutants
