TITLE
Comparison of Hob6-Venus positive and negative Flk-1+ cells (Venus reporter)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
ES derived Flk-1+ cells were separated by sorting into Hoxb6 positive and negative populations by Venus reporter. Gene expression was compared between these two groups.

DESIGN
Duplicate analysis of Hoxb6-Venus positive and negative Flk-1+ cells.
