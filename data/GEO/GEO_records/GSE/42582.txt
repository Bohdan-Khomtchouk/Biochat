TITLE
Oncorhynchus mykiss muscle cells: Small vs. Large fish

ORGANISM
Oncorhynchus mykiss

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of rainbow trout muscle cells comparing muscle cells from small fish with muscle cells from large fish at two time periods.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, small vs. large-fish muscle cells. Sept. and Dec. spawning fish.  Biological replicates: 4 small replicates, 4 large replicates for each time period.

GPL7303 Agilent-020938 S. salar (Salmon) Oligo Microarray (Probe Name version)
