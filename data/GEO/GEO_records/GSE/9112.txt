TITLE
SNP array data for 36 acute myeloid and lymphoblastic leukemia cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Copy number and LOH analysis was performed for 36 acute leukemia cell lines. All cases were genotyped with Affymetrix 250k Sty and Nsp arrays. Keywords: Acute leukemia, BCR-ABL1, cell lines, copy number analysis, loss-of-heterozygosity, genomics *** Due to privacy concerns, the primary SNP array data is no longer available with unrestricted access.
