TITLE
ChIPseq in Mouse Hematopoietic Progenitor Cells (HPCs)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
ChIPseq to detect binding of Nup98, SET1A to chromatin and to observe H3K4me3 on chromatin under various conditions

DESIGN
Extract DNA from BALB/c mouse HPCs, immunopurify protein complexed and associated DNA, deep sequence to identify genomic binding sites. Antibodies used: Nup98 (P671) - Cell Signaling Technology - #2292 H3K4me3 - Abcam - ab8580 SET1A - Abcam - ab70378 Flag - Sigma - Flag M2 affinity gel #A2220

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
