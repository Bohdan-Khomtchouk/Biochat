TITLE
Response of Cupriavidus metallidurans CH34 to different Pt-species

ORGANISM
Cupriavidus metallidurans CH34

SUMMARY
Response of Cupriavidus metallidurans CH34 to cisplatin, Pt(IV)chloride and Au-NP

DESIGN
In this study 7 different treatments were performed (first 2 as 3 replicates) to acquire expression profiles of the total genome of Cupriavidus metallidurans

PLATFORM
GPL16115 MOLMIBI_CH34_array [Agilent-034995]

CITATIONS
B. Etschmann, J. Brugger, L. Fairbrother, C. Grosse, D.H. Nies, G. Martinez-Criado, F. Reithb. Applying the Midas touch: Differing toxicity of mobile gold and platinum complexes drives biomineralization in the bacterium Cupriavidus metallidurans. Chemical Geology, Volume 438, Pages 103-111, 2016.
