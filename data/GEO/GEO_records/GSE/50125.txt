TITLE
S. paradoxus wt strains: glucose vs. xylulose growth

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces paradoxus

SUMMARY
Transcription profile of 12 wt S.paradoxus strains originating from diverse geographical origins and natural habitats. Transcription profiles in two conditioins were compared: growth on glucose and growth on the naturally rare pentose sugar xylulose.

DESIGN
Two condition experiment, growth on glucose and on xylulose, 12 wt S.paradoxus strains
