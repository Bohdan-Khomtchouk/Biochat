TITLE
tatA_vs_WT

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Series of 4 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (tatA) and control (WT) each. Comparison of the Arabidopsis tata antisense line that is defective in the ion gradient dependent twin-arginine translocation (TAT) pathway for uptake from the stroma into, or passage through, the thylakoid membrane in the chloroplasts of higher plants versus WT. J. Marques, unpublished Keywords: repeat sample
