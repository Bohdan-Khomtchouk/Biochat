TITLE
Responses in Medicago truncatula to Sinorhizobium meliloti wild type or the succinoglycan-deficient exoY mutant.

ORGANISM
Medicago truncatula

SUMMARY
For transcript analysis of responses in Medicago truncatula to its symbiont Sinorhizobium meliloti wild type or the succinoglycan-deficient exoY mutant we compared transcripts from line A17 roots inoculated with 25 mL OD = 0.05 S. meliloti wild type or exoY mutant. Keywords: 1 line; 2 S. meliloti samples
