TITLE
Effetcs of several families of persistent organic pollutants on the undifferentiated and adipocyte differentiated human Multipotent Adipose-Derived Stem (hMADS) cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of hMADS cells treated or not by TCDD, PCB126 and PCB153.

DESIGN
Four-condition experiment: 4 control replicates, 4 TCDD-treated, 4 PCB126-treated, 4 PCB153-treated either undiffrentitaed or differentiated cells.
