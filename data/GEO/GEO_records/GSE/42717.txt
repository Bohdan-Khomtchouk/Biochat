TITLE
Mouse ES cell differentiated with Activin

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To find miRNAs that involve in renal epithelial differentiation.

DESIGN
Mouse ES cells, EB3 (129/Ola) were differentiated with Activin 10 ng/ml after 3 days of embryoid body formation.  Cells were harvested on day 18 of the differentiation.
