TITLE
Haemophilus ducreyi 35000HPfis relative to 35000HP

ORGANISM
[Haemophilus] ducreyi

SUMMARY
The goal  of this study was to compare the global trascription profile of a Haemophilus ducreyi fis deletion mutant to that of the wild type parental strain.

DESIGN
This study includes three biological replicates (paired samples of mutant and wild type strains), and all three were subjected to dye swap.
