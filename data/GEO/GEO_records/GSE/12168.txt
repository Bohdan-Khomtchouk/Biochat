TITLE
Aging Time course

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
RNA was isolated from age-synchronous cultures of hermaphrodite worms grown at 25?C at 2, 5, 8, or 11 days of adulthood. We observed no deaths in the worm population at days 2 and 5 of adulthood, 30% at day 8 and 93% death at day 11.  The aging time course was repeated four times. Groups of assays that are related as part of a time series. Age: Age of adult worms (days) Keywords: time_series_design
