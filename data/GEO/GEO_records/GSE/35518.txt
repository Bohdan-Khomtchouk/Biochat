TITLE
WT vs WTmitomycinC comparison

ORGANISM
Streptococcus thermophilus LMD-9

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of S. thermophilus LMD-9 wild-type in the presence of mitomycin C. Cells were grown in CDM medium  supplemented with lactose 1% (CDML), without or with mitomycin C 0.08 microg/ml, and sampled at OD600 = 0.4 for mRNA extraction.
