TITLE
Golden-mantled ground squirrel (Spermophilus lateralis) hibernating brain transcriptome

ORGANISM
Callospermophilus lateralis

SUMMARY
This series represents the brain transcriptome for animals sampled during summer, interbout arousal, and late torpor. Note that the brain expression data for interbout aroused animals was not analyzed in our publication because the group consisted of just 2 animals. Keywords = squirrel, brain, hibernation Keywords: other
