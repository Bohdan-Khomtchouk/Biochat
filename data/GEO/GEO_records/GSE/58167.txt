TITLE
Acinetobacter oleivorans DR1 : Cell treated Kanamycin 4 µg/ml in nutrient media

ORGANISM
Acinetobacter oleivorans DR1

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of A. oleivorans DR1  treated Kanamycin 4µg/ml for 15min  .

DESIGN
To identify effect of kanamycin in A. oleivornas DR1, the cells were grown to exponential phase (OD600 ~0.4) and treated kanamycin 4µg/ml for 15 min.
