TITLE
Regulation of miRNA expression by TGF-b in endothelial cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Effect of transforming growth factor-beta (TGF-b) on miRNA expression profile in MS1 endothelial cells.

DESIGN
MS1 endothelial cells were treated with/without TGF-b (72 hours), and subjected to miRNA microarray analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL21524 Exiqon miRCURY LNA microRNA Array, 6th generation [miRBase Release 16, condensed Probe ID version]
