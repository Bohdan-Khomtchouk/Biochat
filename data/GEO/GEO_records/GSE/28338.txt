TITLE
MicroRNA expression during Hematopoiesis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Murine ES cells were grown on confluent OP-9 and differentiated along the hematopoitic lineage. To assess the effects of Notch in hematopoiesis, activated Notch was overexpressed from Day 5 to Day 8.

DESIGN
Samples included Day 5 (control) and Day 8 Notch On and Day 8 Notch Off, the samples were done in triplicates.

PLATFORM
GPL11432 miRCURY LNA microRNA Array, 5th generation - hsa, mmu & rno
