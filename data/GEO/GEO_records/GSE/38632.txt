TITLE
The rpoS transcriptome of Pseudomonas protegens Pf-5 grown in rich medium

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas protegens Pf-5

SUMMARY
Transcriptomic profiling of an rpoS mutant of Pseudomonas protegens Pf-5 in comparison to the wild-type Pf-5 strain grown in nutrient broth supplemented with 1% glycerol to OD600=2.0-2.4 (early stationary phase).
