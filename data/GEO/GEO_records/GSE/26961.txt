TITLE
Bj H2O2 shock vs. non-treatment

ORGANISM
Bradyrhizobium diazoefficiens USDA 110

SUMMARY
Genome-wide transcriptional profiling of cells subjected to a H2O2  shock treatment (10 mM for 10 minutes).

DESIGN
Three independent biological materials were prepared for cells shock treated with H2O2 and non-treated cells. A total 6 arrays which includes dye swap were analyzed.
