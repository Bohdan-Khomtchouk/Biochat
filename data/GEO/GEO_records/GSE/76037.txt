TITLE
Gene expression analysis of developing Sea Lamprey  (Petromyzon marinus)

ORGANISM
Petromyzon marinus

SUMMARY
This project is designed to measure changes in gene expression during sea lamprey development

DESIGN
RNA was extracted from sea lamprey embryos at 1, 2, 2.5, 3, 4, and 5 days post-fertilization and used to generate RNAseq data

PLATFORM
GPL20963 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Petromyzon marinus)
