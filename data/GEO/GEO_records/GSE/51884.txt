TITLE
Transcriptome-wide analysis of active xylem in White Spruce and Norway Spruce during the growth season

ORGANISM
Picea abies ; Picea glauca

SUMMARY
The goal is to look at changes in the pattern of expression of the xylem transcriptome through the growth season in two spruces (Picea glauca and Picea abies).

DESIGN
One-color comparison of active xylem collected in June, July, August and September, in two spruce species. Six biological repetitions per time point and specie, for a total of 48 slides.
