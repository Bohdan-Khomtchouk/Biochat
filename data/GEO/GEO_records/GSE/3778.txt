TITLE
Gender dependent expression

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Gender dependent gene expression in the kidney of 36 day old rats using the Affymetrix GeneChip rat expression set 230 array RAE230A. mixed model ANOVA using log2 transformed signal intensity of PM. Keywords: repeat

DESIGN
Three rat strains Mhm, Us and MhmBN were examined. Three arrays were performed for each gender and strain combination. Total RNA from three rats was pooled for each array.

PLATFORM
GPL3240 Affymetrix GeneChip Rat Expression Set 230 Array RAE230A PM probes
