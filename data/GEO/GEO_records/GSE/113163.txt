TITLE
Vex-seq: high-throughput identification of genetic variation impact on pre-mRNA splicing efficiency

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Vex-seq is a method developed to study the impact of genetic variants on pre-mRNA splicing efficiency

DESIGN
Barcoded splice reporter plasmids are used to identify splicing efficiency using Illumina paired-end sequencing

PLATFORM
GPL15520 Illumina MiSeq (Homo sapiens)
