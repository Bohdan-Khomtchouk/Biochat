TITLE
The methylome of Alu repeats in colon cancer (CGH)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Next Generation Sequencing of Unmethylated Alu (NSUMA) interrogation of more than 130,000 individual Alus for differential methylation with concomitant analysis of copy number variations applied to the study of hypomethylation in colorectal cancer.

DESIGN
normal colon tissues, matched primary tumors, and colon derived cell lines
