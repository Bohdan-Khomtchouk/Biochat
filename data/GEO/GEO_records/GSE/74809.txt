TITLE
Growth-phase dependent regulation of transcription by Rsd and 6S RNA

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli str. K-12 substr. MG1655

SUMMARY
This entry refers to transcriptome analysis of five E. coli strains: E. coli K-12 MG1655, single deletions of rsd, ssrS, and rpoS, as well as a double deletion of rsd and ssrS, in five growth phases.

DESIGN
25 samples (5 strains in 5 growth phases) with 2 replicates each.

PLATFORM
GPL15982 Illumina HiSeq 1000 (Escherichia coli str. K-12 substr. MG1655)
