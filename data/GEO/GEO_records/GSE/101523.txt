TITLE
Hop stunt viroid: Effect on host (Humulus lupulus) transcriptome and its interactions with hop powdery mildew (Podospheara macularis)

ORGANISM
Humulus lupulus

SUMMARY
RNA sequence analysis of hop leaves infected with Hop stunt viriod revealed dynamic changes in hop gene expression.

DESIGN
High Throughput Sequencing analysis was used to identify the effect of Hop stunt viroid and hop powdery mildew on hop transcriptome in single and mixed infections

PLATFORM
GPL23790 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Humulus lupulus)
