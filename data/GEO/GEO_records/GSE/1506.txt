TITLE
A404 Smooth Muscle Cell Differentiation

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
In vitro time course experiment using the A404 clone of P19 mouse embryonal carcinoma cells to evaluate SMC differentiation in response to retinoic acid followed by phenotype-specific selection with puromycin. Four time points of 5-6 biological replicates each were performed. Keywords: time-course
