TITLE
total RNA-seq from M059J (ENCSR696SMK)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from the whole cell fraction of independent growths of cell line M059J. They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
