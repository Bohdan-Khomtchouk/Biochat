TITLE
Bmi1 mutant vs. WT dental epithelia

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Bmi1 mutant dental epithelia including the stem cell compartment to determine which genes are upregulated in response to loss of Bmi1.

DESIGN
Two condition experiment: dental epithelia homozygous null for Bmi1 and WT dental epithelia. 4 replicates each
