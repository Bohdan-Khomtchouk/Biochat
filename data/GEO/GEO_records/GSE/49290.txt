TITLE
Psychosocial stress reflected by the liver transcriptome in the pig

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
Unfamiliar individuals (German Landrace pigs) with different aggressiveness were mixed. Subsequently hepatic transcriptome profiles were analysed at postnatal stage (190 dpn)

DESIGN
per psychosocial group 6 pigs were collected for expression profiling

PLATFORM
GPL3533 [Porcine] Affymetrix Porcine Genome Array
