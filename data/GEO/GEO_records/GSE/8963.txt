TITLE
Transcriptome profiling of wheat caryopsis development

ORGANISM
Triticum aestivum

SUMMARY
Global gene expression analysis of 7,835 tentatively unique wheat genes during caryopsis development Keywords: Time-course study; developing wheat caryopsis

DESIGN
The expression of 7,835 genes in developing wheat caryopses was analyzed by direct RNA comparisons using an interconnected loop-design with 6 time-points X 3 biological replicates per time-point X dye-swap

PLATFORM
GPL5808 Wheat 8K cDNA microarray version 2 (WA.v2)
