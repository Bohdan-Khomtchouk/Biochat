TITLE
Novel single nucleotide polymorphism markers for low dose aspirin-associated small bowel bleeding

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Blood from 17 patients taking enteric-coated low-dose aspirin (LDA) and with suspected bleeding from small intestine and 18 control patients taking aspirin were analyzed. Results provide insight into the risk for aspirin-induced small bowel bleeding.

DESIGN
35 samples; 1 array per sample.

PLATFORM
GPL17860 [DMET_Plus] Affymetrix human DMET Plus array
