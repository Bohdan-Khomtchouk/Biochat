TITLE
Whole Genome CGH array on patients with acute myeloid leukemia-M5 (AML-M5)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Unbalanced chromosomal abnormalities might play a major role in the leukemogenesis of AML-M5 Oligonucelotide array CGH were performed on 24 patients with AML-M5

DESIGN
To assess the possible existence of unbalanced chromosomal abnormalities and delineate the characterization of copy number alterations (CNAs) of acute myeloid leukemia-M5 (AML-M5)

PLATFORM
GPL14965 NimbleGen Human 3x720k Whole Genome CGH [100718_HG18_WG_CGH_v3.1_HX3]
