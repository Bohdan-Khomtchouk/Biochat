TITLE
4NQO treatment may modulate gene expression in mouse tongue

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Determine whether 4NQO treatment may modulate gene expression in mouse tongue. C57BL/6J mice were given 4NQO (100ug/ml in drink) for 8 weeks; Non-treated control samples were used for comparison.

DESIGN
Negative control; 8-week 4NQO treated

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
