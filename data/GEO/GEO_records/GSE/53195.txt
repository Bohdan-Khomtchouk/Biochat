TITLE
Brisbane Systems Genetics Study

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Brisbane Systems Genetics Study comprises of a total of 862 individuals from 374 families. Families consist of combinations of both MZ and DZ twin pairs, their siblings and for 72 families their parents.

DESIGN
Whole blood for expression profiling was collected directly into PAXgene tubes and total RNA was extracted using the WB gene RNA purification kit. RNA from all samples was run on an Agilent Bioanalyzer to assess quality.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
