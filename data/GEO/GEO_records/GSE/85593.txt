TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of Al stress response genes (Al stress induced genes and repressed genes) in Arabidopsis

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Comparison of gene expression pattern of Al response genes between the two Arabidopsis genotypes, wild type (Columbia-0) and the AvSAMS1-expressing transformant

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Al treated plants vs. untreated plants. Biological replicates: 2 replicates in wild types, 2 transgenic lines.
