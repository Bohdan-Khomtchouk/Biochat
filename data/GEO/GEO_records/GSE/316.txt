TITLE
Sal-DG vs MK-DG

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Sal: Saline. Young male C57BL/6J mice received an injection of saline (5 microL per g body weight). RNA from control mice were extracted 1hr, 2hr, 4hr, and 6hr after MK experimental mice received their last shock treatment. MK: MK-801. Young male C57BL/6J mice received an injection of MK-801 (300 micro-g per g body weight). RNA from control mice were extracted 1hr, 2hr, 4hr, and 6hr after MK experimental mice received their last shock treatment. Keywords: time-course
