TITLE
t(11;18)-positive vs. t(11;18)-negative MALT lymphoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparison of t(11;18)-positive MALT lymphoma to t(11;18)-negative MALT lymphoma, with a special focus on the NF-KB pathway and it's targets

DESIGN
8 t(11;18)-negative and 6 t(11;18)-positive cases of MALT lymphoma, as well as six spleen control samples (1 mix of spleens and 5 singular spleen samples)

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
21273489 , 22058210
