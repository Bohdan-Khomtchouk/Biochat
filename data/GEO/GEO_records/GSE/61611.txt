TITLE
Epigenetic analysis of genomic DNA isolated from whole blood of normal and leukemia individuals

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA methylation profiling of whole blood using Illumina's Infinium HumanMehtylation27 Beadchip array.

DESIGN
The dataset encompasses profiles of 4 normal and 8 leukemia samples individuals. Bisulfite converted DNA from 12 samples were hybridized to the Illumina Infinium HumanMehtylation27 Beadchip
