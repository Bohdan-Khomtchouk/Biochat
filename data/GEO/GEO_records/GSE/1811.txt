TITLE
response to HDI

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
C57BL/6J mouse were exposed to 10 mg/m3 of aerosolic HDI-BT (hexamethylene diisocyanate biuret trimer) and followed at 6h, 18h, and 90h after exposure together with unexposed mice. Whole lungs were collected for RNA (4 mice for each group, RNA pooled). Keywords: time-course
