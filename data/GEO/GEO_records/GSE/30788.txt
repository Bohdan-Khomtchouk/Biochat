TITLE
Validation of an expression signature for lymph node metastasis detection in oral cavity squamous cell carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Validation of a previously established gene expression profile for detection of lymph node metastasis (ArrayExpress E-UMCU-11).

DESIGN
Two cohorts of 94 and 222 primary HNSCC tumors were analyzed against the Universal human reference cell line (UHR)
