TITLE
Response of cauline buds to apical auxin and basal cytokinin treatment

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Identification of genes regulated by apical auxin and basal cytokinin treatment of the nodal stem in cauline buds of Arabidopsis thaliana

DESIGN
Three biological replicates each of mock (ethanol control), apical auxin alone and apical auxin plus basal cytokinin

PLATFORM
GPL198 [ATH1-121501] Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array
