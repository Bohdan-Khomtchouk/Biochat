TITLE
Diabetic vs. Menopausal/diabetic kidney cortex

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Gene expression in kidney cortex of diabetic mice and menopausal diabetic mice following 6 weeks of diabetes.  Diabetes was induced with low-dose streptozotocin injections and menopause was induced by injection of 4-vinylcyclohexene diepoxide.

DESIGN
Samples = 12

PLATFORM
GPL3004 NIA Mouse 15K Custom Array
