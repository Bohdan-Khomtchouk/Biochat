TITLE
DDX20 knockdown effect on hepatocellular carcinoma cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of DDX20 knockdown - hepatocellular carcinoma cells. The expression levels of genes driven by NF-kB and related with carcinogenesis, were significantly enhanced in DDX20-knockdown cells.

DESIGN
