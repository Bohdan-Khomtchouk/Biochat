TITLE
effect of types of olive oil on hepatic gene expression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Two isocaloric, isonitrogenous diets containing 10% (w/w) olive oil or unsaponifiable-enriched olive oil have been provided to male apo E knockout mice for 11 weeks. Then RNA was isolated and  hepatic gene expression analyzed. Keywords: other
