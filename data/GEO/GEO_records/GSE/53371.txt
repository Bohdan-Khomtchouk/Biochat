TITLE
Neuroblastoma: MYCN normal vs. MYCN amplified

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To evaluate the expression of genes associated with MYCN in NB, 10 tumors with MYCN amplification and 10 with normal MYCN copy number were subjected to oligonucleotide microarray using Agilent oligo microarray chips.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, MYCN normal vs. MYCN amplified 10 NB patient tissues for each group.

PLATFORM
GPL887 Agilent-012097 Human 1A Microarray (V2) G4110B (Feature Number version)
