TITLE
Rhinovirus infection of polarized human macrophages induces differential gene expression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of differentially polarized human macrophages in response to Rhniovirus A16 treatment

DESIGN
This study was designed to compare total RNA between unpolarized, M1 polarized and M2 polarized human macrophages in response to Rhinovirus A16 treatment

PLATFORM
GPL17692 [HuGene-2_1-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 2.1 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
