TITLE
Profiling muscle from aged rat model for sarcopenia

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
This program aims at identifying a muscle gene signature associated with aging in rat sarcopenia model

DESIGN
The profiling data was analyzed by identifying genes that were up- and down-regulated at selected p value and fold change in the muscle of aged rats compared to the young controls.

PLATFORM
GPL15572 Rosetta/Merck Rat RSTA Custom Affymetrix 1.0 microarray
