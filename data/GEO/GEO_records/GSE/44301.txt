TITLE
RNA-mediated paternal heredity of diet-induced obesity and type II diabetes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Gene expression analysis of testis RNA obtained from mice fed on High Fat and Standard diets. The aim of the study is to identify differentially expressed transcript in testis of the obese males which might be involved in the RNA-mediated paternal heredity of diet-induced obesity and typeII diabete

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from testis of mice fed on High Fat and standard diets

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
