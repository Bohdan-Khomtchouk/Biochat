SUMMARY
The EAGLE (Environmental and Genetic Lung Cancer Etiology) gene expression study is case-control study of lung cancer conducted in Milan, Italy, designed to identify molecular alteration, particularly gene expression variation induced by smoking in lung carcinoma in this data set. The study is initiated by the Division of Cancer Epidemiology and Genetics (DCEG).
