TITLE
AltitudeOmics methylation data

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Acclimatization to high altitude over 21 days, and upon re-ascent 7 and 21 days post-descent. See Subudhi et al. (2014) PLOS ONE 9(3):e92191, PMCID PMC3962396 for experiment details.

DESIGN
107 total samples representing 21 subjects at 7 time points (not all subjects represented at all time points) were taken, plus one sample not used for quality control reasons.  Significance was determined by change vs. baseline (sea level).

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
