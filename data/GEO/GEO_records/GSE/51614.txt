TITLE
Transcriptome of oviducts from non-hormonally stimulated mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA was prepared from the oviducts of CD-1 female mice. Data sets were from d21 (sexually immature) and sexually mature naturally cycling females in either estrus or diestrus phase of the cycle

DESIGN
9 samples

PLATFORM
GPL6246 [MoGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
