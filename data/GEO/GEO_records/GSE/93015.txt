TITLE
Time course RNA-seq experimant invesitgating two isolates of Puccinia graminis infecting a common wheat host.

ORGANISM
Triticum aestivum

SUMMARY
We report a time series RNA-seq experiment involving two isolates of Puccinia graminis separately infecting Triticum aestivum cv. Morocco points post inoculation

DESIGN
3 biological replicates of infected leaf tissue were collected from 5 time points post inoculation plus a mock inoculation as a control
