TITLE
Mouse embryonic heart transcriptomic response to Ddc (Ddc_exon1a isoform) ablation

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression in mouse E15.5 embryonic heart tissue, either wildtype, Ddc-/+ (maternal transmission of Ddc^Gt(OST129277)Lex knockout allele), Ddc+/- (paternal transmission), or Ddc-/-.

DESIGN
A single experiment involving 12 samples: 3x wildtype, 4x Ddc-/+ (MatKO), 4x Ddc+/- (PatKO), 1x Ddc-/- (KO) ---------------------------------------------------------- NIHR BRC Genomics Facility at Guy's Hospital

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
