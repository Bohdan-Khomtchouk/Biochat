TITLE
Murine Spleen

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This project investigates immune response differences in male and female during puberty. Keywords: Time-Series

DESIGN
The specific aim is to expression profile of mouse male and femail spleens at pre-puberty (3-4 week old), puberty (6-9 week old), and post-puberty (24-28 week old).

PLATFORM
GPL81 [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
