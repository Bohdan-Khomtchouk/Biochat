TITLE
Evaluation of animal models of AD

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study was designed to measure expressional profile of genes from brain tissues of animal models of AD.

DESIGN
Expressions of genes in the brain was measured from diverse models of AD. Total RNA was isolated and was subject to single channel microarray.

PLATFORM
GPL11202 Agilent-026655 Whole Mouse Genome Microarray 4x44K v2 (Probe Name version)
