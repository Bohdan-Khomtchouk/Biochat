TITLE
Metabolic syndrome exercise training

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We assessed vastus lateralis muscle gene expression levels of 6 women with the metabolic syndrome before and after a 6 month exercise training program

DESIGN
RNA was isolated from a vastus lateralis muscle biopsy of 12 women with the metabolic syndrome before and after a 6 month guided endurance exercise training program

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
