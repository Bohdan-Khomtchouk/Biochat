TITLE
Calcium treatment can delay tomato (Solanum lycopersicum L.) fruit senescence

ORGANISM
Solanum lycopersicum

SUMMARY
In the present study, we demonstrated that application of CaCl2 to ‘Micro Tom’ tomato fruit (mature green stage) delayed fruit senescence and mature.

DESIGN
Examination of Ca2+ and water injected tomato fruit

PLATFORM
GPL16345 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Solanum lycopersicum)
