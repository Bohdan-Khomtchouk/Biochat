TITLE
Gated SA effect on transcriptome

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
We used microarrays to identify genes that respond to SA differently during the subjective morning and subjective night.

DESIGN
23-day-old wild type Arabidopsis seedlings. 24 samples total. Three biological replicates were used

PLATFORM
GPL198 [ATH1-121501] Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array
