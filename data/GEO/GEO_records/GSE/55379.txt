TITLE
Analysis of global gene expression in pluripotent stem cell (PSC) derived neural stem cells (NSCs) and neurons and astrocytes differentiated from these NSCs

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression was compared between two control PSC lines and neurons and astrocytes differentiated from each line.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from fetal astrocytes from two sources compared to total RNA obtained from two neuronal stem cell lines.
