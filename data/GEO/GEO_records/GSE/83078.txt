TITLE
Pseudomonas aeruginosa PAO1 response to sphingomyelin

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa

SUMMARY
Analysis of Pseudomonas aeruginosa PAO1 treated with 200 µM sphingomyelin. Results provide insight into the response to sphingomyelin in P. aeruginosa.

DESIGN
P. aeruginosa PAO1 was cultured in the presence and absence of sphingomyelin. The orginal culture was divided into two parts. One was treated with 200 μM sphingomyelin and the other served as a control. The experiment was performed in tripricates.
