TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of Spinocerebellar Ataxia 17  Aug

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis of extracted total RNA from the the cerebellum and muscle of Spinocerebellar Ataxia 17 knock-in mouse model

DESIGN
We analyzed mRNA from 3 wild type mice and 3 SCA17 KI mice (both cerebellum and TA muscle) using the Gene Expression 8 Affymetrix Mouse Gene 1.0ST

PLATFORM
GPL6246 [MoGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
