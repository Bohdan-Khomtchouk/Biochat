TITLE
RNA-SEQ profiling of dopaminergic neurons from the mid-brain of mice that received intraperitoneal injections of MPTP

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA-SEQ of dopaminergic neurons from the mid-brain of mice that received one daily intraperitoneal injection of MPTP-HCl (30 mg/kg free base per day) or saline for five consecutive days. Samples were taken 4 days.

DESIGN
Murine midbrain dopaminergic neurons that were treated with MPTP-HCl

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
