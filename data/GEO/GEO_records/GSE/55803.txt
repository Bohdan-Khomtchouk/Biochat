TITLE
Rat hypothalamus: Methimazole (MMI)-saline vs MMI-T3

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Sprague Dawley rats rendered hypothyroid with 22 days of MMI treatment with half the receiving T3 injection on day 21. Rats killed 24h later and hypothalamic tissue recovered.

DESIGN
Two condition experiment MMI vs MMI-T3  hypothalami 4 replicates per treatment
