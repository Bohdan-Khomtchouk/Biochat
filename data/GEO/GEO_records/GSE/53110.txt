TITLE
Gene expression in thymic epithelial cells [RNA-seq]

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study set out to assay the (polyA+) transcriptomes of specific FACS sorted populations of mouse thymic epithelial cells (TEC).

DESIGN
Two biological replicates of each of seven murine TEC populations were FACS sorted and sequenced.

PLATFORM
GPL11002 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Mus musculus)
