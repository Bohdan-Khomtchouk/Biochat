TITLE
Gene expression profiling of dendritic cells (DCs) in antibiotic-treated mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression in DCs from peripheral lymph node (LN) of antibiotic-treated mice

DESIGN
Total RNA were isolated from DCs of mice treated with broad-spectrum antibiotics for 10 days. DCs were isolated and FACS-sorted before RNA isolation
