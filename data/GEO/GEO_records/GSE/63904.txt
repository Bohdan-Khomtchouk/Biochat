TITLE
Bovine granulosa cells of dominant follicles; comparison of growing vs plateau

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Granulosa cells originating from two different phases of antral follicle growth (growing and plateau) were compared in follicles with a diameter of 9mm or more. Growing follicle is the reference.

DESIGN
Two conditions experiment (Growing and Plateau); Four biological replicates for each group; Two technical replicates per samples (dye-swap).
