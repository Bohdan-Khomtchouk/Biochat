TITLE
B. thetaiotaomicron before and after lactose addition to tryptone media 2

ORGANISM
Bacteroides thetaiotaomicron

SUMMARY
B. thetaiotaomicron was grown in a batch culture fermenter containing tryptone media. Lactose (final concentration 0.2%) was added at OD600 = 0.3. Samples were taken immediately before and 60 minutes after addition. Keywords: time course
