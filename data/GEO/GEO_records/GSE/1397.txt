TITLE
Trisomy 21 and TS13

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression was measured in trisomy 21 and trisomy 13 human fetal samples. For TS21, regions assayed were cerebrum, cerebellum, heart, and cerebrum-derived astrocyte cell lines.
