TITLE
Microbial communities in the gut of the beetle Odontotaenius disjunctus

ORGANISM
beetle metagenome

SUMMARY
16S amplicon pool analyses of the four gut sections of the wood-feeding beetle, Odontotaenius disjunctus The beetle is purely wood feeding, and we aim to first characterize the community that exist within the gut sections

DESIGN
4 beetles, four gut sections per beetle, one PhyloChip per gut section, total = 16 chips

PLATFORM
GPL15537 [PhyloChip] Affymetrix G2 PhyloChip
