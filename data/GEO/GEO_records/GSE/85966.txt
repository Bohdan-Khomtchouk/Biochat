TITLE
CCAR2 is required for proliferation and tumor maintenance in human Squamous Cell Carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study was designed to elucidate the role of CCAR2 in human Squamous Cell Carcinoma Cells.

DESIGN
Total RNA from human Squamous Cell Carcinoma cell lines (SCC-13, HO1N1,and SCC-15) was isolated 48 hours after lentiviral infection with either control shRNA or shRNA directed against CCAR2
