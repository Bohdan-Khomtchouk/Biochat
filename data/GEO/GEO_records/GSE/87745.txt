TITLE
IMA1 OE #7-4 line compared to EYFP OE line (control)

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
RNAseq transcriptome of IRON MAN1 OE line. IMA1 OE plants accumulate Fe in their tissue and exhibit a constitutive induction of their root Fe uptake system.

DESIGN
Control and Fe-deficient IMA1 OE leaves and root compared to EYFP OE (control plants) grown under control conditions
