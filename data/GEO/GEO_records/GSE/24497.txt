TITLE
ER stress impairs the insulin signaling pathway through mitochondrial damage in SH-SY5Y human neuroblastoma cells (part 1)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To investigate thapsigargin or tunicamycin-induced transcriptomes in SH-SY5Y cells

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated from SH-SY5Y cells were treated with control vehicle (DMSO), thapsigargin (thap) or tunicamycin (tuni).
