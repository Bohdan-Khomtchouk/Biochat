TITLE
Analysis of RNA from liver tumors in SRSF3 hepatocyte knockout mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA from three hepatocellular carcinomas from 24-month-old SRSF3HKO mice compared to RNA from non-tumorous liver from 24-month-old SRSF3HKO mice for changes in exon utilization and gene expression.

DESIGN
Liver tumor vs. normal liver, three replicates each.

PLATFORM
GPL6096 [MoEx-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Exon 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
