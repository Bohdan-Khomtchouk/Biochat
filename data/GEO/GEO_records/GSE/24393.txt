TITLE
Effect of earthworms on Arabidopsis transcriptome

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
au07-05_groundworms - groundworms - Do earthworms affect plant growth through signal molecules? - Plants were grown in a sandy soil from the Centre de Recherche en Ecologie Expérimentale et Prédictive (CEREEP, France), in the presence or absence of earthworms. Soil was dried and sieved at 2 mm before to be put in the pots. There was 2 levels of treatment (presence or absence of earthworms in the soil), and 5 plants per treatments. Keywords: treated vs untreated comparison

DESIGN
4 dye-swap - CATMA arrays
