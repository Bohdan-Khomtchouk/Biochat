TITLE
Array CGH analysis of single spheroids from glioblastoma spheroid cultures

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to investigate genomic aberrations of glioblastoma spheroid cultures. Keywords: Comparative genomic hybridization

DESIGN
We performed array comparative genomic hybridization of spheroid cultures derived from 2 glioblastoma spheroid cultures using as a reference DNA from healthy donors.
