TITLE
The budding yeast Centromere DNA Element II wraps a stable Cse4 hemisome in either orientation in vivo

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
High resolution ChIP-seq mapping, supported by in vitro reconstitution studies, suggests that the Cse4 nucleosome is a hemisome that occupies the ~80-bp AT-rich CDEII sequence.

DESIGN
H4S47C-anchored cleavage mapping of S. cerevisiae centromeres

PLATFORM
GPL17342 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Saccharomyces cerevisiae)
