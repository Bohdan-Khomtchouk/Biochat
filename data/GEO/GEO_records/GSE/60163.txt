TITLE
mRNA sequencing of small intestinal tissue of germfree mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
With this study we wanted to evaluate the impact of murine norovirus infection of germfree mice and to compare it to germfree mice which have received fecal transplants of conventional mice.

DESIGN
whole small intestinal tissue analysis of 3 germfree, 3 germfree mice infected with murine norovirus and 3 conventionalized germfree mice
