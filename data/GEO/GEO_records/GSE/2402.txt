TITLE
Aquaporin-1 Knockout vs Wild type Mice (Re-spot found)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This dataset was derived (re-spot found) from the original images used in GSE1298. This experiment compared aRNA derived from the kidney inner medula of three mice lacking a functional aquaporin-1 gene to three wild type mice. Each sample was hybridized 4 times, against 4 of the other 5 samples, using an interwoven loop experimental design. Keywords: repeat sample
