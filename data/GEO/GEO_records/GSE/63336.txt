TITLE
Enterohemorrhagic Escherichia coli (EHEC) deletions of glmY and glmZ

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
Transcriptional analysis of the effects of the deletion of the sRNAs glmY and glmZ in EHEC

DESIGN
Wt, ΔglmY, and ΔglmZ strains were grown for 6 hours in low glucose DMEM under static conditions and then processed according to the manufacters instructions.
