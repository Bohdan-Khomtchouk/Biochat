TITLE
Keloid tissue: active keloid lesion vs. adjacent normal skin tissue

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of active keloid lesion and adjacent control normal skin tissue from human. Each control and lesion sample was derived from a matched individual.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, active keloid lesion vs. adjacent control normal skin tissue. each condition was available in seven biological replicates.
