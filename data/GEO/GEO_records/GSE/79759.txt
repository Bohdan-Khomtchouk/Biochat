TITLE
Human embryonic stem cells vs. fibroblast-derived iPS cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression analysis by microarray between human ES cells and fibroblast derived iPS cells established by measles virus vector

DESIGN
Total RNA derived from ES cells and iPS cells were extracted and analyzed for expression changes using oligomieroarray.
