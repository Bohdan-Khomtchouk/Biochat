TITLE
Laughter specific genes and laughter sensitive genes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The changes of mRNA expression levels were analyzed in peripheral leukocytes after laughing with or without " Effective laugh maker " training. Keywords: stress response

DESIGN
Relative changes in gene expression accompanied with laughter before/after laughing training. Over-expressed and under-expressed genes were discriminated in " Effective laugh maker " training responder group.
