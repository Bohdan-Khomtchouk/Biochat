TITLE
Global transcriptome analysis of gene expression changes in Mesorhizobium alhagi CCNWXJ12-2 under salt stress

ORGANISM
Mesorhizobium alhagi CCNWXJ12-2

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression changes of Mesorhizobium alhagi CCNWXJ12-2 under high salt stress. Mesorhizobium alhagi CCNWXJ12-2 is isolated  from Alhagi sparsifolia in northwest of China.

DESIGN
Total RNA extracted from Mesorhizobium alhagi CCNWXJ12-2 growing in TY medium containing 0.4 M NaCl and 0 M NaCl.

PLATFORM
GPL18650 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mesorhizobium alhagi CCNWXJ12-2)
