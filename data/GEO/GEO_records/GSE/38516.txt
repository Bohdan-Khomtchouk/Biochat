TITLE
HT-29 cells treated with IFN-γ

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression data from HT-29 human colon adenocarcinoma cells treated with IFN-γ for 24 hr

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated from HT-29 cells after 24h stimulation with 200 U ml-1 IFN-γ (Roche). The experiment was done on three biological replicates.
