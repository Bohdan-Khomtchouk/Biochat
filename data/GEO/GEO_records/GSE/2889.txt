TITLE
Gene Expression Profiling of 17 beta-Estradiol and Genistein Effects on Mouse Thymus

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Compared the effects of Estrogen and its analog Genistein on mouse thymus. Endogenous estrogen were minimized by removal of ovaries. Samples were collected at 4 time points: 0 days, 2 days, 3 days and 6 days from the start of the treatment. Two biological replicates at each time point. Keywords: comparison of hormone and its analog; Time course
