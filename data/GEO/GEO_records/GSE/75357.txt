TITLE
PS2APP whole tissue RNAseq

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA was purified from intact cerebrocortical tissue of female PS2APP or non-transgenic mice, perfused at 7 or 13 months of age. The " SAMPLE_ID " sample characteristic is a sample identifier internal to Genentech. The ID of this project in Genentech's ExpressionPlot database is PRJ0007648

DESIGN
RNA from whole cortex of female PS2APP or non-transgenic mice at 7 or 13 months.
