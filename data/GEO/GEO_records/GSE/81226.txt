TITLE
Acute p53 knock-out in mouse liver

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Here we investigated the effects of acute p53 deletion on the fasting response in mice.

DESIGN
p53 was depleted using p53 fl/fl mice with Adeno-Cre injection. Adeno-GFP was used as control. Before sacrificing, mice were fasted for 24 hours or fed ad-libitum.
