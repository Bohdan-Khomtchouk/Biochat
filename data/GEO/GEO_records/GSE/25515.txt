TITLE
Effect of Lira treatment on heart in obese mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
C57BL/6 Male mice were treated with high fat diet at the age of 8 weeks and continue the diet for 16 weeks(4 mo) and then injected them with placebo/drug for 1  week before scarifice. Their were sacrifised at the age of 6 months. Drug: Lira is used to reduce morbidity and mortality due to obesity and its effect on heart.

DESIGN
10 C57BL/6 Male mice under high fat diet for 16 weeks treated with eithe Placebo/drug were chosen for the microarray analysis.  Heart from each mouse was disected and the left ventricle was used for TRNA extraction.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
