TITLE
RNA-seq analysis of Retinoic Acid and Non-treated Control hiPSCs

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this experiment is to identify down stream gene networks differentially regulated upon treatment of retinoic acid in hiPSC derived cardiomyocytes after 30 days of differentiation

DESIGN
Examination of 3 RA treated and 3 CT hiPSCs after 30 days of differentiation

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
