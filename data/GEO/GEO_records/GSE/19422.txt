TITLE
Gene expression profiling of pheochromocytoma (PCC)/paraganglioma (PGL) tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional analysis of 84 primary pheochromocytoma (PCC)/paraganglioma tumors.

DESIGN
84 samples (primary pheochromocytoma (PCC)/paraganglioma tumors) were hybridized onto a cDNA microarray in order to investigate possible heterogeneity within these tumors
