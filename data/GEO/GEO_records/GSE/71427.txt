TITLE
Adamts1 Adipocyte Progenitor Cell (APC) expression profile

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression profiling comparing primary adipocyte progenitor cells from Adamts1 transgenic mice to wild-type littermates

DESIGN
FACS isolated adipocyte progenitor cells from male mice at 4 weeks of age from the subcutaneous adipose depots of Adamts1 transgenic mice and wild-type controls
