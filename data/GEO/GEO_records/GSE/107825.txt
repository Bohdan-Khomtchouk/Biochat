TITLE
RNA-seq study comparing gene expression of wild-type and B. abortus ∆baaR deletion strain

ORGANISM
Brucella abortus

SUMMARY
Comparison of the transcriptome profiles of wild-type Brucella abortus and a strain deleted for bab2_0215 (baaR), an IclR-family transcriptional regulator

DESIGN
3 samples for each strain (WT and ∆baaR) were analyzed

PLATFORM
GPL24353 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Brucella abortus)
