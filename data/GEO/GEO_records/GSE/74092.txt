TITLE
TB disease classification

ORGANISM

SUMMARY
Validation of gene expression levels to assess classification of TB patients and healthy controls

DESIGN
qPCR gene expression profiling.  Whole blood gene expression from TB patients (positive in GenXpert assay) and healthy controls; both tuberculin skin test positive (TSTpos) and -negative (TSTneg).
