TITLE
Microarray analysis in the zebrafish liver and telencephalon after exposure to low concentration of 17a-EE2

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Experiment exposed adult male zebrafish to 10ng/L 17-alpha ethinylestradiol for three weeks. Gene expression profiling of liver and telencephalon following exposure. Keywords: estrogenic exposure

DESIGN
4 arrays per group: Liver = slides 1, 2, 3, 4 (slides 3 and 4 are dye swaps) Telencephalon = slides 5, 6, 7, 8 (slides 7 and 8 are dye swaps)
