TITLE
Plasmodium falciparum: hyperoxic vs normoxic conditions

ORGANISM
Plasmodium falciparum

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of P. falciparum comparing parasites exposed to 5% and 21% oxygen environmental conditions  in the late ring stage.

DESIGN
Two experimental conditions: 5% vs 21%. Biological replicates: 3 normoxia and 3 hyperoxia replicates
