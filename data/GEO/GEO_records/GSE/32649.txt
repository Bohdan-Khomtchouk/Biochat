TITLE
SNP data from 125 hepatocellular carcinomas and 112 paired normal samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
SNP array data from 125 hepatocellular carcinomas were used to detect recurrent copy number alterations.

DESIGN
99 hepatocellular carcinomas and 86 matched normal samples were analyzed with Illumina HumanCNV370-Duo v1.0 chips. 26 hepatocellular carcinomas and 26 matched normal samples were analyzed with Illumina HumanOmniExpress BeadChip.
