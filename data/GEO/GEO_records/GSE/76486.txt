TITLE
MBNL1 HITS-CLIP in MDA-MB-231 breast cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
HITS-CLIP was performed to identify endogenous MBNL1 binding sites on RNAs in MDA-MB-231 breast cancer cells.

DESIGN
A previously published method was used to perform HITS-CLIP for endogenous MBNL1 in MDA-MB-231 breast cancer cells (Moore et al. 2014. Nat Protoc. 9:263-293.)
