TITLE
ChIP-chip analysis of RNAP and Sigma factor binding to the Salmonella enterica serovar Typhimurium chromosome

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium

SUMMARY
ChIP-chip analysis of RNA Polymerase (RNAP), RpoD, RpoE, RpoH and RpoN in exponential phase (OD 0.2) and early stationary phase (OD 2.0) Salmonella enterica serovar Typhimurium SL1344 cultures

DESIGN
Analysis of RNA Polymerase and Sigma factor binding in Salmonella Typhimurium SL1344 cultures grown in Lennox Broth (LB).

PLATFORM
GPL10008 Oxford Gene Technology Chip2 Salmonella typhimurium microarray SL1344 strain product code: 010021
