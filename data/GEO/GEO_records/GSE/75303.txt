TITLE
Transcriptome Analysis Of Human Rett Syndrome Brain

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis of postmortem brain samples frrom frontal and temporal cortex of Rett Syndrome cases and matched controls. These data identify genes differentially expressed in postmortem brain tissue from Rett Syndrome cases.

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from 100mg of postmortem brain tissue.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
