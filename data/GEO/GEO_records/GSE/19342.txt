TITLE
Adrenal gland gene expression from adult (12-week old) hypertensive BPH and adult (12-week old) hypotensive BPL mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The objective of the experiment is to determine the genes differentially expressed in the adrenal gland of adult (12-week) BPH and BPL mice.

DESIGN
Adrenal glands from adult (12-week) hypertensive BPH mice (n=3) and adult (12-week) hypotensive BPL mice (n=3).
