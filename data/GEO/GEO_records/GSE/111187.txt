TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of Sulfolobus islandicus E233S1 and ∆tfb3 upon NQO treatment

ORGANISM
Sulfolobus islandicus REY15A

SUMMARY
Transcriptome sequencing was carried out with an Illumina HiSeq platform to investigate the differently expressed genes in wide type strain E233S1 and tfb3 gene deletion mutant ∆tfb3 with or without 2 µM NQO treatment for 6 hours.

DESIGN
Examination of  different gene expression in E233S1 and ∆tfb3  with or without NQO treatment

PLATFORM
GPL24667 Illumina HiSeq 4000 (Sulfolobus islandicus REY15A)
