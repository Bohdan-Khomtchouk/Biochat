TITLE
Gene expression profile of myeloma cells treated with IGF-1

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Objective of this study was to find changes in gene expression of mouse multiple myeloma cells upon treatment with IGF-1

DESIGN
Murine multiple myeloma cells were incubated in serum free medium for 12 hours in the absence (3 replicates) or presence (3 replicates) of 100 ng/ml IGF-1

PLATFORM
GPL339 [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
