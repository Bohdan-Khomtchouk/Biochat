TITLE
DIP-chip:  Rapid and accurate determination of DNA-binding specificity

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Validation of DNA ImmunoPrecipitation with microarray analysis (DIP-chip) technique using the Leu3 DNA binding domain. Keywords: DIP-chip, Leu3

DESIGN
Detected in vitro Leu3 DBD binding with 4 replicates at 40nM Leu3 DBD.  Detected in vitro Leu3 DBD binding with 3 replicates at 4nM Leu3 DBD.  Determined in vivo binding of Leu3DB with 5 ChIP-chip replicates.  Determined in vivo binding of Leu3AD with 7 ChIP-chip replicates
