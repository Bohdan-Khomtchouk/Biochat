TITLE
Next Generation Sequencing Facilitates Quantitative Analysis of  Bone Marrow Macrophages and Spleenic Macrophages Transcriptomes in mouse T cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The goals of this study aim to reveal functional and phenotypic diversity of leukemia-associated macrophages in response to the microenvironmental cues in mouse T cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia

DESIGN
Compare Transcriptomes of macrophages in T cell acute leukemia which are suggested as leukemia-associated macrophages (LAMs) with homeostasis
