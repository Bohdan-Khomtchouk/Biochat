TITLE
Pseudomonas aeruginosa gene expression in artificial sputum medium using a broad capture array designated the PANarray

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa UCBPP-PA14

SUMMARY
The PANarray design (GPL13324) contains the genes of eight P. aeruginosa genomes in non-redundant format, thus allowing identification of expression of non-PAO1 and other P. aeruginosa genes.
