TITLE
modENCODE RNA-Seq of Drosophila cell lines Kc167, CME_W1_Cl.8+, S2-DRSC and ML-DmBG3-c2

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Deep Sequencing of mRNA from the Drosophila melanogaster cell lines Kc167, CME_W1_Cl.8+, S2-DRSC and ML-DmBG3-c2. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODEDataReleasePolicyFinal2008.pdf

DESIGN
Analysis of poly(A)+ RNA from 2 biological replicates of Kc167, CME_W1_Cl.8+, S2-DRSC and ML-DmBG3-c2 cell lines.

PLATFORM
GPL9061 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Drosophila melanogaster)
