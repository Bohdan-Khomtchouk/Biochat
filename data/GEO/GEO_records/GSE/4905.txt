TITLE
Suz12 target promoters in Ntera, MCF7 and SW480 cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
For each sample a matched total was produced in parallel.  Amplicons were made from each and hybridized on human 5.0 kb promoter arrays from Nimblegen. Keywords: ChIP-chip

DESIGN
Goal was to determine Suz12 target promoters in Ntera, MCF7 and SW480 cells using ChIP-chip methodology.

PLATFORM
GPL3791 Nimblegen Human 5K promoter array 2
