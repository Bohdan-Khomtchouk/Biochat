TITLE
total RNA-seq from K562 (ENCSR000CPY)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from the chromatin fraction of independent growths of cell line K562. They are stranded PE76 Illumina GAIIx RNA-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
