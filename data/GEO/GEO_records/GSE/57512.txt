TITLE
Identification of TLR3 regulated genes in CD8 positive Dendritic cell line

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
CD8 positive dendritic cell line , stimulated with or without TLR3 ligand polyI:C We used microarray to obtain global gene expression data of TLR3 regulated genes

DESIGN
CD8 positive dendritic cell line , stimulated with or without TLR3 ligand polyI:C 5ug/ml  for 0h (ctrl), 2h, 6h or 18h in biological triplicates

PLATFORM
GPL6246 [MoGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
