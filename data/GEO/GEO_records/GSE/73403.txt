TITLE
Sixty-nine samples obtained from surgical excision with overall survival.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To further development of our gene expression signature for survival prediction, we conducted expression profiles of lung squamous carcinoma samples with overall survival information as the training cohort.

DESIGN
Sixty-nine LSQC samples with OS information were obtained from patients at the Cancer Institute and Hospital, Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences
