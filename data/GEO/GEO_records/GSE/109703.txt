TITLE
Expression of Adipocyte/Macrophage Fatty Acid Binding Protein in Tumor Associated Macrophages Promotes Breast Cancer Progression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Identification of A-FABP as a new functional marker for pro-tumor associated macrophages.

DESIGN
Using tumor/macrophages co-culutre experiments to investigate the molecular mechanisms by which A-FABP expression in macrophages promotes tumor progression.

PLATFORM
