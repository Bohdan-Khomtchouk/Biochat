TITLE
RNAseq of xylem tissue of transgenic and wildtype Populus trichocarpa (NSF Plant Genome Research Program Project 0922391)

ORGANISM
Populus trichocarpa

SUMMARY
We transformed Populus trichocarpa and generated transgenics with knockdown or overexpression of monolignol genes and transcription factors

DESIGN
RNAseq of xylem tissue of transgenic and wildtype Populus trichocarpa. 378 samples.
