TITLE
Gene expression analysis of mouse ear samples treated with chemical allergen

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Identification of chemical allergen inducible genes in mouse skin. Ear samples were isolated from CL57BL/6 mice 6 hours after topical application of a prototypic chemical allergen, a skin irritant or vehicle alone.

DESIGN
Total RNA were extracted from ear skin samples treated with a chemical allergen, a skin irritant, or vehicle alone for 6 hours.
