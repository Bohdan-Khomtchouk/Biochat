TITLE
Coordinated regulation of mitochondrial turnover by estrogen related receptor alpha and and thyroid hormone

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis showed that majot mitochondrial pathways are co-regulated by ESRRA and TH together.

DESIGN
Mice received daily intraperitoneal injection of XCT790 (ESRRA inverse agonist; Sigma) for 4 days and L-T3 (liothyronine; Sigma) for last 3 days, and were sacrificed.
