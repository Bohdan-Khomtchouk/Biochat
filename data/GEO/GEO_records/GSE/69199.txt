TITLE
Effect of Prox1 in tibialis anterior skeletal muscle

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We show that Prox1 overexpression induces slow muscle fiber type gene program in fast muscle and activates calcineurin signaling

DESIGN
Three AAV8-Prox1 overexpressing TA muscles were compared to three AAV8-mCherry controls

GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
