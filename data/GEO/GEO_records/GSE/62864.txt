TITLE
Epigenetic marks and factors binding to chromatin in mouse embryonic stem cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The goal is to profile several histone modifications and hnRNP L in untreated or Adriamycin-treated mouse embryonic stem cells.

DESIGN
Fifty million of R1E mES cells were untreated or treated with 0.5 uM Adriamycin for 8 hours. Fixed cells were subject to ChIP-seq assay.
