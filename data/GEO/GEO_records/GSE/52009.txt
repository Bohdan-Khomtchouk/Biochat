TITLE
Whole genome expression profile of 120 human glioma samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed whole genome expression profile for 120 human glioma samples to explore significantly aberrantly expressed genes.

DESIGN
Grade 2, 3 and 4 gliomas are included in our experiment.
