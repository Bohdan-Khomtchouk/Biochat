TITLE
Life cycle analysis of the model organism Rhodopirellula baltica by transcritpome studies

ORGANISM
Rhodopirellula baltica

SUMMARY
Gene expression was monitored throughout a growth curve of R. baltica culture: Cultures were harvested at different time points (44 h, 62 h, 82h, 96 h and 240 h) and compared with each other

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, one time point compared to another for each Sample.

PLATFORM
GPL7654 Max Planck Institute, Whole Genome Array for Rhodopirellula baltica SH1T 7.4K version 1
