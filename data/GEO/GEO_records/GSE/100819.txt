TITLE
Fire salamander gene expression data from field samples in Germany

ORGANISM
Salamandra salamandra

SUMMARY
Goal of the study was to compare gene expression patterns between sites and habitats

DESIGN
Salamandra salamandra tail-tip tissue was collected from different field sites, used for total RNA extraction and analyzed using an Agilent microarray

PLATFORM
GPL23661 Agilent-069636 Salamandra salamandra  Microarray (GeneName version)
