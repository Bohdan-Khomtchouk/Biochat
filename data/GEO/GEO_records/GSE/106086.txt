TITLE
RNA-Seq study of migratory bird under two seasonal states

ORGANISM
Emberiza melanocephala

SUMMARY
We report the mRNA found in hypothalamus and liver tissue of a migratory bird. The aim of this study was to study how the mRNA makeup of tissue varies under different seasonal states.

DESIGN
Study of total mRNA from hypothalamus and liver under two different seasonal states (short day and long day), using 454 pryrosequencing.

PLATFORM
GPL24173 454 GS FLX+ (Emberiza melanocephala)
