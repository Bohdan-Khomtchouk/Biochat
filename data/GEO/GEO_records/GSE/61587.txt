TITLE
Gene expression in normal and tumoral esophageal squamous cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study was designed to identify genes aberrantly expressed in esophageal squamous cell carcinoma (ESCC) cells.

DESIGN
Three esophageal squamous cell carcinoma-derived cell lines and one normal human esophageal squamous cell line were analyzed.

PLATFORM
GPL16699 Agilent-039494 SurePrint G3 Human GE v2 8x60K Microarray 039381 (Feature Number version)
