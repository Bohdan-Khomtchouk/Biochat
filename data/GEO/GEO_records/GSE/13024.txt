TITLE
Halibut Microdiet Series

ORGANISM
Hippoglossus hippoglossus

SUMMARY
Halibut switched to a microdiet and compared to control fed diet at 2, 10, 20 and 30 day post switch. 3 individual controls isolated and RNA pooled for a global control for each time point. Keywords: Diet comparison over a time course, for 1 diet compared to a reference.
