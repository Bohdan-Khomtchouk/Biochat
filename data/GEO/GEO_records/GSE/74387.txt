TITLE
A Comparative Analysis of Gene Expression Profiles During Skin Regeneration in Mus and Acomys

ORGANISM
Acomys cahirinus ; Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Acomys exhibits a blunted immune response to wounding, and shares characteristics with fetal wound healing

DESIGN
We used mouse microarrays to compare gene expression profiles during wound healing between the African spiny mouse (Acomys) and the house mouse (Mus)

PLATFORM
GPL16570 [MoGene-2_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 2.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
