TITLE
Juvenile hormone (JH) response in integuments of silkworm larvae

ORGANISM
Bombyx mori

SUMMARY
Expression changes in silkworm integuments after JH analogue (JHA) methoprene treatment.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiments, namely JH analogue (JHA) methoprene treatment (Test) and acetone treatment (Control). Time course: 12 hours after treatment (Hat), 24 Hat, 36 Hat, and 48 Hat.

PLATFORM
GPL9527 Silkworm 23K oligo array version 2.0
