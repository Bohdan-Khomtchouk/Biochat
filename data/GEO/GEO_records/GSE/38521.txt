TITLE
Microarray analysis of gene expression changes during non-climacteric pineapple fruit ripening

ORGANISM
Ananas comosus

SUMMARY
Pineapple is a non climacteric fruit. This study investigates changes in gene expression between the mature green fruit and mature yellow fruit ripening stages

DESIGN
Pineapple 3 (mature green) and Pineapple 9 (mature yellow) fruit total RNA were labelled with either Cy3 or Cy5 and hybridised to the pineapple microarray.
