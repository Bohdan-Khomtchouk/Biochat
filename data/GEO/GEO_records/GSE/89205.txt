TITLE
RNA-sequencing in TGF-beta treated MDA-231-D cells transfected with ZEB1/ZEB2 siRNAs [RNA-seq]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We searched for roles of ZEB1 during EMT by RNA-seq in breast cancer cells.

DESIGN
Expression of mRNA in a basal type breast cancer cell line MDA-231-D transfected with ZEB1/ZEB2 siRNAs and stimulated with TGF-beta for 24 h.
