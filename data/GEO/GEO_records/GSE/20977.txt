TITLE
Evaluation of the effects of 16p13.11 deletions on gene expression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We analyzed seven epilepsy patients with a heterozygous chr16p13.11 deletion and eight controls with no deletion at this locus to evaluate the impact of the deletion on gene expression.

DESIGN
Seven subjects with a 16p13.11 deletion compared to eight controls with no deletion at this locus, no replicates were performed.
