TITLE
Gene expression data from CD4pos human childhood study

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Reveal differentially regulated genes and cellular pathways within allergic and non-allergic asthmatic children compared to healthy controls

DESIGN
CD4pos cells were obtained from allergic asthmatics, non-allergic asthmatics and healthy controls and kept without stimulation.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
