TITLE
E47 KO versus WT HSCs

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Genome-wide gene expression pattern of E47 KO versus WT HSCs from primary and secondary recipient mice were analysis using Agilent one-color micro-array analysis.

DESIGN
Two replicates from 4 samples are analyzed. The sample from the WT mouse were set as their reference sample.

GPL4134 Agilent-014868 Whole Mouse Genome Microarray 4x44K G4122F (Feature Number version)
