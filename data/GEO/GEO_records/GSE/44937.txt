TITLE
Defining a Th9 transcriptome

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray analyses were performed to compare gene expression in cultured mouse Th9, Th2 and Treg cells and resting versus activated Th9 cells.

DESIGN
Three replicates were analyzed for each culture condition; Th9 unstim, Th2 unstim, Treg unstim, Th9 stim
