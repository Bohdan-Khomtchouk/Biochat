TITLE
Transcriptomic profiles of blood in murine polymicrobial sepsis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional effects in circulating leukocytes from mice challenged to peritoneal contamination and infection (PCI) compared to sham (vehicle control). We gratefully acknowledge the BMBF grant for the Center for Sepsis Control and Care (CSCC) for this study

DESIGN
Time resolved murine PCI challenge groups x four replicates, Sham control x three replicates

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
