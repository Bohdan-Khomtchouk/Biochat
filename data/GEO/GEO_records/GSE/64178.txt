TITLE
Gene expression changes induced by BRD9876 in MM1S cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to identify transcriptional changes induced by a novel compound BRD9876 as a starting point to identify the compound's mechanism of action.

DESIGN
Compound-treated cells are compared in duplicate with cells treated vehicle control (DMSO).

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
