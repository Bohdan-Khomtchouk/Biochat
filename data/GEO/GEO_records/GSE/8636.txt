TITLE
Intestinal xenotransplants infected with Shigella

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
to analyse the transcriptomic response of human intestinal tissue engrafted in SCID mice to Shigella infection Keywords: infection, Shigella, gene expression, intestinal cell

DESIGN
Four experimental conditions with two biological replicates. After 2 hours infection, tissues were removed and ARN were extracted before labeling and hybridizations on U-133A genechip.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
