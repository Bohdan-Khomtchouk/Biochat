TITLE
Tiled Array Experiment of Murine Gammaherpesvirus 68 Transcripts In Newly Infected Fibroblasts

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Murine NIH3T3 fibroblasts were infected with MHV68 and RNA expression was examined over an 18 hour timecourse experiment.

DESIGN
Study includes 8 samples isolated at 0, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, and 18 hours after infection of NIH3T3 fibroblasts with murine gammaherpesvirus 68 at a multiplicity of infection of 10.0.
