TITLE
RNA-Seqs of WT and crwn1 crwn2 double mutant

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
To address the functions of CRWN proteins, we performed RNA-seq of crwn1 crwn2 double mutant and wild-type (WT).

DESIGN
10 days seedling of crwn1 crwn2 double mutant was used with WT as a control

PLATFORM
GPL13222 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Arabidopsis thaliana)
