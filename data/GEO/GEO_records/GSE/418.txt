TITLE
Kc167_Inter-slide_Variability_Test

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
A large batch of Drosophila Kc167 total RNA was prepared and was used for 3 homotypic hybridizations in order to evaluate the inter-slide variability using the CDMC_Drosophila_7k2 platform.  See Neal et al. 2003 for results and discussion. Keywords = Kc167 Keywords = Drosophila Keywords = Platform Variability Keywords: other
