TITLE
Mouse Embryonic Stem Cell E14Tg2A.4 BMP2 Responses

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Study was carried out to investigates the acute responses of mouse ES cells to BMP-2.

DESIGN
E14 cells were treated with BMP-2 at 25ng/ml for 1hour or untreated for the same time. The RNA were extracted and labeled to applied for microaaray.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
