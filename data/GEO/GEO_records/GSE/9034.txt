TITLE
Gene expression profiling in Bclaf1 deficient versus wild type post natal day1 lung

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
In this study we compared the gene expression pattern in Bclaf1-deficient vs wild type lung tissue Keywords: Gene expression profiling

DESIGN
RNA was extracted from dissected post natal day1 Bclaf1-deficient mice. Labeling, hybridization and data acquisition was conducted at the University Health Network microarray centre. http://www.microarrays.ca

PLATFORM
GPL5784 mouse 22.4k cDNA array
