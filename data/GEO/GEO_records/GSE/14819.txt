TITLE
Array CGH analysis of glioblastoma serum grown adherent cultures

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed array comparative genomic hybridization of spheroid cultures derived from 13 glioblastomas. Keywords: comparative genomic hybridization

DESIGN
Comparative genomic hybridization of spheroid cultures derived from glioblastomas using as a reference DNA from healthy donors.

PLATFORM
GPL8175 DKFZ human 8k BAC chip
