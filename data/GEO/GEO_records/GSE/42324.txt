TITLE
Transcriptional Responses to Sleep in Peripheral Tissues (Lung)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Molecular profiles in sleep and sleep deprivation in peripheral tissues using microarrays

DESIGN
Time point study. Mice were sacrificed by cervical dislocaton following 3, 6, 9, and 12 h of total sleep deprivation (n = 8 or 9 at each time point). Deprivation was initiated at lights-on and performed through gentle handling.

PLATFORM
GPL11533 [MoGene-1_1-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 1.1 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
