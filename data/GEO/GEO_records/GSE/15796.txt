TITLE
Spatiotemporal Analysis of Transcriptome in the paraxial mesoderm of zebrafish embryos

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Differentially expressed genes along the paraxial mesoderm of 12 somite stage zebrafish embryos are identified

DESIGN
Tissue samples from 6 different spatial positions were dissected from wild-type (AB) embryos, RNA is amplified and hybridized to arrays

PLATFORM
GPL1319 [Zebrafish] Affymetrix Zebrafish Genome Array
