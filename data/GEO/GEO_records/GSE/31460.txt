TITLE
Changes in gene expression profiles in patients with 5q- syndrome in CD14+ monocytes caused by lenalidomide treatment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We detected changes in genes expression in patients with 5q- syndrome in CD14+ monocytes due to treatment with lenalidomide.

DESIGN
Total RNA was obtained from CD14+ monocytes from peripheral blood of 6 controls, 6 patients before treatment, and 5 patients during treatment.

PLATFORM
GPL6883 Illumina HumanRef-8 v3.0 expression beadchip
