TITLE
Rat muscle tissue group comparisons (skeletal-extraocular, skeletal-hindlimb, smooth-stomach, and cardiac; Porter lab)

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Comparison of normal adult rat extraocular muscle, cardiac muscle, leg (gastrocnemius-soleus) muscle and smooth muscle (stomach wall).  Affymetrix microarray chip RG-U34A was used. MAS version 5 was used to analyze the muscle group differences.  Data form part of publication: FASEB Journal 17: 1370-1372, 2003 (full length article available at ). Keywords: other
