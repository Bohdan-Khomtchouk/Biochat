TITLE
ChIP-Seq sequencing of H3K9me2 in third instar Drosophila larvae

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
This study describes the epigenetic profiling of the H3K9me2 in wt Drosophila larvae, as well as in Drosophila larvae for which the euchromatic catalytic enzyme depositing H3K9me2 (EHMT) is knocked out.

DESIGN
ChIP-Seq profiling of H3K9me2 in wt and EHMT KO third instar Drosophila larvae

PLATFORM
GPL9061 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Drosophila melanogaster)
