TITLE
The Promyelocytic Leukemia Zinc Finger Dependent Transcriptome during Human Endometrial Stromal Cell Decidualization

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNA-sequencing of mRNA isolated from in vitro decidualizaing human endometrial stromal cells with or without siRNA-mediated knockdown of PLZF

DESIGN
Primary human endometrial stromal cells isolated from 3 healthy volunteers. Transfected with nontargeting or PLZF siRNA. Treated with estradiol, medroxyprogesterone acetate, and cAMP (EPC) for 0 or 3 days

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
