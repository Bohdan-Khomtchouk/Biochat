TITLE
Coordinated over-expression of genes in the EGFR pathway predicts sensitivity to EGFR inhibition in pancreatic cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Tumors from pancreatic cancer specimens obtained at surgery were used for efficacy testing and biologic analysis Keywords: Pharmacogenetics

DESIGN
Eighteen tumor samples were profiled in duplicates.  Ten samples were used in the training set and eight samples were used as the validation set.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
