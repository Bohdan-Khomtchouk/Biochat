TITLE
Dietary flaxseed- and radiation-sensitive microRNAs in murine lung

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Murine lung miRNAs were profiled by rodent TaqMan OpenArray after flaxseed feeding and/or radiation exposure

DESIGN
Lung tissue was obtained 48 hours after radiation or no exposure from three animals each: flaxseed/no radiation, flaxseed/radiation, control diet/no radiation, control diet/radiation. miRNA profiles were determined by OpenArray.
