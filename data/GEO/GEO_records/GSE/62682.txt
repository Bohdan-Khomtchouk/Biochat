TITLE
Affymetrix HG-U133A 2.0 Expression data from 6 human cell lines derived from metastasized melanoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Study of global gene expression differences in malignant melanoma cell lines with or without specific abberations in BRAF NRAS or CDKN2A.

DESIGN
Expression .CEL files from Affymetrix HG-U133A 2.0 arrays using total RNA from 6 human cell lines derived from metastasized melanoma
