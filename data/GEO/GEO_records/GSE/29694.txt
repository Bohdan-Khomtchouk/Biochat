TITLE
Compare gene expression patterns in Chsy-1 wt and ko mouse 13.5 days embryo forelimbs

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of 13.5 day mouse embryo forelimbs. Gene expression comparison done between wild type and Chsy-1 gene knockout mice.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, control (wild type) vs. test (Chsy-1 knockout). Biological replicates: 9 control replicates, 13 test replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL4134 Agilent-014868 Whole Mouse Genome Microarray 4x44K G4122F (Feature Number version)
