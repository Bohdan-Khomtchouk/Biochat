TITLE
Whole genome RNA micro array analysis of Plasmodium berghei sporozoites infected host cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
mRNA Transcripts expression profile of Plasmodium berghei infected host HepG2 cells with and without SUMO1 over expression. To check the changes in the transcript profiles upon SUMO1 over expression which is responsible for Plasmodium berghei parasite's growth arrest in host HepG2 cells.

DESIGN
Agilent one-color experiment, Organism: Homo sapiens, Custom Agilent 8x60k Human Whole Genome Microarray Gene expression (AMADID: 039494), Labeling kit: Agilent Quick-Amp labeling Kit (p/n5190-0442).
