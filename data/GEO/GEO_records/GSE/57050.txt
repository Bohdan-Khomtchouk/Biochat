TITLE
miRNA Profile in Normal and Preeclamptic Placenta

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study was to determine the difference of the miRNA profiles between normal and preeclamptic placenta.

DESIGN
Ten placental samples were analyzed.  Six were from preeclamptic patients and four were from normal pregnancies.

PLATFORM
GPL14613 [miRNA-2] Affymetrix Multispecies miRNA-2 Array
