TITLE
ChIP-on-chip analyses of JmjC protein Epe1 in fission yeast

ORGANISM
Schizosaccharomyces pombe

SUMMARY
ChIP-on-chip analyses of JmjC protein Epe1  in fission yeast. Keywords: ChIP-on_chip

DESIGN
ChIP-on-chip analyses using custom Agilent 44K in situ 60mer DNA microarray that tiles the entire Schizosaccharomyces pombe genome every 300 bp alternately on both strands were used to interrogate Swi6/HP1 and Epe1 in fission yeast genome.
