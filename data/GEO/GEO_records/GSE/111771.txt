TITLE
Differentially expressed miRNAs in granulosa cell of diminished ovarian reserve (DOR) patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To investigate the pathogenesis of diminished ovarian reserve, differentially expressed miRNAs in granulosa cells were constructed through miRCURYTM LNA expression Array.

DESIGN
In the study presented here, granulosa cells were collected from 3 DOR patients and 3 normal cycling women, respectively.

PLATFORM
GPL11434 miRCURY LNA microRNA Array, 6th generation - hsa, mmu & rno
