TITLE
Hs 294T Cells Treated with Elesclomol Alone or in Combination with Paclitaxel Compared to DMSO Treated

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We used microarrays to detail gene expression changes in Hs 294T human melanoma cells after treatment with elesclomol alone, or in combination with paclitaxel, to aide in identifing the mechnism of action of elesclomol. Keywords: treatment
