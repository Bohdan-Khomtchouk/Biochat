ORGANISM

SUMMARY
This series represents the samples used to generate the associated gene listing. The samples contained belong to two Platform accessions GPL561 and GPL562. There is a slight redundancy in the gene list due to multiple strong hits for a given gene. Clone selection was based on a fold > = 1.2 with a p-value < = 0.1 in at least 2 probe samples.
