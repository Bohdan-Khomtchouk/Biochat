TITLE
DNA methylation profiles of human prefrontal cortex

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Using post mortem prefrontal cortex material provided by the Harvard Brain Tissue Resource Center at McLean Hospital, DNA methylation data was generated using CHARM.

DESIGN
Genomic DNA was isolated and hybridized to custom-designed Nimblegen microarrays (CHARM human array v1) (n=22)

PLATFORM
GPL9275 NimbleGen Human 2.1M array
