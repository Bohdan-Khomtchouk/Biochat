TITLE
Quantitative Analysis of Wild Type and Tlx3f/f;SNS-cre dorsal root ganglion Transcriptomes by RNAseq

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
purpose: to analyse the differentially expressed genes in dorsal root ganglion between Wild Type and Tlx3f/f;SNS-cre mice

DESIGN
methods: total RNA from dorsal root ganglion of Wild Type and Tlx3f/f;SNS-cre mice were obtained for RNAseq,in triplicate, using Illumina HiSeq4000

GPL21103 Illumina HiSeq 4000 (Mus musculus)
