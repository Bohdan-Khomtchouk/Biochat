TITLE
Hypermethylation in the ZBTB20 gene is associated with major depressive disorder

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide MeDIP-Sequencing of 23 monozygotic twin pairs (n=46) from Australia discordant for major depressive disorder (MDD).

DESIGN
MeDIP-seq of 23 monozygotic twin pairs discordant for major depressive disorder. MZ twin pairs were compared to identify significantly differently methylated sites associated with MDD.
