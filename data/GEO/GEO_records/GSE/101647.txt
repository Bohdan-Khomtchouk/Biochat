TITLE
Transcriptomic analysis of control and Myzocytiopsis humicola-infected C. elegans

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
We used RNA-seq to identify gene expression changes in C. elegans after 12 hrs and 24 hrs of exposure to pathogen Myzocytiopsis humicola

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from whole C. elegans adults after exposure to Myzocytiopsis humicola

PLATFORM
GPL18245 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Caenorhabditis elegans)
