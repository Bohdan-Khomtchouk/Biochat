TITLE
Variation in DNA methylation is not consistently reflected by sociality in Hymenoptera

ORGANISM
Lasioglossum albipes

SUMMARY
We used pooled samples of adult females from 3 species of Hymenoptera to generate single-base resolution DNA methylation maps.

DESIGN
Mapping of 5-methylcytosine in 3 species of Hymenoptera by MethylC-Seq
