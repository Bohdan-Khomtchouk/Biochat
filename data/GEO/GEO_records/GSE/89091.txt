TITLE
The effect of Bcl6 on the liver sex difference

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To confirm the effect of Bcl6 on the liver sex differrence, we performed the gene expression analysis in male and female wile type mice and male and female liver specific Bcl-6 deficient mice.

DESIGN
Male and female wild type mice and  male and female liver specific Bcl-6 deficient mice were dissected. RNA extraction was performed from livers for the microarray analysis.
