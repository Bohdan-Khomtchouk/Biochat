TITLE
PAR-CLIP of RBM4 and HIF-2a

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To identify which mRNAs bind to RBM4/HIF-2a

DESIGN
Two PAR-CLIPs were performed: One of an RBM4 immunoprecipitation, and the other of a HIF-2a immunoprecipitation and excising the associated RBM4 band.
