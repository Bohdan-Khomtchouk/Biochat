TITLE
Effects of two strains of Lactobacillus fermentum on germ free rats

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Transcription profiling of intestinal tissue from germ free rats mono-colonised with one of two strains of Lactobacillus fermentum; AGR1485 or AGR 1487

DESIGN
Two colour microarray, reference design. 2 treatments (AGR1485 or AGR1487) and 2 tissues analysed (colon or duodenum): Biological replicates: 6

PLATFORM
GPL4135 Agilent-014879 Whole Rat Genome Microarray 4x44K G4131F (Feature Number version)
