TITLE
Essential role of IRF4 and MYC signaling for survival of anaplastic large cell lymphoma survival

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Three ALCL cell lines DEL, FEPD and K299 were induced with an IRF4-specific shRNA for up to 96 hours.

DESIGN
The three ALCL cell lines DEL, FEPD and K299 were induced with an IRF4-specific shRNA for four durations and compared with the corresponding not-induced control.
