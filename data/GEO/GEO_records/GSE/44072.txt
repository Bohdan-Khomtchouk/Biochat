TITLE
Genome-wide analysis and comparison of E15 and adult rat lateral ventricular choroid plexus epithelial cells.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis of lateral ventricular choroid plexus epithelial cells of embryonic day 15 (E15) and adult control Sprague-Dawley rats.

DESIGN
We analyzed total RNA extracted from plexus epithelial cells from 6 pooled samples (3 E15, 3 adult) using the Illumina HiSeq 2000 platform.
