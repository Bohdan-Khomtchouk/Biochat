TITLE
Quantitative reverse transcription PCR analysis of human breast tumor cells in the PAM50 genes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human formalin-fixed paraffin-embedded breast tumor tissue (1-4 1.5mm punches) was processed to RNA using High Pure RNA Paraffin Kit (Roche Applied Science, Indianapolis, IN) for quantitative reverse transcription polymerase chain reaction (qRT-PCR).

DESIGN
qRT-PCR expression profiling of PAM50 genes in breast cancer tumors

PLATFORM
GPL24747 Bernard Lab PAM50 qRT-PCR assay
