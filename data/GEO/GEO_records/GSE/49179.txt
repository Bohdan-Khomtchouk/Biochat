TITLE
Comparision of Psip1/p75 binding and Mll1 binding sites on Hox and non-Hox genes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Psip1/p75 binds to Hox genes and colocalizes with Mll1 and in Psip1 KO MEFs Mll1 occupancy is reduced over Hox genes

DESIGN
Psip1/p75 ChIP using A300-848 abtibody (recognises p75 isoform of Psip1) and Mll1 ChIP from WT and Psip1 KO MEFs ChIP-chip

PLATFORM
GPL13276 NimbleGen Custom Mouse Hox Tiling Array
