TITLE
Age-dependent Response of Isolated Male Germ Cells to Oxidative Stress

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Male germ cells from young and aged Rats were Isolated and cultured and then treated with pro-oxidant SIN-1 and antioxidant EUK134 Study of the response of isolated male germ cells from young and aged Brown Norway Rats to oxidative stress.

DESIGN
Treatment of Isolated and cultured germ cells with pro-oxidant and antioxidant.
