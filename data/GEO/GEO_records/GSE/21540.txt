TITLE
An integrative multi-dimensional genetic and epigenetic strategy to identify aberrant genes and pathways in cancer: CGH

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study was undertaken to identify novel tumour suppressor genes in lung adenocarcinoma.  EYA4, located at 6q23.2 was identified as a frequently lost and hypermethylated gene in the analyzed samples.

DESIGN
7 Breast cancer cell lines profiled on the SMRTr array in conjunction with matched DNA methylation data, allelic status and gene expression data for integrative analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL2616 BCCRC Lam SMRTr array
