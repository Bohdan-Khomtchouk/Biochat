TITLE
Differential transcript abundance in BMAL-/- mouse at ZT14

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Investigation of RNA transcript abundance in aortic tissue of BMAL-/- versus BMAL+/+ control mice at ZT14 Keywords: Differential RNA abundance in genetic mutant

DESIGN
One-way ANOVA design with Strain (BMAL-/- versus BMAL+/+) as the independent variable. Control group has five biological replicates, BMAL-/- group has four biological replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
