TITLE
Characterizing the expression of human olfactory receptor genes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We aim, among other things, to characterize olfactory receptor (OR) genes wich are differentially expressed between olfactory epithelium (OE) and other organ tissues, in humans. Keywords: Comparative

DESIGN
Six OE samples and ten non-OE samples, including two samples of the following tissues: liver, kidney, lung, testis, and heart.

PLATFORM
GPL4335 Affymetrix custom made array SFORV2_2
