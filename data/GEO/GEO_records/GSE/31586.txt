TITLE
Breast, colon, glioblastoma, lung, melanoma, ovarian, pancreas, prostate tumors and cell lines.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA copy number profiling for all primary tumor samples and cell lines was performed by ROMA, a form of comparative genomic hybridization.  The aim was to identify commonly amplifiied and deleted regions.

DESIGN
538 primary tumor samples and 253 human cell lines were analyzed.
