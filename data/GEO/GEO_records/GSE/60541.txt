TITLE
Exposure of radiation on mouse lung

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study was designed to identify molecular changes induced by radiation in mouse lung.

DESIGN
Mouse left lung was irradiated with a single dose of radiation. Total RNA from the irradiated lung tissue was subject to single channnel microarray.

PLATFORM
GPL10333 Agilent-026655 Whole Mouse Genome Microarray 4x44K v2 (Feature Number version)
