TITLE
Cotreatment effects of estrogen with progestogens or dihydrotestosterone in the breast cancer cell line T-74D

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Cells were cotreated with estrogen and dihydrotestosterone, progesterone or medroxyprogesterone acetate to assess the combinatorial effects of progestogens or androgens with estrogen in T47D breast cancer cells

DESIGN
Cells were plated in hormone stripped media for 56h, followed by treatment for 16h with 10nM of the nominated hormone(s)
