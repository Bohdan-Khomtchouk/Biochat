TITLE
Comparison of collection strategies for germline analysis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Peripheral blood was collected from 3 patients in two types of tubes, EDTA and Streck. After removing plasma, DNA was extracted from the remaining blood and subjected to array profiling

DESIGN
Three patients, each patient with an EDTA sample and a Streck sample

PLATFORM
GPL21727 Agilent-042300 CGH array G4884A [Feature number version]
