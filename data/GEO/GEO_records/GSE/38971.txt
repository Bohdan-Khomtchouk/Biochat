TITLE
Response to dexamethasone in A549, U2OS, and Nalm6

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of A549, U2OS, and Nalm6 comparing dexamethason with vehicle control, 3 hour timepoint

DESIGN
Two conditions (treated and untreated), three cell types. Biological replicates: 3 treated and 3 vehicle controls for each cell type. Each array is a two-color hyb of treated vs control in a single cell type (9 arrays total). One dye swap for each condition
