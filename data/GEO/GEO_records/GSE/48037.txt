TITLE
RNA methylation-dependent RNA processing controls the speed of the circadian clock

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To investigate the major components of the response to the methylation inhibitor 3-deazadenosine

DESIGN
U2OS cells were plated in 20 cm dishes and grown until confluence. Cells were then treated with 100 microM deazaadenosine or vehicle (PBS) for 24 hours. Three replicate dishes for each treatment were analysed.

GPL10999 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Homo sapiens)
