TITLE
Transcription factor overexpression in HeLa cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Experiments measuring effects of overexpression of the transcription factors  RUNX1 and CBF-beta (in combination), and TELRUNX1 (fusion protein of TEL and RUNX1).  Overexpression is achieved via adenovirus infection of HeLa cell lines. Keywords: other
