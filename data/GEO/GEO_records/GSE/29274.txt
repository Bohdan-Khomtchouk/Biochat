TITLE
phytoplasma in plants vs. phytoplasma in insects (single hyb expts)

ORGANISM
Candidatus Phytoplasma asteris

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of phytoplasma grown in plant (Chrysanthemum coronarium) and grown in insect (Macrosteles striifrons).

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, phytoplasma-infected plant and phytoplasma-infected insect. Biological replicates: 4 phytoplasma-infected plants and 4 phytoplasma-infected insects, independently grown and harvested. One replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL13531 Phytoplasma asteris OY-M DNA array
