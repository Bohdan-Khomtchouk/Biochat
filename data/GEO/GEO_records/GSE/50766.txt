TITLE
Extensive divergence of transcription factor binding in Drosophila embryos with highly conserved gene expression (part 1)

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster ; Drosophila pseudoobscura ; Drosophila virilis ; Drosophila yakuba

SUMMARY
We sequenced mRNA from blastoderm embryos of Drosophila melanogaster, Drosophila yakuba, Drosophila pseudoobscura and Drosophila virilis. Two samples contain pooled mRNA from several species, and the remaining 24 samples contain mRNA from a single species. Methods: Retinal mRNA profiles of Blastoderm embryos

DESIGN
Comparison of the evolution of gene expression and regulatory TF binding in early Drosophila embryos.
