TITLE
Juvenile Rheumatoid Arthritis Profiles In PBMC and SFMC

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Biotinylated cRNA was synthesized from total RNA (Enzo; Farmingdale, NY) and processed according to the Affymetrix GeneChip Expression Analysis Technical Manual (Affymetrix; Santa Clara, CA).  57 samples: 5 pauciarticular PBMC, 15 polyarticular PBMC, 11 control PMBC, 6 JSpA PBMC, 5 pauciarticular SFMC, 10 polyarticular PBMC, 5 JSpA SFMC classified by course. Keywords = juvenile rheumatoid arthritis (JRA) Keywords = peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMC) Keywords = synovial fluid mononuclear cells (SFMC) Keywords: other
