TITLE
The dentate gyrus after traumatic brain injury [mRNA]

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Analysis of the dentate gyrus of traumatic brain injury model. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanism underlying TBI.

DESIGN
This study was designed to estimate changes in gene expression levels after lateral fluid-percussion injury as a model of TBI.

PLATFORM
GPL11534 [RaGene-1_1-st] Affymetrix Rat Gene 1.1 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
