TITLE
Transcriptional profiling of rag1 deficient (rag1-/-) and rag1 heterozygous (rag1+/-) zebrafish kidney and intestine

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Four dual hybridizations of rag1 deficient (rag1-/-) and rag1 heterozygous (rag1+/-) zebrafish cRNAs from kidney and intestine. Keywords: Gene knockout

DESIGN
Four dual hybridizations: 1) Cy3-rag1+/- kidney cRNA and Cy5-rag1-/- kidney cRNA, 2) Cy5-rag1+/- kidney cRNA and Cy3-rag1-/- kidney cRNA, 3) Cy3-rag1+/- intestine cRNA and Cy5-rag1-/- intestine cRNA, and 4) Cy5-rag1+/- intestine cRNA and Cy3-rag1-/- intestine cRNA.
