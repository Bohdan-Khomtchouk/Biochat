TITLE
Rapid changes of gene expression in rat adipose tissue when food was removed

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
When food was removed for 6 hours, 43 genes, including Angptl4, changed their expression more than two-fold.

DESIGN
Food was removed from young rats at 07:30. Six hours later, samples of epididymal adipose tissue was taken for analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL6101 Illumina ratRef-12 v1.0 expression beadchip
