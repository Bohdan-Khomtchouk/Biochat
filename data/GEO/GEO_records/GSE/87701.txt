TITLE
Effects of plasticizers (bisphenol A, bisphenol AF) and an herbicide in MCF7 human breast cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We studied alterations in gene expression profiles of the MCF7 human breast cancer cells caused by bisphenol A, bisphenol AF and glyphosate using Illumina RNA sequencing platform.

DESIGN
Examination of endocrine disrupting effects of xenobiotics using the MCF7 cell line

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
