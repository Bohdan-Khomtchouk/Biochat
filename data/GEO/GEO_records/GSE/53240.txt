TITLE
RNAi silencing of the SoxE gene suppresses cell proliferation in silkworm BmN4 cells

ORGANISM
Bombyx mori

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of silkworm BmN4-SID1 cells comparing the test of  BmSoxE knockdown with the control of EGFP Knockdown.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment,  BmSoxE knockdown vs EGFP Knockdown. Biological replicates: 3. One replicate per array.
