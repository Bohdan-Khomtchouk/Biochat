TITLE
Deinococcus radiodurans R1 Cells was treated with MMC (5 μg/ml) : Control treated with MMC (5 μg/ml) vs. bphPR deletion mutant treated with MMC (5 μg/ml)

ORGANISM
Deinococcus radiodurans R1

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Deinococcus radiodurans comparing control wild type cells with bphPR deletion mutant were treated with MMC (5 μg/ml)

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, R1 MMC (5 μg/ml) vs. bphPR deletion cell MMC (5 μg/ml). Biological replicates: 3 control replicates, 3 bphPR deletion mutant replicates.
