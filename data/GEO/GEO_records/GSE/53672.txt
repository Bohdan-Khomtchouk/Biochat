TITLE
Expression data from cultured human dermal fibroblasts

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We used microarrays to identify the molecular targets of a novel small compound HSc025.

DESIGN
Fibroblasts were exposed to four different stimulants, DMSO, HSc025, TGF-bata or HSc025 plus TGF-beta.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
