TITLE
Comparison of the response to bile of Lactobacillus casei BL23 and its derivative strain TC01

ORGANISM
Lactobacillus paracasei ATCC 334

SUMMARY
The response to  bile of Lactobacillus casei BL23 and its derivative strain TC01 was investigated. TC01 strain carries a complete deletion of gene LCABL_02080 which encodes a two component signal transduction response regulator.
