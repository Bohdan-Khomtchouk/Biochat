TITLE
Gene expression in small intestinal and colonic goblet cells from C57Bl/6 mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of goblet cells isolated from the small intestine or colon was completed to compare the cells between the two parts of the intestine.

DESIGN
Total epithelium was isolated from the intestine by incubation in HBSS+0.05% EDTA at 37 C for 15 minutes. Goblet cells were isolated by FACS sorting CD45-CD24-CK18+UEA-I+ cells.

PLATFORM
GPL6885 Illumina MouseRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
