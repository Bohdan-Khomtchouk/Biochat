TITLE
Chitin Oligomer Experiment

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Using the ATH1 Affymetrix microarrays consisting of about 23,000 genes, we examined the response of Arabidopsis seedlings to chito-tetramers, chito-octamers and hydrolyzed chitin after 30 min of treatment.
