TITLE
B-cell chronic lymphocytic leukemia side population cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of Hoechst dye 33342-effluxing side population (SP) cells from B-CLL peripheral blood mononuclear cells.

DESIGN
9 biological replicates from B-CLL patients sorted into CD5+CD19+ SP and non-SP subsets. Two color comparative gene expression using Agilent microarrays.

PLATFORM
GPL4133 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Feature Number version)
