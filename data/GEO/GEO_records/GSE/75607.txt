TITLE
Effects of perfluoroethylcyclohexanesulfonate (PFECHS)

ORGANISM
Daphnia magna

SUMMARY
Sublethal effects of perfluoroethylcyclohexanesulfonate (PFECHS) on the gene expression and protein activity of Daphnia magna Reference sample is a composite of equal amounts of total RNA (500 ng each) from n=3 culture media control

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment:  control (culture media) and three sublethal doses of PFECHS (0.06 mg/L, 0.6 mg/L, 6 mg/L). Biological replicates:  3 replicates each group.

PLATFORM
GPL7349 EcoArray 8 x 15K Daphnia magna Microarray v1.0
