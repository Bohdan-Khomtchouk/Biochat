TITLE
Mammary gland gene expression timecourse with or without NMU treatment between Cop and F344 rat

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles in mammary glands of different rat strains are genetically defined. Keywords: Different rat strains and Timecourse

DESIGN
18 Cop rats with or without N-nitrosomethylurea (NMU) treatment at 6h, 1d, and 30 d compared to 18 F344 rats with or without NMU treatment at 6h, 1d, and 30 day

PLATFORM
GPL1355 [Rat230_2] Affymetrix Rat Genome 230 2.0 Array
