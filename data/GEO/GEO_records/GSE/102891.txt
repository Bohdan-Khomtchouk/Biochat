TITLE
Transcriptomic profiling of MDA-MB-231 cells treated with Boswellia Serrata Extract or 3-O-Acetyl-β-boswellic acid; ER/UPR mediated programmed cell death.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The effects of BSE and 3-OAβBA on MDA-MB-231 cells were evaluated for effects on the whole transcriptome: including mRNAs and long intergenic non-coding RNA transcripts (lincRNA) using GeneChip™ Human Gene 2.1 ST Arrays by Affymetrix Inc.
