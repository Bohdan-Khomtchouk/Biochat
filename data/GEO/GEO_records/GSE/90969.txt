TITLE
EMC3 conditional KO in developing lung epithelium

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
EMC3 is a critical component of an endoplasmic reticulum complex that mediates the processing and trafficking of surfactant associated proteins and lipids by AT2 cells and is required for lung function and respiration at birth.

DESIGN
Lung E18.5 Epcam sorted KO vs. Control RNASeq

PLATFORM
