TITLE
Transcriptional Changes in the Aging Mouse Hippocampus (expression)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We sequenced mRNA from three age groups (3months (3M), 24 months (24M) and 29 months (29M)) from the full hippocampus and compared this to microarray analysis.

DESIGN
Young (3 months (3M)) mice were compared to aged mice (29 months (29M)), n=4
