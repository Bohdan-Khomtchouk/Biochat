TITLE
Expression data from wild type (wt) and Ikbke knockout (Ikke) embryonic fibroblasts (EF)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
WT and Ikbke-/- EF cells were stimulated with recombinant interferon beta for 6 hours.  Cells lacking IKKe kinase show a defect in a subset of interferon stimulated gene transcription Keywords: comparative study

DESIGN
WT and Ikbke cells were either stimulated and left untreated to compare their response to interferon.
