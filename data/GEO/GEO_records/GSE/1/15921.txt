TITLE
Expression analysis for nasopharyngeal carcinoma cell lines 5-8F

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
High metastatic nasopharyngeal carcinoma cell line 5-8F expression patterns.

DESIGN
This study was done to detect genes expressed in NPC cell line 5-8F. The microarray analyses were done in a same batch. cells are cultured and harvested at two different time points.
