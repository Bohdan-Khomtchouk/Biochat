TITLE
Rader HHDL and BioBank genotyping

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The presence of a coding variant affecting plasma high density lipoprotein cholesterol (HDLC) levels was evaluated in subjects with elevated or normal plasma levels of HDLC. Carriers of the variant were further analyzed phenotypically

DESIGN
19 DNA samples from subjects with normal or high plasma HDLC levels were analyzed.
