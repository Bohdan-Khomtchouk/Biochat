TITLE
Liver and cecum from mice exposed to aflatoxin B1 (AFB1) and/or Helicobacter hepaticus

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We evaluated aflatoxin B1-induced liver tumor promotion by H. hepaticus. Microarrays of liver and cecum from female mice were used to evaluate the individual and combined transcriptional effects of AFB1 and H. hepaticus Keywords: Tumor co-promotion study
