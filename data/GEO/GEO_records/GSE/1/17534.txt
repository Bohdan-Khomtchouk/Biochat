TITLE
Affymetrix 250K StyI SNP array data from PBMCs and cell lines of metastatic melanoma patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Allele call files from on 250K StyI SNP array using DNA from 60 human cell lines from metastasized melanoma and from 44 corresponding peripheral blood mononuclear cells (CEL and CHP files provided).
