TITLE
ERRγ Regulates Cardiac, Gastric and Renal Potassium Homeostasis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Genomic analysis of expression in brain, kidney, stomach, liver, and salivary tissues of perinatal ERRγ null and wild type mice.

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from brain, kidney, stomach, liver, and salivary tissues of perinatal ERRγ null and wild type mice and hybridized to Affymetrix MOE430A gene chips in triplicate.
