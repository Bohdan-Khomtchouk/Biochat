TITLE
Analysis of murine liver tissue

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Functional genomic analysis of liver tissue extracted from polygenic obese mice fed either a control (1% linoleic acid) or treatment (1% trans 10, cis 12 conjugated linoleic acid) diet after fourteen days.
