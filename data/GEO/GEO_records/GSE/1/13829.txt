TITLE
UV treated Escherichia coli reveals a high number of differentially expressed regions and many novel transcripts

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli str. K-12 substr. MG1655

SUMMARY
The transcriptional changes in Escherichia coli upon induction of the SOS response are investigated by utilizing custom designed oligonucleotide microarrays. Keywords: Gene expression during the SOS response in Escherichia coli
