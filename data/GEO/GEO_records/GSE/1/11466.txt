TITLE
Integrin alpha6-beta4 control the expression of genes associated with cell motility, invasion and metastasis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The objective of this study was to determine the gene expression changes mediated by the alpha6beta4 integrin using MDA-MB-435 breast carcinoma cell line under normal culturing conditions (10% FCS in DMEM). Keywords: Transfection (stable)
