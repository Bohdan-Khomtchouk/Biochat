TITLE
Expression profiles of ES cells, epiblast stem cells, and epiblast stem cells reverted to an ES-like state

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This analysis compares the expression profiles of CGR8 ES cells, E3 epiblast stem cells, and E3R cells (E3 EpiSCs reverted to an ES-like state).

DESIGN
All three lines were grown under their respective standard conditions.
