TITLE
M. smegmatis response to energy- and oxygen-limitation in continuous culture

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium smegmatis

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of M. smegmatis grown at 69h doubling time compared to 4.6h doubling time both at 50% air saturation. Transcriptional profiling of M. smegmatis grown at 69h doubling time with 50%, 2.5% and 0.6% air saturation.
