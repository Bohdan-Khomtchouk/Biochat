TITLE
Transcriptome profiling of Pacific oyster Crassostrea gigas families that differ in tolerance of heat shock

ORGANISM
Crassostrea virginica

SUMMARY
ESTs in which transcription levels in oyster gill differed among sampling times after heat shock and between families with high or low survival of heat shock were identified through expression profiling of 1,675 spot pairs Keywords: comparison of families characterized by low or high survival after heat shock
