TITLE
Gene expression from primary brain cell cultures and RNA mixtures.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Gene expression from primary neuronal, astrocytic, oligodendrocytic and microglial cultures, as well as from RNA mixtures thereof. Keywords: Primary cell cultures

DESIGN
Primary neuronal, astrocytic, oligodendrocytic and microglial cultures (4 biological replicates of each), as well as RNA mixtures thereof (5 different mixing proportions, 2 independent replicates of each mixing proportion).
