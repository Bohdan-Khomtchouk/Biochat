TITLE
Male BALB/c Mouse Testosterone-Treated Submandibular/Lacrimal/Meibomian Glands

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Series includes pooled (n = 5 mice per biological replicate) samples from lacrimal, meibomian, and submandibular glands of male palcebo- and testosterone-treated BALB/c mice. All experiments were run in triplicate (pooled biological replicates) on CodeLink Mouse Uniset I Microarrays. Keywords: repeat sample
