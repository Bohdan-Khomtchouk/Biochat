TITLE
Card9 dependent activation of macrophages by TDB

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Bone marrow derived macrophages from wt and card9 KO mice were stimulated  with CpG, Curdlan or TDB, an analogon to the mycobacterial cord factor TDM for 48h, respectively. Keywords: card9 knockout, macropphage, TDB
