TITLE
C3H10T1/2 Adipogenesis: Effects of EGF and TCDD

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Confluent C3H10T1/2 cells stimulated to differentiate using IDMB (1uM BRL49653, 1uM Dexamethasone, 0.5uM IBMX, 10ug/mL Insulin).  Expression was examined 24 hours after IDMB treatment.  Additionally, the effects of addition of TCDD 48 hours prior to IDMB treatment, and EGF addition concurrent with IDMB treatment were examined.
