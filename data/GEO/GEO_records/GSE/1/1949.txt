TITLE
Time series serum stimulation of ES cells of different Srf genotype

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Murine ES cells of different Srf genotype (E14wt, E100-/-, E100-/- rescue (Srf transgene)) were stimulated with FBS for 0-180 min. RNA was harvested and hybridized to Affymetrix MGU74A arrays. Keywords: time-course
