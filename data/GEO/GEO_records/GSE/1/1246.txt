ORGANISM

SUMMARY
Series of 4 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (red200micE) and control (red20micE) each. Comparison of plants grown for 48 hours at 200µE red light versus plants exposed for 48 hours at 20µE to red light. E. Lopez, unpublished Keywords: repeat sample
