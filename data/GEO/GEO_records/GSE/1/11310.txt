TITLE
Characterization of canine mesenchymal stromal cell lines (MSC)

ORGANISM
Canis lupus familiaris

SUMMARY
The bone marrow microenvironment is a complex mixture of cells that function in concert to regulate hematopoiesis. Cellular components include fixed nonhematopoietic stromal elements (MSC) as well as monocytes and resident macrophages, which are derived from the hematopoietic stem cells.
