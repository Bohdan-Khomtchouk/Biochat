TITLE
C. perfringens 13 strain: growth with 1 mM homocysteine vs growth with 0.5 mM cystine

ORGANISM
Clostridium perfringens

SUMMARY
Transcriptionnal profiling of C. perfringens 13 strain comparing growth in minimal medium with 1 mM homocysteine with growth in minimal medium with 0.5 mM cystine.

DESIGN
two-condition experiment, homocysteine vs cystine, 4 biological replicates for each condition
