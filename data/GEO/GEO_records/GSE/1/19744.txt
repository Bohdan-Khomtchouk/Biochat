TITLE
Whole-genome analysis of heat shock factor binding sites in Drosophila melanogaster during heat shock

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Whole-genome analysis of heat shock factor binding sites in Drosophila melanogaster. Heat shock factor IP DNA or Mock IP DNA from heat shocked Kc 167 cells compared to whole cell extract on Agilent 2x244k tiling arrays.

DESIGN
Heat Shock Factor IP or mock IP vs whole cell extract from heat shocked Kc cells.
