TITLE
Adipose tissue of gonadectomized mice injected with dihydrotestosterone

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Comparison of retroperitoneal adipose tissue of intact male mice and gonadectomized C57BL6 male mice. Gonadectomized mice were untreated or injected with dihydrotestosterone (DHT) and sacrificed 3 or 24 hours after injection. Keywords: other
