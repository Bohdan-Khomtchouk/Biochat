TITLE
Synthetic Fire Ant Venom Transcriptosome Analysis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human U937 cells were maintained in RPMI 1640 media. Solenopsin B added at a concentration equivalent to the IC50, 13 micro Molar.  Triplicate cultures were prepared at two separate time points, 1 and 6 hours. Keywords: Transcriptosome analysis employing DNA/cDNA glass microarray.
