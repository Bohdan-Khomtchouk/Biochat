TITLE
KPAW and wild type eyes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Mice over-expressing human PITX2A in cornea by the keratocan upstream regulatory sequences. Normalization procedure: Data normalized to spikes. Values below 0.01 were set to 0.01.  All of the genes in each sample were divided by the median of a user specified list of positive control genes (chip-to-chip normalization). Keywords: repeat sample
