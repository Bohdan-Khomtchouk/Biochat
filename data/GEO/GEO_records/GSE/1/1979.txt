TITLE
Effect of Diabetes on Retinal Müller Cells

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Rat Retinal Müller cells from diabetic rats (diabetes duration 6 months) compared to Rat Retinal Müller cells from healthy rats.  Diabetes was induced by streptozotozine.  Diabetic rats were treated with small doses of insulin to prevent catabolism. Keywords: ordered
