TITLE
Rat lung gene expression during acute pulmonary embolism

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Rats were given pulmonary embolism by i.v. injection of 25 micron polystyrene microspheres or 0.01% Tween20 solution as vehicle control Embolism of microspheres is irreversible and causes dose dependent pulmonary hypertension Keywords: time course and dose response
