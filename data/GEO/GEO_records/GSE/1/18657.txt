TITLE
Response to nicotine (100 µM) in heads of the tobacco aphid Myzus persicae

ORGANISM
Myzus persicae

SUMMARY
Response of the aphid head transcriptome to nicotine in artificial diets (100 µM nicotine, 24 hours feeding)

DESIGN
Two condition experiment: heads of aphids feeding on control diet vs heads of aphids feeding on 100 µM nicotine containing diet
