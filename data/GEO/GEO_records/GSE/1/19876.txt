TITLE
Yeast genomic DNA: SH6484 vs. FY834

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
To detect genome amplification or deletion of genome reconstructed strain SH6484 compare to parental strain FY833. SH6484 genome was reconstructed using PCS technology. Keywords: Comparative genomic hybridization

DESIGN
SH6484 vs. FY834. There are no biological replicates.
