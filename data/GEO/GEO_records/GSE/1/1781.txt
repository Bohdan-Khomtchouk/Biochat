TITLE
Burn-CLP

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
The experimental design included three biological replicates for each of the three conditions: Sham-Sham, Sham-CLP and burn-CLP. Liver samples were collected from the rats and the total RNA was analyzed on a Affymetrix RAE230A chip. No technical replicates were included in the study Keywords: other
