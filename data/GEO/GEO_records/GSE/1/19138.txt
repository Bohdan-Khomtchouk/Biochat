TITLE
Comprehensive discovery of endogenous Argonaute binding sites in C. elegans

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
Comprehensive discovery of endogenous Argonaute binding sites in C. elegans

DESIGN
Total RNA were extracted from whole alg-1(gk214) (alg-1(-)) and N2 (wildtype) worms and used to compare the expression profiles of genes that were targeted by ALG-1 protein.
