TITLE
Effect of amphotericin B on Aspergillus fumigatus

ORGANISM
Aspergillus fumigatus

SUMMARY
Amphotericin B (AMB) is the most widely used polyene antifungal drug for the treatment of systemic fungal infections including invasive aspergillosis. We aimed  to understand molecular targets of AMB in Aspergillus fumigatus (Afu) by genomic approaches. Keywords: Aspergillus fumigatus treated with amphotericin B for 24 hours
