TITLE
Low temperature stress in cv. Dicktoo

ORGANISM
Hordeum vulgare

SUMMARY
In this study we used the Affymetrix Barley 1 GeneChip to investigate transcriptome responses of barley cv. Dicktoo to low temperature, including triplicated measurements of cold, freeze/thaw cycles and de-acclimation over 33 days. Keywords: stress response
