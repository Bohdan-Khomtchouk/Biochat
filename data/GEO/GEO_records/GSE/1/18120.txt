TITLE
Challenge of vaccinated and unvaccinated salmon with Aeromonas salmonicida

ORGANISM
Oncorhynchus mykiss

SUMMARY
The study searched for the correlates of vaccine protection against furunculosis. After pathogen challenge of vaccinated (V) and unvaccinated (N) fish, hepatic gene expression was compared in salmon with high resistance (HR, individual samples) and low resistance (LR, pooled samples)
