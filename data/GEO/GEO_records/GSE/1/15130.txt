TITLE
PIK3CB aCGH Patient Results

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study was to screen pre-treatment breast cancer patients for genomic amplification of the PIK3CB gene. Keywords: comparative genomic hybridization

DESIGN
This experiment design includes 35 patient hybridizations using Agilent's 244k whole genome cgh array.  DNA from both the patient's tumor tissue and the patient's blood (germline) were processed and competitively hybridized to the same microarray.
