TITLE
Next Generation Sequencing Facilitates Quantitative Analysis of Wild Type and L2hgdh-/- mice brain Transcriptomes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report the transcriptional difference of L2hgdh gene knock out mice brain with wild type control using RNA-seq technology.

DESIGN
brain mRNA profiles of 2-month old wild type and L2hgdh knock out mice

PLATFORM
