TITLE
DNA Methylation profile of twins with pulmonary tuberculosis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling in the blood between Pulmonary tuberculosis patient's and normal people's who have genetic relationship wtih each other.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the  the blood between Pulmonary tuberculosis patient's and normal people's who have genetic relationship wtih each other were hybridised to the Illumina Infinium HumanMethylation850 BeadChip

PLATFORM
GPL23976 Illumina Infinium HumanMethylation850 BeadChip
