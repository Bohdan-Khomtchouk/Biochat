TITLE
Loss of CREBBP results in H3K27ac loss at enhancers in mouse lymphoma cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
KD of Crebbp at mouse cells results in reduced H3K27ac enrichment at a subset of promoters and enhancers.

DESIGN
Genome-wide H3K27Ac ChIPseq binding sites in mouse cells with shCrebbp and scramble control.
