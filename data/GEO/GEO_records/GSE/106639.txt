TITLE
Liver array following CMPF treatment

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Examining the effect of CMPF treatment in the livers of mice. This study examines both the prevention and reversal of steatosis. We used arrays to determine the pathways through which CMPF prevents and reverses steatosis

DESIGN
In the prevention model, mice were treated for one week prior to being changed to a HFD for 4-5 weeks. The reversal model, mice were fed a HFD for 6 weeks followed by 2 weeks of CMPF treatment.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
