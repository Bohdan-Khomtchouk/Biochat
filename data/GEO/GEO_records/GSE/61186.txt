TITLE
Zebrafish larvae response to AgNPs and AgNO3

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of zebrafish larvae comparing control with AgNO3 or AgNPs exposed zebrafish larvae.

DESIGN
Three-condition experiment, Control vs. AgNO3 and Control vs. AgNPs exposed zebrafish. Biological replicates: 6 control replicates, 6 AgNO3 replicates and 6 AgNPs replicates.
