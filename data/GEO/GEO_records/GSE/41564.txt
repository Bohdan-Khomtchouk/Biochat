TITLE
Identifying changes in host responses to Yersinia infection in the presence or absence of YopM virulence factor

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of total splenic RNA isolated from Yersinia pestis infected mice, comparing samples from YopM-competent parental strain (D27) infected mice to infected mice with YopM-deficient strain (M') at 1hr and 16hr post infection

DESIGN
4 groups. Biological replicates per group: n=3. One replicate per array.
