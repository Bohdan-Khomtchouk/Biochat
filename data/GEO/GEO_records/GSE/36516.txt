TITLE
Gene expression profiling in Tie-2-Etv2 endothelial and/or hematopoietic precursors

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Etv2 transgene was expressed from ROSA26 locus by removing floxed STOP cassette by Tie-2 Cre transgene. VE-Cad+/CD45= or VE-Cad+/CD45+ cells were sorted from control or tie-2-Etv2 E11.5 embryos (YS;yolk sac and Emb;embryo proper)and compared for gene expression in duplicate.

DESIGN
