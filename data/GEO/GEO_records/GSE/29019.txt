TITLE
Effect of 5-amino-4-imidazolecarboxamide ribotide (AICAr) supplementation on global transcription

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
We investigated the effects of AICAr supplementation on the global transcriptome of Y2950.

DESIGN
The Y2950 strain was grown with or without AICAr. For each comparison, we made 2 biological replicates, swapping dyes.
