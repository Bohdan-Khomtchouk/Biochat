TITLE
Transcriptomics in response to internal exposure in Flemisch adults

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptomic analysis of the effects of environmental exposure to cadmium, lead, PCBs, dioxin, hexachlorobenzene, p,p'-DDE, benzene and PAHs on the gene expression in blood in humans (biomonitoring study)

DESIGN
Correlation analysis of 9 biomarkers of exposure with gene expression levels in peripheral blood lymphocytes of 20 females or 20 males.

PLATFORM
GPL6480 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Probe Name version)
