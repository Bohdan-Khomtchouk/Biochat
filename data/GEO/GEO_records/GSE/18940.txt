TITLE
Bobwhite Quail 2,6-DNT

ORGANISM
Colinus virginianus

SUMMARY
Investigating the effects of a subchronic (60d) exposure to the munitions constituent (MC) 2,6-DNT on Northern bobwhite.

DESIGN
Gene expression among liver and feather pulp in Northern bobwhite at control, 10 and 60 mg/kg/d doses. 4 replicate for each level.
