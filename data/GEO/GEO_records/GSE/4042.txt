TITLE
Gene expression profiling of a temperature-sensitive strain of Neospora caninum

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus ; Neospora caninum

SUMMARY
The gene expression profiles of a temperature-sensitive strain, ts-8, was compared agains its wild type counterpart, NC-1. Keywords: mutant comparision, two class unpaired design

DESIGN
Eight single channel hybridization were performed (ts-8 vs NC-1), four biological replicates each.
