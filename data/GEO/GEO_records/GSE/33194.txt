TITLE
R. sphaeroides Δlov vs. R. sphaeroides 2.4.1 (microarobic conditions)

ORGANISM
Rhodobacter sphaeroides 2.4.1

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of R. sphaeroides Δlov comparing to control R. sphaeroides 2.4.1 under microarobic conditions

DESIGN
Two strain experiment under microarobic conditions. 6 Biological replicas, independetly grown and harvested at OD660=0,8 - 1-3 pooled in replicate 1 and 4-6 pooled in replicate 2
