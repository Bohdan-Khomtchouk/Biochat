TITLE
Effect of bacterial ACC deaminase on plant gene expression

ORGANISM
Brassica napus

SUMMARY
Canola plants inoculated with plant growth-promoting bacteria either expressing ACC deaminase or not to determine the effect on plant gene expression using an Arabidopsis microarray.

DESIGN
3 replicates for each ACD+ and ACD- bacteria, each compared with untreated control.

GPL7265 Agilent-011839 Arabidopsis 2 Oligo Microarray G4136A (Probe Name version)
