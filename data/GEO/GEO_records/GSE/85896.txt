TITLE
Plasmodium-exhausted Nfat1+/+ versus Nfat1-/- CD4+ T cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression data from antigen-experienced Nfat1+/+ and Nfat1-/- CD4+ T cells following 21 days of Plasmodium yoelii 17XNL infection.

DESIGN
Total RNA was isolated from CD4+ CD49d+ CD11a-high cells sorted from spleens following 21 days Plasmodium yoelii 17XNL infection and subjected to microarray analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL16570 [MoGene-2_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 2.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
