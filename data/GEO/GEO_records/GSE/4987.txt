TITLE
Yeast cell cycle

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Transcription profiles across the yeast cell cycle. W303a cells were arrested with alpha factor in YEPD media. After release from arrest cells were sampled every 5 min for 2 hr. Keywords: cell cycle

DESIGN
Alpha factor synchronized cells were followed across 2 cell cycles.  Dye-swap technical replicates were included in the experiment.
