TITLE
RNA-Seq of eye tissues from A/J, BALB/c, and C57BL/6 background mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report RNA-Seq experiments of whole eye tissues from A/J, BALB/c, and C57BL/6 background mice.

DESIGN
Examine ocular tissue from 3 different background mice that display varying rates of retinal degeneration.
