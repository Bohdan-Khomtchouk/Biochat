TITLE
Whole transcriptome expression of trigeminal ganglia compared to dorsal root ganglia in Rattus norvegicus

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
RNA-Sequencing of the trigeminal ganglia and dorsal root ganglia in male naive rats (Wistar Han) of 10 weeks old.

DESIGN
6 naive rats were killed after 2 weeks of arrival, both trigeminal ganglia and dorsal root ganglia were dissected of each rat.
