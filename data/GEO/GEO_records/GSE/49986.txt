TITLE
The organizational effects of testosterone on brain DNA methylome

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression in the bed nucleus of the stria terminalis/preoptic area and striatum in response to perinatal testosterone exposure. The hypothesis tested was that differences in DNA methylation would be reflected in differences in gene expression.

DESIGN
Analysis of gene expression in the bed nucleus of the stria terminalis/preoptic area and striatum in response to perinatal testosterone exposure. The hypothesis tested was that differences in DNA methylation would be reflected in differences in gene expression.
