TITLE
Connectivity Map dataset (build01)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A reference collection of genome-wide transcriptional expression data for bioactive small molecules. Keywords: small molecule perturbation

DESIGN
The current collection (build01) contains data for 164 distinct small molecules applied to freely cycling human cell lines, represented by 453 individual treatment and matched vehicle control pairs.
