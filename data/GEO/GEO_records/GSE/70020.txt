TITLE
Expression profiles of innate and Th2 lymphocytes   

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We compare the transcription profiles of IL-5-reporter marked ILC2s and Th2 cells sorted from mouse lung tissue after Nippostrongylus brasiliensis infection

DESIGN
mRNA sequencing comparing material from 2 cell populations sorted from the lungs of 7 Red5/Red5 mice, comprising 2 independent infections, 14 days after N.b. infection

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
