TITLE
Gene expression profile of HLRCC associated renal cell cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles of normal kidney (3 technical replicates) and a renal tumor (3 technical replicates) from a hereditary leiomyomatosis and renal cell cancer (HLRCC) patient carrying a germline mutation in the fumarate hydratase (FH) gene.

DESIGN
Tumor and normal tissue from one patient, both in 3 techincal replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL10184 Affymetrix GeneChip Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array [CDF: Hs133P_Hs_ENSG_8]
