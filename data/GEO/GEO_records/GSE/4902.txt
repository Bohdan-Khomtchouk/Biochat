TITLE
Suz12, EZH2, EED1, H3me3K27 binding sites in Ntera cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
All samples are from the same batch of crosslinked Ntera cells with a matched total produced in parallel.  Amplicons were made from each and hybridized on ENCODE region tiling arrays.  Two color arrays were used; ChIP was red, Total was green. Keywords: ChIP-chip

DESIGN
Goal was determination of Suz12, EZH2, EED1, H3me3K27 binding sites in Ntera cells using ChIP-chip methodology.

PLATFORM
GPL3792 Nimblegen Human ENCODE array
