TITLE
Excretory/secretory microRNAs from the gastrointestinal nematode Trichuris muris in culture

ORGANISM
Mus musculus ; Trichuris muris

SUMMARY
We report analyses of the types and amounts of microRNAs found in culture media recovered after incubating approximately 3000 T. muris adult worms for 48 hours.

DESIGN
Examination of excretory/secretory miRNAs of the mouse gastrointestinal nematode parasite Trichuris muris released in culture media.

PLATFORM
GPL22928 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus; Trichuris muris)
