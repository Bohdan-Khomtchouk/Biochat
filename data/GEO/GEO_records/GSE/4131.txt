TITLE
Determination of myotube and myoblast expression levels

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression was determined for both myotubes and myoblasts using Affymetrix HG-U133 A/B arrays. Keywords: Comparative expression

DESIGN
Triplicate Affymetrix HG-U133A/B expression chips were hybridized to RNA extracted from myoblasts and myotubes. 12 samples in total.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array GPL97 [HG-U133B] Affymetrix Human Genome U133B Array
