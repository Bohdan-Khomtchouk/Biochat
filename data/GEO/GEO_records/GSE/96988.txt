TITLE
RNA sequencing analysis of HL-1 cardiomyocytes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of murine cardiomyocyte cell line HL-1 treated with Ivermectin or Importazole. Results provide insight into the pathways regulated by the treatments.

DESIGN
RNA-seq of mouse HL-1 cardiomyocytes treated with vehicle (DMSO), Ivermectin, or Importazole for 24 hours, in triplicate, using Ion Proton System.
