TITLE
RAMPAGE from myotube (ENCSR187NWZ)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from independent growths of primary cell line HSMM that has been differentiated into myotubes. They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RAMPAGE libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
