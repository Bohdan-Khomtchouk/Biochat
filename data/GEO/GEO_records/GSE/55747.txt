TITLE
Gene Expression Analysis of Fibrotic Liver

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis at the gene epression level of mice that had CCl4 induced liver fibrosis with either FSP1+ cells or aSMA+ cells ablated.

DESIGN
Total RNA was collected from mice with healthy, diseased, FSP1 ablated, and aSMA ablated liver

PLATFORM
