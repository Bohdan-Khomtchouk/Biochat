TITLE
O377 homozygotes versus wt

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
O377 mutant mouseline with progressive lens opacity. Mutation mapped to distal chromosome 5 and finally characterized by an A - T bp substiution at the end of intro 5 of Crybb2. Crybb2 expression also found in olfactory bulb, pyramidal cells of the cortex, hippocampus, Purkinje and stellate cells of cerebellum. Keywords: other
