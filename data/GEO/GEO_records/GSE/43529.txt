TITLE
Global Changes in Gene Expression in Mesorhizobium loti MAFF303099 upon an heat shock

ORGANISM
Mesorhizobium loti MAFF303099

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of a total of 7281 transcripts was compared between control and heat shock conditions.

DESIGN
In the present study, RNA was extracted from three independent cell cultures for each treatment (control and heat shock).
