TITLE
Next-gen RNA sequencing of dog osteosarcoma tumors

ORGANISM
Canis lupus familiaris

SUMMARY
Trascriptome analysis of osteosarcoma samples were performed

DESIGN
Tumor samples were obtained from Rottweilers and Golden Retrievers with proper consents,  cell lines were derived from privious primary tumors and sequenced using Illumina HiSeq 2000
