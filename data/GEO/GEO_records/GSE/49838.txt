TITLE
Transcriptional comparison of cynomolgus macaques (Macaca fascicularis) following infection with Lassa or Lujo viruses

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of PBMC from cynomolgus macaques collected longitudinally over the course of infection with Lassa-Josiah, Lassa-Z132, Lassa-SorombaR, or Lujo viruses (n=3 animals/infection condition).
