TITLE
lin-35(n745) mutants vs. wild type

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of C. elegans first larval stage whole animals comparing lin-35(n745) mutants and N2 (wild type) at 26 degrees.

DESIGN
One-condition experiment, mutant (lin-35) vs. WT.  Biological replicates 4 mutant, 4 wildtype harvested indepedently.  One lin-35 replicate and one WT replicate per array.
