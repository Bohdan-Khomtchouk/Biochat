TITLE
Toll-like receptor 7 is a primary sensor of malaria infection

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray profiling of amplified total splenic RNA isolated from TLR9-, TLR7-, UNC93b1- or MYD88- deficient mice or their C57BL/6 controls 24h after mock infection or infection with Plasmodium chabaudi.

DESIGN
4 independent replicates collected from each genotype/infection group, RNA isolated and amplified from whole spleen. Hybridized as a one color experiment.

GPL13912 Agilent-028005 SurePrint G3 Mouse GE 8x60K Microarray (Feature Number version)
