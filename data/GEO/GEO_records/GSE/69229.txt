TITLE
Epigenetic landscape correlates with genetic subtype but does not predict outcome in childhood acute lymphoblastic leukemia

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We analyzed 52 Diagnostic samples from childhood Acute lymphoblastic leukemia samples

DESIGN
We compared methylation profiles with four ALL cytogentic subtypes using (n=26) long term remission vs. (n=26) that went on to relapse
