TITLE
Transcription analysis of E. coli O103:H25 in co-culture with Bacteroides thetaiotaomicron CCUG 10774

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli O103:H2 str. 12009

SUMMARY
Investigation of whole genome gene expression level changes in E. coli O103:H25 in co-culture with Bacteroides thetaiotaomicron CCUG 10774 compared to when E. coli is cultured individually.
