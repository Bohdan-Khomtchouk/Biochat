TITLE
Age-related epigenetic drift in the pathogenesis of MDS and AML part 2

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Genomic DNA is isolated from spleen (RUNX1 model and normal control) and bone marrow (NHD13 model and normal control). We used MCAM to identify genes that have methylation changes.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, MDS model mouse vs. normal control.
