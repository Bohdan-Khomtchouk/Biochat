TITLE
Analysis of differentially expressed genes in A549 after treatment with Sulforaphene

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study aimed to identify differential expressed genes before and after treatment with Sulforaphene, using the A549 lung cancer cell line as a model.

DESIGN
There were a total of 4 samples examined. Two replicates have been included (2 control samples and 2 test samples).
