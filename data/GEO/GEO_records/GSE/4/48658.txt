TITLE
Melita plumulosa toxicant response: What is contaminant mode of action?

ORGANISM
Melita plumulosa

SUMMARY
The southeastern Australian epibenthic amphipod Melita plumulosa was exposed to eight different contaminants for 48 hours.

DESIGN
RNA from exposed animals (10 individuals, pooled) was hybridised against reference RNA from unexposed animals.  Each treatment was replicated 5 times.  Alternate arrays were dye swapped.  Data are presented (exposed/unexposed).
