TITLE
Sheep skin lymph  CD103 and CD11b -type DC in vitro response to Canine Adenovirus vector

ORGANISM
Ovis aries

SUMMARY
Transcriptomic profiling of sheep skin lymph DC subsets after 16h in vitro culture in medium or stimulated by non replicative Canine Adenovirus serotype 2

DESIGN
Reference design: total enriched lymph dendritic cells- Biological replicates: 4 sheep (70958, 50274, 30066, 11261)- Samples: FACS sorted CD103 -type and CD11b type cultured in medium or with CAV2
