TITLE
UTX and JMDJ3 regulate NKT development

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to understand the transcriptional differences between UTX/JMJD3 DKO NKT precursors (P1) and WT litter mater controls.  Two female and one male sample for each is included.

DESIGN
6 samples, 3 WT(2F, 1M), 3 DKO (2F, 1M)
