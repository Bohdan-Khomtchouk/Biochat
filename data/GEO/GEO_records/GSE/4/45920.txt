TITLE
Expression analysis of prostate tissue on locus 8q24 part 1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Investigation on expression levels of normal tissue from prostate cancer patients on locus 8q24. The region chr8:127640000-129120000 is tiled with 60 nt probes at 10 nt interval (hg18)

DESIGN
7 chip study, using 7 independent samples.
