TITLE
Ectopically expressed MSL2 females compared with normal females

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
We used H83M2 P element to ectopically expressed MSL2 protein in females to test if the novel formed MSL complex is the directly reason of dosage compensation

DESIGN
Compare the MSL2 females with normal females and normal males; MSL2 males also included.
