TITLE
Leishmania infantum: Alba20 null mutant Vs wild type

ORGANISM
Leishmania

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis of an LiAlba20 (LinJ.34.2410) null mutant strain versus Wild type. Experiments were run with promastigotes and axenic amastigotes independently. Null mutant was produced by homologous recombination, replacing both alleles with Hygromycin phosphotransferase and Puromycin.
