TITLE
Gene expression profile of duodenal follicular lymphoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To clarify the molecular pathogenesis of DFL pathogenesis using GEP. DFL shares characteristics of both NFL and MALT lymphoma.

DESIGN
55 total samples were examined; 10 DFL, 18 NFL, 10 gastric MALT lymphoma, 5 normal duodenal mucosa, 8 nodal reactive lymphoid hyperplasia, and 4 normal gastric mucosa.
