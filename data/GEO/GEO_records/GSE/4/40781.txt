TITLE
CD3/CD28 Stimulated versus unstimulated Double-negative T Cells From Sooty Mangabeys

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Double Negative (CD4-/CD8-) T-cells isolated from SIV infected Sooty Mangebys. DN T-cells were stimulated through the T-Cell receptor using anti CD3/CD28 antibodies for 4 hours and compared to unstimulated DN T-cells.
