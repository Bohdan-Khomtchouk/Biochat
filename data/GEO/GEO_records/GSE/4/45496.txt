TITLE
Comparison of gene expression of miltefosine responsive Vs miltefosine unresponsive  Leishmania donovani promastigotes

ORGANISM
Leishmania donovani

SUMMARY
The current study aims to study change in gene expression profile of miltefosine resistance L. donovani with respect to miltefosine responsive L. donovani.

DESIGN
Agilent one-color experiment,Organism: Leishmania donovani, Microarray GeneExpression 8x15k designed by Genotypic Technology Private Limited. (AMADID: 035638)
