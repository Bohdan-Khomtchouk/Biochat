TITLE
Transcriptome sequencing of Brassica oleracea tissues

ORGANISM
Brassica oleracea

SUMMARY
Deep sequencing of mRNA from seven different tissues of Brassica oleracea

DESIGN
Analysis of ploy(A)+ RNA of multiple different tissues of Brassica oleracea containing Bud, Callus, Root, Stem, Leaf, Flower and Silique.
