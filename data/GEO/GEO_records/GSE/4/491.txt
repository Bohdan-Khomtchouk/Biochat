TITLE
Effect of formula feeding and hypoxia on Necrotizing Enterocolitis (NEC) development

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Necrotizing Enterocolitis (NEC) is an inflammation causing injury to the bowel in newborns. This project uses a rodent model that mimics the intestinal pathological changes seen in NEC to study the effect of formula feeding and hypoxia on NEC development Keywords: time series, diet, hypoxia
