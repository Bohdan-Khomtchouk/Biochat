TITLE
Comparison of Hob6-RFP positive and negative Flk-1+ cells (RFP reporter)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
ES derived Flk-1+ cells were separated by sorting into Hoxb6 positive and negative populations by RFP Hoxb6 reporter. Gene expression was compared between these two groups.

DESIGN
Duplicate analysis of Hoxb6-RFP positive and negative Flk-1+ cells.
