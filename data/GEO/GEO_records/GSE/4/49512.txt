TITLE
Genome-wide nucleosome maps for wild type and Rsc8-depleted Saccharomyces cerevisiae

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Paired-end sequencing study of nucleosomes from MNase-digested nuclei.

DESIGN
Nucleosomal DNA from wild type and Rsc8-depleted cells was subjected to paired-end sequencing.
