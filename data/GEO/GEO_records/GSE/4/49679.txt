TITLE
Transcriptional profiling of Neurospora crassa conidia undergoing a time-course: Δpp-1 vs WT

ORGANISM
Neurospora crassa

SUMMARY
To study the consequences of transcription factor pp-1 deletion during vegetative cell fusion, we performed whole genome microarrays of WT and Δpp-1 conidia during their initial growth period (3h - 8h).
