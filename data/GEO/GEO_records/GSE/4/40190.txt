TITLE
High-throughput sequencing of mouse liver transcriptome.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Circadian profile of polyA RNA by RNA-Seq, collected from mouse liver at CT23, CT29, CT35, CT41, CT47, CT53, CT59, CT65. RNA from three livers pooled per time point.

DESIGN
8 wildtype samples with no replicates.
