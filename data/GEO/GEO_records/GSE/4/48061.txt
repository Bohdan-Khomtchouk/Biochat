TITLE
Genome and transcriptome adaptations of S. Typhimurium

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium str. SL1344

SUMMARY
Part of a study to determine the impact of genome evolution on host adaptation of a group of Salmonella Typhimurium. The experiment is designed to determine the impact of culture at elevated temperature on gene experssion.
