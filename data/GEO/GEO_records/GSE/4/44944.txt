TITLE
Amygdala-Dependent Fear Is Regulated by Oprl1 in Mice and Humans with PTSD

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of amygdala RNA expression 2 hours after auditory fear expression test in mice with and without previous exposure to acute immobilization stress.

DESIGN
Total RNA from three groups was obtained: 1) Home Cage (HC) 2) Fear Expression / Extinction 3) Immobilization (IMO) + Fear Expression / Extinction.
