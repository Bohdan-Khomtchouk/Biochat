TITLE
Gene expression in skeletal muscle in AMPK gamma3 mutant (R225Q) transgenic mice and wild type littermates.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of AMPK gamma3-dependent transcriptional responses by analyzing global gene expression in the white portion of the gastrocnemius muscle in AMPK gamma3 mutant (R225Q) transgenic mice and corresponding wild type littermates. Keywords: Genetic modification
