TITLE
Agilent 4x44K expression array for squamous cell carcinoma of the lung

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Total RNA of 24 SCC tumors were isolated and profiled for mRNA expression measurement with  Agilent 4x44K Array

DESIGN
Tumor samples were assayed on Agilent Human gene expression 4x44K Microarray with manufacturer's recommended procedures for microarray-based one-color.
