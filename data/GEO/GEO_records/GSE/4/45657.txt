TITLE
AA Effector CD8 T cell profiling

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Flow sorted CD3+CD8+CD44+CXCR3+NKG2D+ lymph node cells from C3H/HeJ mice with alopecia were flow sorted and profiled in relation to CD3+CD8+CD44+CXCR3+NKG2D- lymph node cells from the same host

DESIGN
Cell populations were sorted from three separate mice
