TITLE
High-throughput RNA sequencing analysis of WT, rdm16-2 and sta1 mutants

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Using Illumina paired-end RNA-seq technology to examine the genome-wide gene expression and intron-retention events in WT, rdm16-2 and sta1

DESIGN
RNA-seq analysis of 4-week-old leaves of WT, rdm16-2 and sta1 mutants
