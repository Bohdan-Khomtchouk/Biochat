TITLE
Comparative transcriptional analysis of Desulfovibrio G20 grown under syntrophic and respiratory condtions

ORGANISM
Desulfovibrio alaskensis G20

SUMMARY
Expression data for Desulfovibrio alaskensis strain G20 grown on lactate in sulfate-limited monoculture and syntrophic coculture with Methanococcus maripaludis in chemostats at a high growth rate of 0.047h-1

DESIGN
5 replicates of coculture and 3 replicates of sulfate-limited monoculture
