TITLE
Leucegene: ALL sequencing

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNA sequencing of human leukemia

DESIGN
The goals of this project are to obtain a comprehensive study of mutations and gene expression in human T ALL (acute lymphoblastic leukemia). Methods: T ALL cells were thawed. DNA and RNA (polyA) was extracted and sequences were obtained with an Illumina HiSeq 2000 sequencer.
