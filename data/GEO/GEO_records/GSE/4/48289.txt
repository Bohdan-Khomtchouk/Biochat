TITLE
Homo sapiens Ewing sarcoma cell line RD-ES: Negative control vs. HDGF knockdown

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparing the gene expression profiling of HDGF-silenced RD-ES cells and control RD-ES cells to identify genes regulated by HDGF in RD-ES cells. Keywords: expression analysis

DESIGN
Control RD-ES cells and HDGF-silenced RD-ES cells were profiled on 22K Human Genome Array
