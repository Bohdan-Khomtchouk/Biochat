TITLE
small RNA Next Generation Sequencing of human primary macrophages treated with staurosporine

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human primary macrophages were treated  with 1 µM staurosporine (STS) and profiled for small RNAs.

DESIGN
Human primary macrophages were treated  with 1 µM staurosporine (STS) for 0, 4 and 6h.
