TITLE
Effect of dietary supplementation with direct fed microbials (DFM) in chicken

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
This study utilized comparative global gene expression microarray analysis to evaluate the effects of DFM on intestinal immunity of chicken.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, DFM-fed chickens vs. Non-treated control chickens. Biological replicates: 2 control replicates, 2 DFM-fed replicates with dye-switching.
