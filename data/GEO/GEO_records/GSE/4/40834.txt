TITLE
Array comparative genomic hybridization data from 313 CLL specimens to study outcome associations

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study investigates genomic imbalance in chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL) and aims to identify genomic gains and losses with prognostic significance.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Test CLL specimens vs. Reference human genome DNA equimixture of normal male and normal female.
