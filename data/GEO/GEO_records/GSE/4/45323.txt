TITLE
Genome-wide high-throughput small RNA sequence analysis of B. cinerea-infected Arabidopsis rosette leaves

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
We profiled small RNAs obtained from B. cinerea-infected Arabidopsis rosette leaves at four different time points after inoculation.

DESIGN
Examination of small RNA profiles of B. cinerea-infected Arabidopsis rosette leaves over a time course.
