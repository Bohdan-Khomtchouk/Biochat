TITLE
Expression analysis of trichostatin A-treated Aspergillus fumigatus Af293

ORGANISM
Aspergillus fumigatus Af293

SUMMARY
Investigation of whole genome gene expression level changes in trichostatin A (TSA)-treated A. fumigatus Af293 compared to non-treated A. fumigatus Af293.

DESIGN
Species: Aspergillus fumigatus; Strain: Af293; Type of array: Eukaryotic Expression (4plex, 2plex for a TSA-treated sample and 2plex for a non-treated sample); Technical replicates: Two per treatment.

PLATFORM
GPL9829 NimbleGen/RIKEN Aspergillus fumigatus 4x72K array
