TITLE

ORGANISM

SUMMARY
We analyzed the expression of 23,720 transcripts in the adipose tissue of 701 individuals and the blood of 1021 individuals. Keywords: microarray profiling, genetics, gene expression

DESIGN
Adipose tissue samples from 701 individuals and blood samples from 1021 individuals were profiled for gene expression.
