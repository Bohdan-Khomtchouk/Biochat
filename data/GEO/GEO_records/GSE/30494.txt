TITLE
Expression data in cancer cell lines using Affymetrix GeneChip

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis to identify down-stream genes after treatment with siKDM3A

DESIGN
Purified total RNA  was labeled and hybridized onto Affymetrix GeneChip U133 Plus 2.0 oligonucleotide arrays in cancer cell lines after treatment with siKDM3A
