TITLE
An EGFR autocrine loop encodes a slow-reacting but dominant mode of mechanotransduction in a polarized epithelium

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Normal human bronchial epithelial cells were studied under four different conditions: control, pressure 30 cmH2O, AG1478 (1 microM), and pressure plus AG1478 at 1, 3, and 8 hours, all in the absence of exogenous EGF. Keywords: Normal human bronchial epithelial cells.

DESIGN
Time series under 4 different stimuli with no replicate measurements.
