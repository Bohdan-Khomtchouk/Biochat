TITLE
Gene expression from mouse lung tissues

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Comparison of gene expression of lung tissues between WT, IKKa floxed, Kras, and Kras;IKKa floxed mice

DESIGN
Lung tissues were collected at 4 months post adeno-cre injection. Total RNAs from lung tissues were subjected to hybridization for Affymetrix microarray.
