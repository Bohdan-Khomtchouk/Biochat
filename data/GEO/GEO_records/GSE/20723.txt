TITLE
Glioblastoma Cell lines vs. Normal Glial Cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Examination of alternative splicing in 4 glioblastoma cell lines (U118, t98g, a172, U87) vs. FGG cells grown in culture. SUBMITTER_CITATION: Jonathan Gelfond, Lee Ann Zarzabal, Tarea Burton, Suzanne Burns, Mari Sogayar, Luiz O. F. Penalva. Latent rank change detection for analysis of splice-junction microarrays with nonlinear effects. Annals of Applied Statistics 2011, Vol. 5, No. 1, 364-380

DESIGN
4 Cancer Cell lines vs. Normal Cells in a dye-swap design with 2 replicates in each dye orientation (4 tumor lines x 2 reps x 2 dye-orientations = 16 arrays)

PLATFORM
GPL10127 JIVAN Homo sapiens 38.4K array for Glioblastoma Splicing
