TITLE
ZIKV can infect human CD14+ monocytes and update multiple cellular pathways

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The objective of this assay was to determine the effect of ZIKV on host cellular pathways

DESIGN
Purified human CD14+ monocytes were infected with two strains of ZIKV (PR and NIG) and RNA was subjected for differential gene expression by next generation sequence analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
