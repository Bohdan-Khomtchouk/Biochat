TITLE
Comparison of in vivo collected oocytes from animals with high and low oocyte developmental potential (HOP, LOP) at both the immature and mature stage

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Comparative transcriptomic analyses were performed at both the immature and at the mature stages between oocytes collected from cows with a high or low embryo development potential (HOP, LOP) as characterized by in vitro fertilization and embryo development.

DESIGN
1 biological replicate each from 3 HOP animals and 3 LOP animals, both at the immature stage and mature stage

PLATFORM
GPL6695 BOAC CattleOligo 13.2K + 8.7K 70-mer microarray v2
