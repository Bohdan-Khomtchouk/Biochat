TITLE
Epigenome analysis of human epidermal samples with aging and sun exposure

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of epidermal samples obtained from sum-exposed and sun-protected body sites from younger ( < 35 years old) and older ( > 60 years old) individuals.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from 12 samples was sequenced using Illumina HiSeq.

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
