TITLE
IL-4 and IL-13 treatment of murine hepatic progenitor cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to determine the response of murine hepatic progenitor cells to the type 2 cytokines IL-4 and IL-13.

DESIGN
12 samples were analyzed. 4 controls samples, 4 IL-4 samples, and 4 IL-13 samples.

GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
