TITLE
Salt/NaCl stress in Desulfovibrio vulgaris Hildenborough

ORGANISM
Desulfovibrio vulgaris str. Hildenborough

SUMMARY
This set of microarray data was used to investigate the effects of D. vulgaris adaptation to excess NaCl. Keywords: stress response, one time point (the mid-log phase for both control and treated cells)

DESIGN
Comparison of cells treated with 250 Salt:NaCl to untreated cells at times of mid-log phase min.
