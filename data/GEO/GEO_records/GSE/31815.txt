TITLE
ccpA mutant compared to D39 wild-type in Streptococcus pneumoniae in CDM + Glucose at MID-log growth phase

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pneumoniae D39

SUMMARY
Comparison of Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 ccpA mutant compared to D39 wild type in CDM with glucose as sole carbon source to define the regulon of carbon catabolite control protein CcpA under this condition Details described in Carvalho SM, Kloosterman TG, Neves AR, Kuipers OP. CcpA Ensures Optimal Metabolic Fitness of Streptococcus pneumoniae. Plos One 2011

DESIGN
One condition design comparision of two strains including a dye swap
