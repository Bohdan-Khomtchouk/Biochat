TITLE
RNAseq of wild type and Toddler mutant zebrafish embryos

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Goal of this study is differential gene expression between wild type and Toddler mutant during early zebrafish embryogenesis

DESIGN
Four timepoints - 4 hours post fertilization (hpf), 5 hpf, 6 hpf, and 7 hpf; one replicate of wild type at each time point, one replicate Toddler mutant at each time point

PLATFORM
GPL18413 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Danio rerio)
