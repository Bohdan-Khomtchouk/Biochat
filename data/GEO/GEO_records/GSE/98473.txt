TITLE
Androgen receptor agonists increase muscle mass and function in preclinical models of DMD

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Mdx mice were castrated and treated with selective androgen receptor modulator (SARM) GTx-026. Ten weeks after treatment, gastrocnemious muscle were isolated, RNA extracted, and sequenced using ion torrent sequencer.

DESIGN
n=3/group. Group-1 wildtype mice, group-2 mdx mice treated with vehicle, and group-3 mdx mice treated with 10 mg/kg s.c. GTx-026
