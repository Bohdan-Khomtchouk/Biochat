TITLE
rhythmic transcriptome in the murine wt adrenal

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study was performed to characterize the rhythmic transcirptome of the murine adrenal gland with a focus on the corticosterone synthesis pathway. Keywords: time course

DESIGN
LD entrained male animals were released into DD and from the second day on whole adrenals were prepared from 12 time points at 4 hrs intervals.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
