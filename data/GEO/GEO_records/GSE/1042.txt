TITLE
Cardiac endothelial in vivo responses to LPS

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional responses of cardiac endothelial cells isolated from Tie-2 GFP mice 4 h and 24 h after intraperitoneal LPS injection.  Operon V1 long oligo set.  Hybridization using Cy3 and Cy5 labeled amplified RNA. Keywords: parallel sample
