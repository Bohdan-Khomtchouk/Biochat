TITLE
mRNA profiles of activated and resting T cells and their exosomes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of exosomal and cellular mRNAs in activated and resting primary human T lymphoblasts

DESIGN
Primary human T lymphoblasts were obtained from eight donors and total RNA was isolated from cells and derived exosomes before and after activation with PMA plus ionomycin.
