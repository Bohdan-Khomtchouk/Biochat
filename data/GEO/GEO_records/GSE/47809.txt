TITLE
Transcriptomic profiling associated with temozolomide (TMZ) response of glioblastoma mouse model

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The gene expression of an orthotopic mouse model developed from human glioblastoma neurospheres was compared between temozolomide treated and untreated mice.

DESIGN
Sampling consisted of seven samples: 3 untreated cerebral tumors versus 4 cerebral tumors treated with Temodal.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
