TITLE
Genes upregulated in old Candida glabrata

ORGANISM
[Candida] glabrata

SUMMARY
Comparison of young and old Candida glabrata cells reveals regulation of genes involved in cell wall remodeling, sterol biogenesis, stress pathways

DESIGN
Transcriptome of 0-2 generation-old (young) Candida glabrata cells of strain BG2 were compared to 12-14 generation-old (old) Candida glabrata cells of strain BG2

PLATFORM
GPL21066 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Candida glabrata)
