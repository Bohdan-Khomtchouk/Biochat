TITLE
Subgroup specific somatic copy number aberrations in the medulloblastoma genome [gDNA]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Affymetrix SNP6 profiling of 1087 medulloblastoma samples and 10 medulloblastoma cell lines.

DESIGN
Genomic DNA was extracted from primary medulloblastoma samples and medulloblastoma cell lines and hybridized to Affymetrix SNP6 arrays according to the manufacturer's instructions.

PLATFORM
GPL6801 [GenomeWideSNP_6] Affymetrix Genome-Wide Human SNP 6.0 Array
