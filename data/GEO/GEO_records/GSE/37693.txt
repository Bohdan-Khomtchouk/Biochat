TITLE
Gene Expression Effects of IL-13 on Primary Human Airway Epithelial Cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Primary culture airway epithelial cells, grown under physiologic air-liquid interface conditions, with, or without IL-13 in order to study the effects of this cytokine on mucous cell metaplasia, an important feature of asthma and COPD. Keywords: IL13, mucus, goblet cell

DESIGN
RNA was isolated from primary culture airway epithelial cells grown at air-liquid interface, treated with or without IL-13 for 21 days.
