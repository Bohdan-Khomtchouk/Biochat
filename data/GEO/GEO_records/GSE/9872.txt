TITLE
Gene expression in the Antarctic fish Trematomus bernacchii shows a muted response to heat stress

ORGANISM
Gillichthys mirabilis

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to measure the effect of heat stress on the transcriptome of a cold-adapted fish (Trematomus bernacchii) and compare it to a temperate species (Gillichthys mirabilis). This SuperSeries is composed of the SubSeries listed below.
