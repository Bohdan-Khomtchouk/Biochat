TITLE
OKF6/Tert2 Human oral cells : Control vs transfected with E6E7 HPV16

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of OKF6/Tert2 oral cell transfected with E6E7 HPV-16 genes using pLXSN vector. Control cells were transfected with the empty pLXSN  vector

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, OKF6/Tert2 E6E7 vs OKF6/tert2 V. Two dye-swap Cy3/Cy5 replicates per sample.

PLATFORM
GPL14838 HEEBO Human 48.5K Microarrays Inc
