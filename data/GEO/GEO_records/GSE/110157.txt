TITLE
BMS_Prednisolone_WB_InVitro_study

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study is to validate genes that are regulated by glucocorticoids or partial GR agonists in whole blood in vitro.

DESIGN
Human whole blood was cultured for 5 hours with either dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO) vehicle,  5 μM prednisolone, 5 μM BMS-791826, or 10 μM BMS-776532.

PLATFORM
GPL3921 [HT_HG-U133A] Affymetrix HT Human Genome U133A Array
