TITLE
Tiling-set aCGH data for oral dysplasias and oral squamous cell carcinomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of DNA from 89 oral lesions by whole genome tiling-path array comparative genomic hybridization. Keywords: array comparative genomic hybridization

DESIGN
Genomic DNA isolated from 89 formalin-fixed paraffin-embedded oral dysplasias and tumors, then profiled by whole genome tiling-path array CGH to identify DNA copy alterations for each case.
