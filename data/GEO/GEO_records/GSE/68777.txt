TITLE
Association of DNA Methylation with Acute Mania and Inflammatory Markers

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of serum samples from 20 individuals hospitalized with acute mania and 20 unaffected controls using the Illumina 450K methylation arrays.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 40 samples were hybridised to the Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip arrays
