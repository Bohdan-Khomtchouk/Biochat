TITLE
FREP3 is a determinant of resistance to schistosome infection in snails

ORGANISM
Biomphalaria glabrata

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profile of snails exposed to irradiated E. paraensei miricidia and four days later challenged with S. mansoni miricidia.  Compared to snails exposed to only irradiated E. paraensei miricidia.

DESIGN
Each replicate is comprised of 1 individual snail from the specified treatment group.  Six replicates of each treatment were analyzed on the Snail oligo array.

PLATFORM
GPL9483 Biomphalaria glabrata 70-mer oligo array version 1
