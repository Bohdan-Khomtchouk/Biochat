TITLE
Development of a prognostic gene signature in pancreatic cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Microarray analysis of 63 patients with pancreatic cancer tissues resulted in the identification of a 15-gene signature to predict overall survival

DESIGN
RNA was extracted from microdissected frozen pancreatic tissues for gene array analysis
