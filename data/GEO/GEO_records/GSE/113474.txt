TITLE
RNA Sequencing of adult high grade glioblastoma tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNA extracted from fresh frozen adult high grade glioblastomas were sequenced for transcriptomic analysis.

DESIGN
Fresh frozen glioblastoma human primary tumor samples (24 IDH WT glioblastomas, Age: Min = 29, Max = 84, median = 65; 12 Male, 12 Female)

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
