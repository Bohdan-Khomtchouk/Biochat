TITLE
RNA-Seq of human astrocytes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens ; Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Astrocytes were purified from fetal and adult human brain tissue using an immunopanning method with the HepaCAM antibody. Samples were taken from otherwise 'healthy' pieces of tissue, unless otherwise specified.

DESIGN
6 fetal astrocyte samples, 12 adult astrocyte samples, 8 GBM or sclerotic hippocampal samples, 4 whole human cortex samples, 4 adult mouse astrocyte samples, and 11 human samples of other purified CNS cell types

PLATFORM
GPL18573 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Homo sapiens) GPL19057 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Mus musculus)
