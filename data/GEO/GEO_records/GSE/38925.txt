TITLE
Hepatic gene expression in chinook salmon (Oncorhynchus tshawytscha) exposed to sewage

ORGANISM
Oncorhynchus mykiss ; Oncorhynchus tshawytscha

SUMMARY
Hepatic transcriptional profiling of fish exposed to sewage to evaluate temporal and concentration trends.

DESIGN
Two experiments (Year 1 and 2), each with 6 concentrations (0%, 0.05%, 0.1%, 0.7%, 2% and 5/10%) of sewage diluted in seawater at 4 timepoints (1, 4, 8 and 16 days).

PLATFORM
GPL15697 Environment Canada PESC 207 gene rainbow trout
