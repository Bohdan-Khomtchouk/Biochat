TITLE
DNA methylation Landscape of body size variation in sheep

ORGANISM
Ovis aries

SUMMARY
Examination the DNA methylation statues of the main subpopulation of Chinese Mongolian sheep. A high quality methylome of Chinese Mongolian sheep was obtained, and established a list of DMRs potentially association with sheep body size

DESIGN
Blood DNA methylation level of main Chinese Mongolian sheep was generated by deep sequencing. Subpopulation (breed) of Chinese Mongolian sheep: UQ: Ujumqin sheep Tan: Tan sheep StH: Small-tailed-Han sheep Hu: Hu sheep
