TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of rice infected with Rice black streak dwarf virus

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa Japonica Group

SUMMARY
Rice black streak dwarf virus (RBSDV) is the causal agent of rice black streak dwarf disease which causes severe loss of rice yield in Asia countries. In this study, we have analyzed the relationship between symptom and host gene responses by RBSDV infection.

DESIGN
Comparison between RBSDV and mock infected rice. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 infected, independently grown and harvested. 1 samples derived from 5 plants grown under same conditons

PLATFORM
GPL7252 Custom GER rice oligoarray
