TITLE
The methylome of Alu repeats in human stem cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Next Generation Sequencing of Unmethylated Alu (NSUMA) interrogation of more than 130,000 individual Alus for differential methylation with concomitant analysis of copy number variations applied to the study of hypomethylation in human stem cells and during differentiation.

DESIGN
hESC, colorectal cancer cell lines, iPSC, keratinocyte derived cell lines

PLATFORM
GPL9115 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Homo sapiens) GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
