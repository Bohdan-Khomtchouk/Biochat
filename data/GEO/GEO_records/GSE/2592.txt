TITLE
RUNX1 (AML1) knockout in mouse embryo

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Series of samples studying effect of knocking out RUNX1 (a.k.a. AML1) in mouse embryos at 8.5 and 12 days.  Wildtype and knockout animals at the two time points were generated and all conditions done in duplicate. Keywords: other
