TITLE
Transcriptome Analysis of Cold Acclimation in Barley Albina and Xantha Mutants1

ORGANISM
Hordeum vulgare

SUMMARY
we investigated the transcriptome of barley albina and xantha mutants and the corresponding wild type to assess the effect of the chloroplast on expression of cold-regulated genes Keywords: stress response

DESIGN
five barley genotypes with two treatments and three replicates were analysed
