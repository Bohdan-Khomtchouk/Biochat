TITLE
Ovarian Cancer: 19 ovarian tumors of diverse histologic type vs. pool of all 19 tumors.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of 19 ovarian tumors of 5 distinct histologic types (serous, endometrioid, clear cell, mucinous and small cell), to investigate similar and distinct biology associated with each histologic type.

DESIGN
RNA from fresh-frozen ovarian tumor samples were individually hybridized to a pooled RNA from all 19 tumor cases

PLATFORM
GPL10332 Agilent-026652 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K v2 (Feature Number version)
