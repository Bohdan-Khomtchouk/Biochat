TITLE
Variants at multiple loci implicated in both innate and adaptive immune responses are associated with Sjögren’s syndrome

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This is a genome-wide association study performed in Sjogrens syndrome in which gene expression data was used in conjunction with genotype data to perform expression quantitative trait loci (eQTL) analysis.

DESIGN
This is a case/control study.

PLATFORM
GPL6884 Illumina HumanWG-6 v3.0 expression beadchip
