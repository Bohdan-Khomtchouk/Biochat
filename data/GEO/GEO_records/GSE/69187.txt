TITLE
Aging and atherosclerosis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To examine gene expression in young and aged aortas with and without atherosclerosis

DESIGN
Comparison of the aortas of young (2-4 months of age) and aged (12-13 months of age) mice. WT= wild type, KO = LDLr deficient. HDF= high fat diet. CHOW= chow diet
