TITLE
EWS siRNA knock-down in HeLa cell line

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
HeLa cell line was trasfected by a EWS siRNA oligo causing knock-down of EWS or a siRNA scrambled oligo

DESIGN
Three biological replicates were labeled in direct and dye-swap microarray experiments and hybridized onto an Agilent custom splicing-sensitive microarray platform.
