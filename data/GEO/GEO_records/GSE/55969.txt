TITLE
Dynamic study of In vitro murine follicle maturation in 3% alginate gels

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Multilayer murine follicles were grown in 3% alginate and follicles were isolated for transcriptomics analysis at day 0, 2, 4,5,6,and 8 after encapsulation. This data complements to the GSE42795, where follicles were grown in vitro in 0.5% alginate gels

DESIGN
A total of 8 samples were analyzed, each of them repeted in triplicate

PLATFORM
GPL6885 Illumina MouseRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
