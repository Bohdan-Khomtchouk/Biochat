TITLE
Gene expression analysis of Staphyloccoccus aureus USA300 vs USA300 sae knockout

ORGANISM
Staphylococcus aureus

SUMMARY
The study aims to compare gene expression patterns of Staphyloccoccus aureus USA300 vs USA300 sae knockout. Samples were hybridized on aminosilane coated slides with 70-mer oligos.

DESIGN
12 arrays total.
