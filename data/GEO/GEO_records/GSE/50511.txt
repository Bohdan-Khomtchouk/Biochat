ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Methylation analysis of normal lymphocytes, HCT116, RKO and SW480

DESIGN
In vitro methylated DNA (IVD) generated from normal lymphocytes, HCT116, RKO and SW480 genomic DNA was subjected to sodium bisulfite treatment and the DNA was analyzed for CpG methylation using the Infinium Methylation Array.
