TITLE
Transcriptome sequencing of Myotis brandtii

ORGANISM
Myotis brandtii

SUMMARY
Deep sequencing of mRNA from Myotis brandtii.  Analysis of poly(A)+ RNA of livers, kidneys and brains from M. brandtii hibernated for 2 months, hibernated for 6 months and summer active animals.

DESIGN
Livers, kidneys and brains from M. brandtii hibernated for 2 and 6 months, and summer active animals.
