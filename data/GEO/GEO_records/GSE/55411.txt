TITLE
Transcriptional response to tetanus toxoid in pigs with divergent temperament (High-Reactive vs. Low-Reactive) [day 0]

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
in vivo microarray study of transcriptional changes in porcine peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) during the immune response to tetanus vaccination.

DESIGN
Per temperament group, 23 pigs were collected for expression profiling. High-Reactive vs. Low-Reactive.
