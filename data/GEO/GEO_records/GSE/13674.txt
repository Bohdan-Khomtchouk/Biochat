TITLE
microRNA-101 represses EZH2 in cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To determine the phenotypic overlap between restoring miR-101 expression in bladder cancer cells and knocking down EZH2.

DESIGN
Either control precursors, pre-miR-101, control siRNA, or EZH2 siRNA were transfected in triplicate into UM-UC-3 cells for 72 hours at a final concentration of 50 nM.

PLATFORM
GPL5799 Illumina Human-6 v2 expression beadchip
