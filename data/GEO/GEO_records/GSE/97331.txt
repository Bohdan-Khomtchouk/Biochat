TITLE
AML cell line expression response to SY-1425 (tamibarotene)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
AML cell lines were treated with either vehicle or SY-1425 (tamibarotene), a potent and selective agonist of retinoic acid receptor alpha (RARa), and assayed by microarray expression analysis.
