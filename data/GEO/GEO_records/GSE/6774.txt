SUMMARY
Ion channel splice array data from temporal cortex brain tissue samples collected from control subjects (no Alzheimer's disease). Keywords: disease associated splicing changes

DESIGN
Temporal cortex (Alzheimer's disease affected brain tissue structure) and cerebellum (Alzheimer's disease unaffected brain tissue structure) samples from control subjects were compared to temporal cortex and cerebellum of patients with Alzheimer's disease.
