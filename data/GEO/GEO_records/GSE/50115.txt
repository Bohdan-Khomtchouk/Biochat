TITLE
Identification of a gene expression signature in colorectal adenocarcinoma in comparison with colorectal adenoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We showed that a lot of genes were deregulated in colorectal adenocarcinomas in comparison with colorectal adenomas.

DESIGN
37 colorectal adenoma and 9 colorectal adenocarcinoma samples were analyzed. We generated a comparison between adenocarcinomas and adenomas.
