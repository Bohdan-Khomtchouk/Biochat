TITLE
Time course of PBL derived avian macrophages stimulated with whole Ecoli or LPS

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
This series compares gene expression in chicken peripheral blood lymphocyte (PBL) derived macrophages following stimulation with either XL10 E. coli or S. typh LPS over an 8 hour time course (0,1,2,4,and 8 hours). For the late time points, the stimuli were removed after 2 hours and fresh media was added for the remaining time. Keywords: time-course
