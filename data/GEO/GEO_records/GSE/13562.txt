TITLE
Effects of sidA and AHL on EHEC virulence

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
A study on the effects of an sdiA mutant and the AHL molecule on the virulence of EHEC

DESIGN
Comparison of wild type E. coli 8624 with and without AHL to and E. coli 8624 sdiA mutant with and without AHL to determine the effects of each factor on gene expression

PLATFORM
GPL3154 [E_coli_2] Affymetrix E. coli Genome 2.0 Array
