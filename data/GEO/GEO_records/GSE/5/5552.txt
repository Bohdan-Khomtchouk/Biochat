TITLE
E coli O157:H7 w/t LB-Glu 7 hr biofilm cells with various chemicals

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
E coli O157H7 (EHEC) wildtype 7 hour biofilm cells studied in LB glucose medium with and without chemicals - Epinephrine, Norepinephrine and Indole. Biofilm cells were cultured from glass wool. Keywords: Effect of different chemicals on EHEC gene expression
