TITLE
CD8+ memory T cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression profiles of in vitro generated CD8+ central memory (CM) and effector/effector memory (E/EM) T cells on Affymetrix GeneChip Murine Genome U74 Version 2 Set MG-U74A, MG-U74B, and MG-U74C. T cells were generated from female P14 T cell receptor transgenic mice on the C57Bl/6 genetic background. Keywords: other
