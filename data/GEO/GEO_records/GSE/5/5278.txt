TITLE
B. thetaiotaomicron harvested from cecum of P17 and P30 NMRI mice

ORGANISM
Bacteroides thetaiotaomicron

SUMMARY
Gnotobiotic NMRI mice were colonized at birth with B. thetaiotaomicron. Mice were sacrificed at P17 or P30, cecal contents were harvested and used for transcriptional profiling. Keywords: developmental timepoints, in vivo
