TITLE
Effect of photoperiod on gene expression in Peromyscus polionotus brain nuclei

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The effect of photoperiod on gene expression was assessed in punch samples of the bed nucleus of the stria terminalis and medial preoptic area.  Long day mice were housed in 16L:8D and short day mice were housed in 8L:16D. Keywords: cDNA microarray
