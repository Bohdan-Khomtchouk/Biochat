TITLE
MKN28 Cells: Vector vs ZIC1 Transfected

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
MKN28 cell lines were transfected with pCDNA3.1-ZIC1 or pCDNA3.1 empty vector. Then, an Agilent oligonucleotide microarray was conducted to systematically determine downstream targets of ZIC1.

DESIGN
Two samples are analyzed with one two channnel experiment.
