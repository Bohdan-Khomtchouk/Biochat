TITLE
Gene expression profile of Samd7 KO retina at P12

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To analyze the expression profile in the Samd7 KO retina, we performed a microarray analysis using wild-type and Samd7 KO retina at P12.

DESIGN
We performed a microarray analysis using wild-type and Samd7 KO retina at P12, and compared the expression profiles.
