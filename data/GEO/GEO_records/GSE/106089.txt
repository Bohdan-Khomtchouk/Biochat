TITLE
Epigenome analysis of placenta samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of placental samples from the Extremely Low Gestational Age Newborn (ELGAN) study

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from 84 placenta samples were hybridised to the Illumina Human Methylation450 BeaChip array

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
