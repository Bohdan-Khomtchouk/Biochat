TITLE
Pombe_44k_ChIP-on-chip

ORGANISM
Schizosaccharomyces pombe

SUMMARY
ChIP-on-chip analyses of heterochromatin and RNAi components in fission yeast. Keywords: ChIP-on-chip

DESIGN
ChIP-on-chip analyses using custom Agilent 44K  in situ 60mer DNA microarray that tiles the entire Schizosaccharomyces pombe genome every 300 bp alternately on both strands were used to interrogate heterochromatin and RNAi components in fission yeast genome.
