TITLE
Activated Cell Death Pathway

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Identifying key genes to evaluate the therapeutic responses of chemoradiotherapy. Keywords: Cervical cancer, chemoradiotherapy, biopsy, CDKN1A, Bax

DESIGN
Twenty-eight squamous cell carcinoma of cervix from 24 patients were taken as biopsy sample before treatment and during treatment; twice for each patient. Duplicate of each sample were analyzed. Fourteen patients received radiotherapy alone and 10 patients received chemoradiotherapy.
