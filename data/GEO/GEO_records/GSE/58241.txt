TITLE
Gene expression influenced by SF3B1 inhibitor, FR901464 or SF3B1 gene mutations in colorectal cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DLD1, HCT116, DLD1 - derived FR resistant clone DLD FR1, and HCT116 - derived FR resistant clone CT FR1, were assessed for change of gene expression induced by FR901464

DESIGN
Cells were incubated with or without 10 ng/ml of FR901464 for 24h.
