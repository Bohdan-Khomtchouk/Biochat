TITLE
Expression profiling of DRG from L5 spinal nerve ligation model of neuropathic pain in the rat

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
DRG samples were extracted at 28 and 50 days from L5 spinal nerve ligated rats and sham operated rats.  Samples were pooled for each time point per group and hybridised to duplicate to RG_u34 genchips (A, B and C chips). Keywords: Disease state analysis
