TITLE
Individualized analysis of subcutaneous and omental abdominal adipose tissue from non-obese and obese subjects

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Individualized analysis through expression profiling of 20,000 probes in 28 tissue samples evaluated in subcutaneous and omental adipose tissue obtained during surgical intervention in non-obese and obese patients. Patients consisted of men and women of varying body size (lean to severely obese). Samples were collected at the time of operation in the fasting state.

DESIGN
Samples consisted of subcutaneous and omental adipose tissue as well as a blood sample from lean and obese men and women removed in the fasting state at the time of surgery.

PLATFORM
GPL4044 CodeLink UniSet Human 20K I Bioarray
