Listeria monocytogenes

SUMMARY
Affect of growth on cantaloupe at 10°C on the transcriptome of Listeria monocytogenes

DESIGN
A strain isolated from the 2011 cantaloupe outbreak was used to determine what changes in transcription are observed in the organism after reaching stationary phase growth on cantaloupe versus BHI broth.
