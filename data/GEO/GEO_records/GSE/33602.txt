TITLE
Affymetrix SNP array data for acute T-cell leukemia/lymphoma (ATL)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
SNP array analysis was performed using 168 ATL specimens.

DESIGN
To obtain genotypes and intensities for each SNP, Affymetrix 250K SNP arrays were performed for 168 ATL samples.
