TITLE
Next Generation Sequencing Facilitates Quantitative Analysis of miRNA

ORGANISM
Pyrus x bretschneideri ; Pyrus x bretschneideri x Pyrus pyrifolia

SUMMARY
Next-generation sequencing (NGS) has revolutionized systems-based analysis of cellular pathways. The goals of this study are to compare miRNA expressions of pear in two different conditions.

DESIGN
Fruit mRNA profiles after 2 different parent pollination to same female parent

PLATFORM
GPL21787 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Pyrus x bretschneideri) GPL21788 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Pyrus x bretschneideri x Pyrus pyrifolia)
