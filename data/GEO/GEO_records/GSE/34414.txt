TITLE
Identification of the genes regulated by YvfI transcriptional factor

ORGANISM
Bacillus subtilis

SUMMARY
Genome-wide comparative transcriptome analysis of the Bacillus subtilis parent strain PY79 and its yvfI derivative with yvfI::T10::spc (TEK1) grown in PA medium Detailed description of sample preparation and microarray conditions can be also found in Irigul et al to be submitted

DESIGN
One condition design comparision of treated vs non treated control including a dye swap, 3 biological replicates
