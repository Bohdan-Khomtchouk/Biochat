TITLE
Inner Ear Tissues

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
MPSS analysis of signatures from microdissections of mouse inner ear tissues. Keywords: other

DESIGN
Microdissections of day 7 C57Bl/6 animals, male and female, both ears, pooled together. Approximately 14 - 20 ears for each of three libraries:  organ of Corti, sensory epithelia of vestibular system, and stria vascularis.
