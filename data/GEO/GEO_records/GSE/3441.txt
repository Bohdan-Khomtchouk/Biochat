TITLE
Transcript analysis of early nodulation events in Medicago truncatula

ORGANISM
Medicago truncatula

SUMMARY
For transcript analysis of early nodulation events in Medicago truncatula we compared transcripts from inoculated and uninoculated roots corresponding to defined stages between 1 and 72 h post inoculation (hpi). Keywords: time course

DESIGN
Time course of 6 time points consists of 36 microarrays. Each time point experiment consists of three direct (reps1, 2, 3) and three dye-swapped (reps 4, 5, 6) technical replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL2930 Medicago truncatula 6K array
