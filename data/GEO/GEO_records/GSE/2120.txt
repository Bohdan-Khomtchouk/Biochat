TITLE
Growth Hormone (GH) Treatment of 3T3-F442A Adipocytes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of 3T3-F442A adipocytes treated with growth hormone (GH, 500 nM) or vehicle (DMEM + 1% BSA) control for 30 min., 4 hr., or 48 hr in three independent experiments. Chronic GH treatment induces metabolic changes consistent with insulin resistance in 3T3-F442A adipocytes. Keywords: time-course
