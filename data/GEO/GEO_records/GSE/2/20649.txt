TITLE
MicroRNA-Directed siRNA Biogenesis in Caenorhabditis elegans

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
C.elegans small RNAs from HA::ALG-1, HA::ALG-2 and HA::RDE-1 IP and rde-1 mutants

DESIGN
Small RNAs were cloned from transgenic or mutant C. elegans adults. Sequencing was performed using 454 and Illumina platforms.
