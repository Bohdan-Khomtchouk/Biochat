TITLE
Landscape of somatic allelic imbalances and copy number alterations in human lung carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Lung cancer is the worldwide leading cause of death from cancer. This GEO series correspond to one of the BAC aCGH data sets used as validation cohort for the study: Landscape of somatic allelic imbalances and copy number alterations in human lung cancer, Int J Cancer 2013.

DESIGN
Genomic profiling of 78 lung carcinomas using 32K BAC aCGH microarrays.
