TITLE
CommercialWineYeastStrainsCGH

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Microarray karyotyping (aCGH) of three independent isolates each of four different commerical wine yeast strains was performed.  Duplicate arrays (labeled " A " and " B " ) were performed for each isolate. An all pairs experiment design type is where all labeled extracts are compared to every other labeled extract. Keywords: all_pairs
