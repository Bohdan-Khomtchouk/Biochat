TITLE
ChIP-chip mapping of twist, dorsal, snail, weckle in Drosophila embryos

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
We performed ChIP-chip analysis using sheared chromatin isolated from Drosophila embryos of 1-3 hours in age. Keywords: high-resolution tiling array, drosophila, twist, dorsal, snail, weckle

DESIGN
ChIP-chip of twist, dorsal, snail, and weckle
