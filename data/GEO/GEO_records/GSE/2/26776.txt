TITLE
Transcriptome-wide changes in HeLa cell line investigated in response to Heat-shock stress

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The repertoire of transcripts that are differentially regulated in response to Heat-shock were studied using Illumina WG-6 v2.0 BeadChip.

DESIGN
Study was undertaken with 3 biological replicates for each condition of Heat-Shock Treated and Untreated condition.
