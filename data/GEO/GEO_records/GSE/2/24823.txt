TITLE
Genomic profiling of typical and atypical chronic lymphocytic leukemia patients using Agilent Whole Human Genome microarrays

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Deciphering typical and atypical chronic lymphocytic leukemia genomics.

DESIGN
DNA was isolated from peripheral blood leucocyte samples of each patient using the QIAamp kit in order to compare typical and atypical chronic lymphocytic leukemia patients using Agilent Whole Human Genome 44K and 244K microarrays.
