TITLE
Germline PTEN promoter variants affect the expression profile in breast tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparison of the tumors PTEN promoter variant carrier and non carrier gene expression profiles.

DESIGN
Expression profiles of 10 breast cancer patients with PTEN promoter variant and 10 controls matches for histology, ER, PR, T, N, M, Grade, HER2, Ki67, and p53.
