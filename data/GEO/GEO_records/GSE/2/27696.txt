TITLE
Effect of ovarian stimulation on oocyte gene expression in cow

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
The objective of the study was to analyze the impact of FSH on transcriptome changes of in vivo bovine oocytes. Oocytes were collected from naturally ovulated and superovulated animals at 2 hours pre-LH surge, 6 hours post-LH surge, and 22 hours post-LH surge.

DESIGN
Six-condition experiment. Biological replicates: 1 . Technical replicate:2.
