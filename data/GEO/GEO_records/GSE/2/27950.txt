TITLE
Genome-wide Expression Differences in Colonocytes Isolated From Conventionally Raised or Germfree Mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray transcriptome analysis of colonocytes from conventionally raised and germfree mice.

DESIGN
12 samples were analyzed, with 6 germfree (GF) samples vs. 6 conventionally raised (CONV) samples.
