TITLE
Expression profiling of DHR38-dependent transcripts in Drosophila larvae

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Drosophila third instar larvae comparing control (w1118) and DHR38 mutants (DHR38Y214/Df(2)KetelRX32)

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, control vs. DHR38 mutant larvae. Biological replicates: 3 replicates.
