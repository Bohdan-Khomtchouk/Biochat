TITLE
Host pathogen interaction between Staphylococcus aureus ATCC25923 and murine RAW264.7 macrophage-like cells

ORGANISM
Staphylococcus aureus

SUMMARY
Macrophages infected with S. aureus were subjected to gene expression profiling to undertake a complete understanding of the interaction induced gene expression changes in both, S.aureus and the RAW macrophages.
