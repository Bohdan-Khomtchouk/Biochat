TITLE
Expression data from human CD34+ HPC subpopulations isolated from umbilical cord blood (Haddad et al. Blood 104:3918, 2004)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We used microarrays to analyze the gene expression profile of CD34+CD45RA+CD7+, CD34+CD45RA+CD10+CD19- and CD34+CD45+CD7-CD10-CD19- HPCs isolated from umbilical cord blood CD34+CD45RA+CD7+(CD10-) and CD34+CD45RA+CD10+(CD7-CD19-) HPCs correspond respectively to prothymocytes and early pre-proB precursors. CD34+CD45RA+CD7-CD10-CD19- HPCs  correspond to lympho-granulo-macrophagic precursors
