TITLE
Hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) tumors and cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA copy number profiling for all primary tumor samples and HCC cell lines was performed by ROMA, a form of comparative genomic hybridization.  The aim was to identify commonly amplifiied and deleted regions across human HCC.

DESIGN
88 primary HCC samples and 12 human HCC cell lines were analyzed.
