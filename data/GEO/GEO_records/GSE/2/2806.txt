TITLE
Transcriptional profile of an acs2Ts1 mutant yeast

ORGANISM
Schizosaccharomyces pombe

SUMMARY
Wild-type and the acs2Ts1 mutant yeasts were shifted from 25deg to 37deg. After 60 minutes, Yeasts were harvested and divided into 2 x 2 cell samples. Total RNAs were purified from 4 populations. Keywords: WT vs mutant
