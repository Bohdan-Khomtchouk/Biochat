TITLE
Mapping Determinants of Human Gene Expression by Regional and Whole Genome Association

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Baseline expression levels of genes in CEPH individuals from the International HapMap Project were measured using the Affymetrix Human Genome Focus Arrays.  Arrays were analyzed using MAS 5.0 software (Affymetrix). Keywords = Gene Expression, lymphoblastoid cells, human Keywords: other
