TITLE
Sex-biased suppression of chemically induced neural carcinogenesis in congenic BDIX-BDIV-Mss4a rats

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Gender specific expression profiling of congenic BDIX-BDIV-Mss4a rats vs. BDIX and BDIV parental strains using trigeminal nerve tissue

DESIGN
Total RNA obtaines from pooled, isolated trigeminal nerves from untreated 85 days old males and females of inbred strains BDIX, BDIV and BDIX.BDIV-Mss4a were compared
