TITLE
Genome-wide maps of chromatin state in staged Drosophila embryos and cell lines.

ORGANISM
modENCODE

SUMMARY
This is a dataset generated by the Drosophila Regulatory Elements modENCODE Project led by Kevin P. White at the University of Chicago. It contains ChIP-seq data generated on Solexa Genome Analyzer for several Transcription Factors and Chromatin associated Proteins. This dataset is called February_1st_datafreeze and is part of the regular datafreezes we produce for the modENCODE project.
