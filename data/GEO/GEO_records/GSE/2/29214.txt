TITLE
Macrophages in Human Colorectal Cancer are Pro-inflammatory and Prime T Cells towards the Anti-Tumor Type-1 Inflammatory Response

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of interaction between tumor cells and macrophages at the gene expression level.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from tumor cells cultured as (1) spheroids alone, (2) tumour cells sorted out from co-culture spheroids with macrophages, and (3) co-culture spheroids of tumor cells and macrophages
