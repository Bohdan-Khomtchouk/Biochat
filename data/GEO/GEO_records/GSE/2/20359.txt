TITLE
Comparison of normal mouse hematopoietic stem cells (HSC) to mobilized and leukemia-inducing hematopoietic stem cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Highly purified Hematopoietic stem cells (HSC) from mouse bone marrow (BM) were compared to HSC after two days of cytoxan/GCSF treatment (Cyclophosphamide/granulocyte colony-stimulating factor) (Day2Mob) and leukemic HSC from mice lacking JunB. See Forsberg et. al. 2010 for details. CML - chronic myelogenous leukemia.

DESIGN
Biological Replicate
