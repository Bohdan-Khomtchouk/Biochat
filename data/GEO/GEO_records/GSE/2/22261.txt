TITLE
Fathead minnow pharmaceuticals

ORGANISM
Pimephales promelas

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles of fathead minnow brain tissue following treatment to fluoxetine (Prozac), carbamazepine, venlafaxine, and a mixture of all three; compared to untreated controls.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, treated vs. controls. Biological replicates of 4 treatment and a control.  Additional alcohol control (solvent carrier) has only one array.
