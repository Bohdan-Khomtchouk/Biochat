TITLE
Sugar utilization in L. plantarum WCFS1

ORGANISM
Lactobacillus plantarum WCFS1

SUMMARY
Lactobacillus plantarum was grown anaerobically on 4 different sugars (Mannose Lactose Fructose and Sucrose) to OD600 = 1.0. Samples were compared with a similar grown culture on glucose. An independnet biological duplicate of tht experimnet was performed (samples 1 and 2).
