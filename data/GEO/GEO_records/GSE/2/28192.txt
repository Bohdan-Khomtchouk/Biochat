TITLE
MB_xenograft_models

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Classification of medulloblastoma xengrafts models

DESIGN
primary human patient medulloblastoma samples were implanted into the corresponding position in SCID mice, the subsequent tumors gene expression profiles were compared with those of primary tumor samples to see if the overall transcriptomes were preserved. Repeat of each sample was the same labeled aRNA loaded onto another chip. Control samples include normal fetal brain, and normal adult cerebellum.
