TITLE
Hoxc13 overexpression in skin

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
A study of the effects of Hoxc13 overexpression in skin using Hoxc13 transgenic mice (GC13) known to develop severe hair growth defects and alopecia. Total skin from GC13 mice 5 dpn is compared to total skin from normal FVB mice 5 dpn using Affymetrix HG-U74Av2 GeneChips. Keywords: repeat sample
