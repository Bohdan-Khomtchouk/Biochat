TITLE
Cell of origin strongly influences genetic selection in a mouse model of T-ALL.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Thymic lymphomas were generated by inducing Sleeping Beauty transposon mutagenesis at different stages of T-cell development.

DESIGN
This dataset includes exon array results from 14 tumor samples from two different Sleeping Beauty models of T-ALL (7 Vav-SB and 7 CD4-SB samples).
