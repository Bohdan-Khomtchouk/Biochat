TITLE
Circulating miRNAs as biomarkers and potential functional mediators of early stage breast cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of the levels of circulating miRNAs from women with early stage breast cancer and matched healthy controls.

DESIGN
miRNAs in plasma samples from 20 women with early stage breast cancer (10 Caucasian American and 10 African American) compared with 20 matched healthy controls (10 Caucasian American and 10 African American).
