TITLE
SAGE analysis of normal human thyroid tissue

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This library was generated from normal human thyroid tissue collected at routine autopsy. Standard SAGE protocol was used (Velculescu et al. Science 1995). Data from this library was published in: 'Pauws E, Moreno JC, Tijssen M, Baas F, de Vijlder JJ, Ris-Stalpers C. J Clin Endocrinol Metab. 2000 May;85(5):1923-7' Data was generated and analyzed at the laboratory of Pediatric Endocrinology, Academic Medial Center, Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

DESIGN
This GEO Series was created by the GEO staff as part of a cleanup effort to ensure that all GEO Samples are included within a Series entry.
