TITLE
Whole-genome microarray of adipose derived mesenchymal stem cells from differentially-aged mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
whole-genome microarray profiling of mesenchymal stem cells isolated from the inguinal fat pad of mice of different age.

DESIGN
Whole genome transcriptional profile of mesenchymal stem cells isolated from FVB mice of 1 (n=3) or 12 (n=3) months of age.
