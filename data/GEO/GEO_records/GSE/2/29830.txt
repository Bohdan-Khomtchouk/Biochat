TITLE
Expression analysis of colorectal carcinoma cells with overexpressed TP53 using MAT

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Whole transcriptome expression analysis of colorectal carcinoma cells with induced expression of TP53 on Affymetrix Human Tiling 1.0 array set. Expression data were processed with MAT.

DESIGN
We analyzed one Affymetrix Human Tiling 1.0R set
