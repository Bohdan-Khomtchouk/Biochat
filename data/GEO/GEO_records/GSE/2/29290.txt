TITLE
Evaluation of the Infinium Methylation 450K technology

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide DNA methylation profiling of HCT116 WT, HCT116 DNMT1 and DNMT3B double KO, and breast cancer tumors by next generation Infinium assay

DESIGN
Bisulfite converted DNA from 22 samples were hybridized to the Illumina's HumanMethylation450 BeadChip
