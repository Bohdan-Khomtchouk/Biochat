TITLE
Expression data from transgenic rice with OsEATB

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
Rice ethylene-response AP2/ERF factor OsEATB restricts internode elongation by down-regulating gibberellin biosynthetic gene

DESIGN
Leaf tissues of 4-leaf-old transgenic and nontransgenic seedlings (10 plants each), respectively, were selected.
