TITLE
Rat mammary expression in individuals and pools

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Rat mammary glands were obtained from individual rats in RXR treated (a) and control (b) conditions (12 rats in each condition). The 24 samples were hybridized individually.  Also, in each condition, samples were combined into different pools of 2, pools of 3, pools of 12. Technical replicates were also run. Keywords: other
