TITLE
hPTTG1-/- HCT116 human colorectal cancer cells: pcDNA3.1 control vs. pcDNA3.1-hPTTG1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of  hPTTG1-/- HCT116 human colorectal cancer cells comparing hPTTG1-/- HCT116 cells transfected with pcDNA3.1, and with hPTTG1-/- HCT116 cells transfected with pcDNA3.1-hPTTG1 plasmid.
