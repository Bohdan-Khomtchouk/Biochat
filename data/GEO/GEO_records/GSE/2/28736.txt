TITLE
BATF knockout B cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
compare wild type and Batf-/- B cells activated for 0 1 or 2 days in vitro. WT or Batf deficient B cells were activated in vitro with LPS for 1 or 2 days, or left untreated.

DESIGN
B cells were purified by MACS and activated, harvested and prepped for affymetrix gene expression profiling.
