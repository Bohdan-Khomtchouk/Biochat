TITLE
Gene expression profiles of in vitro fertilized bovine embryos in comparison to those of bovine adult tissues

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Affymetrix Human GeneChips are used to profile gene expression of bovine tissues and embryos to identify uniquely expressed genes in bovine in-vitro fertilized embryos by comparing with seven bovine adult tissues through gene clustering Keywords: other

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
