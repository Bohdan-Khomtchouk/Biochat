TITLE
Exosomal microRNA analysis of patients with multiple myeloma and healthy adults

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We report the microRNA expression in patients with multiple myeloma and healthy adults. RNA sequencing was performed for circulating exosomes obtained from the serum of 10 MM patients and 5 healthy individuals.

DESIGN
Exosomal microRNAs in multiple myeloma patients and healthy adults were extracted and sequenced.
