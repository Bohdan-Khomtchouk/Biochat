TITLE
Avian duodenum tissues: primary vs. secondary E. acervulina infected

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
Global transcriptional responses in duodenal intestinal epithelia of chickens following primary and secondary Eimeria acervulina infections.

DESIGN
Simple loop hybridization (day 0 vs. days 1-2, days 1-2 vs. 3-4, days 3-4 vs. 5-6, and days 5-6 vs. 7-8) per primary or secondary infections.

PLATFORM
GPL8595 USDA 9.6K avian intestinal intraepithelial lymphocyte cDNA microarray (AVIELA) version 1
