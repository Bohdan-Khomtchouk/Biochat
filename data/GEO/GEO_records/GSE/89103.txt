TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of T. reesei CBS999.97, backcrossed female fertile strains in QM6a genetic background and QM6a upon growth on cellulose

ORGANISM
Trichoderma reesei

SUMMARY
The aim of this study was to evaluate changes in the transcriptome in the mentioned strains upon growth on cellulose

DESIGN
Strains of this study were grown on Mandels Andreotti Minimal Medium with 1 % w/v microcrystalline cellulose as sole carbon source in  constant darkness for 72 hours; 2 biological replicates were included
