TITLE
irs4 versus CAI-12

ORGANISM
Candida albicans

SUMMARY
Comparison of expression profiling of irs4 null mutant versus isogenic strain (CAI-12) grown in blood (3 replicates)

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, irs4 null mutant versus CAI-12 wild-type. Biological replicates: 3 irs4 null mutant, 3 wild-type. Two replicates per array.
