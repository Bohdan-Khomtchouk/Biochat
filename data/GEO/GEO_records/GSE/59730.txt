TITLE
CRTC1 overexpression in Caco-2 colon cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To evaluate the genes differentially regulated by CRTC1 in colonic epithelial tumor.

DESIGN
Subconfluent Caco-2 cells were transfected with 200ng of CRTC1 expression plasmid or corresponding empty vector (pBS) overnight. N = 4 per group.

PLATFORM
GPL17930 [HuGene-2_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 2.0 ST Array [HuGene20stv1_Hs_ENTREZG_17.0.0]
