TITLE
Effect of Growth hormone on podocytes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis of growth hormone dependant genes in glomerular podocytes

DESIGN
Differentiated human glomerular podocytes in culture exposed to growth hormone for 0 min, 2 min, 5 min, 15 min, and 30 min. Total RNA is extracted and subjected to microarray analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
