TITLE
Brain RNA-seq one day intra-head injection of corazonin or control peptide in Harpegnathos ants.

ORGANISM
Harpegnathos saltator

SUMMARY
This experiment aimed at determining the immediate (d1) transcriptional consequences of corazonin in the brain.

DESIGN
Six dueling Harpegnathos individuals were removed from the transition colony and injected with corazonin or control peptide.

GPL22089 Illumina NextSeq 500 (Harpegnathos saltator)
