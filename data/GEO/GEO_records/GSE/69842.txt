TITLE
Illumina Total RNA-seq in HeLa

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Purpose: Examine the effect of the long non-coding RNA PARROT on the transcriptome in HeLa cells.

DESIGN
Total RNA-seq of  RNA from cells treated with the control knock-down (NK) or depleted of ENST00000046668 (PARROT) with two different siRNAs (si1 and si2) for 24h.
