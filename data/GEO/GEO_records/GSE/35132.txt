TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of EGR2/SOX10 binding in myelinating peripheral nerve

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
We provide ChIP-Seq analysis of Egr2 and Sox10 transcription factor binding in Schwann cells of rat peripheral nerve

DESIGN
ChIP-Seq analysis of Egr2 and Sox10 binding in P15 rat sciatic nerve. Wiggle files of negative log of posterior probability determined by Mosaics using rat genome Rn4

PLATFORM
GPL10669 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Rattus norvegicus)
