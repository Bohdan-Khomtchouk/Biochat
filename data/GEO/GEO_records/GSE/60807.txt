TITLE
Renal 0-hour biopsies

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Profiling of 0-hour wedge biopsies from deceased kidney donors (without signs of AKI) to recognize differences in age groups.

DESIGN
mRNA profiling of n=37 samples from from human subjects with no sign of AKI, thus healthy kidney; comparison of three age groups ( < 40; 40 - 60; > 60 years)
