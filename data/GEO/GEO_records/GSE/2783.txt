TITLE
GSC vs Kc

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
This series compares gene expression between germ line stem cells (GSCs) purified either from bam mutant or Dpp-overexpressing ovaries, with gene expression in Kc cells Keywords: bam mutant, Dpp overexpression

DESIGN
3 replicates of GSCs from bam mutant ovaries 3 replicates of GSCs from Dpp overexpressing ovaries 3 replicates of Kc tissue culture cells

PLATFORM
GPL72 [DrosGenome1] Affymetrix Drosophila Genome Array
