TITLE
The pitfalls of platform comparison: DNA copy number array technologies assessed

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A study of the relative merits of four leading copy number profiling platforms.

DESIGN
Breast tumours and their corresponding matched normals or a set of pooled normals, cancer cell-lines, and HapMap samples were hybridized to the Affymetrix SNP5.0, Agilent 244A,  Illumina HumanCNV370-duo BeadChips, and Nimblegen 385k array.
