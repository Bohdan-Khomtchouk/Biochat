TITLE
Small RNA Profiling of Murine Adult Stomach

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Murine Adult Stomach Small RNA

DESIGN
Stomach samples were collected from adult mice.  Small RNA was isolated from samples and cDNA libraries were generated for PGM sequencing.  Sequences were aligned with Torrent Server tMap.
