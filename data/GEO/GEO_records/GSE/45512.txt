TITLE
Human Alopecia Areata Skin Profiling

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Our goal was to develop a transcriptomic description of affected alopecic scalp skin from patients with alopecia areata.

DESIGN
5 biological replicates of skin samples from 5 separate AA patients compared with 5 similar scalp samples from healthy control patients

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
