TITLE
Evaluation of microRNA expression profiles and their associations with risk alleles in lymphoblastoid cell lines of familial ovarian cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We evaluated the genome-wide microRNA expression profiles in lymphoblastoid cell lines of familial ovarian cancer patients and controls

DESIGN
We used microarray to perform microRNA profiling in lymphoblastoid cell lines from 74 women with familial ovarian cancer patients and 47 controls.

PLATFORM
GPL8179 Illumina Human v2 MicroRNA expression beadchip
