TITLE
Gene regulation and DNA damage in the aging human brain

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of the human frontal cortex from individuals ranging from 26 to 106 years of age. The postmortem brain tissue samples used in this study were neuropathologically normal for age, and were derived from non-demented individuals. Keywords: other
