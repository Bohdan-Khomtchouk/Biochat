TITLE
Rp1 knockout mice retinal development

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Mice were a mixed background between 129/SvEv and C57BL/6. Rp1 knockout mice (Rp1-/-) and wildtype littermates examined. At P7, P10, P14, P18 and P21, mice were sacrificed and retinas removed. Each RNA sample included a pool of neural retinas from 3-4 mice. Retinas were all collected at 1-2 pm. Keywords: other

GPL81 [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
