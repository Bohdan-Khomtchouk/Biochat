TITLE
A familial disorder of altered DNA-methylation

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
In a subset of imprinting disorders caused by epimutations, multiple imprinted loci are affected. We have investigated the clinical and molecular features of a familial DNA methylation disorder.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 60 samples were hybridised to the Illumina Infinium 27k Human Methylation Beadchip
