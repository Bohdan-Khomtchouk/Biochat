TITLE
Single-base resolution methylome of Xenopus laevis embryos

ORGANISM
Xenopus laevis

SUMMARY
Here we describe a base-resolution DNA methylation map of Xenopus laevis gastrula (st.10.5) embryos generated by whole genome bisulfite sequencing

DESIGN
WGBS profiling of Xenopus laevis st.10.5 embryos
