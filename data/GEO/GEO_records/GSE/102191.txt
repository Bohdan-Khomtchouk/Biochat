TITLE
RNA sequencing of Hordeum vulgare near-isogenic lines

ORGANISM
Hordeum vulgare

SUMMARY
RNA sequencing of ILs of vrs1, vrs3, vrs4 and int-c.5 in cv. Bowman Purpose: Expression analysis and variant calling

DESIGN
Enriched apical meristem tissue was isolated at Waddington stage (W) 3.5 and W5.0. The samples were harvested at the middle of the day, 6 to 8 h before the end of the light period.

PLATFORM
GPL22077 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Hordeum vulgare)
