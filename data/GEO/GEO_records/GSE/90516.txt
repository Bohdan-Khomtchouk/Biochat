TITLE
RNA-Seq of mESCs based on sex chromosome complement

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA-Seq was performed on mESCs generated from F1 hybrids from reciprocal crosses of C57BL/6J and CAST/EiJ, denoted B and C hereafter; cell lines are designated as BC or CB, with the maternal line symbolized first

DESIGN
3 XX and 3 XY lines were used from each cross as well as two XO lines for a total of 14 samples.

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
