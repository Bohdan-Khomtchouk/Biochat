TITLE
CD24 targets

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression were identified with or without CD24 gene silencing in Du145 cells.

DESIGN
RNA isolated from 5 independent cultures of Scramble control, 3 cultures of CD24-ShRNA1 and 2 cultures of CD24-ShRNA2 were tested using Affimatrx Human U133 Plus 2.0 microarrays.
