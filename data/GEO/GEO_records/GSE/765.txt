TITLE
Control vs CF Small Intestine

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Total RNA was prepared from the entire small intestines of 40 day old Control and CFTR null mice (2 males and 1 female of each genotype), congenic on the black6 background, using TRIzol reagent. Mice were fed Peptamen from age 10 days to prevent intestinal obstruction. Keywords: parallel sample
