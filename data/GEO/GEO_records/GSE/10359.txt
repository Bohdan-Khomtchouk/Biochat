TITLE
p53 status and AAI gene expression response

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human colon carcinoma cells (HCT116) differing in TP53 status were exposed to aristolochic acid I (AAI) (50 and 100 uM for up to 48 h), and their gene expression responses compared by cDNA microarray technology. Keywords: other

DESIGN
36 samples  - cells exposed to AAI dissolved in water were compared to cells treated to water alone
