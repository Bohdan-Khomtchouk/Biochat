TITLE
Saccharomyces cerevisiae cells: W303-1A vs W303-1A delta MSN2, MSN4

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Saccharomyces cerevisiae cells comparing the W303-1A wildtype with the W303-1A double mutant for MSN2 and MSN4 during zinc deficient conditions Keywords: Genetic modification with zinc limitation

DESIGN
Two condition experiment, W303-1A vs W303-1A delta MSN2, MSN4. Biological replicates: 2 wildtype, 2 knock-out, independently grown and harvested.
