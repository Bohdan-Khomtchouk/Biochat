TITLE
Porphyromonas gingivalis: Control (Wild-type W83) vs. its htrA mutant

ORGANISM
Porphyromonas gingivalis

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of the wild-type and its htrA mutant. Identification of genes that are affected by the htrA mutation in P. gingivalis Keywords: Genetic modification

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, W83 vs. htrA mutant late-log growth phase. Biological replicates: 4 control, 4 mutant, independently grown. One replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL6324 P. gingivalis W83 microarray glass slides (version 2)
