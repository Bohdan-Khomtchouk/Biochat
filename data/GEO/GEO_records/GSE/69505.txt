TITLE
Bisulfite sequencing coupled to target enrichment in MCF-7

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Sequencing of bisulfite-converted MCF-7 genomic DNA captured on SureSelect Methyl-Seq (Agilent).

DESIGN
Genomic DNA from estradiol starved and estradiol induced MCF-7 cells was processed according to the SureSelect MethylSeq Target Enrichment System (Agilent).

PLATFORM
GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
