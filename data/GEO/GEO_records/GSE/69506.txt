TITLE
Chromatin-associated RNA-seq in MCF-7

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Deep sequencing of total RNA isolated from the chromatin fraction of MCF-7 cells.

DESIGN
Stranded total RNA-seq (rRNA-minus) of chromatin-isolated RNA from estradiol starved and estradiol induced MCF-7 cells.
