TITLE
Differential Methylation Of The TRPA1 Promoter In Pain Sensitivity: DNA methylation 450k array

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome wide DNA methylation profiling of normal whole blood samples. The data consist of 43 samples with Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip data.

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from 43 of these samples were hybridized to the Illumina Infinium 450k Human Methylation Beadchip.

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
