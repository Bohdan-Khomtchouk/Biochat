TITLE
Plasmodium falciparum NF54 Transcriptome

ORGANISM
Plasmodium falciparum NF54

SUMMARY
Transcriptomic Analysis of Cultured Sporozoites of P. falciparum

DESIGN
RNA-seq reads from each of three developmental stages (2 replicates per sample) were mapped to the reference Plasmodium falciparum genome, and gene expression levels were calculated for each sample.

PLATFORM
GPL18008 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Plasmodium falciparum NF54)
