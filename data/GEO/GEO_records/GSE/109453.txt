TITLE
Clostridium difficile response to iron limitation

ORGANISM
Clostridioides difficile 630

SUMMARY
C. difficile was grown in BHIS broth or BHIS broth with 75uM dipridyl and gene expression was compared.

DESIGN
Two conditions were tested at three timepoints with three replicates each (18 samples total).
