TITLE
The functional interplay between the t(9;22)-associated fusion proteins BCR/ABL and ABL/BCR in Philadelphia chromosome positive acute lymphatic leukemia

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
p96ABL/BCR fusion protein plays an important role in the pathogenesis of Ph+ ALL

DESIGN
Isolation of murine bone marrow stem cells, transduction of the cells with the desired construct, tranplantation of the cells into lethaly irradiated mice, isolation of RNA from the spleens 12 days after the transplantation, investigation of gene expression profile

PLATFORM
GPL6246 [MoGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
