TITLE
Transcription profile of the Plasmodium falciparum intraerythrocytic cycle

ORGANISM
Plasmodium falciparum 3D7

SUMMARY
This experiment characterizes the transcriptome of the human malaria parasite, P. falciparum at 8 different stages of the intraerythrocytic cycle

DESIGN
Examination of polyA selected RNA in Plasmodium falciparum 3D7 strain at 8 different stages using RNA-seq

PLATFORM
GPL10866 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Plasmodium falciparum 3D7)
