TITLE
GATA6 dependent gene regulation in peritoneal macrophages

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Peritoneal macrophages from control and Mac-Gata6 KO (LysM-cre;Gata6-floxed) mice were determined for genome wide gene expression.

DESIGN
Sorted peritoneal macrophages from control and Mac-Gata6 KO mice were performed for whole genome expression analysis by Illumina microarray

PLATFORM
GPL6887 Illumina MouseWG-6 v2.0 expression beadchip
