TITLE
Pyruvate kinase M1 and M2 isoform knock in ES cells differentiate into three germ layers

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of wild type, PKm1 KI, and PKm2 KI ES cells.

DESIGN
Each ES cells were differentiated into three germ layer using embryoid body formation culture. Total RNAs were isolated from embryoid body at day 0, 3, and 7
