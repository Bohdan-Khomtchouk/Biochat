TITLE
Activated YAP1 stimulates cardiomyocyte proliferation

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Postnatal day 4 neonatal rat cardiomyocytes transduced with LacZ or flag-tagged, activated YAP1 (S127A) expressing adenovirus After transduction, cells were cultured in serum free media and collected 48 hours later.

DESIGN
2 group comparison, n=4 biological replicates per group
