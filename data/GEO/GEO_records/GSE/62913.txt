TITLE
Genome wide transcript and miRNAanalysis of invitro and in-vivo  generated human cardiac samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We report the identification of miRNAs and transcripts that are regulated during human cardiac maturation.

DESIGN
Examination of  hESC-derived cardiomyocytes , human fetal  tissue and human adult heart tissue for cardiomyocyte maturation.
