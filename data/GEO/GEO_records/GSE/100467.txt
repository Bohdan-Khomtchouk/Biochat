TITLE
A comprehensive, cell specific microRNA catalogue of human peripheral blood

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A comprehensive, cell specific microRNA catalogue of human peripheral blood

DESIGN
miRNomes from seven distinct blood cell populations namely, CD4, CD8, CD14, CD15, CD19, CD56 and CD235a of 43 healthy individuals using smallRNA-seq. Additionally examination of miRNA profiles from 77 whole-blood samples and 38 exosome and serum samples.

GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
