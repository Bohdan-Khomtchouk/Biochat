TITLE
The AtGenExpress: hormone inhibitor or other chemical treatment of seedlings in Arabidopsis

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Arabidopsis seedlings of the wild-type were treated with inhibitors.

DESIGN
60 samples were analyzed by Affymetrix GeneChip, ATH1. Sample correspondence between control (mock) and inhibitor treatment is described in brackets (F series or G series).
