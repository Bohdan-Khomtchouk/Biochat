TITLE
Revealing different roles of the mTOR-targets S6K1 and S6K2 in breast cancer by expression profiling and structural analysis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression profiles of ZR751 breast cancer cells after downregulation of S6K1, S6K2 and S6K1+S6K2.

DESIGN
Triplicates of control, S6K1siRNA, S6K2siRNA and S6K1+S6K2siRNA, in total twelve samples.
