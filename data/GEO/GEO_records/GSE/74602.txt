TITLE
30 paired normal and tumor colorectal samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of differentially regulated genes in patients normal and colorectal tumor samples. The aim of this study was to identify genes that were differentially up- or down-regulated in the tumor samples as compared to the normal colon tissue

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from patients' normal and tumor tissue. Please note that the patient tumor samples as these were obtained from the Singapore tissue bank and no further information were provided.

PLATFORM
GPL6104 Illumina humanRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
