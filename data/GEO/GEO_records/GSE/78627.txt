TITLE
total RNA-seq from SK-N-DZ (ENCSR136WGP)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this experiment come from independent growths of cell line SK-N-DZ, a metastatic neuroblastoma from a 2 year old female.  They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
