TITLE
RNA-Seq of L4 C. elegans

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
RNA-seq libraries were generated for L4 wildtype worms to analyze RNA abundance at this point in development.

DESIGN
Three independent replicates of wildtype C. elegans were collected at the L4 stage for RNA.
