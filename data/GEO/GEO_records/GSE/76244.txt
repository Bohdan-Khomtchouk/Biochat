TITLE
Arabidopsis thaliana seedlings: Wild type vs. zrf1 mutants

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of 6-day-old seedlings of Arabidopsis wild type control and zrf1 mutants is performed using Agilent's Whole Arabidopsis Gene Expression Microarray (4x44K).

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment: seedlings of wild type control vs. mutant zrf1a-1 zrf1b-1 or zrf1a-2 zrf1b-1 seedlings. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 zrf1a-1 zrf1b-1 mutant and 2 zrf1a-2 zrf1b-1 mutant, independently grown under 12h light/ 12h dark photoperiods and harvested.

PLATFORM
GPL12621 Agilent-021169 Arabidopsis 4 Oligo Microarray (V4) (Probe Name version)
