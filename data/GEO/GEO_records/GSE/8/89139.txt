TITLE
Expression data from pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Three PDAC tissue samples and their matched adjacent non-tumor samples were obtained and were used for microarray assay.

DESIGN
Tissue samples were distributed into two experimental groups: control group and PDAC group which were taken during surgery, immediately frozen in liquid nitrogen, and stored at -80ËšC for further analysis.
