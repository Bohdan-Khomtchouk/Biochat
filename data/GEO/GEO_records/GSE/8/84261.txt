TITLE
Adult Female Rat Hippocampal Tissue: Naïve vs. Contextual Fear Conditioned (Trained)[Experiment 1]

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
naïve and trained rats 24 hours after exposure to the contextual fear conditioning paradigm. Biological replicates: 3 independent pools of 3 animals each, 6 naïve and 6 trained animals, independently conditioned and tissues were independently isolated. One replicate per array.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Naïve vs. Trained tissue. Biological replicates: 6 naïve, 6 trained, in two separate pools, independently trained and isoalted. One replicate per array.
