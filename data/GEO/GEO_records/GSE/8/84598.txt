TITLE
Adverse genomic alterations and stemness features are induced by field cancerization in the microenvironment of hepatocellular carcinomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Integrative whole genome and transcriptome analyses of tumor tissue, invasive border region as well as tumor-surrounding liver (SL) in 28 HCC patients.

DESIGN
Gene expression analyses of 3 different liver regions (tumor, border, normal) in 22 HCC patients.
