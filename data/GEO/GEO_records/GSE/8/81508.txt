TITLE
Impact of MEF2C on chromatin accessibility of mouse BM pre-B and myeloid cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We analyzed the chromatin accessibility of control and Mef2c deficient mouse BM pre-B and myeloid cells by ATAC-seq analysis.

DESIGN
Examination of chromatin accessibility of 2 cell types from both WT and Mef2c deficient mice.
