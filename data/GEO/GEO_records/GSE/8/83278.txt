TITLE
The Beagle Dog MicroRNA Tissue Atlas: Identifying Translatable Biomarkers of Organ Toxicity

ORGANISM
Canis lupus familiaris

SUMMARY
Purpose : Identification of novel microRNA biomarkers specific of organ toxicity

DESIGN
micoRNA-SEQ and Q-RT-PCR were used to analyze changes in miRNA profiles of 16 tissues from beagle dogs
