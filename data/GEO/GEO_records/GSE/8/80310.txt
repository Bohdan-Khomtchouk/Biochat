TITLE
UGA Folate and Pregnancy

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Umbilical cord blood was collected from live deliveries at the Athens Regional Midwifery Clinic. Neonatal sex, race and gestational age was obtained by review of the delivery records.

DESIGN
Bisulfite converted DNA from 24 cord blood samples collected at delivery was hybridized to the Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip.
