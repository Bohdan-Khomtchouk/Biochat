TITLE
Comparative expression analysis between sigE (TTHB211) deletion mutant and wild-type of T. thermophilus HB8

ORGANISM
Thermus thermophilus HB8

SUMMARY
We observed the expression profile of the total mRNA of sigE (TTHB211) deletion mutant of Thermus thermophilus HB8 strain grown in a rich (TT) medium at 70oC compared with that of wild type. Keywords: cell type comparison
