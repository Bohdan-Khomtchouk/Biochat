TITLE
Transcriptome response of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type to N-acetyleglucosamine (NAG)

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pneumoniae D39

SUMMARY
Transcriptome comparison of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type grown at in CDM with 0.5% Glucose to grown in CDM with 0.5 % N-acetyleglucosamine (NAG)

DESIGN
Two conditions design comparison of one strain including a dye swap.
