TITLE
Gene expression in ABHD5-knockdown RAW264.7 cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RAW cells or ABHD5-KD RAW cells were subjected to microarray assays. Each group contain 3 samples.

DESIGN
To explore the regulatory role of ABHD5 in metabolic pathways, RAW cells or ABHD5-KD RAW cells were subjected to microarray assays.
