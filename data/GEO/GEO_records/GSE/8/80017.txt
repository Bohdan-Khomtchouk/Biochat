TITLE
Meta-Analysis of Autism Cortical Transcriptome Reveals Discordant Region-Specific Changes   Linked to Radial Glia and Inhibitory Interneurons

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
DNA methylation analysis of the prefrontal cortex samples from autistic patients and neurologically normal controls

DESIGN
18 samples (9 autism and 9 controls) were analyzed using Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip
