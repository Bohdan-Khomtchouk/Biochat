TITLE
RNA-seq Transcriptome analysis of REG9.1 (Tb927.11.14220) when RNAi is induced, or not

ORGANISM
Trypanosoma brucei

SUMMARY
Bloodstream forms of T brucei AnTat1.1 were grown in vitro and REG9.1 was silenced by tetracycline induction of RNAi

DESIGN
Two replicates of REG9.1 without RNAi induction (-tet) or with RNAi induction (+tet) were analysed
