TITLE
HuMiChip2 for Strain Level Identification and Functional Profiling of Human Microbiomes

ORGANISM
human metagenome

SUMMARY
HuMiChip2 was applied to analyze perform both strain-level identification and the functional profiling of human gut microbiomes from alcoholic cirrhosis patients and healthy individuals with alcohol abuse.

DESIGN
A total of 18 individuals were recruited for the study, among whom 9 were diagnosed with alcoholic cirrhosis and 9 were healthy individuals with alcohol abuse.
