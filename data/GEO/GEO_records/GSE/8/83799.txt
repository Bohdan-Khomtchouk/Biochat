TITLE
Whole-body RNA-seq from Camponotus floridanus workers and queen

ORGANISM
Camponotus floridanus

SUMMARY
RNA-seq from whole bodies of workers (major and minor) and queen from C. floridanus to increase replicate number from 2010 genome study

DESIGN
Two workers (one major and one minor) and one queen were snap frozen and the whole body processed for RNA-seq
