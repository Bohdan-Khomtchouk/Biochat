TITLE
BevR-IgG array in glioblastoma xenografts

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of anti-angiogenic therapy resistance in xenografts of patient glioma samples serially passaged across generations of athymic nude mice

DESIGN
3 independent biological replicates with control (IgG) treatment; 9 samples with drug treatment (bevacizumab), comprised of three independent biological replicates from three separate generations of xenografts
