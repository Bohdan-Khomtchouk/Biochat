TITLE
Amplification of KRAS in a low grade glioma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We describe the case of a low grade glioma in a 37 year old male patient showing an amplification of KRAS

DESIGN
Sample was analysed by arrayCGH method using Agilent recommendations. Sample was labeled in Cy5 and co-hybridized with control DNA cy3 labeled.
