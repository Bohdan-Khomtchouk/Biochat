TITLE
Profiling of microRNAs in tumor interstitial fluid of breast cancer patients - a novel resource to identify biomarkers for prognostic classification and detection of cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study was to identify biomarkers and to elucidate the crosstalk between stromal cells.

DESIGN
Compare the level of microRNA in tumor interstitial fluid (TIF) and breast tumor tissue of breast cancer patients.
