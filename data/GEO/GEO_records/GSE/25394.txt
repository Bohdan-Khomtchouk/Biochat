TITLE
Candida Iron deprived Cells: Control vs. BPS treated

ORGANISM
Candida albicans

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of iron deprived Candida cells comparing the WT vs BPS (Iron deprived) cells

DESIGN
One-condition experiment, WT vs. BPS treated cells. Biological replicates: 3 control, 3 treated, independently grown and harvested.
