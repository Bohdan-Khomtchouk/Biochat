TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of miR-509-3p-mediated gene silencing in A549 lung cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of changes in gene expression after transfection with miR-509-3p mimic in A549 cells

DESIGN
Total RNA was obtained from  A549 cells transfected with miR-509-3p and negative control mimics, and gene expression was compared using microarrays.
