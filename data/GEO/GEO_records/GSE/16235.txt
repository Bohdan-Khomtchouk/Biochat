TITLE
Equine skeletal muscle: pre versus post-exercise

ORGANISM
Equus caballus

SUMMARY
Unconditioned thoroughbred geldings were exercised to maximal heart rate or fatigue on an equine high-speed treadmill. Skeletal muscle biopsies were taken from the middle gluteal muscle before, immediately after and four hours after exercise.Â
