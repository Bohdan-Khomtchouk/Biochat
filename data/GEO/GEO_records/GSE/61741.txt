TITLE
miRNA Profiles in patients and controls

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This project analyzes peripheral blood profiles of patients of various diseases and controls.

DESIGN
n = 94 normal controls, n = 15 long lived individuals and n = 940 patients have been screened for the complete miRNA repertoire according to miRNAse V12-14. Please note that each miRNA has been measured in at least seven replicates and the median of the replica has been computed.
