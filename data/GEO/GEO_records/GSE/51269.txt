TITLE
Homeostatic intestinal epithelial gene expression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Intestinal epithelial cells of 28-day-old female mice were isolated according to a recently published protocol (Lotz et al., J Exp. Med. 2006).

DESIGN
Isolated intestinal epithelial cells of four individual animals (four biological replicates) were examined. Single color array.
