TITLE
Genome wide gene expression analysis of chronic granulomatous disease patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Whole blood was collected from chronic granulomatous patients, carriers and healthy controls.

DESIGN
RNA was isolated from whole blood and globin removal was performed for all samples. The cells were not selecter or stimulated ex vivo.

PLATFORM
GPL6947 Illumina HumanHT-12 V3.0 expression beadchip
