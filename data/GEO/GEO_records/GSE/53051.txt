TITLE
Large hypomethylated blocks related to large heterochromatin regions as a universal defining epigenetic alteration in human solid tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Examined methylation status using HumanMethylation450 BeadChip for samples from 5 different tissue of normal, hyperplastic, adenoma, cancer and metastatic samples.

DESIGN
Bisulfite converted DNA from the samples were hybridized to the Illumina Infinium 450k Human Methylation Beadchip.

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
