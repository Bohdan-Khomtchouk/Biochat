TITLE
Interferon signaling during early experimental malaria infection

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray profiling of whole blood of immunodeficient mouse strains at 24h after infection with Plasmodium chabaudi.

DESIGN
Multiple independent replicates collected for each knockout.  RNA isolated and amplified.  Hybridized as two-color experiment against a common pooled reference.
