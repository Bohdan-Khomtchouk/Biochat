TITLE
Chromatin marking is associated to transcriptional stability during fly development

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
We have conducted ChIP-Seq and RNA-Seq analyses in order to perform a comparative study between wing imaginal discs and eye imaginal discs from Drosophila melanogaster.

DESIGN
Examination of several histone modifications and gene expression quantification in wing/eye imaginal discs cells
