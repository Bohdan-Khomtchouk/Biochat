TITLE
Profiling miRNAs in human retinoblastoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Total RNA samples from three different retinoblastoma samples were profiled for a panel of 160 human miRNAs.  Three replicates of normal retina was also included. Keywords: disease-state analysis

DESIGN
Absolute intensities were measured following hybridixzation of direct labelled total RNA from the samples listed above.

PLATFORM
GPL4879 Human miRNA 2k custom array
