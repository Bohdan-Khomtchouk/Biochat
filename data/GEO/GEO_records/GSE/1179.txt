SUMMARY
Series of 6 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (blue200micE) and control (blue20micE) each. Comparison of plants  grown for 48 hours at 200µE blue light versus plants exposed for 48 hours at 20µE to blue light. E. Lopez, unpublished Keywords: repeat sample
