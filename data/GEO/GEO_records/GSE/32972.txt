TITLE
Gene expression comparison of L. reuteri ATCC PTA 6475 and tetrahydrofolate synthase 2 mutant

ORGANISM
Lactobacillus reuteri

SUMMARY
Gene expression comparison of L. reuteri ATCC PTA 6475 and tetrahydrofolate synthase 2 mutant in MRS medium in anaerobic conditions at 37C.

DESIGN
Includes 3 biological replicates and dye-swaps for ATCC PTA 6475 versus tetrahydrofolate synthase 2 mutant. Reference time point is L. reuteri ATCC PTA 6475.
