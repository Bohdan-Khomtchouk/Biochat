TITLE
Differential gene expression in primary ovarian tumors from depressed and non-depressed patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling was carried out on primary ovarian carcinomas from 10 patients.  The primary research question is whether gene expression differs in tissues from individuals with high vs low symptoms of psychological depression. Keywords: Risk prediction
