TITLE
VI-28 treatment induced alteration of gene expression in human lymphocytes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The aim of the present study was to evaluate whether Chinese herbs VI-28 has any potential effect on the immune system in a human cell culture system. An inflammatory cDNA array was used in the study to analyze the profile of gene expression in cells treated by VI-28. Keywords: other
