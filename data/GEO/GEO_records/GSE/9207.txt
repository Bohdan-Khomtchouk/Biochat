TITLE
Gene expression-based, HOXC6 WT versus KO mouse prostates

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This study was used to identify biologically relevant targets of HOXC6 in normal prostate. Keywords: gene expression profiling

DESIGN
Three HOXC6 knockout and three HOXC6 WT mouse prostates were collected at 32 weeks of age and analyzed for gene expression on Illumina mouse-v6 platform

PLATFORM
GPL5943 Emory_University Mouse-6 Expression BeadChip 47K
