TITLE
RNA-sequencing of MHC-cycD2 transgenic mice after Transverse Aortic Constriction and Aortocaval Shunt surgery

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing of mice overexpressing cyclin D2 compared to WT control, and of TG mice after pressure and volume overload

DESIGN
Examination of 4 Groups of mice: WT (n=5), MHC-cycD2 sham (n=5), MHC-cycD2 TAC (n=3), MHC-cycD2 Shunt (n=5) and 4 comparisons: GroupIcontrolWT_vs_GroupIicontrolsham, GroupIicontrolsham_vs_GroupIIITreatmentTAC, GroupIicontrolsham_vs_GroupIVTreatmentShunt, GroupIIITreatmentTAC_vs_GroupIVTreatmentShunt

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
