TITLE
Rice leaf transcriptome from 6 week old plants

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
Three biological replicates from the leaves of rice genotype Kitaake were obtained for transcriptome analyses. This is part of a study comparing expression and protein levels.

DESIGN
Three replicates from plants growing at the same time in greenhouse conditions

PLATFORM
GPL2025 [Rice] Affymetrix Rice Genome Array
