TITLE
Microarray analysis of Rgg-dependent transcriptional regulation in Streptococcus suis serotype 2 strain 05ZYH33

ORGANISM
Streptococcus suis 05ZYH33

SUMMARY
Streptococcus suis 2 Rgg-dependent transcription was analyzed.

DESIGN
Microarray analysis was performed using RNA samples isolated from Streptococcus suis 2 wild-type strain 05ZYH33 as well as RNA isolated from 05ZYH33 rgg isogenic mutant strain during postexponential phases of growth.
