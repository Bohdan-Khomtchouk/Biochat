TITLE
DNA methylation screening of small bowel adenocarcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Illumina Infinium 450k screening of normal and tumour tissue in a retrospective 28 sample cohort of small bowel adenocarcinoma patients. Average signal and beta values normalised to internal Illumina controls.

DESIGN
Comparing DNA methylation differences in a matched normal tumour cohort of 28 small bowel adenocarcinoma patients.
