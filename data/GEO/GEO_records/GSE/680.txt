TITLE
Transcript Profiling of Arabidopsis Plant Life Cycle

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
This series contain all stages Arabidopsis plant development.  Stages of development includes unfertilized ovule, 24-Hr post-fertilization seed, globular stage seed, cotyledon stage seed, mature green seed, post-mature green seed, post-germination seedling, rosette leaf, root, stem, and floral bud.
