TITLE
Lyl-1 knockout vs wildtype Lymphoid Primed Multipotent Progenitors (LMPPs)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We compared gene expression differences in Lyl-1 knockout vs wildtype LMPPs KO allele described in Pub Med ID: 21387538

DESIGN
LMPPS were purified as Lineage-negative, Sca-1+ c-Kit+ (LSK) and Flt3-high from both Lyl1-null and WT mice on C57Bl/6 background
