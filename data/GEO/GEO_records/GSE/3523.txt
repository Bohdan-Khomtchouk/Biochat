TITLE
Temporal expression profiling of beta- and gamma-sarcoglycan deficient mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Temporal expression profiling of hindlimb muscle of beta- and gamma-sarcoglycan deficient mice aged 1, 2.5, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14 and 20 weeks. Keywords: time course

DESIGN
4 closed loop-designs where subsequent time points are co-hybridised. Two closed loops per strain. Two individuals per strain per time point.
