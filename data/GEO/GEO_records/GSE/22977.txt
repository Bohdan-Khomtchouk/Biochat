TITLE
Rat hepatocytes: Advanced cirrhosis vs Control liver, Advanced cirrhosis vs Early cirrhosis

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Gene profiling of hepatocytes in early and advanced cirrhotic Rats

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Advanced cirrhosis vs Control liver, Advanced cirrhosis vs Early cirrhosis. Biological replicates: 5 Advanced cirrhosis, 5 Early cirrhosis, 5 control liver. Each hepatocyte was isolated independently. One replicate per array.
