TITLE
microRNA profiling in the parasite Neospora caninum using a high-throughput approach

ORGANISM
Neospora caninum

SUMMARY
Small RNA libraries from N. caninum tachyzoites were analyzed using a high-throughput RNA sequencing technology combined with systematic bioinformatics analysis.

DESIGN
Two small RNA libraries from N. caninum tachyzoites were analyzed using a high-throughput RNA sequencing technology combined with systematic bioinformatics analysis

PLATFORM
GPL24286 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Neospora caninum)
