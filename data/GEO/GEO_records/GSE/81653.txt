TITLE
Development of complete personalized treatment plans for early stage colorectal cancer patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
593 FFPE colorectal cancer samples were used to generate three prediction models: Recurrence prediction, 5FU efficacy prediction, and FOLFOX efficacy prediction The recurrence prediction study has 235 samples , 5FU efficacy prediction study has 192 samples, FOLFOX had 166 samples

DESIGN
Microarray used to study gene expression difference to generate prediciton models
