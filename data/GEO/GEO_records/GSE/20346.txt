TITLE
Expression profiling of critically ill influenza and bacterial pneumonia patients, also influenza vaccination recipients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Longitudinal Gene expression profiling of whole blood from critically ill influenza and bacterial pneumonia patients.  In addition before vs 7 days post influenza vaccination volunteer samples are assayed.

DESIGN
3 groups of samples.  First is bacterial pneumonia patients with 6 subjects sampled for up to 5 days.  Second group is severe influenza infection with 4 subjects sampled for up to 5 days.  Third group is influenza vaccination with 18 subjects sampled before and 7 days post vaccination.

PLATFORM
GPL6947 Illumina HumanHT-12 V3.0 expression beadchip
