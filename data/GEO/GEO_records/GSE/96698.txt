TITLE
Genome-wide transcriptional profiles of biopsies from the MUCUS trial

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The MUCUS clinical trial ( https://clinicaltrials.gov: NCT01433471) was established to investigate the effect of Trichuris suis ova on ulcerative colitis patients.

DESIGN
Colon biopsies were collected from two research subjects (ENR1 and ENR3) over three different time-points.
