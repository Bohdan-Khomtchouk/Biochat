TITLE
Molecular analysis of heterosis in alfalfa

ORGANISM
Medicago truncatula

SUMMARY
The aim of the experiment was to investigate the transcriptome changes in a double hybrid of alfala. The transcriptomes of a double hybrid (B2xB5) was compared to the two parental lines.

DESIGN
9 samples were used in the experiment, 3 biologcal replicates of the double hybrid and two parental lines.

PLATFORM
GPL4652 [Medicago] Affymetrix Medicago Genome Array
