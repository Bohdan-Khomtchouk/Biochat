TITLE
In utero allergen exposure reprograms primary developmental patterns of lung gene expression and influences respiratory phenotype in rat models of asthma susceptibility.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Transcriptome of whole lung of rat from 3 genetic strains, 3 postnatal time points and 2 exposures (saline versus ovalbumin) in (mainly) 4 replicate measurements per condition.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from whole lung of rat from 3 genetic strains, 3 postnatal time points and 2 exposures (saline versus ovalbumin) in (mainly) 4 replicate measurements per condition.
