TITLE
Deep RNA Sequencing Reveals Dynamic Regulation of Myocardial Noncoding RNA in Failing Human Heart and Remodeling with Mechanical Circulatory Support

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Complete transcriptome profiling in human failing and non-failing control hearts using next-gen sequencing

DESIGN
Poly-A selected RNA and small RNA sequencing carried out in 5 groups of samples: NF, ICM, NICM, ICM+LVAD, NICM+LVAD
