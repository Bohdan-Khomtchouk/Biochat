TITLE
Spleen vs Liver - Oligo - Single

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Spleen total RNA (Catalog #64044-1) and liver total RNA (Catalog #64042-1) were purchased from Clontech and used as the starting material. Spleen was labeled with Cyanine-3 (Green) while liver was labeled with Cyanine-5 (Red). cRNA was made by the single round amplification method. Keywords: repeat sample
