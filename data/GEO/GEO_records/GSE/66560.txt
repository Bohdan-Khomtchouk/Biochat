TITLE
Transcriptional response of Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 to sialic acid

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pneumoniae D39

SUMMARY
Transcriptome comparison of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type grown in M17 medium toD39 wild-type grown in M17 medium + 0.5% sialic acid (SM17).

DESIGN
Two conditions design comparison of one strain including a dye swap.
