TITLE
Yeast carbon source comparison

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
This experiment compares the transcriptional profiles of a WT yeast strain grown in either 2% glucose or 3% pyruvate.  The goal was to identify genes whose expression is either induced or repressed by glucose (catabolite repression). Keywords: nutrient response

DESIGN
This experiment used dye-swap replicates.
