TITLE
Acid sensitivity and opp transport regulation in L. acidophilus

ORGANISM
Lactobacillus acidophilus

SUMMARY
A whole-genome microarray containing 97.4 % of the annotated genes of L. acidophilus was employed to compare genome-wide patterns of transcription at varying pHs between the wild type parent and a histidine protein kinase mutant Keywords: ordered
