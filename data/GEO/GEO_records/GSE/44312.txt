TITLE
Gene expression is a circular process

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
This study involves the role of yeast mRNA decay factors in transcription. The experiment included here are the ChIP-exo results of three decay factors: Xrn1, Dcp2 & Lsm1.

DESIGN
Four experiments were made: Xrn1, Dcp2, Lsm1 and control (no-TAP tag), in two replicates.
