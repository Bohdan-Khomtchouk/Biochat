TITLE
Gene expression profiling of small bowel adenocarcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The whole genome DASL HT assay was used in combination with the HumanHT-12 v4 BeadChip for screening  normal and tumour tissue in a retrospective 28 sample cohort of small bowel adenocarcinoma patients. Non-normalized and average normalized (background subtracted) data

DESIGN
Comparing differences in gene expression in a matched normal tumour cohort of 28 small bowel adenocarcinoma patients.

PLATFORM
GPL14951 Illumina HumanHT-12 WG-DASL V4.0 R2 expression beadchip
