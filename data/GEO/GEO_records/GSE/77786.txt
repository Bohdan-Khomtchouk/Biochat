TITLE
Mouse Hepatocytes +/- GW

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Identification of novel LXR target genes

DESIGN
Primary mouse hepatocytes were isolated from Bl6 mice and plated on 6 well plates. Cells serum starved and treated with statin/MVA followed by overnight adminstration of DMSO or LXR agonist GW3965. Total RNA isolation using TRIZOL and Qiagen Column.
