TITLE
MicroRNA expression of clear cell papillary renal cell carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
to see if CCPRCC has a distinct miRNA profile from CCRCC and PRCC

DESIGN
miRNA v.2.0 chips on two portions of five CCPRCC cases (total 10 samples), three portions of two CCRCC cases and a further three CCRCC cases (total 9 samples), and five type I PRCC samples; a total of 24 samples
