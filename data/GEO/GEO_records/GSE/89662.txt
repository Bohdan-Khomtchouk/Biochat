TITLE
Genome-wide map of H3K4me3 and H3K27Ac in the protozoan parasite Trichomonas vaginalis

ORGANISM
Trichomonas vaginalis

SUMMARY
In this study, we investigated genome-wide transcriptional and epigenetic responses of T. vaginalis to histone deacetylase (HDAC) inhibitors.

DESIGN
Examination of mRNA expression and 2 different histone modifications in T. vaginalis in the absence or presence of HDAC inhibitors, apicidin or Trichostatin A
