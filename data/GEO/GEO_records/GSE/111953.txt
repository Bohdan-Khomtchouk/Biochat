TITLE
The transcriptome profile of SLAdd pigs fed a standard diet

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa

SUMMARY
Blood samples from 60-day old SLAdd and Large White animals fed a standard diet were extracted in order to characterize the health status of the SLdd individuals, which showed symptoms of a NAFLD-related disorder

DESIGN
Large White animals are used as controls for the study of SLAdd animals. There are 10 biological replicates for the SLAdd breed and 11 biological replicates for the Large White breed

PLATFORM
GPL19893 Agilent-037880/INRA Sus scrofa 60K v1 (enriched with immune system muscle and adipose tissue genes) [Probe names]
