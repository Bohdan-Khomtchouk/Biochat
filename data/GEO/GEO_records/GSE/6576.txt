TITLE
Prostate cancer cell line with slienceing of PDEF or control (GFP) gene using siRNA.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
PDEF(Prostate Derived Ets factor) is a member of Ets transcription factor with specific expression in Prostate. We identified target genes for PDEF in prostate cancer cell line PC-3 using Affymetrix Genechip Microarray technology. Keywords: target gene identification for transcription factor
