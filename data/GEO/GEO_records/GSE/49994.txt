TITLE
mRNA and RNC-mRNA deep sequencing of three hepatocellular carcinoma cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We sequenced the total mRNA and translating mRNA (RNC-mRNA) of three hepatocellular carcinoma cell lines Hep3B, HCCLM3 and MHCC97H

DESIGN
For each cell line, samples prepared from three independent and identical cell cultures were pooled with equal amounts. C-HPP China Team

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
