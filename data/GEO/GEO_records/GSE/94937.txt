TITLE
Generation of KRAS signatures using immortalized isogenic lung cells.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We generated RNAseq profiles from Small Airway Epithelial Cells (SALE) expressing either KRAS G12V or GFP control.

DESIGN
