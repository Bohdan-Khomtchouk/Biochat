TITLE
Oral mucosal response to SIV infection

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

SUMMARY
The objectives of this study were to determine the molecular mechanisms of host response during transition from primary to chronic SIV infection in the oral mucosa of rhesus macaques

DESIGN
Transcriptioinal profiles were determined for 2 rhesus macaques infected for 6 wk with SIVmac251 and compared to profiles of 3 uninfected healthy controls

PLATFORM
GPL3535 [Rhesus] Affymetrix Rhesus Macaque Genome Array
