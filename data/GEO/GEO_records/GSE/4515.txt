TITLE
KCl Stress in Desulfovibrio vulgaris Hildenborough (Series 1)

ORGANISM
Desulfovibrio vulgaris str. Hildenborough

SUMMARY
Increased salinity constitutes an important and frequent fluctuation faced by D. vulgaris in its natural habitat. Using data from microarray experiments, we investigated the effects of excess KCl on D. vulgaris. Keywords: stress response, time course
