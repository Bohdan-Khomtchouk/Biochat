TITLE
The renoprotective effects of soy protein in the aging kidney.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Late-life intervention with a soy-enriched diet attenuated age-dependent changes in renal structure and dysfunction in male Fischer 344 rats.

DESIGN
Examination of a 14% soy diet vs. a control diet on the attenuation of chronic kidney disease in aged F344 rats
