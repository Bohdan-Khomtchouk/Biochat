TITLE
ChIP-on-chip of BCL6 in follicular lymphoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Identification of BCL6 target genes in primary follicular lymphomas by ChIP-on-chip.

DESIGN
ChIP-on-Chip was performed on four follicular lymphoma (FL) primary samples (WC15, WC16, WC17 and WC18) using an anti-BCL6 antibody.
