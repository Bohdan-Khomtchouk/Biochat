TITLE
Integration of molecular gene signatures with clinicopathological variables improves staging and outcome prediction of oral cancer.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The aim of this study is to identify and validate clinically implementable gene signatures for outcome prediction of patients with oral squamous cell carcinoma.

DESIGN
Tumor gene expression profiles of surgical specimen of patients with oral squamous cell carcinoma (OSCC)

PLATFORM
GPL6480 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Probe Name version)
