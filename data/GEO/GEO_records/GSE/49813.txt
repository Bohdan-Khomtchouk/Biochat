TITLE
Cisplatin sensitivity in nasopharyngeal carcinoma cells (NPC)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of cisplatin sensitive NPCs

DESIGN
6-condition experiment, CNE2 vs. S16  cells at 0, 2 or 8 hr treatment. Biological replicates: 2 for each control (0 hr), 2 for each treatment (2 hr and 8 hr), independently grown and harvested, dye-swapped.
