TITLE
Analysis of the mesSR regulon in M. catarrhalis

ORGANISM
Moraxella catarrhalis

SUMMARY
Transcription profile analysis of a wild type ETSU9 strain and its isogenic non-polar in frame mesR mutant.

DESIGN
One condition experiment, WT-mesR mutant. WT-mesR mutant, biological replicates: 3 independently grown bacterial cultures. One replicate per array. Each WT-mesR mutant, biological replicate was independently labelled with each of the dyes
