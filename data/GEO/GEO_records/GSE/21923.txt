TITLE
Genomic profiling of HL60 and U266 blood cancer cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genomic profiling of HL60 and U266 blood cancer cell lines with (P as positive) or without (N as negative) CD34 cells using Agilent-014950 Human Genome CGH Microarray 4x44K.

DESIGN
HL60 and U266 blood cancer cell lines were extracted and their sub-cell lines (with or without CD34 cells) were compared using Agilent-014950 Human Genome CGH Microarray 4x44K.

PLATFORM
GPL5477 Agilent-014950 Human Genome CGH Microarray 4x44K (Feature Number version)
