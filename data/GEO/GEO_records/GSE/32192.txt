TITLE
Study of Huntington disease by analyzing RNA-Seq data using fly and mouse models

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster ; Mus musculus

SUMMARY
In this study, we perform deep mRNA sequencing to study the effect of a few gene modifiers in Huntington disease using fly models.

DESIGN
Analysis of 3 fly RNA-Seq samples and 4 mouse RNA-Seq samples

PLATFORM
GPL9061 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Drosophila melanogaster) GPL9250 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Mus musculus)
