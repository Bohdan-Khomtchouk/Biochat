TITLE
Patterns of Copy-Number Loss in Gastric Adenocarcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genome-wide DNA copy number profiling of gastric tumors and matched non-maligant samples to determine patterns of copy-number loss. Other samples included in this study can be found under accession number GSE31168.

DESIGN
Affymetrix SNP6 arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's instructions on DNA extracted from 14 tumors and 14 matched non-malignant samples.

PLATFORM
GPL6801 [GenomeWideSNP_6] Affymetrix Genome-Wide Human SNP 6.0 Array
