TITLE
Human lung minimally invasive adenocarcinoma (MIA): lepidic growth (LG) cancer cell pool vs. micro-invasion (MI) cancer cell pool

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human lung minimally invasive adenocarcinoma cells comparing control lepidic growth (LG) cell pool with micro-invasion (MI) cell pool.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, LG vs. MI cell pools. Biological replicates: 1 control LG cancer cell, 1 MI cancer cell in an individual MIA tumor. One replicate per array.
