TITLE
Definition of Salmonella rpoN regulon

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium

SUMMARY
comparison of total RNA profiles obtained from wt and rpoN mutant strains in presence of the dctDAAA peptide and chIP/chip experiments to define rpoN targeted DNA segments

DESIGN
chIP/chip using rabbit anti-RpoN under various conditions and transcriptional profiling in rich medium with dctDAAA peptide
