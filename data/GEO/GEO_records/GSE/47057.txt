TITLE
Contrasted transcriptomic responses to a rhabdovirus and a togavirus in zebrafish embryo

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
Characterization of the zebrafish embryonic host response to viral infection (CHIKV, IHNV) by means of global expression analysis

DESIGN
Three  sets of larvae infected by CHIKV or IHNV were analyzed (2 color hybridizations against control sets of larvae)
