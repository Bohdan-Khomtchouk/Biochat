TITLE
Synovial fibroblasts, RA versus OA

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
mRNA expression levels in synovial fibroblasts in 6 rheumatoid arthritis patients versus 6 osteoarthritis patients. Keywords: disease type comparison, mRNA expression study

DESIGN
Synovial tissue was obtained from open joint replacement surgery or arthroscopic  synovectomy. Patients with RA or OA (n = 6 each for gene expression analysis and further patients for validation experiments) were classified according to the ACR criteria.

PLATFORM
GPL8300 [HG_U95Av2] Affymetrix Human Genome U95 Version 2 Array
