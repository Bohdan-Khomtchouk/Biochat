TITLE
CHANGES IN THE HUMAN TRANSCRIPTOME UPON VITAMIN D SUPPLEMENTATION

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Subjects of a randomized controlled trial (RCT) received either vitamin D3 (n = 47) in a weekly dose of 20,000 IU or placebo (n = 47) for a period of three to five years

DESIGN
Blood samples for preparation of RNA were drawn from the subjects and mRNA gene expression in blood was determined using microarray analyses.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
