TITLE
Induction of similar multistage transcriptional responses in the mouse uterus by E2 and GEN

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Immature (19/20 days of age) Alpk:APfCD-1 mice were treated with arachis oil (AO) vehicle, 0.4mg/kg 17beta-estradiol (E2), or 250mg/kg genistein (GEN), via a single subcutaneous injection, and sacrificed at 1hr, 2hr, 4hr, 8hr, 24hr, 48hr, 72hr post dose. Keywords: other
