TITLE
Heat stress response in Trematomus bernacchii

ORGANISM
Trematomus bernacchii

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to measure the effect of heat stress on the transcriptome of a cold-adapted fish species - Trematomus bernacchii - an Antarctic fish species. Keywords: Stress response

DESIGN
Three control fish are compared to 3 heat-shocked fish, 3 short-term recovery fish and 3 longer term recovery fish.

PLATFORM
GPL5573 Gillichthys mirabilis cDNA microarray
