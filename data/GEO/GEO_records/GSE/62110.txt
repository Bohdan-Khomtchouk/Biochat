TITLE
Plasmablast response to influenza vaccines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To study the plasmablast response to influenza vaccines We want to test and see that LAIV, TIV and ID TIV induced different plasmablast response

DESIGN
We take peripheral plasmablast and test its response to influenza vaccines at day 7 after vaccination. This array data set is for day 0 (before vaccines)
