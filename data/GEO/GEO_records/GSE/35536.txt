TITLE
Identification of the Bacteroides fragilis EcfO regulon 1

ORGANISM
Bacteroides fragilis NCTC 9343

SUMMARY
Genome expression study of Bacteroides fragilis ATCC25285 strain containing the EcfO gene constitutively expressed from plasmid pFD340

DESIGN
Two chip study with 5 technical replicates on each chip comparing the EcfO overexpressing strain to an empty vector control in the wild type strain.

GPL15187 NimbleGen-TI272559 Bacteroides fragilis NCTC 9343 385K Expression Array
