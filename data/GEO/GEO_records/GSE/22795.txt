TITLE
PRDM14 Knockdown in human embryonic stem cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To identify the gene changes after PRDM14 knockdown

DESIGN
6 samples were collected in total. Each sample is done in triplicate and samples were collected at 3 days after transfection. Luciferase knockdown serves as negative control and PRDM14 knockdown is the experimental sample.
