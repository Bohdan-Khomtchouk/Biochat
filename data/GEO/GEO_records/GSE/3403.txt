TITLE
SHR-SP rats on Normal and High Salt+Fat diets, 2 vs. 8 weeks

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Supporting microarray data for manuscript entitled " OSTEOPONTIN AND PAI-1 EXPRESSION IN MALIGNANT HYPERTENSION: SUPPRESSION BY p38 MAPK INHIBITORS " submitted to the HYPERTENSION journal. Keywords: timecourse, diet

DESIGN
Male spontaneously hypertensive stroke-prone rats (SHR-SP) were obtained from Charles River (Raleigh, NC). At 11 weeks of age, the SHR-SP were randomized into 2 groups, and fed either powdered chow diet (Purina 5001) with water ad lib; or a high-salt/high-fat diet consisting of 1% NaCl in the drinking water and 24.5% fat in the chow (from Harlan TekLad, Madison, Wisconsin). 6 replicate animals per diet per time point.

PLATFORM
GPL341 [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
