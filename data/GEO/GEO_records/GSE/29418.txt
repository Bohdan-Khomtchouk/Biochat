TITLE
Tdrd1 acts as a molecular scaffold for Piwi proteins and piRNA targets in zebrafish.

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

SUMMARY
RNA libraries from immunoprecipitates of Tdrd1, Ziwi and Zili, total testis RNA, total RNA from 3 week old wild-type and tdrd1 mutant gonads.

DESIGN
Both size selected and non-size selected libraries were made. Sequencing was performed using Illumina platform.
