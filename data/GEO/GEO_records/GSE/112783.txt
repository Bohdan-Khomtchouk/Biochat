TITLE
Effect of KLK5 knockdown in PC9 lung cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Response of PC9 cells to infection with NT (control) lentivirus or one of two different KLK5 knockdown lentiviruses.

DESIGN
Cells were plated in 35 mm dishes, infected with NT lentivirus, KLK5 KD #40 lentivirus (NM_012427.31248s1c1, Sigma), or KLK5 KD #42 lentivirus (NM_012427.3650s1c1, Sigma).

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
