TITLE
Mouse Models for Muscular Dystrophy

ORGANISM

SUMMARY
Comparative analysis of gene expression levels from hindlimb muscle tissue from 8 week old mouse models for muscular dystrophy. We have used mouse models with dystrophin-, sarcoglycan-, sarcospan-, or dysferlin-deficiency. Keywords = muscular dystrophy Keywords: other
