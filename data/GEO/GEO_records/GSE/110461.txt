TITLE
Study of the function of COX19 by transcriptional profiling of Arabidopsis thaliana transformed with artificial microRNA to reduce expression of COX19

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Analysis of transcriptional profiling of Arabidopsis Col-0 leaves from wild-type and plants transformed with artificial microRNA to reduced expression of AtCOX19-1 and AtCOX19-2

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Control vs. Silenced Plants. Biological replicates: 2 control, 2 silenced
