TITLE
AML genome hybridization to HGU133 Plus 2.0

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
CD34-positive and negative fractions were used for genomic DNA preparation, which was hybridized to HGU133 Plus 2.0 arrays. Keywords: Disease state analysis

DESIGN
Samples with a suffix " -C " are CD34-negative controls, and those with a suffix " -L " are CD34-positive leukemic fractions. A total of 23 pairs were analyzed.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
