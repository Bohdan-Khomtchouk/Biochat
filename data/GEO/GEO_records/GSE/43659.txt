TITLE
Enterohemorrhagic Escherichia coli (EHEC) 8624 double deletions of kdpe and cra grown in DMEM

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli 8624

SUMMARY
Analysis of effect of deletion of the two transcriptional factors KdpE and Cra on gene expression of EHEC 8624

DESIGN
A double mutant of kdpE and cra in the EHEC 8624 was grown  to OD600=1.0 in DMEM then processed according to manufacturer's specifications: http//www.affymetrix.com/support/technical/manual/expression_manual.affx

GPL3154 [E_coli_2] Affymetrix E. coli Genome 2.0 Array
