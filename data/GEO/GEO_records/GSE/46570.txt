TITLE
Gene expression analysis of PST-Dox nanoparticles on isolated normal lymphocytes and in cancer cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of expression variation of PST-Dox nanoaprticles for elucidating the molecular mechanism of action in cancer cell lines and normal lymphocytes.

DESIGN
Total RNA isolated from PST-Dox treated cells subjected to 24 hr treatment compared to untreated samples.

PLATFORM
GPL15207 [PrimeView] Affymetrix Human Gene Expression Array
