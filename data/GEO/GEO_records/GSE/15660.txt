TITLE
Gene Expression analysis of Kc and Mbn2 cell lines from Drosophila melanogaster.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Comparison of gene expression between Kc and Mbn2 cell lines derived from Drosophila melanogaster.

DESIGN
Microarray analysis was performed with two biological replicates for each cell line.  Each array contains 15,634 Drosophila genes in duplicate with 12, 60 mer probes per gene.

PLATFORM
GPL8443 NimbleGen Drosophila melanogaster 385K Array
