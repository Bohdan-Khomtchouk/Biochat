TITLE
Transcriptomic changes associated with DNA pol epsilon deficiency

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
What is the impact of DNA pol epsilon deficiency on gene expression as well as on the expression of ncRNA, gene silencing etc.? Plantlets were grown in vitro on half strength MS for 9 days and used for total RNA extraction.

DESIGN
mRNA seq on wild-type Col0, two DPB2oe mutants overexpress and one abo4-1 mutant knock out.

PLATFORM
GPL20696 Ion Torrent Proton (Arabidopsis thaliana)
