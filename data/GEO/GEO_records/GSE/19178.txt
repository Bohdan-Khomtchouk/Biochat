TITLE
Arabidopsis Col-5 challenged with Ralstonia solanacearum BCCF401

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Arabidopsis plants were challenged with Ralstonia solanacearum isolate BCCF401 and expression profiles investigated during early and late wilt symptom development. Keywords: Disease state analysis

DESIGN
A direct comparison was performed. Two replicate biological experiments were performed and two technical replicates with dye swap were included in each biological replicate.
