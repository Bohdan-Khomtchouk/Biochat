TITLE
PPD stimulated PBMCs from tuberculosis patients, latent infectious individuals and healthy controls.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comprehensively compare the transcriptional difference in PPD stimulated PBMCs from individuals with different tuberculosis infectious status: tuberculosis patients, latent infectious individuals and healthy controls using the microarray analysis.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, PBMCs vs. PPD-PBMCs. 12 individuals: 4 TB patients, 4 latent infectious individuals and 4 healthy controls.
