TITLE
SF370 vs SF370.10R (corR-)

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pyogenes

SUMMARY
The aim of this study was to elucidate genes affected by a mutation in the M1 SF370 CovR-repressed TCS corR (Spy1587).  Leday et al 2007 Keywords: wild type vs corR-

DESIGN
series contains wild type (SF370) and corR- (SF370.10R) from three independent isolations. cDNA ratios from these isolations as well as their dye swap are included
