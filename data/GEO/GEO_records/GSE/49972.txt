TITLE
Clear cell sarcoma of the kidney demonstrates an embryonic signature indicative of a primitive nephrogenic origin

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling of CCSK in comparison with non-neoplastic foetal and adult kidney

DESIGN
Total RNA extracted from 22 frozen CCSKs and 6 adult non-neoplastic kidneys and comercially obtained  RNA from 4 foetal kidneys, were hybridized to Illumina humanHT-12 v4.0 Expression BeadChip at the SCIBLU Lund University Sweden ( http://www.lth.se/sciblu ).

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
