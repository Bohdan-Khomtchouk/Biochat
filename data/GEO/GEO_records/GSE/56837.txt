TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of gene expression in whole blood from HIV-1 progressors and non-progressors.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of whole blood from HIV-1 progressors and non-progressors. The comparison results showed differently expressed genes associated with disease progress.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from whole blood from 26 patients differently numbered.

PLATFORM
GPL6884 Illumina HumanWG-6 v3.0 expression beadchip
