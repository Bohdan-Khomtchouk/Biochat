TITLE
A member of the mycobiota modulates purine metabolism to increase intestinal permeability and colitis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis of colons from germfree mice mono-associated with either S. cerevisae or R. aurantiaca

DESIGN
Differential gene expression study comparing samples from germ-free control mice (4 replicates), mice infected with Saccharomyces cerevisiae (3 replicates) and mice infected with Rhodotorula aurantiaca. (4 replicates).
