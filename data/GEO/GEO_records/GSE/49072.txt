TITLE
Alveolar Macrophage Gene Expression in Human Pulmonary Fibrosis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles for patients affected with Sporadic and Familial Pulmonary Fibrosis.

DESIGN
Case/control study of alveolar macrophage response in 15 IPF and 8 familial pulmonary fibrosis patients; and 45 normal volunteers and 16 unaffected relatives.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
