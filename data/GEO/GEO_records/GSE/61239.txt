TITLE
RNAseq analysis of the global retinal transcriptome of rhodopsin knockout mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We report RNAseq analysis of the transcriptome of retinas from mature rhodopsin knockout mice

DESIGN
Examine retinal transcriptome of 3 biological replicates for each genotype from 4-week-old animals with tissue collected between 8:00 - 10:00AM

PLATFORM
GPL11002 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Mus musculus)
