TITLE
RNA-seq of mouse cultured retinal pigment epithelium (RPE) challenged with photoreceptor outer segments (POS).

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Primary culture of mouse RPE cells (C57BL/6J background) was challenged with POS for 90 min and subjected for RNA-seq.

DESIGN
Cells treated with POS (Trt) compared with untreated culture (Veh).

PLATFORM
GPL11002 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Mus musculus)
