TITLE
H3K4me2 profiling in OCI-Ly1 and OCI-Ly7 cell lines.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Define and compare H3K4me2 enrichment in OCI-Ly1 and OCI-Ly7 cell lines.

DESIGN
Using ChIP-seq, we examined the H3K4me2 genomic enrichment locations in two biological replicates in each cell line
