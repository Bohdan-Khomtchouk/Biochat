TITLE
Genome-wide analysis of gene expression in placentas derived from patients with preeclampsia

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The purpose of this study was to perform a comprehensive analysis of gene expression profile in placentas from preeclamptic pregnancies vs normal placentas.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from placental tissues of patients with normal pregnancies and patients with preeclampsia.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
