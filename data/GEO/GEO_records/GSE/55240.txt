TITLE
Assessment of the role of the BCRs in transgenic models of chronic lymphocytic leukemia

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
we evaluated the capacity of different types of antigen/BCR interactions to induce leukemia in a transgenic mouse model of CLL Key words; B-cell antigen receptor (BCR), chronic lymphocytic leukaemia (CLL), T-cell leukemia (TCL)

DESIGN
GEP analysis of 7 leukemias with an anti-Sm BCR and 9 leukemias with an anti-PtC BCR

PLATFORM
GPL6885 Illumina MouseRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
