TITLE
Transcriptome of dehydrated vs hydrated Salmonella cells

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium str. SL1344

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiles of dehydrated Salmonella Typhimurium SL1344 cells post 22h air drying were compared to those of SL1344 cells incubated for 22 h in DDW

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, dehydrated versus hydrated (control) Salmonella cells. Biological replicates: 4 control, 4 dehydrated, independently grown and harvested. One replicate per array.
