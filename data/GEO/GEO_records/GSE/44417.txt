TITLE
Whole genome bisulfite sequencing of rdm16ros1, ros1, nrpd1ros1 mutants and C24 wild-type

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Using whole genome bisulfite sequencing to provide single-base resolution of DNA methylation status in rdm16ros1, ros1, nrpd1ros1 mutants and examine the effect of RDM16 on DNA methylation

DESIGN
4 samples examined: C24 wild type with RD29A-LUC transgene, rdm16ros1 double mutant, ros1 mutant, nrpd1ros1 mutant (all in C24 background with RD29A-LUC transgene)

PLATFORM
GPL13222 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Arabidopsis thaliana)

CITATIONS
24068953

LIBSTRATS
