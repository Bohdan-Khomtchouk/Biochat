TITLE
Human Esophageal cells: Normal epithelial vs squamous cancer cell line

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Micro RNA expression profiling of human esophageal normal epithelial (hESO) cells comparing two human esophageal squamous cancer cell lines (TE7 and TE10).

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, Normal epithelial vs. tumor cells. Biological replicates: 2 normal epithelial  and 2 tumor cells replicates each respectively, independently grown and harvested. One replicate per array.

PLATFORM
GPL19906 miRCURY LNA microRNA Array, 7th Gen [lot35006]
