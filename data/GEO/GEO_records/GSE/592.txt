TITLE
Effect of Age on Fracture Healing in the Rat:  Series 2

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Study of rat femur fracture healing in young (6 weeks old), adult (26 weeks old), and older (52 weeks old) rats with samples collected at 0 time (no fracture) and at 0.4, 1, 2, 4, and 6 weeks after fracture.  RNA from two rats were pooled for each array. Keywords = rat, femur, fracture, age, time Keywords: time-course

PLATFORM
GPL85 [RG_U34A] Affymetrix Rat Genome U34 Array
