TITLE
Examination of the transcirptional response of Acinetobacter baumannii to antibiotics

ORGANISM

SUMMARY
This SuperSeries is composed of the SubSeries listed below. Strains of A. baumannii were exposed to sub-MIC doses of clinically relevant antibiotics to determine the transcriptional response
