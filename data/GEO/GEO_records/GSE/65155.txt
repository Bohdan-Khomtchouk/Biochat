TITLE
Transcriptome comparison of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type treated with 1mM to with 0mM H2O2

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pneumoniae D39

SUMMARY
Transcriptome comparison of the Streptococcus pneumoniae D39 wild-type grown in THY medium till OD620=0.25 and then treated with or without 1mM H2O2 for 40mins.

DESIGN
Two conditions design comparison of one strain including a dye swap.
