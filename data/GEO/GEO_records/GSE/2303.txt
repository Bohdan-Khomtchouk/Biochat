TITLE
Rat liver response to Clofibrate, DEHP or VPA

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
The project had 2 goals: 1) To evaluate the transcriptional response of 3 prototypical toxicants (Clofibrate, VPA, and DEHP) on rat lever. 2) To evaluate the impact pooling samples has on data analysis.
