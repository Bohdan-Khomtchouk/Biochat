TITLE
blue200micE_vs_white20micE

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Series of 6 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (blue200micE) and control (white20micE) each. Comparison of plants  grown for 48 hours at 200µE blue light versus plants exposed for 48 hours at 20µE to white light. E. Lopez, unpublished Keywords: repeat sample
