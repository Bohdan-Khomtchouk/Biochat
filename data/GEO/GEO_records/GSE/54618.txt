TITLE
Microarray analysis of human pre-eclamptic placenta

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
RNA was extracted from placentas selected based on normotensive pregnancy with low GBA messenger RNA expression and hypertensive pregnancy with relatively high expression of GBA messenger RNA.

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from placental tissue biopsies stored in RNALater within 2 hours after delivery.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
