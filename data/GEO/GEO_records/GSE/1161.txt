TITLE
tpt1_vs_WT

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Series of 6 repetitions of hybridization of treatment (tpt1) and control (WT) each. Comparison of the Arabidopsis tpt1 mutant exhibiting a reduction of triose phosphate (TP)-specific transport activities in chloroplasts to below 5%. A. Schneider et al., Plant J 32 (2002), pp. 685-699 Keywords: repeat sample
