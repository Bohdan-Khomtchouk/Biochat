TITLE
Autoantibody Profiles in Collagen Disease Patients with Interstitial Lung Disease

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Auto-antibody (Ab) profiles between acute-onset diffuse ILD (AoDILD) and stable states of 25 collagen disease patients were compared to screen biomarkers or pathogenic auto-Abs.

DESIGN
Auto-Ab levels in pooled sera were compared between the AoDILD and stable states with ProtoArray.

PLATFORM
GPL14689 Invitrogen ProtoArray v5.0 (full array layout)
