TITLE
mRNA expression in ttcsp1 deletion mutant and wild type Thermus thermophilus HB8 strains grown on a rich medium

ORGANISM
Thermus thermophilus HB8

SUMMARY
We observed the expression profile of the total mRNA of ttcsp1 deletion mutant of Thermus thermophilus HB8 strain grown in a rich (TR) medium at 45oC compared with that of 70oC. Keywords: temperature comparison
