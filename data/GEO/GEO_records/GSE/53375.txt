TITLE
Transcriptome expression profile of placental tissue from BAHD1+/+ (WT)  and BAHD1-/- (KO) mice at embryonic day E18.5

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Comparisons of placenta gene expression profiles of BAHD1-KO mice to those of wild-type littermate mice.

DESIGN
We used DNA microarrays to identify the repertoire of genes differentially expressed by ablation of the BAHD1 gene in the placenta at embryonic day E18.5

PLATFORM
GPL6096 [MoEx-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Exon 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
