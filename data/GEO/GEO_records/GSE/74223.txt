TITLE
Human DLD1 Cells: shRNA_Control vs. shRNA_FOXQ1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human colorectal cell DLD1 transfected by shRNA_Control or shRNA_FOXQ1 respectively.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, DLD1_shRNA_Control cell vs. DLD1_shRNA_FOXQ1 cell. Biological replicates: 2 DLD1_shRNA_Control replicates, 2 DLD1_shRNA_FOXQ1 replicates.
