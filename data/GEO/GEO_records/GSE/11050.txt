TITLE
cross-hybridization of woodchuck HCCs using human, rat and mouse arrays

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Woodchucks that were infected with WHC virus developed HCCs. HCCs and surrounding tissues were removed and RNA was extracted from microarray study. Human, rat and mouse arrays were used due to the lack of a woodchuck array. Same procedures and parameters were used to conduct this experiment. Keywords: comparative study
