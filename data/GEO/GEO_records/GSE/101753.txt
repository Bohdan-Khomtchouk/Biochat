TITLE
Differential effects of FTY720 on the B cell compartment in a mouse model of multiple sclerosis

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Oral FTY720 treatment of mice with experimental autoimmune encephalomyelitis leads to B cell-specific effects of the drug, which comprise the induction of apoptosis and the modulation of B cell receptor signaling.

DESIGN
Examination of the influence of FTY720 on B cell function.
