TITLE
Next Generation Sequencing of PKD2 mutant mice kidneys

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This Study sought to understand the differential gene expression profile of Pkd2 mutant mice kidneys in the setting of miR-21 deletion

DESIGN
4 Wild type mice, 3 Pkd2 Knockout mice and 3 Pkd2-miR-21 knockout mice were analyzed

PLATFORM
GPL17021 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Mus musculus)
