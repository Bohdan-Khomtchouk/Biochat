TITLE
CGH profiles of sporadic basal-like breast tumors

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genomic DNA from sporadic breast tumours was isolated and analysed using array CGH. The NKI 1MB BAC/PAC micro array was used to identify chromosomal aberrations of all tumours. Other profiles are located at: GSE9114 Keywords: sporadic breast tumour, CGH.

DESIGN
Locate chromosomal aberrations in sporadic breast tumors.

PLATFORM
GPL4560 Netherlands Cancer Institute human 3.5k BAC array
