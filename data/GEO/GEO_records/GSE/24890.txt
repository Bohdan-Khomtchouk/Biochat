TITLE
Genome-wide copy number variation in human metastatic melanoma cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Illumina HumanExon510S-DUO bead arrays (Illumina Inc) were performed according to the manufacturer's protocol.

DESIGN
Genome-wide copy number variation analysis was performed on the genomic DNA of two pairs of cell lines comprising of parental melanoma cell lines and corresponding resistant sub-lines.

PLATFORM
GPL6988 Illumina HumanExon510-Sv1 DNA Analysis BeadChip (HumanExon510Sv1_D)
