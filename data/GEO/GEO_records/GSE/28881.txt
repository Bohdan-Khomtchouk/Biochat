TITLE
The role of Stk in Streptococcus pyogenes

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pyogenes

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profile of Streptococcus pyogenes stk mutant strain JRS2516 vs its wild type parent strain MGAS2221

DESIGN
The RNA prepared from triplicate cultures for the wild type and the stk mutant strains was each labeled with Cy3 and hybridized to duplicate arrays. Reference RNA consisted of pooled RNA for the wild type and stk mutant, labeled with Cy5.

PLATFORM
GPL10822 JCVI PFGRC Streptococcus pyogenes 21K v2 array designed primarily based on strain MGAS5005
