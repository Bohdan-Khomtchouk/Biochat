TITLE
Chorionic villus sampling (CVS) microarray in preeclampsia

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
4 chorionic villus sampling specimens in pregnancies destined for preeclampsia and 8 matched controls were analyzed 36 genes were differentially expressed using J5 score and prediction modeling in caGEDA computer program Keywords: snap frozen, banked CVS specimens with known pregnancy outcomes revealed genes differentially expressed in preeclampsia pregnancies
