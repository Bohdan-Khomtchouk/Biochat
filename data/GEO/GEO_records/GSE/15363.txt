TITLE
Heterogeneity of Breast Cancer Vasculature

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Tumor-associated breast vasculature was laser-cappture microdissected from IDC breast cancer cases. The goal of the study was to characterize the heterogeneity of breast tumor-associated vasculature and identify gene expression signatures predictive of clinical outcome.

DESIGN
common reference design, 32 samples
