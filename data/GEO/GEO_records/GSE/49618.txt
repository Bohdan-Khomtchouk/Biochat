TITLE
Sorted normal bone marrow cells from healthy volunteers at Washington University

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Normal healthy cells (monocytes, promyelocytes, polymorphonuclear cells, B cells, T cells, CD34+CD38- HSPCs) run as controls for TCGA AML marker publication. Bone marrow cells collected from healthy donors were sorted and DNA extracted at Washington University in St. Louis, microarrays were then run at USC

DESIGN
Bisulphite converted DNA from the 21 samples was hybridised to the Illumina Infinium 450k Human Methylation Beadchip v1.2

PLATFORM
GPL13534 Illumina HumanMethylation450 BeadChip (HumanMethylation450_15017482)
