TITLE
Cancer Outlier Gene Profile Sets Elucidate Pathways and Patient-Specific Targets in Head and Neck Squamous Cell Carcinoma [Affymetrix SNP6]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study integrated Affymetrix SNPchip data for CNV estimation, Affymetrix HuEx1.0 data for gene expression estimation, and Illumina HumanMethylation27k BeadChip data for promoter methylation to estimate pathway activity.

DESIGN
Case:Control with HNSCC tumors and uvulopalatopharyngoplasty (UPPP) samples

PLATFORM
GPL6801 [GenomeWideSNP_6] Affymetrix Genome-Wide Human SNP 6.0 Array
