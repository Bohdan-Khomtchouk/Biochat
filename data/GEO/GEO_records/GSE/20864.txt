TITLE
Human peripheral blood cells: systemic lupus erythematosus vs healthy individual.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling of peripheral blood cells from patients with  systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE)  vs healthy individual (HI).

DESIGN
Peripheral blood was obtained from patients with SLE (n=21) and HI (n=45). Blood samples from 45 HI are used as control.
