TITLE
Gene expression in double positive thymocytes from mice with CD4-Cre-mediated deletion of Ikaros

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Double positive thymocytes (CD4+CD8+CD3lo) were sorted from 3-4-week old mice from Ikf/f CD4-Cre+  or Ikf/f CD4-Cre- mice (2 mice per genotype) and their transcriptome analyzed.

DESIGN
4 samples

PLATFORM
