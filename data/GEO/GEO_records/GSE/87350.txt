TITLE
Impact of Sphingosine Kinase-1 knock out in neonatal mice lungs

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Sphingosine Kinase-1 knock out protects against hyperoxic lung injury One day old Wild type (WT) control and Sphingosine Kinase-1 knock out  (SphK-1 KO)pups exposed to room air (RA) or hyperoxia (HO). Microarray based profiling of lung tissue after 7 days of hyperoxia of 75%

DESIGN
WT RA, WT HO, SphK-1 KO RA, and Sph K-1 KO HO studied in triplicates

PLATFORM
GPL16570 [MoGene-2_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 2.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
