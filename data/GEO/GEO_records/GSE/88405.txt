TITLE
RAMPAGE from cardiac muscle cell (ENCSR613JNT)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The libraries contained in this Experiment come from hESC's that were differentiated into cardiomyocytes. They are stranded PE101 Illumina Hi-Seq RAMPAGE libraries from rRNA-depleted Total RNA > 200 nucleotides in size. For data usage terms and conditions, please refer to http://www.genome.gov/27528022 and http://www.genome.gov/Pages/Research/ENCODE/ENCODE_Data_Use_Policy_for_External_Users_03-07-14.pdf
