TITLE
Modular repertoire analyses identify dynamic type I and type II interferon transcriptional signatures in adult SLE patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling (compared to healthy controls) to identify patients with active SLE disease and patients with quiescent disease

DESIGN
157 “SLE” and 20 “Healthy controls of SLE were followed up for up to 3 years. Clinical assessment, current treatment, laboratory results and SLEDAI score were collected at each visit (V1, V2...). SLE patients are compared to control groups.
