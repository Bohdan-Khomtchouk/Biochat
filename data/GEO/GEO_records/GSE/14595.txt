TITLE
Comparing ERG expression and vector control in 293HEK cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study was to identify potential genes regulated by ERG

DESIGN
293HEK cells were transiently transfected in duplicate with CMV-Tg2B ERG or control vector independently for a total of 4 samples.
