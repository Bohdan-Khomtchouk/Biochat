TITLE
C2C12 differentiation time course to observe changes between myoblasts and myotubes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This analysis compares various timepoints from Day -1, 50% confluency myoblasts to Day 5 post differentiation myotubes. Consecutive timepoints and myoblasts vs. myotubes are compared. Keywords: time course http://www.biodatamining.org/content/1/1/4

DESIGN
8 time points from Day -1 to Day 5, three replicates for each time point.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
