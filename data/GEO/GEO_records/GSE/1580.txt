TITLE
Analysis of murine epididymal adipose tissue

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Functional genomic analysis of epididymal adipose tissue extracted from polygenic obese mice fed either a control (1% linoleic acid) or treatment (1% trans 10, cis 12 conjugated linoleic acid) diet after five or fourteen days.
