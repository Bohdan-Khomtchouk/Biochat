TITLE
Effect of RNA concentration in cross-species hybridization

ORGANISM
Marmota monax

SUMMARY
Total RNA from adult woodchuck liver was analyzed on human membrane arrays.  The effect of RNA concentration on signal intensity was determined by using a series of RNA concentrations (0.1 to 10ug) Keywords: other
