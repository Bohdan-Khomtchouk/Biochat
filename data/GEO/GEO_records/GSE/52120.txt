TITLE
Rice leaves in the field in 2008 with 1- or 2-min interval

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
Time-course transcriptional profiling of rice leaf in the field in 2008. This experiment was performed to evaluate the resolution of two time points by inference from gene expressions.

DESIGN
Rice leaves (cv. Norin8) were collected with 1- or 2-min interval. No biological replicate.

PLATFORM
GPL6864 Agilent-015241 Rice Gene Expression 4x44K Microarray (Feature Number version)
