TITLE
Response to bile of Lactobacillus casei BL23

ORGANISM
Lactobacillus casei

SUMMARY
The response to bile of Lactobacillus casei BL23 was investigated.

DESIGN
Lactobacillus casei BL23 was grown in MRS medium at 37C without shaking until O.D. 595 nm. reached 0.5. The cultures were then two fold diluted with prewarmed MRS medium (control) or MRS supplemented with 0.2% bovine bile (treatment) and incubation continued for 45 min. Cells were then harvested and total RNA purified.
