TITLE
P.falciparum strain 3D7, SIR2 knockout

ORGANISM
Plasmodium falciparum

SUMMARY
This experiment studies the effect of knocking out the transcriptional regulator SIR2 in 3D7 parasites.  RNA was harvested from ring-stage parasites at 8 hours post-infection.
