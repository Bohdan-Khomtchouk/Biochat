TITLE
Arabidopsis thaliana: cis-cinnamic acid-treated seedlings versus control seedlings

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of Arabidopsis thaliana seedlings treated with cis-cinnamic acid.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, cis-cinnamic acid-treated seedlings vs. control seedlings. Biological replicates: 2 control replicates, 2 cis-cinnamic acid treated.
