TITLE
Transcriptome analysis by expression microarrays  of colorectal cancer derived from the Cancer Institute Hospital of Japanese Foundation for Cancer Research.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed the expression microarray experiments for mRNA of colorectal cancer tissue.

DESIGN
Messenger RNA were extracted from primary tumor of 31 colorectal cancer patients, hybridized and scanned with Affymetrix Human Genome GeneChip U133 Plus 2.0 array.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
