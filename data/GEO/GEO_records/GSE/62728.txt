TITLE
Cow mammary nutrigenomics

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Twelve midlactation cows received 4 diets differing in forage-to-concentrate ration (High (HF) versus Low (LF) forage supplemented or not with lipids (HF with whole intact rapeseeds (HF-RS) and LF with sunflower oil (LF-SO))

DESIGN
12 cows got into 4 groups, each cow was received 4 different diets in a latin square design Green*txt and Red_*txt raw data files contain Cy3 and Cy5 signal intensities, respectively.

GPL4594 URH-TALL_Bovine_Operon V1.1_1
