TITLE
JMJ and PRC2 ChIP-chip data from mouse embryonic stem cells

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We used ChIP-chip to reveal genome-wide locationation of JMJ and Polycomb proteins including EZH2, SUZ12 and EED in undifferentiated wild-type (CJ7) embryonic stem (ES) cells. Keywords: ChIP-chip

DESIGN
DNA fragments pulled down by chromatin immunoprecipitation, amplified and hybridization on Affymetrix tiling arrays.
