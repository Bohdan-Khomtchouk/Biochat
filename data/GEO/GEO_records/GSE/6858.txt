TITLE
Expression data from experimental murine asthma

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Experimental asthma was induced in BALB/c mice by sensitization and challenge with the allergen ovalbumin. Control groups received PBS. To investigate the innate immune component of experimental asthma, we also analyzed recombinase activating gene (RAG) deficient mice following exposure to ovalbumin and control PBS Keywords: case control
