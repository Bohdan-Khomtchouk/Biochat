TITLE
High-throughput sequencing of small RNAs from Drosophila

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
High-throughput sequencing of Drosophila melanogaster small RNAs. total RNA, ~18-26nt RNAs isolated using PAGE, ligation to adapters requires 5' monophosphate and 3' OH Keywords: high-throughput 454 sequencing

DESIGN
Small RNAs were sequenced from D. melanogaster at various developmental stages.
