TITLE
Gene expression profile of cancer cell lines of different origin

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profile of cancer cell lines of breast, lung, pancreatic, gasctric, ovarian, hepatocellular, prostate carcinomas and melanomas.

DESIGN
Cells were harvested after 24h of seeding. RNA isolation was performed usign Qiagen RNeasy kit. Hybridisation was performed using Affymetrix protocols. No replicate measurements were performed.

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
