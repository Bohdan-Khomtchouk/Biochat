TITLE
Impact of Verteporfin on HCC cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Together with TEAD4 the Hippo pathway effector YAP stimulates chromosomal instability. Verteporfin is known to disrupt the physical YAP/TEAD interaction and tehrefore might prevent from chromosomal instability in liver cancer.

DESIGN
Immortalized HCC cell line hepG2 is treated with Verteporfin for 24 hours.
