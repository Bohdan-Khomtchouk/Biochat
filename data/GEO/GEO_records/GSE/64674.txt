TITLE
Genome-wide transcriptional analyses of islet-specific CD4+ T cells identify Idd9 genes controlling diabetogenic T cell function

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression levels in ex-vivo and p79-stimulated splenic CD4+ T cells.

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from ex-vivo CD4+TCRVβ4+ T cells from BDC-Idd9 mice compared to BDC mice and p79-stimulated CD4+TCRVβ4+ T cells from BDC-Idd9 mice compared to BDC mice.

PLATFORM
