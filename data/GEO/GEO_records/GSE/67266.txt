TITLE
WT and Mk2/3 KO MEFs response to etoposide

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Primary mouse embryonic fibroblasts (MEFs) from wt and MK2/3 KO mice were treated with 20µM etoposide.

DESIGN
Primary MEFs from wt and MK2/3 KO mice were treated with 20µM etoposide and RNA extracted after 1h and 6h.

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
