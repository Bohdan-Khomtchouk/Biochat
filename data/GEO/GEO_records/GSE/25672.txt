TITLE
NK cells and immune memory

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarray profiling of adoptively transferred GFP-marked NK cells during MCMV infection in C57BL/6 mice

DESIGN
Multiple independent replicates were collected per time point.  NK cells were isolated from spleens of mice by flow cytometry.  RNA isolated, amplified, and hybridized as two-color experiment against a common pooled reference.
