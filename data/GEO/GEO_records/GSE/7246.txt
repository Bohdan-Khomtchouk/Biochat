TITLE
Human epithelial cell line (HL) infected with Chlamydia pneumoniae

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human epithelial cell line (HL) infected with Chlamydia penumoniae compared to control cells at time points 12h, 24h, 48h, 72h after the infection. Keywords: Chlamydia peneumoniae infection

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment over the time. Biological replicates: 2 infected, 2 controls at each time point (12h, 24h, 48h, and 72h), independently grown.
