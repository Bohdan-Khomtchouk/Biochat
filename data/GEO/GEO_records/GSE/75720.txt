TITLE
Effect of IL12 on clonal CD8+ CD45RA+ population

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Effector memory T-cell clones against well known tumor antigens (STEAP1, PRAME; CD8+, CD45RA+) were or were not  treated with 10ng/ml IL12 and analyzed after 12 and 48 hours

DESIGN
Global expression profiling of two T-cell clones upon interleukin 12 stimulation.
