TITLE
BRAF expression in a mouse melanoma model

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression was generated on tumors from 41 iBIP mice, which were previously described (Kwong, JCI, 2015).

DESIGN
The BRAF-driven iBIP mouse model of melanoma was taken off dox and expression analyzed at various timepoints.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
