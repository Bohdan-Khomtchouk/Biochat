TITLE
Expression analysis of microRNA  by expression microarrays  of rectal cancer derived from the Cancer Institute Hospital of Japanese Foundation for Cancer Research.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We performed the expression microarray experiments for miRNA of rectal cancer tissue.

DESIGN
microRNA were extracted from primary tumor of 137 rectal cancer patients, hybridized and scanned with Affymetrix miRNA 3.0 Arrays

PLATFORM
GPL16384 [miRNA-3] Affymetrix Multispecies miRNA-3 Array
