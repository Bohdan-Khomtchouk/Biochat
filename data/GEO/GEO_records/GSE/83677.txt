TITLE
Transcriptional profile of Beijing/East-Asian Mycobacterium tuberculosis isolates

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profile comparison among Beijing and non-Beijing M. tuberculosis isolates.

DESIGN
Three M. tuberculosis strains were compared. The laboratory reference strain, H37Rv, belongs to the Euro-American or lineage 4. Two clinical isolates of the East-Asian or lineage 2: 98_1663 is a pre-Beijing or Group 1 isolate, and HN878 is a Beijing or Group 5 isolate. Three replicates were performed for each comparison using two different biological samples.
