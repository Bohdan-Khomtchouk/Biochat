TITLE
MCT4 knockdown in pancreatic cancer cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression levels were determined with control or MCT4 knockdown pancreatic cancer cells.

DESIGN
MiaPaca2, Capan-2, and PL45 cells were transfected with non-specific or MCT4-specific RNAi. RNA was harvested at 72 hours post transfection and analyzed on Illumina microarrays.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
