TITLE
Expression analysis of colorectal carcinoma cells with defunct TP53 using TAS

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Whole transcriptome expression analysis of colorectal carcinoma cells with defunct TP53 on Affymetrix Human Tiling 1.0 array set. Expression data were processed with TAS.

DESIGN
We analyzed one Affymetrix Human Tiling 1.0R set
