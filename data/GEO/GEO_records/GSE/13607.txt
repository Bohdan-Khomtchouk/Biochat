TITLE
Basal gene expression of primary osteoblasts

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Osteoblasts derived from different functional skeletal sites were analysed for gene expression profiles Keywords: Cell type comparison

DESIGN
Collagenase digested osteoblasts were derived from two bone sites (calvaria and ulna) of an adult rat, maintained in culture for 3 passages then prepared for RNA analysis
