TITLE
Xiphophorus maculatus JP163 A RNA-Seq data for PacBio Genome Annotation (Nov.2017)

ORGANISM
Xiphophorus maculatus

SUMMARY
RNA from Xiphophorus maculatus (X. maculatus) are isolated and sequenced for annotation of enhanced X. maculatus genome

DESIGN
26 Xiphophorus maculatus (X. maculatus) fish at various developmental stages were sacrified for tissue specific RNA or whole fish RNA isolation, RNA-sequencing and for genome annotation.

PLATFORM
GPL24317 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Xiphophorus maculatus)
