TITLE
Expression data from kidney of Grhl1wt and Grhl1 ko mice.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
The aim of our study was to investigate the functions of Grhl transcription factor in  the kidney. We used microarrays to detect altered expression of genes knowed to be involved in susceptibility to hypertension and/or in the regulation of blood pressure.

DESIGN
We used 8 Grhl1-/- and 7 Grhl1+/+ mice of age about 6 months.

PLATFORM
GPL16570 [MoGene-2_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 2.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
