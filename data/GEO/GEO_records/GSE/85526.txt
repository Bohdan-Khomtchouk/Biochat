TITLE
SMC1A ChIP-seq in primary human keratinocytes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study examines the DNA binding regions of SMC1A, a subunit of the Cohesin complex, in undifferentiated human keratinocytes.

DESIGN
ChIP-seq was used to determine the localization of SMC1A in the genome

PLATFORM
GPL11154 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Homo sapiens)
