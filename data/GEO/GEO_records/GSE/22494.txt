TITLE
Population genetics context of ancient human genome sequence of an extinct Palaeo-Eskimo

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To study the population genetics context of the Saqqaq individual we carried out Illumina Bead-Array-based genotyping on four native North American and twelve north Asian populations.

DESIGN
169 samples are analysed on two different Illumina platforms.
