TITLE
Sublethal costs of salinity stress contribute to habitat limitation in an endangered estuarine fish

ORGANISM
Hypomesus transpacificus

SUMMARY
We exposed adult delta smelt to treatments of increased salinities to quantify the transcriptomic responses involved in coping with osmotic stress

DESIGN
70 samples were run on 70 arrays, with 1-4 replicates for each treatment or control
