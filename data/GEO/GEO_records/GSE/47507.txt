TITLE
Affymetrix SNP array data for 4 MYC-negative B-cell lymphoma cases and 2 B-cell lymphoma cell lines (SU-DHL-5 and HT)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Affymetrix SNP arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's directions on DNA extracted from blood samples and cell lines

DESIGN
Copy number analysis of Affymetrix SNP 6.0 arrays was performed for 4 MYC-negative B-cell lymphoma and 2 B-cell lymphoma cell lines
