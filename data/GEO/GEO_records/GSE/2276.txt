TITLE
Effect of PGE receptor EP3 subtype agonist on mouse asthma model

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
We examined the effect of PGE receptor EP3 subtype on mouse OVA-induced asthma model. We treat EP3 agonist 3h after OVA challenge.  After 24 h of 3rd OVA challenge, whole lung was isolated and the gene expression change was determined. Keywords: other
