TITLE
Development of gene expression signatures of GPCR family.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
In human HCC tumors, matching paracancerous specimens, and normal liver specimens, a targeted gene expression microanalysis was used to screen alterations in GPCR family gene expression.

DESIGN
A targeted gene expression microanalysis was used to screen alterations in GPCR family in 3 human HCC tumors, matching paracancerous specimens, and 3 normal liver specimens.
