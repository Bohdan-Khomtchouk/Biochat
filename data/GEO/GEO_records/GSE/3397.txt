TITLE
RSV gene expression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human bronchial epithelial cells (BEAS-2B cells) were treated with RSV at 1.0 MOI for 4 and 24 hours or control (vehicle-treated for 4 hours). Global gene expression was measured by Affy Hu133 plus 2.0 microarray chips. Keywords: Inflammation, microtubules, RSV, time course

DESIGN
4 controls (vehicle-treated for 4 hours) 4 RSV-treated for 4 hours 4 RSV-treated for 24 hours

PLATFORM
