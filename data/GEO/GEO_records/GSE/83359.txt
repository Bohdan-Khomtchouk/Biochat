TITLE
Host response in cells from broncho-alveolar lavage (BAL) isolated from mice infected with influenza virus (PR8) and/or Staphylococcus aureus (Newman)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Array analysis of total RNA from broncho-alveolar lavage (BAL) from mice infected with influenza virus (PR8) and/or Staphylococcus aureus (Newman)

DESIGN
Primary infection: mock or influenza virus (PR8) at 100 PFU on day 0 (intranasal); Secondary infection: mock or Staphylococcus aureus (Newman) at 1x10^8 CFU on day 7 (non-invasive intratracheal); Time point:  4 h post secondary infection

GPL10787 Agilent-028005 SurePrint G3 Mouse GE 8x60K Microarray (Probe Name version)
