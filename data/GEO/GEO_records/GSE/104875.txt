TITLE
RNA Seq of Jag1Ndr/ndr and Jag1+/+ liver

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA sequencing of livers from postnatal day 10 (P10) control mice (Jag1+/+) and an Alagille mouse model (Jag1Ndr/Ndr).

DESIGN
Transcriptome profiling of postnatal day 10 livers from Jag1Ndr/Ndr and Jag1+/+ in triplicates using Illumina TruSeqTM

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
