TITLE
Papillary thyroid cancer cell lines treated with Y15

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
3 papillary thyroid cancer cell lines were compared, treated with Y15 to untreated.

DESIGN
1 million cells of each papillary thyroid cell line (TPC1, K1, BCPAP) were plated, treated 24 hours later with 10uM Y15, and collected 24 hours later by trypsinization.

PLATFORM
GPL10558 Illumina HumanHT-12 V4.0 expression beadchip
