TITLE
Genome-wide profiling of H3K27me1 and 5mC enrichment in young leaves of Arabidopsis thaliana

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Chromatin, DNA and RNA were extracted from young A. thaliana primary stem leaves. Chromatin and DNA methylation immunoprecipitation experiments were performed using commercially available antibodies and analyzed by Illumina sequencing (ChIP-seq and MeDIP-seq).

DESIGN
ChIP-seq analysis of H3K27me1 enrichment profiles and MeDIP-seq analysis of 5mC enrichment profiles in 2 biological replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL11221 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Arabidopsis thaliana)
