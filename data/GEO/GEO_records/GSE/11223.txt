TITLE
Colon biopsies from UC patients and healthy controls

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of colon epithelial biopsies from ulcerative colitis patients and healthy control donors. Study aims to survey and analyze variation from disease in different GI regions. Keywords: disease state analysis

DESIGN
Biopsies from a variety of anatomic locations, from patients of various treatment status or healthy controls.
