TITLE
RNA-sequencing of mouse adult hippocampal progenitor cells in which Nfix was deleted.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
RNA-sequencing of mouse adult hippocampal nestin+ progenitor cells & dcx+ progenitor cells from wild-type animals, and animals in which the gene Nfix was deleted.

DESIGN
Hippocampal nestin+ flox-reporter progenitor cells (3 wt, 3 kos - 60 days post tamoxifen adminstration), dcx+ flox-reporter progenitor cells (3 wt, 3 kos - 7 days post administration)
