TITLE
ChIPseq to profile IFNg response in human primary monocytes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We characterized the regulation of 2 histone marks by IFNg in human monocytes

DESIGN
Human primary monocytes were cultured for 24 hours with or without IFNg. ChIP was performed and the DNA product was subject to ChIPseq
