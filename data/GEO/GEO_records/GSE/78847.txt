TITLE
Analysis of cord blood B cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Purified CD19+ B cells isolated from the cord blood were purified in subsets and activated by diferent stimuli and compared by whole genome microarray analysis.

DESIGN
Neonatal B cell were isolated as CD19+CD3negativeCD5+CD10negative and activated by anti-IgM, R848, RSV or Influenza virus
