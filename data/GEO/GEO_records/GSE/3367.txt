TITLE
GAS M6 serotype, wild-type vs covR mutant

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pyogenes

SUMMARY
JRS4 is our wild-type M6 serotype strain and JRS948 is the isogenic covR strain. Keywords: 2-channel

DESIGN
Four separate RNA preparations from each strain (RNA #A-D) were hybridized to duplicate arrays. Reference RNA consisted of pooled RNA from each preparation, labelled with Cy5.

PLATFORM
GPL1482 M1, M3, M18 GAS Array
