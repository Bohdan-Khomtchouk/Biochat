TITLE
Enhanced skeletal muscle regeneration upon Bmi1 gain of function is driven by Mt1-mediated protection from oxidative stress.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Three independently prepared satellite cell cultures isolated from STOPFloxBmi1 mice, treated with Adeno-Cre (A-Cre) infection and induced to differentiate for 2 days were analysed by whole-genome Illumina platform mouse v2 as compared to the same cells treated with Adeno-GFP (A-GFP).

DESIGN
6 samples,2 condition with 3 biological replicates per condition
