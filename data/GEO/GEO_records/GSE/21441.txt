TITLE
Peroxide stress in Desulfovibrio vulgaris Hildenborough

ORGANISM
Desulfovibrio vulgaris str. Hildenborough

SUMMARY
Using data from microarray experiments, we investigated the effects of excess hydrogen peroxide on D. vulgaris. Keywords: stress response, time course

DESIGN
Comparison of cells treated with 1 mM H2O2,sterile water Peroxide to untreated cells at times of 0,120,240,30,480,60 min.
