TITLE
Transcriptomic profiling of HeLa cells infected with Salmonella Typhimurium

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We evaluated the transcriptome changes induced by infection with Salmonella (20 hpi, MOI 100).

DESIGN
Transcriptmic profiles of HeLa cells infected with Salmonella Typhimurium were generated by deep sequencing, using Illumina HiSeq 2000.
