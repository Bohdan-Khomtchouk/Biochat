TITLE
Gene expression in the oocyte at primary follicle stage

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
oocytes were collected from ovarys of 6 day old female mice.  Targets from four biological replicates of each were generated and the expression profiles were determined using Affymetrix MOE430 A & B. Keywords: repeat

DESIGN
4 oocyte at primary follicle stage biological replicates were analyzed

PLATFORM
GPL339 [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array GPL340 [MOE430B] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430B Array
