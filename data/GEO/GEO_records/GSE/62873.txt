TITLE
Late stage ovarian cancer gene expression profiles

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling on 63 stage III-IV papillary serous ovarian cancer samples resected during primary debulking at the University of Turin, Italy.  Only the primary ovarian mass and no metastases were included in this analysis. The study focused on ovarian cancer chemokine expressions

DESIGN
Only the primary ovarian mass and no metastases were included in this analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
