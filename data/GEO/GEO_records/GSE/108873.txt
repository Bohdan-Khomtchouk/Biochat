TITLE
Transcriptomic responses to MCHM in Saccharomyces cerevisiae

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
This study investigates the transcriptomic responses of Saccharomyces cerevisiae S96 normal and petite cells to 4-Methylcyclohexanemethanol (MCHM), a coal cleaning chemical spilled in the water supply of central West Virginia in 2014.

DESIGN
Examination of two cell types - normal and petite, in two growth media - YM and YPD, treated with MCHM or not treated; two replicates per cell type per growth medium per MCHM treatment

PLATFORM
GPL17143 Illumina MiSeq (Saccharomyces cerevisiae)
