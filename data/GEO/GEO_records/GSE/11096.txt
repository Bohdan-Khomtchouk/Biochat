TITLE
Role of M. tuberculosis dosS and dosT in CO sensing

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of M. tuberculosis dosR and dosS mutants and wild type growing in log phase treated with carbon monoxide or nitric oxide versus untreated controls Keywords: Genetic modification

DESIGN
Two condition experiment, DETANO treated cells at 100 uM or CO treated cells at 20000 ppm versus untreated controls for various M. tuberculosis mutants. Biological replicates: 2 replicates per dose, 1 array per replicate
