TITLE
Chronic Rosiglitazone Mouse Aorta

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To better understand what genes are regulated by PPAR gamma, an experimental dataset was generated by microarray analysis, in duplicate, of pooled aortic mRNA isolated from adult male mice (C57BL/6J strain) treated for 21 days with a PPAR gamma agonist (rosiglitazone) or vehicle. Keywords: other

PLATFORM
GPL81 [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
