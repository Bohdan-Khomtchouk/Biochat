TITLE
SNP data from 12 childhood adrenocortical tumors from outside Brazil

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
SNP array data from 12 childhood adrenocortical tumors (ACT) were used to identify recurrent chromosome rearrangements and candidate driver genes.

DESIGN
12 childhood ACT were analyzed with Illumina Human610-Quad v1.0 BeadChips.
