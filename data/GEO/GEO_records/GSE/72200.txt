TITLE
Arid1a is essential for endometrial function during early pregnancy

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Arid1a has a critical role for modulating epithelial proliferation which is a critical requisite for fertility We used microarrays for detail the mechanism underlying Arid1a loss during early pregnancy.

DESIGN
Uteri of Arid1a f/f and Arid1a d/d mice were selected at 3.5 dpc for RNA extraction and hybridization on Affymetrix microarrays.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
