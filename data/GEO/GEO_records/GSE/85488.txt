TITLE
Regulation of stem cell maintenance and lymphoid specification by the ubiquitin ligase Huwe1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The ubiquitin ligase Huwe1 regulates stem cell quiescence, maintenance and lymphoid specification by controlling the expression of N-Myc.

DESIGN
Thirty million human bone marrow CD34+ cells were fixed and used for ChIP and precipitated using 3 micrograms of antibody.
