TITLE
IL17 and IL22 stimulation of T84 colonic epithelial cell line

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The effects of stimulating intestinal epithelial cells with Th17 cytokines, IL17 and IL22, was investigated Keywords: dose response

DESIGN
The human colonic epithelial cell line, T84 was grown to confluency in standard transwell plates and either mock treated, or treated with cytokines IL17 and IL22
