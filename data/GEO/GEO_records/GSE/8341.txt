TITLE
ChIP-on-chip experiments of normal and failed hearts of Dahl rat

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Left ventricle myocytes from Dahl rats with a normal or failed heart was subjected to mRNA quantitation or ChIP-on-chip experiments with Affymetrix Rat Genome 230 2.0 microarrays. Keywords: ChIP-chip

DESIGN
One left venctricle specimen was used for each mRNA quantitation/ChIP-on-chip experiment with a normal or failed heart.

PLATFORM
GPL1355 [Rat230_2] Affymetrix Rat Genome 230 2.0 Array
