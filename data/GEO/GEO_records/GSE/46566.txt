TITLE
Mouse spleen: Histone modification patterns in spleens from wild type and leukemic mice

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
ChIP-Seq for H3K4me3 and H3K27me3 in wild type spleens and spleens from mice having deletion of RBP-J in cells of the renin lineage, which results in B-cell leukemia.

DESIGN
Examination of 2 different histone modifications in wild type and mutant spleens.

PLATFORM
GPL13112 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Mus musculus)
