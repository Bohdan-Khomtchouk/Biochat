TITLE
CLL Patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genomic profiles of CLL (Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia) patients. 11 CLL patients were selected for detection of genomic aberrations, 8 patients with atypical CLL and 3 patients with typical CLL.

DESIGN
Patient's DNA were hybridized against Promega control on Agilent G4410A arrays and scanned with the Agilent G2505B scanner.

PLATFORM
GPL11069 Agilent-012750 Human Genome CGH Microarray 44A (Probe name version)
