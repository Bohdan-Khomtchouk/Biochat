TITLE
Rat intervertebral disc cell was stimulated by NGF

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
The rat-tail intervertbral disc cells was incubated with NGF at a concentration of 100 ng/ml/day for a total of five days.

DESIGN
Primary culture of 8 weeks rat-tail interverbral disc cells. The control group was incubated with PBS and the experimental group was incubated with NGF at a concentration of 100 ng/ml/day for a total of five days.
