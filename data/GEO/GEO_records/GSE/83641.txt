TITLE
Gene expression in HPAECs with ERG and FLI1 knockdown

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
ERG and FLI1 were silenced in HPAECs using siRNA for 48h.

DESIGN
The goal of the study was to identify groups of genes regulated by ERG and FLI1 transcription factors.
