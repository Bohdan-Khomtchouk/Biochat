TITLE
Time course of the mRNA expression after phage ϕYS40 infection in crp deletion mutant of Thermus thermophilus HB8

ORGANISM
Thermus thermophilus HB8

SUMMARY
We observed the expression profile of the total mRNA in crp (TTHA1437) deletion mutant strain of Thermus thermophilus HB8 during infection of bacteriophage ϕYS40.
