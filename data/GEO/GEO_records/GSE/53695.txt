TITLE
nELAVL HITS-CLIP in Alzheimer's Disease patients

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human brain samples from control and advanced Alzheimer's Diseased subjects were subjected to HITS-CLIP to monitor nELAVL binding changes during AD progression

DESIGN
Human brain samples were obtained from the Mount Sinai Brain Bank; samples were UV-irradiated and subjected to nELAVL HITS-CLIP (detailed desription in accompanying paper)
