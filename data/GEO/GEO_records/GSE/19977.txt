TITLE
Gene-expression profiles of formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded human hepatocellular carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene-expression profiles of formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded human hepatocellular carcinoma tissues obtained at the time of surgical resection.

DESIGN
Gene-expression profiling was performed using formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded hepatocellular carcinoma tissues obtained at the time of surgical resection.
