TITLE
Colorectal tumor DNA vs. normal control DNA

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Genomic DNA samples were extracted from microdissected colorectal tumour tissue and then compared against normal DNA extracted from the peripheral blood from the same patients.

DESIGN
53 Normal/Tumor matched samples.
