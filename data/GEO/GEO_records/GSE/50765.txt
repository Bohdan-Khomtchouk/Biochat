TITLE
Sox2 signature in SHH medulloblastomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Affymetrix Human Gene 1.1 ST Array profiling of 83 primary SHH-driven medulloblastoma samples.

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from primary medulloblastoma samples and hybridized to Affymetrix Human Gene 1.1 ST Arrays (24-Array Plates) according to the manufacturer's instructions.

PLATFORM
GPL11532 [HuGene-1_1-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.1 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
