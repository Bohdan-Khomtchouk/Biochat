TITLE
Genome-wide Dose-dependent Inhibition of Histone Deacetylases Reveals Their Roles in Enhancer Remodeling and Suppression of Oncogenic Super-enhancers

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study aims to address the fundamental mechanism(s) underlying histone deacetylase inhibition in regulating gene expression and cytostatic responses in transformed and normal cells.

DESIGN
Examination of epigenetic and transcriptome changes under dose-dependent HDAC inhibition.
