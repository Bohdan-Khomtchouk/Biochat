TITLE
Transcriptome expression profile of embryonic liver and placenta tissue from BAHD1+/+ (WT)  and BAHD1-/- (KO) mice at embryonic day E16.5

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Comparisons of embryonic liver and placenta gene expression profiles of BAHD1-KO mice to those of wild-type littermate mice

DESIGN
We used DNA microarrays to identify the repertoire of genes differentially expressed by ablation of the BAHD1 gene in embryos.

PLATFORM
GPL6246 [MoGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Mouse Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
