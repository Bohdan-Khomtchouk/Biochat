TITLE
Peripheral blood leukocyte genomic response one day post traumatic injury may predict early respiratory recovery

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This data was used as an example to illustrate a computational method for assessing statistical significance in microarray experiments Contributed by 'The Inflammation and the Host Response to Injury Collaborative Research Program.' Keywords: Two group comparison

DESIGN
Genomic response one day post traumatic injury was compared between patients having early or late respiratory recovery
