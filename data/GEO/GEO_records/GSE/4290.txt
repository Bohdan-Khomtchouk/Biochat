TITLE
Expression data of glioma samples from Henry Ford Hospital

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
mRNA expression data were collected from patients with brain tumor to improve diagnostic of gliomas on molecular level. Keywords: survey

DESIGN
23 samples from epilepsy patients are used as  non-tumor samples. 157 tumor samples include 26 astrocytomas, 50 oligodendrogliomas and 81 glioblastomas.

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
