TITLE
The role of the ETS factor ERG in prostate cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Prostate cancer cell lines that express ERG acquire a neuron-like phenotype.

DESIGN
The human prostate tumor cell line LNCap was transfected with lenti-ERG or control lenti-vector. Gene expression profiling was performed to establish the ERG-associated phenotype.
