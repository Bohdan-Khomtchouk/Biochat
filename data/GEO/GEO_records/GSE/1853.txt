TITLE
interaction with bacteria or bacterial supernatant

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Epithelial cells were in contact either with live bacteria  (S. aureus) or with bacterial supernatant during 3 hours. Three dependant experiment for each condition were performed. RNA from epithelial cells were extracted. Each condition was hybridized on 6 slides (3 slides with their corresponding swap) Keywords: parallel sample
