TITLE
Vietnamese malaria patient experiment

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression data from whole-blood collected from healthy Vietnamese subjects (n=8) or from patients admitted to hospital with acute uncomplicated (n=9) or complicated (n=20) Plasmodium falciparum malaria infection. A clinical history design type is where the organisms clinical history of diagnosis, treatments, e.g. vaccinations, surgery etc. Disease State: patient with complicated or uncomplicated Plasmodium falciparum malaria infection or Healthy control

DESIGN
clinical_history_design
