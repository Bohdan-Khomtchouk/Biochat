TITLE
Transcriptome-wide interaction mapping of hnRNP L in human T cells with CLIP-seq

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
CLIP-seq of hnRNP L in human T cells

DESIGN
Triplicate resting and stimulated human samples from both primary CD4+ and cultured JSL1 (Jurkat-derived monoclonal) were subjected to CLIP-seq to comprehensively map hnRNP L-RNA interaction sites.
