TITLE
Telomerase inhibitor TMPyP4 induced gene expression

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The effect of TMPyP4, the well established anticancer telomerase inhibitor on genome wide expression of genes was investigated Keywords: Time course

DESIGN
Cells were treated with TMPyP4 for either 24 or 48 hours before RNA isolation.Untreated cells were taken as controls and the TMPyP4 treated cells as treatments. Experiments at each time point were replicated four times.
