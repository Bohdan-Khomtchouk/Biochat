TITLE
Expression profiles in Blast Bank samples with AML/RAEB

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
CD133-positive immature blasts were purified from bone marrow of the patients. Total RNA was extracted by RNeasy mini spin column (Qiagen, Inc).

PLATFORM
GPL96 [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array GPL97 [HG-U133B] Affymetrix Human Genome U133B Array

CITATIONS

Gene expression profiles of CD133-positive fractions predict the survival of individuals with acute myeloid leukemia 2006, Cancer Genomics & Proteomics, 3:169-182.
