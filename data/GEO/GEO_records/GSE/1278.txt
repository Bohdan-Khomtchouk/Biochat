TITLE
HGPC, in monolayer, 0.5% serum

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human growth plate chondrocytes were isolated from the distal femoral growth plate from 3 different adolescents undergoing epiphysiodesis.  Cell were isolated and grown in monolayers, long term, in 20% serum.  Cells were replated and cultured 48 h in 0.5% serum. Keywords: repeat sample
