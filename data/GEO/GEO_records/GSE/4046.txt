TITLE
Hmo1 ChIP-chip

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Yeast Hmo1 ChIP-chip in triplicate with single untagged control Keywords: ChIP-chip

DESIGN
Yeast Hmo1 ChIP-chip was performed in yDH419 (Hmo1-3myc) in triplicate using anti-myc (9E10 monoclonal antibody). A control experiment was performed using the untagged isogenic strain, BY4742
