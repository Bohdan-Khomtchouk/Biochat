TITLE
Gene expression analysis of human permanent dental pulp

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression analysis data of human permanent dental pulp tissues

DESIGN
human permanent dental pulp tissues was collected from healthy permanent premolars (n=25; from 5 males and 6 females, aged 11-25 yeas) extracted for orthodontic reasons. Gene expression profiles were conducted using cDNA microarray analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL6244 [HuGene-1_0-st] Affymetrix Human Gene 1.0 ST Array [transcript (gene) version]
