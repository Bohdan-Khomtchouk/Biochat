TITLE
mRNA profiling of MCF7 cells transfected with a miR-139-5p mimic

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiling of MCF7 cells transfected with a miR-139-5p or negative control mimic

DESIGN
MCF7 cells were transfected with a miR-139-5p or negative control mimic. Total RNA was extracted 24 hours later and analyzed by Agilent gene expression microarray

PLATFORM
GPL14550 Agilent-028004 SurePrint G3 Human GE 8x60K Microarray (Probe Name Version)
