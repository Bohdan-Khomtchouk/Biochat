TITLE
Regulatory T cells exhibit distinct features in human breast cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The goal of this study is to compare transcriptional profiles of regulatory T cells and conventional CD4 T cells in human breast cancer to regulatory T cells and conventional CD4 T cells in normal breast parenchyma and in peripheral blood.

DESIGN
RNA sequencing of 2 different cell types in 3 different tissues

PLATFORM
