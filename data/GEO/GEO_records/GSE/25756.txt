TITLE
SAGE analysis of Type 2 Gaucher brain

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This library was constructed from mRNA derived from an eight month old female with type 2 Gaucher disease according to the SAGE protocol of V. E. Velculescu et al. (1995), in the laboratory of R. L.  Proia (NIDDK, NIH).

DESIGN
This GEO Series was created by the GEO staff as part of a cleanup effort to ensure that all GEO Samples are included within a Series entry.
