TITLE
Changes in small RNAs upon Viral infection of C.elegans

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
Attempt to identify small non-coding RNAs that change in levels as a result of viral infection of C.elegans

DESIGN
Small non-coding RNA (18-30nt) was extracted from animals  either infected with Orsay virus or uninfected as indicated.

GPL13657 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Caenorhabditis elegans)
