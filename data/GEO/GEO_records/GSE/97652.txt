TITLE
miRNA profiles within lean and obese adipose tissue macrophages-derived exosomes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Small RNA deep sequencing analysis on the microRNA components within exosomes secreted from adipose tissue macrophages of lean and obese mice

DESIGN
Total RNA extracted from exosomes secreted from adipose tissue macrophages (CD11b+F4/80+) and subjected to small RNA seq analysis.
