TITLE
Human renal clear-cell carcinoma cells : control vs MUC1 overexpressing cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of human renal clear-cell carcinoma cells comparing control unexpressing MUC1 cells (82-F7 and 82-65 samples) with MUC1 overexpressing cells (83-2 and 83-5 samples)

DESIGN
Goal was to determine the effect of MUC1 expression on global gene expression
