TITLE
Expression profile of carcinosarcoma (CS), endometrioid adenocarcinoma (EC) and sarcoma (US) of uterine corpus

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
To examine the simlarity of CS, EC and US, we performed microarray analysis of frozen tissues of 46 patients (14 CS, 24 EC and 8 US).

DESIGN
Frozen tissues of 46 patients (14CS, 24EC and 8US) were subjected to cDNA microarray analysis.

PLATFORM
GPL6480 Agilent-014850 Whole Human Genome Microarray 4x44K G4112F (Probe Name version)
