TITLE
Virulence effects and signaling partners modulated by Brucella melitensis light-sensing histidine kinase

ORGANISM
Brucella melitensis

SUMMARY
A whole genome expression comparision between wild type B. melitensis 16M and ΔBM-LOV-HK (ΔBMEII0679) B. melitensis strains grown in broth culture to determine genes directly and indirectly regulated by BM-LOV-HK.
