TITLE
P46 mouse non-deprived vs. deprived visual cortex

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Wild-type (C57Bl6) mice underwent monocular enucleation at P42 and were sacrificed 4 days later at P46. Bilateral (non-deprived and deprived) visual cortex was dissected from 1-mm thick coronal sections, and total RNA was extracted from the tissue lysate using Trizol (Gibco-BRL). Keywords: repeat
