TITLE
LPS induced Cytokines & Chemokines changes in E9.5 pregnant mouse uteri

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Changes of Cytokines and chemokines profiles were sucessfully determined between mouse non-treated vs LPS treated pregnant uteri using Qiagen PCR array.

DESIGN
Pregnant mouse (FVB strain E8.5-9.5) were treated with LPS(2.5ug/mouse) or saline as control. 8h- or 16-h later,  uteri tissues (N=3) were collected.

PLATFORM
GPL19410 Qiagen Mouse Cytokine and Chemokines RT2 profiler PCR array (PAMM-150Z)
