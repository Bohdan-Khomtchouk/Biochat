TITLE
Small RNA sequencing in Arabidopsis plants

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
Identification of microRNA expressed in Arabidopsis plants grown at different ambient CO2 concentrations and different ambient temperatures

DESIGN
Small RNA sequencing of Arabidopsis wild type ecotype Columbia (Col-0) rossette leaves at bolting stage grown at 430 ± 50ppm and 810 ± 50ppm CO2 concentrations and 22 ± 0.5oC and 28 ± 0.5oC temperatures
