TITLE
Excretory/secretory microRNAs from the gastrointestinal nematode Heligmosomoides polygyrus bakeri in culture.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus ; Heligmosomoides polygyrus bakeri

SUMMARY
We report analyses of the types and amounts of microRNAs found in culture media recovered after incubating approximately 3300 H. p. bakeri adult worms for 48 hours.

DESIGN
Examination of excretory/secretory miRNAs of the mouse gastrointestinal nematode parasite H. p. bakeri released in culture media.

PLATFORM
GPL22935 Illumina MiSeq (Heligmosomoides polygyrus bakeri; Mus musculus)
