TITLE
Transcriptomic analysis of a moderately growing subisolate Botryococcus braunii 779 (Chlorophyta) in response to nitrogen deprivation

ORGANISM
Botryococcus braunii

SUMMARY
We report the transcriptomic response of a moderately growing subisolate from the culture of Botryococcus sp. CCALA-779 upon nitrogen deprivation (ND).

DESIGN
Examination of transcriptomes upon nitrogen deprivation (ND). B.braunii_non_redundant_transcriptome_assembly_61220.fasta: non-redundant assembly (with 61220 in number) of B.braunii 779 transcriptome in this study B.braunii_annotated_12292.fasta: investigated ESTs (with 12292 in number, which perfectly matches with our processed RNA expression profiling) in this study

PLATFORM
GPL20730 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Botryococcus braunii)
