TITLE
DNA-Seq of DrosDel deficiency lines and w1118 lines

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
DNASeq reads from pools of female or male DrosDel and w1118 (parental strain for the Df/+ flies). Samples are named in this dataset according to the following sample naming scheme: tissue_genotype shorthand_sex_DNASeq_biological replicate #.
