TITLE
Detectable changes in the blood transcriptome are present after two weeks of antituberculosis therapy

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Analysis of UK blood transcriptional profiles before treatment to indentify changes that occur during (2 weeks, 2 months), at the end of treatment (6 months) and after treatment (12 months) Analysis of South African blood transcriptional profiles before treatment to indentify changes that occur during (2 weeks, 2 months), at the end of treatment (6 months) and after treatment (12 months)

DESIGN
Total RNA obtained from active TB patients before, during and after treatment
