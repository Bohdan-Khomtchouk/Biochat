TITLE
Whole blood RNA sequencing of current and former cigarette smokers

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Purpose: The purpose of this study was to describe transcriptomic changes between current and former smokers using differential gene expression and differential exon usage

DESIGN
Whole blood from 515 subjects (229 current smokers and 286 former smokers) were RNA sequenced. Raw read data will be submitted to dbGaP. -------------------------- Submitter states " Raw read data will be submitted to dbGaP. "
