TITLE
C1 analysis using mixtures of human (HEK) and mouse (3T3) cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens ; Mus musculus

SUMMARY
A cell supsension containing an equal mix of HEK and 3T3 cells was used in the Fluidigm C1

DESIGN
Suspensions of 3T3 and HEK cells were diluted down to a concentration of 250,000 per mL and mixed 1:1, then loaded onto two medium C1 cell capture chips.
