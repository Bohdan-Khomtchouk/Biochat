TITLE
Disinfectant-resistant Listeria monocytogenes strains indentified in a Iberian pork plant: S1 [160908] vs S10_1 [160908]

ORGANISM
Listeria monocytogenes

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of two disinfectant-resistant Listeria monocytogenes strains indentified in a Iberian pork plant, S1 [160908] vs S10_1 [160908], in presence of 1.25 mg/L of Benzalkonium chloride

DESIGN
One condition experiment, S1 [160908] vs. S10_1 [160908] strains. Biological replicates: 3 replicates of S1 [160908], 3 replicates of S10 [160908].

PLATFORM
GPL19673 Agilent-047931 Listeria monocytogenes 15K array Lmo0503
