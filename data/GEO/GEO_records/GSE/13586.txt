TITLE
Gene expression analysis of HESC derived teratomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression analysis of human embryonic stem cell derived tumors (teratomas).

DESIGN
Undifferentiated HESCs were injected into the testis or under the kidney capsule of SCID-NOD mice and were let to grow for one month prior to tumor harvesting.
