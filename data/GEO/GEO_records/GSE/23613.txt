TITLE
Juvenile rainbow trout exposed in situ at site receiving different concentrations of municipal wastewater effluent (MWWE) and an upstream reference site

ORGANISM
Oncorhynchus mykiss

SUMMARY
Juvenile rainbow trout were exposed to MWWE in a caging study, with cages held upstream and downstream of a MWWE outfall for two weeks in the fall of 2007. Whole tissues were sampled and analyzed. Microarray analysis was conducted on liver samples.

DESIGN
Four condition experiment, Control (0%),10%, 50% and 100% MWWE. Four independent pooled samples for each treatment, hybridized against a reference pool. The reference pool and test sample were randomly assigned for each array.
