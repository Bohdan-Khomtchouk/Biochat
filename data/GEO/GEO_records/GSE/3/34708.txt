TITLE
modENCODE_White Lab: genome-wide ChIP data of Ubx from E0-12 on Illumina Genome Analyzer.

ORGANISM
modENCODE

SUMMARY
This is a dataset generated by the Drosophila Regulatory Elements modENCODE Project led by Kevin P. White at the University of Chicago.
