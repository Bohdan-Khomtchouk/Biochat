TITLE
Gene expression profiling in HCV associated liver disease

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression of human genes was compared in liver fibrosis stage F1, F2, F3, and F4 to find markers that can differentiate among each fibrosis stages and cirrhosis.

DESIGN
Four- condition experiment, F1 vs. F2 vs. F3 vs. F4. Biological replicates: 3 Normal, 3 F1, 3 F2, 3 F3 and 3 F4 stage samples.
