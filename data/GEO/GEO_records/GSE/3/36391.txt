TITLE
Transcriptome sequencing of Foxtail millet Setaria italica for different tissues

ORGANISM
Setaria italica

SUMMARY
Transcriptome sequencing of Foxtail millet Setaria italica (Zhang-gu) for different tissues.

DESIGN
Four RNA pools were created corresponding to four different tissues: root, leaf, stem, spica (tassel) at developmental stage, then each pool was sequenced.
