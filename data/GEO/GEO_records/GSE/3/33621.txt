TITLE
PCR array screening for gene expression changes in HCC model comparing sorafenib treated or control samples

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Candidate genes were successfully screened by Cancer PathwayFinder PCR Array

DESIGN
Cancer PathwayFinder PCR Array was applied to compare gene changes in xenograft tumor samples of HCC model. A total of 12 samples were examined with 6 from Sorafenib treated group and 6 from control.
