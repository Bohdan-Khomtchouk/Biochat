TITLE
Identification of the differences between expression patterns of kinin-dependent genes in endometrial cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The present study has focused on the identification of the differences between expression patterns of kinin-dependent genes in endometrial cancer

DESIGN
For microarray analysis  20 samples were used: 7 control samples, 3 - G1 samples, 8 - G2 samples and 2 - G3 samples.
