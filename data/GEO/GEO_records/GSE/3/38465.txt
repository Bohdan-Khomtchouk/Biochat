TITLE
Analysis of brain cortices of SAMP8 and SAMR1 mice after an exercise intervention

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Microarrays has been used to analyze the effect of voluntary wheel running in the SAMP8 mice using the SAMR1 mouse strain as control.

DESIGN
Cortex gene expresion of SAMP8 which have been resting or exercising and SAMR1 sedentary.
