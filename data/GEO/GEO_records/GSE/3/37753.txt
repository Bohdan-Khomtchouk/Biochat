TITLE
AGS cells infected with Epstein Barr Virus

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression profile of AGS gastric carcinoma cell line infected in vitro with Epstein-Barr Virus.  Some samples also contain are stably transfected with a dominant negative LMP1 construct.

DESIGN
8 total samples.  4 biological replicates of EBV infected cells, 2 biological replicates with EBV infected cells with LMP1DN construct, and 2 biological replicates with EBV infected cells with control vector.
