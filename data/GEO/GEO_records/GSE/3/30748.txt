TITLE
Gene expression profile of Tet1 knockout mouse embryonic stem cells (mESCs)

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Global gene expression profile of Tet1 knockout ES cells is compared to wild-type ES cells. All ES lines used are V6.5 (mix 129 C57BL6 backgound).

DESIGN
2 Tet1 KO mice compared to 1 Tet1 wild type mouse.
