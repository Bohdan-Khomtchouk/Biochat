TITLE
Transcription factor profiling of T cells containing stably integrated or transiently transfected HTLV-1 LTR.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Transcription factor profiling of T cells containing stably integrated or transiently transfected HTLV-1 LTR.

DESIGN
Nuclear extract from one million stably or transiently transfected Jurkat (CD4 T cell) cell line was extracted and labeled with TransSignal Probe mix (Panomics) and then hybridized to the array.
