TITLE
Halothane Repetitive Exposure

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
12 Controls, 3 5-exposures, 3 10-exposures. Rats were exposed to 90 minutes of 0.8% halothane twice a day for a total of 5 or 10 exposures. Animals did not require intubation. All exposures and hybridizations were performed at the Univ. of Pennsylvania. Keywords: time-course
