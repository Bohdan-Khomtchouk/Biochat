TITLE
Array CGH of Drosophila polytene chromosomes on heterochromatin custom array.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
CGH analysis of Drosophila polytene chromosomes.

DESIGN
Drosophila polytene chromosomes were isolated from whole salivary glands dissected from roaming third-instar larvae or from ovaries dissected from adult females. Salivary gland has two biological repeats.
