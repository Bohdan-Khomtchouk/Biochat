TITLE
Gene Expression in Cord Blood Derived Lineage Negative and Positive Hematopoeitic Stem Cells After 4 Days in Culture.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression of cord blood derived lineage negative and lineage positive hematopoeitic stem cells was studied by collecting samples following 4 days of culture in Stem span medium. For comparison, samples were also collected following both a mock and fresh medium exchange. Keywords: other
