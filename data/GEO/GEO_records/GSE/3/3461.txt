TITLE
Gene expression in miscellaneous human tissues and cell lines

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Each tissue or cell line polyA+ RNA-labeled with Cy5 was hybridized against a common reference pool consisting of 11 different cell lines described elsewhere (Perou et al. 2000) Set of arrays organized by shared biological context, such as organism, tumors types, processes, etc. Keywords: Logical Set
