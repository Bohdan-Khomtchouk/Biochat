TITLE
Landscape of somatic allelic imbalances and copy number alterations in HER2-amplified breast cancer

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
A survey of the somatic allelic imbalances and copy number alterations in HER2-amplified breast cancer.

DESIGN
Genomic profiling of 26 breast tumors with amplification of HER2 using 1M and 2.5M Illumina SNP beadchips. Sample identifiers correspond to GSE21259 where sample annotations may be extracted.
