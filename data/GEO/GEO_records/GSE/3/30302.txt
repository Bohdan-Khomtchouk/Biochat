TITLE
phytoplasma in plants vs. phytoplasma in insects (co-hybridizations)

ORGANISM
Candidatus Phytoplasma asteris

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of phytoplasma grown in plant (Chrysanthemum coronarium) and grown in insect (Macrosteles striifrons).

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, phytoplasma-infected plant and phytoplasma-infected insect. Biological replicates: 6 phytoplasma-infected plants and 6 phytoplasma-infected insects, independently grown and harvested. One replicate per array.
