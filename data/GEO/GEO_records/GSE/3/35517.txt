TITLE
WT vs KO hdiR comparison

ORGANISM
Streptococcus thermophilus LMD-9

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of S. thermophilus LMD-9 wild-type compared to the isogenic mutant strain CB0082 (deletion of hdiR, STER_0918) for the identification of the HdiR regulon. Cells were grown in CDM medium supplemented with lactose 1% (CDML) and sampled at OD600 = 0.4 for mRNA extraction
