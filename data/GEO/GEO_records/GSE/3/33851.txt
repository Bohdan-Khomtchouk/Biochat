TITLE
Estrogen-like activity and dual roles in cell signaling of brefeldin A

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparing the expression profiles of the genes in response to 17b-estradiol (E2) and brefeldin A (BFA)

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, E2- or BFA-treated vs. control cells. Two biological replicates, independently grown and harvested. Two replicates per array.
