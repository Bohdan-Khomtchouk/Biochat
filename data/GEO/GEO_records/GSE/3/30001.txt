TITLE
Bacillus subtilis 168 moenomycin stimulon

ORGANISM
Bacillus subtilis

SUMMARY
Transcriptional response of Bacillus subtilis to moenomycin in wild-type 168.

DESIGN
Bacillus subtilis 168, WT (-MOE) vs. WT (+MOE). The experiment was conducted in triplicate using three independent total RNA preparations. Untreated samples were labeled with Alexa Fluor 555 and moenomycin treated samples were labeled with Alexa Fluor 647.
