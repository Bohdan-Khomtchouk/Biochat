TITLE
SNP data from 78 pheochromocytomas and paragangliomas

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
SNP profiles from 78 pheochromocytomas and paragangliomas were analyzed to detect copy number changes and LOH.

DESIGN
78 pheochromocytomas and paragangliomas were analyzed with Illumina Human610-Quad v1.0 BeadChips.
