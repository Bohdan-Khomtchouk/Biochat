TITLE
Dual inactivation of Akt and ERK by small molecule TIC10 signals Foxo3a nuclear translocation, TRAIL gene induction and potent anti-tumor effects

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
TIC10-induced transcriptional changes at 48hrs in HCT116 p53-null cells (10uM).

DESIGN
Six samples of HCT116 p53-null cells with TIC10 (10uM) or DMSO treatment. Three replicates each.
