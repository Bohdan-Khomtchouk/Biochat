TITLE
Expression profiling of Arabidopsis wild type Col-0 and pmr5 mutant

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

SUMMARY
The rosettes from genotype Col-0 and pmr5 mutant were collected for RNA expression profiling when the plants were 3 weeks old.

DESIGN
The rosettes were collected when the plants were 3 weeks old for RNA extraction and hybridization on Affymetrix microarrays.
