TITLE
H3K27me3 Marks in Normal and Cancerous Prostate Cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The histone modification H3K27me3 is representative of silenced regions of the genome. Changes between the normal and cancer state were investigated.

DESIGN
Three PrEC samples, a normal cell line. Four LNCaP sample, a cancer cell line. Technical replicates.
