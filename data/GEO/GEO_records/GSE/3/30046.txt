TITLE
Ixr1 and the Saccharomyces cerevisiae hypoxic response

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

SUMMARY
Analysis of the transcriptome of the wild-type strain BY4741 and its isogenic derivative ixr1 null, grown in aerobic, hypoxic conditions and after a hypoxic shift

DESIGN
Two biological replicates in two technical replicates for each growth condition
