TITLE
Identification of 20E-regulated genes in Drosophila cultured larval organs

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
To identify 20E-regulated genes, wandering third instar larvae were dissected and their organs were cultured in the presence of either no hormone, 20E alone, cycloheximide alone, or 20E plus cycloheximide for six hours. Keywords: Hormone response
