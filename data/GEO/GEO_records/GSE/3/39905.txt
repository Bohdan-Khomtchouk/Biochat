TITLE
microRNA profiling of human monocytes and monocyte-derived macrophages

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
microRNA profiling data includes biological replicates of primary monocytes and macrophages from three human donors

DESIGN
Dye swap hybridization arrays were performed for total RNA isolated from fresh monocytes and 7-day monocyte-derived macrophages from each of three human donors
