TITLE
Differentiating factors of cumulus cells related to quality of the human oocyte [CadoretPart1]

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Our  aim  was  to  identify  at  the  level  of cumulus cells any genes related to oocyte developmental competence

DESIGN
three-condition experiment: blastocyst (B), embryo of poor quality (E) AND immature oocytes (VG).
