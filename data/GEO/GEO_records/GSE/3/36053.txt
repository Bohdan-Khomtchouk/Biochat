TITLE
Transcriptomic response of the auxotrophic Mycobacterium avium subspecies paratuberculosis (MAP) leuD mutant under environmental stresses

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium avium subsp. paratuberculosis K-10

SUMMARY
Comparing the transcriptomic responses between the Mycobacterium avium subspecies paratuberculosis (MAP) leuD mutant with the parent strain K-10 under different environmental stresses: nutrition, temperature, anaerobic conditions, high- and low- pH conditions.
