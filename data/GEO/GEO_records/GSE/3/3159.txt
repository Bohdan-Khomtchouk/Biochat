TITLE
ICAT quantitative proteomic analysis of Myc-induced apoptosis

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
Quantitative proteomic analysis of Myc-induced apoptosis in serum-deprived Rat1_Myc fibroblasts. Mitochondrial, chromatin, and soluble fractions analyzed. Original peptide data contained in Supplementary files. Keywords: proteomic, apoptosis, cell fractions
