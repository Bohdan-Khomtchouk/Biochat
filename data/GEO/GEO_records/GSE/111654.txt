TITLE
RNA-seq from animals treated, and non treated,  with cisplatin

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
Transcriptome analysis of a population of control animals vs cisplatin-treated, in duplicate

DESIGN
A mixed population of worms representing all stages and growing under control conditions was exposed to 60 µg/ml of cisplatin for 24 hours at 20ºC. Treated and control samples weer collected in biological replicates.

PLATFORM
GPL13657 Illumina HiSeq 2000 (Caenorhabditis elegans)
