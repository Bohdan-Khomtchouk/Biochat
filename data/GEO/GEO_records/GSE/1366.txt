TITLE
Total-body irradiation dose response

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Duplicate hybridizations with fluorochrome switching were performed following the first two fractions of total body irradiation for accumulated doses of 1.5 Gy and 3.0 Gy.  Control samples for all hybridizations were from the same patient before the beginning of treatment. Keywords: dose response
