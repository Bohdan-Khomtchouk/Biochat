TITLE
Genetic and Phenotypic Intra-Species Variation in Candida albicans: Differential expression analysis

ORGANISM
Candida albicans

SUMMARY
Differential expression of isolates of Candida albicans grown in YPD using RNA-Seq.

DESIGN
We examined transcript level changes in YPD for five strains; P94015 (two replicates) and P60002 were compared to three other strains as controls (SC5314, P87, GC75).
