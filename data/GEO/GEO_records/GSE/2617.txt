TITLE
Diagnostic miniarray of childhood acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL)

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We developed and validated a diagnostic miniarray to recognize different subtypes of ALL. We analyzed blindly the gene expression profiling of 17 patients: 10 ALL-B, 5 ALL-T and 2 MLL/AF4. This miniarray was able to assign all samples to the correct class. We compared the transcription profiles of the leukemic samples by miniarray competitive hybridisation against an arbitrary total RNA reference prepared from normal human bone marrow.
