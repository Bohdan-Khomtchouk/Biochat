TITLE
GABA regulates release of inflammatory cytokines from peripheral blood mononuclear cells and CD4+ T cells and is immunosuppressive in type 1 diabetes

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study compared the transcriptome profiling (RNA-seq) of CD3+ T cells from nondiabetic (ND) individuals and patients with type 1 diabetes (T1D).

DESIGN
Total RNA was extracted from CD3+ T cells of 18 nondiabetic individuals and 31patients with type 1 diabetes and subjected to deep sequencing using Illumina HiSeq 2500.

GPL16791 Illumina HiSeq 2500 (Homo sapiens)
