TITLE
Transcriptional changes in Atlantic salmon (Salmo salar) after embryonic exposure to road salt (NaCl)

ORGANISM
Salmo salar

SUMMARY
The present study aimed to identify the persistent molecular changes occurring in Atlantic Salmon salmon (Salmo salar) eggs after 24h exposure to high concentrations (5000 mg/L) of road salt at fertilization.

DESIGN
Atlantic Salmon (Salmo salar) eggs after fertilization were exposed to high concentrations (5000 mg/L) of road salt for 24 h and used for gene expression analysis.
