TITLE
Transcriptional comparison of Drosophila cell lines using Kc167 as standard

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
A series of Drosophila melanogaster cell lines were each compared directly to the cell line Kc167. Keywords: Cell line comparison

DESIGN
Each slide is a comparison of RNA from a Drosophila cell line to RNA from the cell line Kc167.  3-4 biological replicates were done; dye swaps were included
