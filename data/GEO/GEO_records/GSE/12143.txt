TITLE
Listeria monocytogenes EGD after growth BHI vs. LB vs. MM

ORGANISM
Listeria monocytogenes

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profile of prfA, the dependent genes and the PTS genes after growth in BHI (brain-heart-infusion), LB (Luria Bertani broth) and MM (minimal medium (Premaratne et al. 2001)  (each supplemented with 50 mM Glucose). Keywords: media comparison

DESIGN
A total of three independently isolated RNA samples from each condition were used for the analysis.
