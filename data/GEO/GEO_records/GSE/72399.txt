TITLE
RNA Microarray of 36H liver stages of Pb WT and Pb SSPELD Knock out parasites

ORGANISM
Plasmodium berghei

SUMMARY
Global gene expression profiling of P. berghei liver stages at 36h from WT and Pb SSPELD KO parasites to investigate the changes in RNA expression levels in Pb SSPELD parasites.

DESIGN
HepG2 cells were infected with Plasmodium berghei Wild type and SSPELD knock out sporozoites

PLATFORM
GPL20853 Agilent-067226 Plasmodium berghei Gene Expression Custom Microarray 4x44k
