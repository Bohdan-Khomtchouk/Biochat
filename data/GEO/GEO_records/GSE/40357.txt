TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of Lactococcus garvieae strain 8831 grown at 13ºC and 18ºC

ORGANISM
Lactococcus garvieae ; Lactococcus garvieae 8831 ; Lactococcus garvieae 21881

SUMMARY
Transcritome profiling of the fish pathogen Lactococcus garvieae strain 8831 in response to growth temperature

DESIGN
Reference design experiment with samples of Lactoccocus garvieae 8831 grown at 13ºC and 18ºC. Three biological replicates. The results were referenced to a equimolar mixture of L. garvieae strains 8831 and 21881 grown at 29ºC.
