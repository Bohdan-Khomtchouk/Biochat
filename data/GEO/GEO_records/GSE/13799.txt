TITLE
Hippocampal gene expression changes during age-related cognitive decline

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Comparison of hippocampal gene expression between normal young animals and older animals with good spatial memory and older animals with poor spatial memory

DESIGN
The hippocampus from animals with good spatial memory (unimpaired) were compared to animals with poor spartial memory (impaired)

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
