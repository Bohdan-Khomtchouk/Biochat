TITLE
Transcriptional profiling of six normal Angus tissues

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of six bovine tissues (small intestine, spleen, liver, adrenal gland, anterior pituitary, and thymus). Samples collected from each of 6 Angus steers at 14 months of age. Experiment was duplicated at two locations to measure reproducability of results.

DESIGN
Six tissues, six biological replicates, and two technical replicates of each sample for a total of 72 samples. The experiment was run seperatly at two different locations, for a total of 144 samples.
