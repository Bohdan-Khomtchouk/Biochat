TITLE
rem-1 analysis in C. elegans

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

SUMMARY
small RNA libraries from total RNA isolated from young adult animals

DESIGN
Wild-type and rem-1 mutant animals were used for RNA isolation. Regular libraries were made using adaptor ligations at both ends. In addition, librraies were made from oxidised and TAP treated RNA.

GPL9269 Illumina Genome Analyzer II (Caenorhabditis elegans)
