TITLE
Acute myeloid leukemia presenting with leukopenia: a distinct entity

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Patients with acute myeloid leukemia and low circulating white count have a different gene expression profile compared those with high white count

DESIGN
Bone marrow of ten patients( 5 patients with high and 5 patients with low peripheral blood white count) with blast count higher than 80% were selected for RNA extraction. RNA was used for gene expression analysis
