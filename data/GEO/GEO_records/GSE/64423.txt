TITLE
Gene expression profiling in proximal versus distal mouse colon

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression in proximal versus distal part of the mouse large intestine.

DESIGN
Three (3) animals (biological replicates) were used to isolate tissue from proximal and distal areas of the large intestine.

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
