TITLE
Genome-wide DNA methylation profiling of human dorsolateral prefrontal cortex

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Reduced representation bisulfite sequencing (RRBS) was conducted on dorsolateral prefrontal cortex tissue samples taken from the brains of control individuals not affected by neurological disorder

DESIGN
DNA methylation profiling was conducted using RRBS and the Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx

PLATFORM
GPL10999 Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx (Homo sapiens)
