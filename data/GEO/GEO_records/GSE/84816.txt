TITLE
Candida glabrata WT and mutants under iron starvation

ORGANISM
[Candida] glabrata

SUMMARY
Transcriptional time series of Candida glabrata under iron starvation (SD medium without Fe). Wild type and deletion mutants of the iron-related transcription factors Aft1 and Sef1, as well as of the iron uptake transporter Ftr1 as a positive control.

DESIGN
Common reference design, reference is log-phase wild type in YPD complex medium
