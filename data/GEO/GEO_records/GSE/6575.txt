TITLE
Gene expression in blood of children with autism spectrum disorder

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Gene expression in blood of children with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) was studied. Transcriptional profiles were compared with age and gender matched, typically developing children from the general population (GP) or IQ matched children with mental retardation or developmental delay (MR/DD). Keywords: autism analysis
