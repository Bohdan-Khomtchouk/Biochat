TITLE
Imipramine Blue: A potent therapeutic regimen that suppresses breast cancer growth and metastasis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Imipramine Blue a novel inhibitor that we recently synthesiszed suppressed breast cancer growth and progression by silencing FOXM1 and its associated signaling.

DESIGN
Breast cancer cell-line MDA-MB-231 treated with vehicle (DMSO) and IB, with 2 biological replciates each.
