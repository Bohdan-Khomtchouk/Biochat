TITLE
Oncoscan for subclonal analysis in a lobular breast cancer with classical and solid growth pattern mimicking a solid-papillary carcinoma

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Affymetrix Oncoscan arrays were performed according to the manufacturer's directions on DNA extracted from FFPE-breast cancer tissues.

DESIGN
Copy number analysis using Affymetrix Oncoscan arrays was performed for 3 parts of the tumor

PLATFORM
GPL18602 [OncoScan] Affymetrix OncoScan FFPE Assay
