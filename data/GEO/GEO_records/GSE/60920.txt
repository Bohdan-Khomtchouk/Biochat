TITLE
Memory CD4+ T cells from Vaccinia vaccinated subjects.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Comparsion of the transcriptomes of naive, resting memory and activated memory CD4+ T lymphocytes.  Peripheral blood lymphocytes were collected at baseline and day 13 post-inoculation of a healthy subject administered with vaccinia virus. Cells were isolated by FACs using antibodies against CD4, CD45RO and CD38.

DESIGN
3 separate biological samples analysed

PLATFORM
GPL570 [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
