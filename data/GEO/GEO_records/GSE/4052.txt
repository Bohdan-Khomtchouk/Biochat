TITLE
Near Naked Hairless mouse skin array

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
This is the data from 7 day-old homozygous Hrn mutant mice compared to the wild type mice with mouse 22k compugen oligolibrary 2.0. The data was normalized with GeneSight from BioDiscovery and analyzed with SAM. Keywords: Genetic mutant

DESIGN
7 day-old near naked hairless mutant mice vs 7 day-old wild type mice. Five biological replicates were used from one litter. Dye-swapping was done to remove dye-specific errors

PLATFORM
GPL3361 Compugen Mouse OligoLibrary 2.0
