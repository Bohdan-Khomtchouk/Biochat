TITLE
Skeletal muscle allotypes

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of 8 skeletal muscles from adult C57BL mice.  Extraocular, temporalis, anterior digastric, sternocleidomastoid, triceps, diaphragm, quadraceps femoris (vastus lateralis), and tibialis anterior were evaluated using Affymetrix 430 v.2 arrays. Keywords: parallel sample

PLATFORM
GPL1261 [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
