TITLE
Cyanide response in Escherichia coli

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

SUMMARY
The goal was to ascertain the impact of cyanide treatment on E. coli's transcriptome.

DESIGN
There were 4 biological samples.  For each sample there were two replicate DNA microarray performed.  Data was analyzed with Imagene and lcDNA Hyduke DR, Rohlin L, Kao KC, Liao JC. 2003 A software package for cDNA microarray data normalization and assessing confidence intervals. OMICS 7(3):227-234.

PLATFORM
GPL5113 JCL_Ecoli_1

CITATIONS
AiLing Teh, Donovan Layton, Daniel R. Hyduke, Laura R. Jarboe, and Derrick K. Rollins, Sr. Data Mining Based on Principal Component Analysis: Application to the Nitric Oxide Response in Escherichia coli. Journal of Statistical Science and Application 2 (2014) 1-18
