TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of bacteria challenged with antimicrobial peptides of plant origin

ORGANISM
Sinorhizobium meliloti 1021

SUMMARY
Effect of NCR247 and NCR335 on  S. meliloti transcriptosome using Solid4 sequencer

DESIGN
S. meliloti 1021 strain was treated by 10 mg/L synthetised NCR247 or NCR335 peptides and gene expression were measured after 10 and 30 minutes
