TITLE

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Examine differences in Type-1 alveolar epithelial cells with and without alveolar macrophages during influenza infection

DESIGN
Type-1 alveolar epithelial cells were isolated from mice with and without alveolar macrophages at day 2 post-infection with influenza a virus
