TITLE
mouse liver samples

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Total RNA was extracted from mouse liver samples using an Ambion RNaqueous RNA extraction kit. Targets were produced using modified Affymetrix procedure from 5ug of total RNA. 3 biological replicates were analysed per each group of animals.
