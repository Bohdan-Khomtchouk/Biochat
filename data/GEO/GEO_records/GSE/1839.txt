TITLE
Equivalence of biologic responses induced in the mouse uterus by E2, GEN, and DES

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Three independent replicates of 4 groups of immature (19/20 days of age) Alpk:APfCD-1 mice were treated with arachis oil (AO) vehicle, 2.5ug/kg 17beta-estradiol (E2), 2ug/kg diethylstilbestrol (DES), or 50mg/kg genistein (GEN), via 3 daily subcutaneous injection, and sacrificed 72hr after initial dose.
