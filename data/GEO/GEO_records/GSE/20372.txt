TITLE
Gene expression in E14 TG2a cells grown with or without LIF and sorted for 5T4 expression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
E14 TG2a cells grown with LIF were disaggregated and FACS sorted for cell surface 5T4 negativity versus E14 TG2a cells grown without LIF for 3 days  and sorted for 5T4 positivity.

DESIGN
Two samples in triplicate. 5T4- vs. 5T4+
