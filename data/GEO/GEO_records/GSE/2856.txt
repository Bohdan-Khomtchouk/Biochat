TITLE
Comparison of Rostral, Middle, and Caudal E13.5 murine neocortex

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Rostral, Middle, and Caudal thirds of E13.5 murine neocortex are compared Keywords: Tissue comparison

DESIGN
Three pools of rostral (R1-3), three pools of middle (M1-3), and three pools of caudal (C1-3) E13.5 MF1 neocortex were collected from 4 embryos. All possible 18 comparisons were carried out.
