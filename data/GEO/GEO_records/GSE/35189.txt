TITLE
Paradigm Test Set aCGH Array

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
aCGH data was used in Paradigm analysis for exploration of networks affected by copy number and gene expression changes based on mutation spectra of recurrently mutated genes in breast cancer.

DESIGN
124 patients including 3 Pre-Operative Letrozole and 43 Z1031 patients were arrayed and analyzed
