TITLE
Transcriptome profiling of primary murine fibroblast response to IFNB with or without prior Drosha depletion

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To assess the impact of Drosha on the response to IFNB

DESIGN
Non-replicateive Adeno vectors were used to deliver either GFP or Cre to primary fibroblasts 3 days before a 6hr treatment with 100U IFNB.
