TITLE
Measles Virus-Infected Dendritic Cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Human CD14+ monocytes were isolated and grown in GM-CSF and IL-4 for six days.  The cells were then infected with measles virus, Chicago-1 strain, and RNA was isolated at 3, 6, 12, and 24 hours post-infection. Keywords: time-course
