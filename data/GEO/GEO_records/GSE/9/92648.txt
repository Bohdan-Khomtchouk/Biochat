TITLE
Analysis of differentially expressed genes in SMMC-7721 cell line after transfection with miR-215 and siPCAT1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
This study aimed to identify differential expressed genes before and after tranfection with miR-215 and siPCAT1, using the 7721 liver cancer cell line as a model.

DESIGN
