TITLE
Lung metastatic derivative LM2 cells with and without miR-335

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
the LM2 breast cancer cell line is an in vivo derived line from the MDA-MB-231 parental line.  this LM2 line has been transduced either with a short hairpin control or miR-335 expression vector. Keywords: breast cancer, metastasis, miRNA
