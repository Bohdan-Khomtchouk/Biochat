TITLE
small RNAs produced in the mutant Dicer-2 transgenic flies

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
Examine the abundance and length of siRNAs produced by mutant Dicer-2 in vivo and in vitro in order to understand the mechanism by which Dicer-2 produces highly precise 21 nt siRNAs.
