TITLE
Differential responses of human fetal brain neural stem cells to Zika virus infection

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Purpose: The goal of this study is to investigate the effect of Zika virus infection on neural stem cells Methods: RNA-Seq transcriptome analysis of neural stem cells infected with Zika virus compared to mock infected

DESIGN
RNA-seq transcriptome profiling of Zika infected human fetal neural stem cells
