TITLE
Expression profiling feline peripheral blood monocytes identifies a transcriptional signature associated with type two diabetes mellitus

ORGANISM
Felis catus

SUMMARY
Peripheral blood monocytes were isolated from 3 control and 3 diabetic cats using positive selection and expression profiled using the Affymetrix Feline 1.0ST array.

DESIGN
Comparison of monocyte expression in cats with type 2 diabetes or controls (n=3 per group)
