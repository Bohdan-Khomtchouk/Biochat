TITLE
Yellow-Leaf 1 encodes a magnesium-protoporphyrin IX monomethyl ester cyclase, involved in chlorophyll biosynthesis in rice (Oryza sativa L.)

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

SUMMARY
We performed a transcriptomics analysis in the wild type and the mutant, and the expression levels of photosynthesis and chloroplast development genes were  affected in yl-1 mutant.

DESIGN
RNA-sequencing analysis was performed on an Illumina Hiseq 4000 (LC Sciences, USA) following the vendor's recommended protocol
