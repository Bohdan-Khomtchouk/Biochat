TITLE
Fathead minnow liver samples: control, EE2 and EE2/zm

ORGANISM
Pimephales promelas

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of fathead minnow liver exposed either to the estrogen EE2 or a mixture of EE2 and the anti-estrogen ZM.

DESIGN
Three condition experiment: Control, EE2 or the mixture EE2/ZM; liver tissue; four biological replicates.
