TITLE
ChIP-chip analysis of RNAP and Sigma factor binding to the Salmonella enterica serovar Typhimurium chromosome

ORGANISM
Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Typhimurium str. SL1344

SUMMARY
ChIP-chip analysis of RNA Polymerase (RNAP), RpoD, RpoE, RpoH and RpoN in exponential phase (OD 0.2) and early stationary phase (OD 2.0) Salmonella enterica serovar Typhimurium SL1344 cultures
