TITLE
DNA methylation analysis of bull semen according to peripubertal age (12vs16 mo)

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
The DNA methylation pattern in spermatozoa of bulls at different age was investigated. Spermatozoa collected from 4 different bulls at 12 and 16 month each. Genome-wide DNA methylation analysis was performed by micro array using EDMA platform.

DESIGN
Two condition experiment,  4 bulls, all collected at 12 and 16 months old
