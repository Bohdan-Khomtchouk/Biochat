TITLE
DNA methylome modifications in ME/CFS

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Examination of DNA methylome patterns in a larger cohort of ME/CFS samples using the Illumina Infinium HumanMethylation450 Beadchip Array

DESIGN
Bisulfite-converted DNA from 75 samples were hybridised onto the Illumina Infinium HumanMethylation450 Beadchip Array
