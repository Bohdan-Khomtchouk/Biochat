TITLE
ALK-TPM3 rearrangement in adult renal cell carcinoma: report of a novel case showing misleading TFE3 expression and loss of chromosome 3 and literature review

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
We report a case of a 55 years old women who present a ALK associated renal cell carcinoma, with 3p deletion and measling of TFE3 expression. With CGH analysis and FISH we identify the rearrangment of ALK with TPM3

DESIGN
1 case
