TITLE
Transcriptome analysis of samples from patients with endometriosis

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
The aim of this research was to explore activation/deactivation of signaling pathways in endometrium samples from patients with endometriosis.

DESIGN
Samples of endometriosis, ovary cysts and cervical endometrium were obtained during surgical procedures. Tissues were placed in RNAlater solution immediately after removal and stored at -20Â°C.
