TITLE
Gene expression of GidA mutant Streptococcus pyogenes

ORGANISM
Streptococcus pyogenes

SUMMARY
In Streptococcus pyogenes, mutation of GidA results in avirulence despite the same growth rate as the wild type.  To understand the basis of this effect, global transcription profiling was conducted. Keywords: Wild type vs. GidA mutant Streptococcus pyogenes
