TITLE

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Cell line pairs with different phenotypes were isolated from individual patients to study intra-tumor heterogeneity in neuroblastoma

DESIGN
Expression analysis of cell line pairs with different phenotypes was used to study intra-tumor heterogeneity
