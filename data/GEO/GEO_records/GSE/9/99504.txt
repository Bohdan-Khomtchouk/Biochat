TITLE
Microbiota promotes systemic T-cell survival through suppression of an apoptotic factor

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Examining differences in expression profiles of primary CD4+ T cells overexpressing ERDR1.

DESIGN
Splenic CD4+ T cells with control MIGR1 or ERDR1 overexpressing MIGR1 mRNA profiles were generated using deep sequencing in triplicate.
