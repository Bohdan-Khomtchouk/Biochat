TITLE
The whole human genome expression data from Huh7 cells

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Botanic drug CA regulated the genes expression in tumor cells. We used microarrays to detail the differentiation genes expression treated with CA or not.

DESIGN
The Huh7 cells were untreated, or treated with 50 μM or 100 μM of CA for 24 hrs.
