TITLE
Bovine in-vitro blastocysts vs granulosa cells

ORGANISM
Bos taurus

SUMMARY
Transcriptional profiling of bovine in-vitro produced blastocysts at day 7 and granulosa cells collected at day 8 to 11 post-eostrus of lactating cows.

DESIGN
Two-condition experiment, in-vitro Blastocyst vs. Granulosa cells. Biological replicates: 3 . One replicate per array. Dye swap experiment, two-colors.
