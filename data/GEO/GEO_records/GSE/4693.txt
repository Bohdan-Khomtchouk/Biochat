TITLE
Genes regulated by E47

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
To find the target genes regulated by E47 in hematopoietic progenitor cells Keywords: genetic modification

DESIGN
E2A-deficient hematopoietic progenitor cells were infected by E47ER or bHLHER. 48 hours later, the cells were induced by 4-hydroxytamoxifen for six hours.
