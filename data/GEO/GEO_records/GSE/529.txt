TITLE
New targets of Drosophila adenosine deaminase acting on RNA (dADAR)

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

SUMMARY
The aim of this experiment is to identify new targets of Drosophila adenosine deaminase acting on RNA (dADAR) by enriching Inosine-containing mRNA (I-mRNA) from wild type (C-S) and dADAR mutant flies and then hybridization with Drosophila cDNA arrays. Keywords: other

PLATFORM
GPL358 Kennedy-Krieger Drosophila array
