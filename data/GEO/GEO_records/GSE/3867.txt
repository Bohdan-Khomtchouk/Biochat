TITLE
Gene expression in chicken livers from lean and fat lines

ORGANISM
Gallus gallus

SUMMARY
Fourty RNA samples from the liver of lean and fat line chickens were organized in 10 pools, 4 by 4 and in the order of chicken adiposity. Expression in these pools were compared Keywords: animals with different genetic background comparison

DESIGN
We analyzed 3 arrays for pools 1, 2, 4-7, 9 and 10 and 2 arrays for pools 3 and 8

GPL3265 Chicken cDNA DDMET 1700 array version 1.0
