TITLE
Expression profile of MCF7 and T47D human breast cancer cell lines upon treatment with the BET bromodomian inhibitor JQ1

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

SUMMARY
Expression profile of MCF7 and T47D human breast cancer cell lines upon treatment with the BET bromodomian inhibitor JQ1

DESIGN
Two breast cancer cell lines (MCF7 and T47D) were treated with 1uM JQ1 or vehicle for 24 hours. Three biological replicates were obtained for each condition.
