TITLE
Mouse Retina P7 Rs1h KO versus Control

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

SUMMARY
Genome-wide expression profiling of the retinoschisin deficient retina in C57CL/6 mice. Keywords: Genetic modification

DESIGN
RNAs from retinae from 3 Wildtype Mice versus 3 Retinoschisin-KO mice (Rs1h-/Y) at postnatal day 7 (P7) were hybridized onto Affymetrix microarrays.

PLATFORM
GPL339 [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
