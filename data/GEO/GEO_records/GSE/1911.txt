TITLE
Hippocampal gene expression after brain injury

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

SUMMARY
RNA samples were prepared from ipsilateral rat hippocampi 3 hr and 24 hr following lateral cortical impact injury and compared to samples from sham-operated controls.  For details please see Matzilevich et al., J Neurosci Res 2002 Mar 1;67(5):646-63. Keywords = Brain injury Keywords: time-course
