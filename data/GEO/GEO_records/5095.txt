TITLE
Transciption factor Meis1 deficiency effect on lineage-negative bone marrow cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of lineage-negative bone marrow cells following in vitro deletion of the transcription factor Meis1. Meis1 is a member of the 3-amino acid loop extension family of homeodomain proteins. Results provide insight into the role of Meis1 in hematopoiesis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
