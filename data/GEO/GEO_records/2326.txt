TITLE
Mature zone of the olfactory organ (Lobster2)

SUMMARY
Analysis of the mature zone of the olfactory organ and leg dactyl (a taste organ), two of the major chemosensory structures in lobsters. The lobster olfactory organ is an important model for investigating aspects of the olfactory system. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of olfaction.

ORGANISM
Homarus americanus
