TITLE
Oligodendrocyte progenitor and oligodendrocyte comparison (RAE230B)

SUMMARY
Analysis of FACS-purified A2B5+ oligodendrocyte progenitors and O4+ oligodendrocytes from the brains of postnatal day 7 Sprague Dawley males. Results provide insight into the mechanisms underlying oligodendrocyte maturation.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
