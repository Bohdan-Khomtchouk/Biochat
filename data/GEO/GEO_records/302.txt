TITLE
Ligand screen in B cells: formyl-Met-Leu-Phe

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of effects induced by 0.1 mM formyl-Met-Leu-Phe in male c57BL/6 B cells. Cells incubated in culture with ligand and samples removed at several time points up to 4 hours. Spleen from male c57BL/6 used as reference.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
