TITLE
Ligand screen in B cells: macrophage inflammatory protein-3

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of effects induced by 120 nM macrophage inflammatory protein-3 in male c57BL/6 B cells. Cells incubated in culture with ligand and samples removed at several time points up to 4 hours. Spleen from male c57BL/6 used as reference.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
