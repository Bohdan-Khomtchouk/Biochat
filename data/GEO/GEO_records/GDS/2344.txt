TITLE
Bed nucleus of the stria terminalis, nucleus accumbens, and dorsal striatum comparison

SUMMARY
Analysis of three regions of the brain that are dopamine targets and associated with addiction: bed nucleus of the stria terminalis (BNST), nucleus accumbens, and dorsal striatum. Tissues excised from the brains of 12-week-old C57BL/6J males.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
