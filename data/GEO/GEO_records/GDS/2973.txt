TITLE
Vitamin E supplementation effect on aged brain

SUMMARY
Analysis of brains of aged (30-month-old) B6C3F1 animals fed a diet containing alpha-tocopherol alone or in combination with gamma-tocopherol. Both of these vitamin E homologs exhibit anti-inflammatory properties. Results provide insight into the effects of vitamin E supplementation on aged brain.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
