TITLE
Primary graft dysfunction after lung transplantation

SUMMARY
Analysis of donor lung biopsies from recipients that developed primary graft dysfunction after lung transplantation. Results provide insight into the pathogenesis of primary lung graft dysfunction.

ORGANISM
