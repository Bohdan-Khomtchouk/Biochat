TITLE
Oral mucosal adaptive immune model: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of tongue mucosa keratinocytes from C57BL/6J mice at 48 and 96 hrs after tongue submucosal injection with BALB/c oral keratinocytes. Results provide insight into the role of oral keratinocytes in oral adaptive immune response.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
