TITLE
Asthma and socioeconomic status: peripheral blood CD2+ leukocyte

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood CD2+ leukocytes from asthmatic children of low and high socioeconomic families. Low socioeconomic status is a social factor associated with disease morbidity, including more severe asthma in childhood. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of this link.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
