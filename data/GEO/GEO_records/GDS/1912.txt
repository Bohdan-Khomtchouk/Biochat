TITLE
X-linked recessive dystonia-parkinsonism

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of the caudate nucleus and nucleus accumbens from the brain of a patient with X-linked recessive dystonia-parkinsonism (XDP). XDP is an adult onset disorder characterized by dystonia and parkinsonism. Result provide insight into the pathogenesis of XDP.
