TITLE
Colon tubular adenoma and carcinoma cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of colon tubular adenoma and carcinoma cells microdissected from formalin-fixed paraffin-embedded sections of colon tubular adenomas containing focal adenocarcinomas. Results provide insight into the progression of colon cancers from pre-existing adenomas.
