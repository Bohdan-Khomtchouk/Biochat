TITLE
Heart response to injury: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of adult C57BL/6 or MRL/MpJ hearts at various time points up to 60 days following left anterior descending (LAD) coronary artery ligation-induced myocardial injury. Results provide insight into the reparative myocardial mechanisms in the two strains following injury.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
