TITLE
Embryonic stem cell lines

SUMMARY
Analysis of 5 embryonic stem cell (ESC) lines. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms that maintain ESCs in a pluripotent state, and the differences between human and non-human primate ESCs.

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta

PLATFORM
