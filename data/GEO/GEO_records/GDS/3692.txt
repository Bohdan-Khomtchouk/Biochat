TITLE
Lean B6.C-D7Mit353 strain: various tissues

SUMMARY
Analysis of brain, liver, gastrocnemius muscle, and gonadal white adipose tissue from male B6.C-D7Mit353 and C57BL/6J animals. B6.C-D7Mit353 is a lean congenic stain with less body fat than the background C57BL/6J strain.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
