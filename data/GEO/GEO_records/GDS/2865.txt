TITLE
Metastatic prostate tumor model

SUMMARY
Comparison of poorly and highly metastatic prostate subcutaneous tumors derived from xenotransplanted human PC-3 prostate cancer cells. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms contributing to prostate cancer metastasis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
