TITLE
Synovial sarcoma model

SUMMARY
Analysis of synovial sarcoma-like tumors from transgenics expressing the chimeric human SYT-SSX2 fusion protein. The presence of SYT-SSX transcript is diagnostic for synovial sarcoma. Results provide insight into the role of the SYT-SSX2 fusion protein in the pathogenesis of synovial sarcoma.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
