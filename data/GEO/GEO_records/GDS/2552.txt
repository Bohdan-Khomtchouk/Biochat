TITLE
Small intestine response to Aeromonas caviae infection

SUMMARY
Analysis of neonatal small intestines infected with Aeromonas caviae. A. caviae is associated with human gastrointestinal disease. Strains typically lack virulence factors. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of A. caviae's enteropathogenicity.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
