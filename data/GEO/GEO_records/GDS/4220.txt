TITLE
HIV-associated nephropathy model: kidney

SUMMARY
Analysis of kidney from TgFVB transgenic mouse model of Human Immunodeficiency Virus-Associated Nephropathy (HIVAN). HIVAN is characterized by rapid progressive kidney dysfunction and prominent tubular damage. Results provide insight into molecular mechanism underlying HIVAN-related tubular disease.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
