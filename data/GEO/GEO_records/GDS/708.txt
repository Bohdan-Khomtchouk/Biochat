TITLE
HIV integration sites and gene transcription activity

SUMMARY
Correlation of HIV integration sites to gene activity in SupT1 (T cell), PBMC (peripheral blood mononuclear cell), and IMR90 (lung fibroblast) cell lines infected with an HIV-based vector.  Results provide insight into sites preferred in HIV integration.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
