TITLE
Hypoxia response

SUMMARY
Analysis of wild type, hif-1-deficient, and vhl-1-deficient strains cultured for 4 hours in 21% oxygen (normoxia) or 0.1% oxygen (hypoxia). Results provide insight into pathways for adaptation to oxygen deprivation.

Caenorhabditis elegans
