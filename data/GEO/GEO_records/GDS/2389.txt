TITLE
Iron limitation effect on iron response regulator mutant

SUMMARY
Analysis of parent strain LO and the iron response regulator (Irr) mutant strain LODMT5 grown in iron-limited or iron-replete medium. Results provide insight into the scope of Irr's role in iron metabolism.
