TITLE
Heat-shocked embryonic fibroblast response to lipopolysaccharide

SUMMARY
Analysis of embryonic fibroblasts subjected to heat shock and then treated with lipolysaccharide (LPS). LPS induces the expression of inflammatory genes. Results provide insight into the mechanisms underlying the fever-mediated suppression of inflammatory gene expression.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
