TITLE
Classic Hodgkins lymphoma diagnostic lymph-node biopsies

SUMMARY
Analysis of diagnostic lymph-node biopsies from classic Hodgkins lymphoma HIV- patients before ABVD chemotherapy. Results identify gene expression signatures that correlate with treatment outcomes.
