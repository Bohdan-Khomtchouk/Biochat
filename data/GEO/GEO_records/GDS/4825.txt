TITLE
Monocyte response to influenza A infection ex vivo

SUMMARY
Analysis of blood monocytes following influenza A (IAV) infection ex vivo. IVA infection of monocytes induces differentiation into dendritic cells (DCs).Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying monocyte-to-DC differentiation.
