TITLE
Cold and subsequent immobilization stress effect on adrenal medulla

SUMMARY
Analysis of the adrenal medulla of animals exposed to cold stress for 28 days and subsequently subjected to immobilization stress.  Effect of cold stress exposure for 1 and 7 days examined. Adaptation to cold stress leads to sensitization to a different stressor.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
