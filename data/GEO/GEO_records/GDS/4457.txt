TITLE
Strain PAO1 response to natural quorum sensing inhibitor protoanemonin

SUMMARY
Analysis of laboratory strain PAO1 treated with 125μM protoanemonin (4-methylenebut-2-en-4-olide), a natural quorum sensing (QS) inhibitor. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying protoanemonin inhibition of QS in P. aeruginosa.

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa

PLATFORM
GPL84 : [Pae_G1a] Affymetrix Pseudomonas aeruginosa Array

CITATIONS
