TITLE
RNA binding protein HuD overexpression effect on hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of hippocampus of 60 day old C57BL/6 males overexpressing the RNA binding protein HuD. HuD overexpressor mice exhibit learning deficits and abnormalities in prepulse inhibition.
