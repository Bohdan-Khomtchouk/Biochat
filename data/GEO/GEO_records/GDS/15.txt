TITLE
Heat shock from various temperatures to 37°C

SUMMARY
Samples collected 20 min after temperature shift from either 17°C, 21°C, 25°C, 29°C or 33°C to 37°C. Included as part of a detailed characterization of global expression programs triggered by environmental stress.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

PLATFORM
GPL51 : y11

CITATIONS
Gasch AP, Spellman PT, Kao CM, Carmel-Harel O et al. Genomic expression programs in the response of yeast cells to environmental changes. Mol Biol Cell 2000 Dec;11(12):4241-57. PMID:  11102521
