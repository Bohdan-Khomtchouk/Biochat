TITLE
Lens epithelium-derived growth factor knockdown

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of 293T cells depleted for lens epithelium-derived growth factor (LEDGF/p75). LEDGF/p75 binds to HIV integrase. Results identify LEDGF/p75 regulated genes, which may be preferential targets of HIV integration.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
