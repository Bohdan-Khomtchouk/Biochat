TITLE
Pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma tumor and adjacent non-tumor tissue

SUMMARY
Analysis of 45 matching pairs of pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (PDAC) tumor and adjacent non-tumor tissue. Results provide insight into tumor markers with prognostic significance and molecular mechanisms underlying PDAC.
