TITLE
Hypertension model: aorta

SUMMARY
Analysis of aorta of genetically hypotensive BPL, normotensive BPN, and hypertensive BPH inbred strains. Results provide insight into the molecular pathways affected by hypertension in the aorta.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
