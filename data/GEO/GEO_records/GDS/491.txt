TITLE
Toxicosis induced by endophyte infected fescue seed

SUMMARY
Characterization of fescue toxicosis in luteal tissue. Heifers fed endophyte free fescue seed (EF), endophyte infected fescue seed (EI) or EI supplemented with domperidone (EID). Fescue toxicosis thought to affect dopamine signaling pathways.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
