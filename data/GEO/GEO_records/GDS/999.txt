TITLE
Bronchoalveolar lavage cells of lung transplant recipients with acute rejection

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of bronchoalveolar lavage cells of lung transplant recipients with acute rejection. Rejection status determined from biopsies. Results identify possible gene markers of lung transplant rejection.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
