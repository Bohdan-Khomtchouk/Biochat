TITLE
bHLH transcription factor Math2 overexpression effect on cultured brain cerebral cortical cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of cultured brain cerebral cortical cells overexpressing Math2. Math2 is a bHLH transcription factor that is involved in neuronal differentiation and maturation. Results identify Math2 target genes.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
