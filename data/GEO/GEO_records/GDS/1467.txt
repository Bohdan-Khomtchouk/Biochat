TITLE
TGF-beta-activated kinase 1 deficient B cell response to B cell receptor stimulation

SUMMARY
Analysis of TGF-beta activated kinase 1 (TAK1)-deficient B cells following B cell receptor (BCR) activation by anti-IgM treatment for 4 hours. Results provide insight into how TAK1 regulates BCR-mediated proliferative responses.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
