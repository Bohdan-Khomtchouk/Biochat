TITLE
1,2,4-benzenetriol effect on peripheral blood mononuclear cells in vitro

SUMMARY
Analysis of cultured peripheral blood mononuclear cells treated with the benzene metabolite 1,2,4-benzenetriol. Benzene is an air pollutant and carcinogen. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of benzene cytotoxicity.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
