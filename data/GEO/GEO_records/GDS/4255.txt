TITLE
Cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator expression in airway epithelial cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of Calu-3 airway epithelial cells transfected with miR-138 mimic or SIN3A siRNA. MicroRNA-138 regulates Cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator (CFTR) expression through its interactions with the transcriptional regulatory protein SIN3A.
