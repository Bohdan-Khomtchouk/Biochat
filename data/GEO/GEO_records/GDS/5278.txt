TITLE
Endometrium response to tamoxifen and low dose estradiol combination therapy

SUMMARY
Analysis of endometria of postmenopausal cynomolgus macaques treated with tamoxifen and low dose estradiol. Tamoxifen is a selective estrogen receptor modulator (SERM). Estrogen and SERM co-therapy is an alternative to estrogen and progestin, a treatment associated with increased breast cancer risk.

ORGANISM
Macaca mulatta
