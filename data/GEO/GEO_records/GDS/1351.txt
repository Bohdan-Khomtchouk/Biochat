TITLE
Gastrin target genes in parietal cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of gastrin knockout parietal cells isolated from the stomach fundus by flow cytometry. Results identify gastrin responsive genes and provide insight into the critical components of the acid secretory system.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
