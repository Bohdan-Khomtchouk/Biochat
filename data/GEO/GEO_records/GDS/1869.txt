TITLE
Heregulin and forskolin mitogenic effect on cultured Schwann cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of passage 1 and passage 3 Schwann cell cultures obtained from four donors and maintained in the presence of the heregulin and forskolin. Heregulin and forskolin synergistically drive Schwann cell proliferation in vitro.
