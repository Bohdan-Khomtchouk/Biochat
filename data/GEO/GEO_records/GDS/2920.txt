TITLE
Testosterone effect on skeletal muscles of HIV-infected males experiencing weight loss

SUMMARY
Analysis of skeletal muscles of HIV-infected males experiencing weight loss after treatment with testosterone. HIV-associated wasting and weight loss are clinically significant concerns. Results provide insight into androgen action and the potential of anabolic therapies for wasting and sarcopenia.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
