TITLE
White adipose tissue remodeling: role of peroxisome proliferator-activated receptor alpha

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of epididymal white adipose tissue during remodeling induced by the β3-adrenergic receptor (Adrb3) agonist CL-316,243 in peroxisome proliferator-activated receptor alpha (Ppara) knockouts. Ppara is upregulated by CL-316,243.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL81 : [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
