TITLE
Diurnal and circadian-regulated genes (I)

SUMMARY
Analysis of plant molecular response to day/night cycling. Samples taken at 6 hour intervals throughout the day. Identification of genes that cycle with either a diurnal or a circadian pattern.

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

PLATFORM
GPL13 : MSU-1
