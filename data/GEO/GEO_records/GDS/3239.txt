TITLE
Heart-stage seed compartments

SUMMARY
Analysis of LCM-captured compartments from seeds containing heart-stage embryos. Whole seeds at heart-stage and young trifoliate leaves also examined. The various compartments play distinct roles in seed formation. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms involved in seed development.

ORGANISM
Glycine max
