TITLE
Ras-GRF1 deficiency effect on the hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of laser capture microdissected hippocampi of mutants lacking Ras-GRF1. Ras-GRF-1 is a guanine nucleotide exchange factor predominantly expressed in the central nervous system of mammals. Results provide insight into role of Ras-GRF1 in neural processes.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array

CITATIONS
Fernández-Medarde A, Porteros A, de las Rivas J, Núñez A et al. Laser microdissection and microarray analysis of the hippocampus of Ras-GRF1 knockout mice reveals gene expression changes affecting signal transduction pathways related to memory and learning. Neuroscience 2007 Apr 25;146(1):272-85. PMID:  17321057
