TITLE
Clinical outcome of stage UICC II colon cancer patients

SUMMARY
Analysis of tumor cells from sporadic stage UICC II colon cancer patients who were treated by elective standard oncological resection but developed relapse during follow-up. Results provide insight into the challenges of constructing molecular signatures predictive for patient outcome.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
