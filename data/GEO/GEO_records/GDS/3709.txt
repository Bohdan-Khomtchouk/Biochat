TITLE
Cigarette smoke effect on the oral mucosa

SUMMARY
Analysis of oral mucosae from 40 cigarette smokers and 40 age and gender matched never-smokers. Results provide insight into the carcinogenic effects of cigarette smoke.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
Boyle JO, Gümüs ZH, Kacker A, Choksi VL et al. Effects of cigarette smoke on the human oral mucosal transcriptome. Cancer Prev Res (Phila) 2010 Mar;3(3):266-78. PMID:  20179299
