TITLE
Malpighian tubule expression profiling

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of 7 day adult Oregon R Malpighian tubules in reference to whole organisms.  Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying renal function.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster
