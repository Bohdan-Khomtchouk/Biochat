TITLE
Gastric mucosa ECL cell carcinoid

SUMMARY
Analysis of enriched gastric mucosa enterochromaffin-like (ECL) cell populations obtained from rodent with predisposition for ECL cell carcinoids. Animals treated with loxtidin for 8 or 16 weeks. Spontaneous ECL cell carcinoid included in analysis.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
Mastomys natalensis
