TITLE
Intrauterine growth restriction model: kidneys

SUMMARY
Analysis of kidneys of intrauterine growth-restricted (IUGR) fetuses. Intrauterine growth restriction induced by hyperinsulinemia in pregnant animals. IUGR newborns are prone to adult hypertension. Results provide insight into the basis of the renal abnormalities exhibited by IUGR offsprings.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
