TITLE
Alveolar type I and type II cell comparison

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of freshly isolated alveolar type I (TI) and type II (TII) cells, and cultured alveolar TII cells grown in vitro for 7 days.  Results provide insight into the functional differences between TI and TII cells.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL85 : [RG_U34A] Affymetrix Rat Genome U34 Array
