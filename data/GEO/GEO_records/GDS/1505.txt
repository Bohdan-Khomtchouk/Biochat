TITLE
Cultured skin substitute

SUMMARY
Comparison of cultured skin substitute (CSS), cultured keratinocytes, cultured fibroblasts, and native skin. CSS contains fibroblasts and keratinocytes, and can facilitate wound healing. Results provide insight into physiological and molecular differences between CSS and native skin.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
