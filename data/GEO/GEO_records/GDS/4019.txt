TITLE
Thiazolidinedione pioglitazone effect on Fischer 344 model of aging: hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of hippocampus of aged F344 males fed a thiazolidinedione (TZD) pioglitazone-containing diet. TZDs have benefits in age-related conditions such as type 2 diabetes mellitus and Alzheimer's disease.  Results provide insight into PIO's potentially beneficial actions in normal aging.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
