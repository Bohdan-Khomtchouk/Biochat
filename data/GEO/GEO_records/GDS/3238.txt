TITLE
Globular-stage seed compartments

SUMMARY
Analysis of LCM-captured compartments from seeds containing globular-stage embryos. Whole seeds at globular-stage and young trifoliate leaves also examined. The various compartments play distinct roles in seed formation. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms involved in seed development.

ORGANISM
Glycine max

PLATFORM
GPL4592 : [Soybean] Affymetrix Soybean Genome Array
