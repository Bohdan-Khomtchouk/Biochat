TITLE
Colon carcinoma response to butyrate and aspirin

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of colon carcinoma cell line CC531 response to 4.5 mM butyrate or 3 mM aspirin. Samples taken at 2, 6, 12, 16 and 24 hours.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
