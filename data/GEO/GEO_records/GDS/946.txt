TITLE
Familial combined hyperlipidemia

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of lymphoblastic cell lines derived from the peripheral blood lymphocytes of patients with familial combined hyperlipidemia (FCHL), the most common form of genetically determined dyslipidemia.  Results provide insight into the pathophysiology of FCHL.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
