TITLE
Transcription factor Oct-1 deficient fibroblast response to ionizing radiation (430B)

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of Oct-1 deficient 3T3-immortalized embryonic fibroblasts exposed to gamma radiation. Oct-1 is a POU domain transcription factor. Results provide insight into the role of Oct-1 in the adaptation to cellular stress.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
