TITLE
Aquaporin-1 knockout kidney inner medulla profile (I)

SUMMARY
Gene expression profiles of kidney inner medulla from aquaporin-1 (AQP1) null and wild type mice compared against a wild type common reference. AQP1 null mice manifest a severe defect in urinary concentrating ability.
