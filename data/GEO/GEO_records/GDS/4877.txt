TITLE
Ovarian cancer cell line response to the anticancer drug NSC319726

SUMMARY
Analysis of TOV112D ovarian cancer cells following treatment with NSC319726, a member of the thiosemicarbzone family of metal ion chelators. NSC319726 inhibits the growth of mutant p53 cells. Results provide insight into the mechanism of action of NSC319726.
