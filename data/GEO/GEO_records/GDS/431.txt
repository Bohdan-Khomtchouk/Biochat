TITLE
MHC class II transactivator targets (MG-U74Av2)

SUMMARY
Identification of new targets of major histocompatibility complex class II transactivator CIITA in dendritic cells (DC). Day 10 bone marrow-derived DC isolated from B6 background CIITA-deficient, MHC class II-deficient and wild type mice examined.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
