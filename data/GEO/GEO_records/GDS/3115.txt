TITLE
Heart failure: peripheral blood mononuclear cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood mononuclear cells from chronic heart failure (CHF) patients with ischemic or non-ischemic cardiomyopathy (ICM or NICM). Results provide insight into diagnostic/prognostic markers and CHF gene expression profiles.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
