TITLE
Histone deacetylase-2 deficiency effect on the embryonic heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of heart ventricles from histone deacetylase-2 (Hdac2) deficient embryos at embryonic day E17.5. The role of the HDAC family in cardiac myocyte homeostasis is complex. Results provide insight into the role of class I HDAC gene Hdac2 in cardiac development.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
