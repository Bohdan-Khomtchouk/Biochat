TITLE
Strains resistant to environmental stresses

SUMMARY
Analysis of several lines each selected for resistance to a different environmental stress factor. Results provide insight into the molecular and genetic mechanisms that promote adaptation to environmental stresses.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

PLATFORM
GPL72 : [DrosGenome1] Affymetrix Drosophila Genome Array

CITATIONS
Sørensen JG, Nielsen MM, Loeschcke V. Gene expression profile analysis of Drosophila melanogaster selected for resistance to environmental stressors. J Evol Biol 2007 Jul;20(4):1624-36. PMID:  17584255
