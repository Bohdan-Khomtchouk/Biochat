TITLE
X-ray induced cell death in embryos

SUMMARY
Analysis of X-ray induced cell death in stage 9-11 (irradiation sensitive) and stage 13-15 embryos (irradiation resistant) provides insight into molecular mechanisms of irradiation induced apoptosis.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster
