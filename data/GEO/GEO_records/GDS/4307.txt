TITLE
Post myocardial infarction heart: left ventricle

SUMMARY
Analysis of remote, non-ischemic, regions of the left ventricle from animals that underwent permanent ligation of the left coronary artery to induce myocardial infarction (MI). Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying cardiac remodeling following MI.

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa
