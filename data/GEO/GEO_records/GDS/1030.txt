TITLE
IL-21 and IL-15 synergistic effect on naïve CD8+ T cells

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of naïve CD8+ T cells stimulated with IL-15 or IL-21 or both cytokines for 4 hours.  100 ng/ml of each cytokine used.  Results provide insight into the synergistic effect of IL-21 and IL-15 on T cell homeostasis and effector functions.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
