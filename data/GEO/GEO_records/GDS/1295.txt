TITLE
Lethal congenital contracture syndrome

SUMMARY
Analysis of spinal cord from fetuses with lethal congenital contracture syndrome (LCCS). LCCS is autosomal recessive, causes death in 32nd gestational week, and involves degeneration of anterior horn motor neurons in spinal cord. Results provide insight into pathways active in motoneuron disease.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array

CITATIONS
