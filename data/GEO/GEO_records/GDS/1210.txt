TITLE
Gastric cancer

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of 22 primary advanced gastric cancer tissues. Whole gastric cancer tissues examined in the presence of metastasis and according to histological type. Results provide insight into the progression and diversity of gastric cancer.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL80 : [Hu6800] Affymetrix Human Full Length HuGeneFL Array
