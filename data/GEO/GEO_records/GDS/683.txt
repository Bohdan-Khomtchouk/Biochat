TITLE
Oxidative stress and Snell dwarf liver

SUMMARY
Time course of long-lived Snell dwarf (Pit1 mutation) and wild type response to oxidative stress induced by 3-nitropropionic acid (3-NPA). Livers examined at several time points up to 42 hours.
