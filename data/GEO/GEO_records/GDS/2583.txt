TITLE
Kanamycin treatment: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of MG1655 cells at various time points up to 75 minutes following treatment with 100 ug/ml kanamycin. Kanamycin added to cells grown in M9 media supplemented with glucose. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli
