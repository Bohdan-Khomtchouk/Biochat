TITLE
Indole acetic acid treatment time course (AG)

SUMMARY
Analysis of 10-12 day Columbia-O seedlings treated with 0.1 uM indole acetic acid (IAA) auxin for 1 and 3 hours. Treated seedlings analyzed in reference to untreated control. Part of a study comparing AG and ATH1 arrays.

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

PLATFORM
GPL71 : [AG] Affymetrix Arabidopsis Genome Array
