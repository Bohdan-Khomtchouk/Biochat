TITLE
Cigarette smoke of full flavor brand effect on bronchial epithelial cells in vitro: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of normal bronchial epithelial cells exposed to cigarette smoke from a typical full flavor brand for up to 24 hours. Results provide insight into the impact of cigarette smoke exposure at the molecular level.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
