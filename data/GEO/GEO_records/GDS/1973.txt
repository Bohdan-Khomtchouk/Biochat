TITLE
Various prostate cell types

SUMMARY
Analysis of basal, luminal secretory, stromal fibromuscular, and endothelial cells of the prostate. Cell types separated with magnetic cell sorting (MACS) based on cell-type specific cluster designation (CD) antigens. Results identify gene expression signatures at the level of individual cell types.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
