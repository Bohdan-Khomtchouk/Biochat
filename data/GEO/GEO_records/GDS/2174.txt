TITLE
Infarcted heart response to captopril and 3,5-diiodothyropropionic acid

SUMMARY
Analysis of infarcted left heart ventricle of Spraque-Dawley males following treatment with thyroid hormone analog 3,5-diiodothyropropionic acid (DITPA), or captopril followed by DITPA. Results provide  insight into the effect of these drugs in congestive heart failure.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
