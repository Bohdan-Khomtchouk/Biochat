TITLE
Molecular apocrine breast tumors

SUMMARY
Analysis of tumors of 49 breast cancer patients. Tumors classified into luminal and basal classes, and a novel molecular apocrine class. Apocrine tumors are estrogen receptor negative (ER-) and androgen receptor positive (AR+), while luminal tumors are ER+ and AR+, and basal tumors are ER- and AR-.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
