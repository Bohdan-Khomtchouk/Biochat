TITLE
Estrogen receptor-positive and -negative breast cancer tumors

SUMMARY
Analysis of primary breast carcinoma tumors from estrogen receptor positive or negative (ER+/-) patients. ER+ tumors tend to metastasize to the bone while ER- tumors tend to induce visceral metastasis. Results provide insight into molecular basis of different metastatic phenotypes in breast cancer.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
