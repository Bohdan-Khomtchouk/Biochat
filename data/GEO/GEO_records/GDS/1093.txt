TITLE
NADH-cytochrome P450 reductase deletion effect on liver

SUMMARY
Analysis of liver from male 3 month old hepatic NADH-cytochrome P450 reductase null mutants (POR). Results provide insight into the central role of POR and the P450 system in controlling hepatic homeostasis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
