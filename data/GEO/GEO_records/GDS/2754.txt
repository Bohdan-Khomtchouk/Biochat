TITLE
Exhaustive exercise effect on peripheral blood mononuclear cells and T lymphocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood mononuclear cells and T lymphocytes from individuals before and after a bout of acute exhaustive exercise. White blood cell (WBC) subpopulations shift during physical activities. Results provide insight into the response of sorted WBC subpopulations to acute exercise.
