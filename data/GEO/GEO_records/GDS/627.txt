TITLE
Cardiac development in embryo

SUMMARY
Analysis of strain C57BL/6 embryo heart development from E (embryonic day) 10.5 to E18.5, which encompass stages of septation of atrial and ventricular chambers as well as cardiac outflow tract. Atrial and ventricular chambers analyzed separately.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
