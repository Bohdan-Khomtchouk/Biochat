TITLE
Liver reponse to an atherogenic diet and atorvastatin treatment

SUMMARY
Analysis of livers of C57B/L females fed an atherogenic diet for 10 weeks, with the last 3 weeks of the diet combined with atorvastatin treatment. Atorvastatin is a statin used for lowering cholesterol.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
