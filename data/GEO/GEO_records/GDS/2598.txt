TITLE
Gamma radiation treatment: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of MG1655 cells  in Davis medium at various time points up to 60 minutes following treatment with gamma radiation. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.
