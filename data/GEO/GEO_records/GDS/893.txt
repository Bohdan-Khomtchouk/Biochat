TITLE
GATA3 ectopic expression in 293T cells (dye-swap)

SUMMARY
Examination of 293T epithelial kidney cells stably transfected with either wild type GATA3 or R367L mutant GATA3. GATA3 can be highly expressed in breast tumors. GATA3 variants may contribute to tumorigenesis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL737 : Agilent Human 1A Oligo UNC_custom array
