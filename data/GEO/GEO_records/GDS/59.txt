TITLE
Lung hypertension recovery (U74Bv2)

SUMMARY
Analysis of vascular remodeling following pulmonary hypertension. Lungs of normoxic and hypoxic treated animals analyzed. Hypoxia induces vasoconstriction then hypertrophy/hyperplasia of pulmonary vascular smooth muscle and endothelial cell proliferation.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
