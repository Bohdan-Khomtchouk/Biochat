TITLE
Bile duct ligation effect on the liver

SUMMARY
Analysis of livers of animals following bile duct ligation to induce extrahepatic cholestasis. Biliary tract obstruction is a common mechanism of hepatic injury. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms that influence injury and repair in cholestatic liver disease.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
