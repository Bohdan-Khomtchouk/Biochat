TITLE
High and low level freezing in response to fear: amygdala and hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of the amygdalae and hippocampi of lines exhibiting high or low freezing behavior in response to fear. The amygdala and hippocampus are brain regions important in fear behavior. Results provide insight into the genetic basis of fear behavior.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
