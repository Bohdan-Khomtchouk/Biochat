TITLE
Preeclampsia: uteroplacental tissues

SUMMARY
Analysis of the deciduas, placentas, and fat tissues of preeclamptic women. The decidua and the placenta are two tissues of the uteroplacental unit. Results provide insight into the role of the uteroplacental renin-angiotensin system in the pathogenesis of preeclampsia.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
