TITLE
Calcium chloride and glycerol wash effect: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of MG1655 cells after washing with 0.1 M calcium chloride and 15% glycerol and subsequent incubation on ice. Cells on ice examined at various time points up to 60 minutes. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli
