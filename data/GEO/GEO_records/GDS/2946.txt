TITLE
Diet-induced obesity model: epididymal fat tissue

SUMMARY
Comparison of epididymal fat tissues from diet-induced obese (DIO) and lean animals. DIO animals were fed a high fat, high sucrose diet to induce obesity.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
