TITLE
Allergen-induced goblet cells

SUMMARY
A-J mice treated intratracheally with saline or IL13. Airway epithelial cells analyzed as they differentiate into goblet cells. Goblet cells increase mucus production which is a characteristic phenotype of allergic asthma.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
