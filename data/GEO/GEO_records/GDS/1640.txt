TITLE
Myocilin over-secretion effect on the retina

SUMMARY
Analysis of retinas of transgenics that secret high levels of myocilin (MYOC) into the aqueous humor of the eye. Results provide insight into the role of myocilin in the pathogenesis of glaucoma.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
