TITLE
Balanced and unbalanced chromosomal translocations: lymphoblastoid cell lines

SUMMARY
Analysis of lymphoblastoid cell lines established from individuals with the t(11;22)(q23;q11)-balanced translocation (carriers) or the +der(22)t(11;22)(q23;q11) karyotype (Emanuel syndrome patients). Results provide insight into the effect of balanced chromosomal rearrangements on the transcriptome.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
