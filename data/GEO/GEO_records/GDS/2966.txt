TITLE
Resveratrol effect on lung carcinoma cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of lung carcinoma A549 cells treated with resveratrol. Resveratrol is a phytoestrogen found in red wine. Results provide insight into protective effect of resveratrol against lung cancer.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
