TITLE
Alpha 2B-adrenoceptor deficient embryo and its placenta

SUMMARY
Analysis of placentas of embryonic day E10.5 mutant embryos lacking alpha 2B-adrenoceptor (Adra2b). Adra2b-/- deficient embryos develop a defect in fetal and maternal vessel formation in the placenta labyrinth at E10.5. Results provide insight into the role of Adra2b in placental development.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
