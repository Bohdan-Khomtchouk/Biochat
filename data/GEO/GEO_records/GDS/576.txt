TITLE
Gastric mucosa ECL cell hyperplasia and neoplasia

SUMMARY
Analysis of gastric mucosa from rodent with predisposition for gastric carcinoids derived from enterochromaffin-like (ECL) cells. Animals treated with hypergastrinemia-inducing loxtidine for 8 weeks (hyperplasia) or 16 weeks (neoplasia).

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
