TITLE
Lactobacillus brevis KB290 supplemented diet effect on the spleen: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of spleens of BALB/c animals fed a diet supplemented with Lactobacillus brevis KB290 for up to 14 days. L. brevis KB290 is a probiotic bacterial strain. Results provide insight into the probiotic effect of L. brevis KB290 on spleen cells at the molecular level.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
