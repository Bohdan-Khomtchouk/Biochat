TITLE
Cardiomyopathy, severe dilated

SUMMARY
Examination of ventricles of 10 week mice overexpressing dominant-negative (dn) small GTP binding protein p21ras. dn-p21ras mice show severe cardiac dilation and decreased contractility. p21ras implicated in hypertrophic response of cardiomyocytes.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
