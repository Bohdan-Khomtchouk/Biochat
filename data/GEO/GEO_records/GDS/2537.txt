TITLE
Antipsychotic agents clozapine and haloperidol effects on the brain (I)

SUMMARY
Analysis of brains of animals treated for 4 weeks with the atypical antipsychotic clozapine or the typical antipsychotic haloperidol. Clozapine's use as an antipsychotic is limited by agranulocytosis. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of clozapine's therapeutic and toxic effects.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL81 : [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
