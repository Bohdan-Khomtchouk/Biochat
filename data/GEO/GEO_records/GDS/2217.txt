TITLE
Haemophilus influenzae response to iron and heme availability

SUMMARY
Analysis of Haemophilus influenzae Rk KW20 cells grown in iron and heme depleted media for 90 minutes followed by growth in iron and heme supplemented media for 20 minutes. H. influenzae requires porphyrin and an iron source for aerobic growth. Results identify genes regulated by iron and heme.

ORGANISM
Haemophilus influenzae Rd KW20
