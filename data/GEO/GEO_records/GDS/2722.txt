TITLE
Barley cultivars response to salinity stress

SUMMARY
Analysis of roots and shoots of barley cultivars Maythorpe and Golden Promise subjected to salinity stress. Golden Promise is a salt-tolerant spring barley closely related to Maythorpe. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of this difference in salt-tolerance.

ORGANISM
Hordeum vulgare

PLATFORM
GPL1340 : [Barley1] Affymetrix Barley Genome Array
