TITLE
Never-medicated and medicated bipolar patients: peripheral blood leukocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood leukocytes from first-episode, never-medicated bipolar disorder (BPD) patients and medicated BPD patients. Results provide insight into a peripheral biomarker profile for BPD that is not induced by medication.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
