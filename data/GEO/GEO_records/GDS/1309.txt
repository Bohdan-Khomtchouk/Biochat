TITLE
Myotube response to hydrogen peroxide

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of myotubes following treatment with hydrogen peroxide at various concentrations up to 1000 uM. Myotubes derived from C2C12 myoblasts. Results provide insight into the response of muscle to reactive oxygen species.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
