TITLE
Alternatively activated macrophages

SUMMARY
Study of IL-4 dependent alternatively activated macrophages (AAM). AAM implicated in pulmonary diseases like allergic asthma and nematode infections. Macrophages extracted from peritoneal cavity of wildtype and mutant (IL4--) mice infected with nematode.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
