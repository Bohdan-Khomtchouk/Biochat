TITLE
Placental malaria

SUMMARY
Analysis of inflamed placentas from patients with chronic placental malaria (PM). Chronic inflammation during PM is frequent in first time mothers and is associated with poor maternal and fetal outcomes. Results provide insight into the pathogenesis of chronic PM.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
