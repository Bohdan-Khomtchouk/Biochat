TITLE
Basic helix-loop-helix transcription factor Twist overexpression

SUMMARY
Analysis of animals overexpressing the basic helix-loop-helix (bHLH) transcription factor Twist and its heterodimeric bHLH partner E/DA. Twist is important in mesoderm development. Results provide insight into the function of Twist in mesoderm development.

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans
