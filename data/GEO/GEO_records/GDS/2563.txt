TITLE
Cigarette smoking effect on T lymphocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of T lymphocytes of 3 male heavy smokers. Results provide insight into sensitivity of T lymphocytes to cigarette smoking.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL571 : [HG-U133A_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
Büttner P, Mosig S, Funke H. Gene expression profiles of T lymphocytes are sensitive to the influence of heavy smoking: A pilot study. Immunogenetics 2007 Jan;59(1):37-43. PMID:  17149619
