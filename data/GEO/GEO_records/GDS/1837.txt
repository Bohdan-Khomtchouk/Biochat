TITLE
c-Raf inhibitor GW5074 anti-apoptotic effect on cerebellar granule neurons: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of cultured cerebellar granule neurons in low potassium medium 3 and 6 hours following treatment with the c-Raf inhibitor GW5074. GW5074 blocks low potassium induced cell death. Results provide insight into the neuroprotective action of GW5074.
