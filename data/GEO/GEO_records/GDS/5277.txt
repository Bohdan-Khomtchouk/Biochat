TITLE
Rheumatoid arthritis and response to anti-TNF therapy: blood

SUMMARY
Analysis of blood from rheumatoid arthritis (RA) patients prior to anti-TNF therapy. Response of patients to anti-TNF therapy assessed after 14 weeks. Results identify a gene expression signature for predicting the response to anti-TNF therapy.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
