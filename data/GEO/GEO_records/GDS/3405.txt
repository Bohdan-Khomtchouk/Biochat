TITLE
Embryonic erythroid precursors

SUMMARY
Analysis of primitive erythroid precursors and epithelial cells isolated from frozen sections of the embryonic day 9.5 yolk sac. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying embryonic erythropoiesis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
