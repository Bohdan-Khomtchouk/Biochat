TITLE
Pulmonary fibrosis

SUMMARY
Determination of genetic basis of sensitivity to pulmonary fibrosis induced by bleomycin by comparing susceptible (C57BL6/J) and resistant (Balb/c) strains. C57BL6/J experience inflammatory response then progressive lung disease, Balb/c do not.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
