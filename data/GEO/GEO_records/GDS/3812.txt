TITLE
Krüppel-like zinc finger transcription factor Gli-similar 3 knockout effect on embryonic pancreas

SUMMARY
Analysis of pancreas from E15.5 embryos deficient in transcription factor Gli-similar 3 (Glis3). The Glis3zf/zf mutant pancreas shows a dramatic loss of β and δ cells, contrasting a smaller relative loss of α, PP, and ɛ cells. Results provide insight into the role of Glis3 in pancreas.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
