TITLE
Anesthesia and fear conditioning effect on amygdala

SUMMARY
Analysis of basolateral amygdalae from animals following isoflurane treatment, pain treatment, or fear conditioning alone or in combination with isoflurane treatment. Fear conditioning leads to long-term memory formation. Isoflurane is a general anesthetic and amnestic agent.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
