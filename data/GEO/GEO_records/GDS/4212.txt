TITLE
Chronic lymphocytic leukemia with chromosomal deletion 11q: peripheral blood CD19+ cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of CD19+cells from peripheral blood of chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL) patients with interstitial deletions on the long arm of chromosome 11 (del11q).  Del11q is associated with progressive and aggressive CLL. Results provide insight into the molecular pathogenesis of CLL with del11q.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
