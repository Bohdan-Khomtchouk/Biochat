TITLE
Infant biliary atresia: liver

SUMMARY
Analysis of liver wedge biopsies from infants at diagnosis of biliary atresia (BA). The biopsies underwent histological scoring and were classified as inflammation or fibrosis. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying stages of BA disease at diagnosis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
