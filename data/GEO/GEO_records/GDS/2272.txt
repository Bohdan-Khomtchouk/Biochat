TITLE
Heat stress response: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of heat-resistant females at various time points up to 64 hours following short-term heat treatment at 36 degrees C for 1 hour. Results provide insight into the cellular response to heat stress.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

PLATFORM
GPL72 : [DrosGenome1] Affymetrix Drosophila Genome Array

CITATIONS
Sørensen JG, Nielsen MM, Kruhøffer M, Justesen J et al. Full genome gene expression analysis of the heat stress response in Drosophila melanogaster. Cell Stress Chaperones 2005 Winter;10(4):312-28. PMID:  16333985
