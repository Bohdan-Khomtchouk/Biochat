TITLE
Fluoxetine effect on the hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of hippocampi from DBA/2J males chronically treated with the antidepressant (AD) fluoxetine (Flx). ADs promote the proliferation and survival of neurons in the adult hippocampus. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of the neuronal and behavioral responses to Flx treatment.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
