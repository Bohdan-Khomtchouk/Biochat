TITLE
Regular exercise effect on heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of left heart ventricle of Sprague Dawley males without access to exercise, with access to hour-long twice a week of activity, or with access to a running wheel every other day. Regimen imposed from 3 weeks until 16 months of age. Results identify putative markers of cardiovascular health.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
