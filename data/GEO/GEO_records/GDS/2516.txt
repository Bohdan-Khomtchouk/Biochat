TITLE
Interferons effect on endothelial cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of endothelial cells treated with interferon (IFN) alpha, beta, or gamma for 5 hours.  The effect of IFNs on fibroblasts was also examined.  Results provide insight into the molecular basis of the antiangiogenic activity exhibited by IFNs.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
