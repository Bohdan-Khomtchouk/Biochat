TITLE
Maternal hypercholesterolemia effect on offspring: carotid a

SUMMARY
Analysis of carotid artery from 20 week old, heterozygous apolipoprotein E deficient (apoE-/+) progeny of apoE+/+ or apoE-/- mothers.  Results provide insight into whether in utero exposure to maternal atherosclerotic risk factors increases susceptibility to cardiovascular disease in adult life.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
