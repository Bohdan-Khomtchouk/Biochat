TITLE
Acute myeloid leukemia fusion oncogene-transduced granulocyte monocyte precursors

SUMMARY
Analysis of granulocyte monocyte precursors (GMPs) transduced with GFP alone or with AML-fusion oncogenes MOZ-TIF2 or NUP98-HOXA9 or AML1-ETO. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying transformation and self-renewal downstream of multiple AML-associated oncogenes.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
