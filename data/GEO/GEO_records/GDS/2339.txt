TITLE
Hepatitis B surface antigen effect on hepatoma cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of hepatoma HepG2 cells secreting the hepatitis B surface antigen (HBsAg). Transfected cells examined 4 and 8 days after seeding. Results provide insight into the effect of persistently secreting HBsAg on the host cell.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
