TITLE
Intralobular and interlobular mammary fibroblasts

SUMMARY
Comparison of intralobular and interlobular fibroblasts isolated from breast tissues. Intralobular fibroblasts surround mammary gland epithelial cells, and interlobular fibroblasts surround intralobular fibroblasts. Results provide insight into the differences between the two fibroblast subtypes.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
