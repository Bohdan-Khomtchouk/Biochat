TITLE
Transcription factor Egr3 expression effect on primary myotubes (MG-430A)

SUMMARY
Analysis of primary myotubes expressing wild-type or inactive truncated Egr3. Transcription factor Egr3 activity is critical for the development of muscle spindles, skeletal muscle stretch receptors that mediate axial and limb proprioception.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
