TITLE
Hypoxia effect on a renal proximal tubule epithelial cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of cultured renal proximal tubule epithelial RPTEC cells subjected to hypoxia at 1% oxygen for 24 hours. Results identify hypoxia-controlled genes in epithelial cells.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
