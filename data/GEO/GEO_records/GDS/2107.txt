TITLE
Long-term ethanol consumption effect on pancreas

SUMMARY
Analysis of pancreas from Wistar males fed the ethanol containing Lieber-DeCarli diet for 8 weeks. Long-term ethanol consumption sensitizes the pancreas to subsequent insults. Results provide insight into the mechanisms underlying the ethanol-mediated sensitization of the pancreas to injury.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
