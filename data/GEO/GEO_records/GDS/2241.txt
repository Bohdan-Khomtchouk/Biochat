TITLE
Trophoblast cell lines

SUMMARY
Comparison of BeWo and JEG3 trophoblast cell lines. Trophoblast cell lines are used as surrogates for primary trophoblast cells in the study of placental function. Results provide insight into the differences between the two cell lines.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
