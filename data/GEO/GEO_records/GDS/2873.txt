TITLE
Wip1 mutant embryonic fibroblast response to gamma radiation

SUMMARY
Analysis of Wip1 mutant embryonic fibroblasts following 4 hour after 5 Gy of gamma irradiation. Wip1 is a p53-induced phosphatase overexpressed in several human tumor types.
