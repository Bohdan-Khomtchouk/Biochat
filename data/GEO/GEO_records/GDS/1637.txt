TITLE
Oncogene-induced sensescence in vitro model

SUMMARY
Analysis of transformed cell lines that either exhibit oncogene-induced senescence (OIS) triggered by MEK activation or bypass OIS. Senescent cells exist in premalignant tumors but not in malignant ones. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of OIS.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
