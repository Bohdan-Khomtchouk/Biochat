TITLE
Chronic stress effect on the amygdala and hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of the amygdalae (Amy) and hippocampi (Hipp) of animals subjected to chronic stress induced by daily handling sessions for 14 days. Studies indicate that chronic stress produce a response in Hipp that is opposite of that in Amy.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
