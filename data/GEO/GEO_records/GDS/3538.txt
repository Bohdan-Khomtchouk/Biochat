TITLE
Age and diet effect on canine skeletal muscles

SUMMARY
Analysis of skeletal muscles from 1 and 12 year old female beagles fed an animal-protein based or plant-protein based diet for 12 months. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms in the skeletal muscle affected by age and diet.

ORGANISM
Canis lupus familiaris

PLATFORM
