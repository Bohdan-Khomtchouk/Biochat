TITLE
Skeletal repair in non-union fractures (HG-U95B)

SUMMARY
Identification of mechanisms leading to lack of skeletal repair in non-union fractures. Non-union fractures do not heal six months after injury and may cause advanced arthritis or loss of limb function. Normal and non-union callous bone samples examined.
