TITLE
Cisplatin effect on DNA adenine methyltransferase and mismatch repair mutants

SUMMARY
Analysis of mutants lacking DNA adenine methyltransferase Dam, or mismatch repair protein MutS, or both after treatment with cisplatin. dam mutants are hypersensitive to cisplatin, while dam mutS double mutants display wild type resistance to cisplatin.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli K-12
