TITLE
Thyroid hormone effect on cardiomyocytes (dye-swap)

SUMMARY
Analysis of effect of 48 hours exposure to thyroid hormone triiodothyronine (T3) and structural analogues DITPA and CGS on primary culture cardiomyocytes prepared from 18 day old fetus. Thyroid hormone may be useful in treatment of heart failure.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
