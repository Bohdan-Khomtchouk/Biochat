TITLE
Hypothalamus response to lipopolysaccharide and restraint stress

SUMMARY
Analysis of gene expression profiles in the paraventricular nucleus of the hypothalamus in male C57BL6. Mice subjected to either bacterial lipopolysaccharide (LPS) challenge, restraint stress or a saline control for 1 or 3 hours.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
