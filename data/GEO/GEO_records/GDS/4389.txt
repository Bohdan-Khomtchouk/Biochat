TITLE
Alcoholic hepatitis

SUMMARY
Analysis of alcoholic hepatitis (AH) livers. AH is a severe form of alcoholic liver disease characterized by hepatocellular damage, steatosis and pericellular fibrosis. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying AH pathogenesis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
