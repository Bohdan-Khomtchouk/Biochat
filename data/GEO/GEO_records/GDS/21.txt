TITLE
Carbon sources

SUMMARY
Samples grown in YP media supplemented with 2% weight to volume of glucose, raffinose, galactose, fructose, sucrose or ethanol as carbon source. Included as part of a detailed characterization of global expression programs triggered by stress.

Saccharomyces cerevisiae
