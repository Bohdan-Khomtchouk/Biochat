TITLE
Heat shock from 29°C to 33°C time course

SUMMARY
Heat shock from 29°C to 33°C. Samples collected at 5, 15, 30 and 90 minutes. Included as part of a detailed characterization of global expression programs triggered by environmental stress.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

PLATFORM
GPL56 : y16

CITATIONS
Gasch AP, Spellman PT, Kao CM, Carmel-Harel O et al. Genomic expression programs in the response of yeast cells to environmental changes. Mol Biol Cell 2000 Dec;11(12):4241-57. PMID:  11102521
