TITLE
Ovaries from a high-fertility line

SUMMARY
Analysis of ovaries from high-fertility FL1 animals. FL1 animals exhibit an increase in ovulation number. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of this increase in ovulation number.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array

CITATIONS
Vanselow J, Nürnberg G, Koczan D, Langhammer M et al. Expression profiling of a high-fertility mouse line by microarray analysis and qPCR. BMC Genomics 2008 Jun 27;9:307. PMID:  18588666
