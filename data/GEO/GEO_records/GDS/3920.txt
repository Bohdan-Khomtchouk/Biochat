TITLE
Multiple sclerosis: peripheral blood mononuclear cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood mononuclear immune cells from multiple sclerosis (MS) patients and unaffected controls. MS is an inflammatory, neurodegenerative disease. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying MS etiology.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
