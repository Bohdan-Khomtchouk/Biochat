TITLE
T-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia with CALM-AF10 fusion

SUMMARY
Analysis of T-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL) samples containing t(10;11)(p13;q14-21) translocation that fuses the clathrin-assembly protein-like lymphoid-myeloid leukemia gene CALM to the transcription factor AF10. Results provide insight into the consequences of CALM-AF10 expression.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
