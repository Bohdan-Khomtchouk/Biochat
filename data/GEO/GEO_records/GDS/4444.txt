TITLE
Atopic Dermatitis response to narrow-band UVB treatment

SUMMARY
Analysis of lesional and non-lesional skin biopsies from Atopic Dermatitis patients following NB-UVB phototherapy. Results identify a set of biomarkers of disease response to NB-UVB phototherapy.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
Tintle S, Shemer A, Suárez-Fariñas M, Fujita H et al. Reversal of atopic dermatitis with narrow-band UVB phototherapy and biomarkers for therapeutic response. J Allergy Clin Immunol 2011 Sep;128(3):583-93.e1-4. PMID:  21762976
