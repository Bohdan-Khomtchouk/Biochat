TITLE
HCaRG overexpression in kidney cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of HCaRG9, a stably-transfected HEK293 embryonal kidney cell line over-expressing the hypertension-related, calcium-regulated gene (HCaRG). Results identify a role for HCaRG in kidney repair involving increased renal cell migration and transforming growth factor-alpha (TGF-α) expression.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
