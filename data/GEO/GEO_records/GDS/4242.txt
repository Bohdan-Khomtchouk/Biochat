TITLE
Methicillin-resistant and methicillin-sensitive control strains

SUMMARY
Analysis of methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus (MRSA) ATCC 33591 and methicillin-sensitive (MSSA) ATCC 25923; commonly used positive and negative control strains. Results provide comparison of representative MRSA and MSSA strains and insight into mechanisms of S. aureus drug resistance.

ORGANISM
Staphylococcus aureus
