TITLE
Various adult tissues (Mu11K-B)

SUMMARY
Analysis of various adult tissues, including in white adipose tissue (WAT). The differential expression of genes in WAT was compared to gene expression in differentiating 3T3-L1 preadipocytes (GDS2660).

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
