TITLE
Saponin treatment effect: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of yeast cells for up to 3 days after treatment with the saponins alpha-tomatine or tomatidine or the sterol biosynthesis inhibitor fenpropimorph. Saponins are glycoside compounds accumulated by many plant species, and possess antimicrobial activity and various pharmacological properties.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae
