TITLE
Embryonic development of colon

SUMMARY
Analysis of colon from normal C57BL/6 embryos from E13.5 to E18.5 and from adult (eight week post-natal). Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying the embryonic development of colon.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
