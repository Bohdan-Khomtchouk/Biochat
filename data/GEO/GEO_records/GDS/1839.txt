TITLE
Follicular lymphomas and response to rituximab

SUMMARY
Analysis of lymph node biopsies from 24 follicular lymphoma patients. Biopsies obtained before any systemic therapy. Results analyzed together with the subsequent response of patients to rituximab, a monoclonal antibody directed against the B cell antigen, CD20.
