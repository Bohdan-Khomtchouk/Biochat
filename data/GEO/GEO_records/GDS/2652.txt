TITLE
Neonatal brain response to docosahexaenoic acid supplemented formulas

SUMMARY
Analysis of precentral gyri from neonates fed for 12 weeks with either a formula containing both 0.33% docosahexaenoic acid (DHA) and 0.67% arachidonic acid (ARA) or one with 1.0% DHA and 0.67% ARA. DHA and AHA are the major long chain polyunsaturated fatty acids of the central nervous system.
