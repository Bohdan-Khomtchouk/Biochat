TITLE
Severe pandemic H1N1 influenza effect on peripheral blood: time course

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of peripheral blood from patients with severe pdm(H1N1) influenza, bilateral chest infiltrates, and in need of mechanical ventilation, but without significant co-morbidity. Results provide insight into molecular basis of immune responses in patients with severe pdmH1N1 infection.
