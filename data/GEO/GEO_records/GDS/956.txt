TITLE
Dopamine neurons from substantia nigra pars compacta and ventral tegmental area

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of dopaminergic neurons of the substantia nigra pars compacta (SNc) and the ventral tegmental area.  Neurons from normal animals isolated using laser capture microdissection.  SNc dopaminergic neurons degenerate in Parkinson's disease.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
