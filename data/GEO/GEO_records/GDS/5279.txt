TITLE
Pristimerin effect on myeloma cell lines

SUMMARY
Analysis of H929 and U266 myeloma cells treated with the natural triterpenoid pristimerin. Multiple myeloma tumors exhibit dysregulated cyclin D expression. Pristimerin identified from a chemical library screen for compounds that induce suppression of transcription from the cyclin D2 promoter.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
