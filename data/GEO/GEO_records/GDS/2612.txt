TITLE
Caloric restriction effect on aged skeletal muscle

SUMMARY
Analysis of aged skeletal muscles of C57BL/6NHsd animals fed a calorie-restricted diet. Caloric restriction extends the life of this strain by 40%. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of mammalian aging.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
