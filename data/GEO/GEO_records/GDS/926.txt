TITLE
Photosynthesis and photosystem I mutant

SUMMARY
Comparison of expression profiles of psad1 mutant lacking D subunit of photosystem I (PSI-D) and wild type. Photosynthetic electron flow and growth are affected in psad1 mutant.

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana
