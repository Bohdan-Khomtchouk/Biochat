TITLE
Erythroid progenitor erythropoiesis

SUMMARY
Analysis of three distinct stages of late erythroid progenitor cell development. Developmentally synchronized erythroid progenitor cells (EPCs) were prepared from splenocytes at 80, 100 and 120 hours after thiamphenical withdrawal.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
