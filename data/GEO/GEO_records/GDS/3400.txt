TITLE
Cephaloridine effect on the kidney: dose response

SUMMARY
Analysis of kidneys of animals treated with various doses of cephaloridine (CER). CER is a cephalosporin antibiotic used for studying cephalosporin antibiotic-induced renal tubule damage. Results provide insight into the mechanisms underlying cephalosporin antibiotic-induced nephrotoxicity.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
