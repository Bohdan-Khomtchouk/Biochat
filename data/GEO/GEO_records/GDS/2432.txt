TITLE
Pituitary adenoma predisposition: whole blood

SUMMARY
Analysis of whole blood from individuals with pituitary adenoma predisposition. Pituitary adenomas are common benign neoplasms characterized by oversecretion of growth hormone, leading to acromegaly. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of pituitary adenomas.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
