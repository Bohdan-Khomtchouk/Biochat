TITLE
Ras-cAMP pathway perturbation

SUMMARY
Analysis of cells following perturbation of the Ras-cAMP pathway by disrupting or constitutively activating cAMP signaling, or varying the concentration of cAMP. Results provide insight into the flow of information across a genome.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

PLATFORM
GPL2622 : ISB Yeast Array
