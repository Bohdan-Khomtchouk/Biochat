TITLE
Isolates from chronically-infected burn wound and cystic fibrosis patients

SUMMARY
Analysis of P. aeruginosa strains isolated from chronically-infected lungs of cystic fibrosis (CF) patients, burn wounds, and a burn wound strain infected-murine tumor model. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying chronic biofilm-associated P. aeruginosa infections.

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa

PLATFORM
GPL84 : [Pae_G1a] Affymetrix Pseudomonas aeruginosa Array

CITATIONS
