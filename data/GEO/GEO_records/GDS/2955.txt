TITLE
Estrogen-related receptor beta mutation effect on the stria vascularis

SUMMARY
Analysis of the stria vascularis (SV) of estrogen-related receptor beta mutants (ERR-beta; NR3B2). Results provide insight into the role of ERR-beta in the development of endolymph-producing cells of the inner ear.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
