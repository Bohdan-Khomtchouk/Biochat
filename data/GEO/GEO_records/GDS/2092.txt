TITLE
Septic CD4+ splenocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of CD4+ splenocytes 6 hours after surgically induced sepsis. CD4+ splenocytes either undergo apoptosis or become Th2 polarized within 24 hours of the onset of sepsis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
