TITLE
Cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator CFTR transgene overexpression effect on lungs

SUMMARY
Analysis of lungs of adult Cftr+/+ animals overexpressing the normal human cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator gene (CFTR) under control of the Sftpc promoter in respiratory epithelial cells. Homozygous transgenic, heterozygous transgenic and wild type mice were compared.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
