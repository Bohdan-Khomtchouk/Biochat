TITLE
Apelin knockout effect on the heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of hearts from Apelin knockouts (KO) subjected to pressure overload by aortic banding (AB). Banded Apelin KOs develop severe and progressive heart failure. Results provide insight into the role of Apelin in maintaining cardiovascular homeostasis on pressure overload.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
