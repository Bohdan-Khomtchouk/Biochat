TITLE
L5 spinal nerve ligation model of neuropathic pain: dorsal root ganglion (RG-U34C)

SUMMARY
Analysis of L4 and L5 dorsal root ganglia (DRG) from animals 28 and 50 days following L5 spinal nerve ligation. L5 spinal nerve ligation is a model of neuropathic pain.
