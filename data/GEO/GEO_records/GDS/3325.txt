TITLE
Air pollution exposure effect on children and adults: peripheral blood

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood from children and adults living in two regions of the Czech Republic (Teplice and Prachatice) with considerably different levels of air pollutants. Results provide insight into whether children and their adult parents respond differently to air pollution.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
