TITLE
Cervical cancer response to chemoradiotherapy

SUMMARY
Analysis of cervical cancer biopsy samples from patients receiving radiotherapy alone or radiotherapy plus concomitant chemotherapy with cisplatin (CRT). Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying the therapeutic response to CRT.

Homo sapiens
