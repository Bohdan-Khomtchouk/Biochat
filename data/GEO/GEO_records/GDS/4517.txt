TITLE
Model for the manic pole of bipolar disorder: hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of hippocampi from Madison (MSN) and hsd:ICR (ICR) adult males. The inbred MSN strain displays a naturally manic phenotype relative to the outbred ICR ancestral strain. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying the manic pole of bipolar disorder.
