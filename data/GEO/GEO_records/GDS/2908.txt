TITLE
T-cell prolymphocytic leukemia with inv(14)(q11q32)

SUMMARY
Analysis of T-cell prolymphocytic leukemia (T-PLL) cells with the cytogenetic abnormality inv(14)(q11q32). T-PLL is a rare aggressive lymphoma derived from mature T cells. Results provide insight into the pathogenesis of T-PLL.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array

CITATIONS
