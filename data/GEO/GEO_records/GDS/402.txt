TITLE
Type 2 diabetes and renal function

SUMMARY
Kidney tissue from a genetic model of non-insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus (NIDDM) type 2 diabetic db/db mice compared with control nondiabetic db/m littermates. 8 and 16 week old mice examined. Renal failure is common with diabetes.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
