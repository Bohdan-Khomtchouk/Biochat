TITLE
Sporulation time course

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of the developmental program of sporulation, which consists of meiosis and spore morphogenesis. Seven distinct temporal patterns of induction are observed. Samples recovered at several time points up to 11 hours.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

PLATFORM
