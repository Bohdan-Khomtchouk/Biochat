TITLE
Candida albicans effect on endothelial cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of HUVEC umbilical vein endothelial cells (EC) exposed to Candida albicans blastopsores. ECs actively participate in the innate defense against microbial pathogens. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying the endothelial stress response to Candida infection.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array

CITATIONS
