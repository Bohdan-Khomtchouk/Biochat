TITLE
Heart failure and nitric oxide

SUMMARY
Analysis of hearts from 21 month old endothelial nitric oxide synthase (eNOS) knockout males. These animals exhibit cardiac dysfunction with age and 50% mortality at 21 month. Results provide clues to molecular mechanisms leading to heart failure.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
