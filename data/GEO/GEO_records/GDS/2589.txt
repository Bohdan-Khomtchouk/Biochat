TITLE
Anaerobic growth (II)

SUMMARY
Analysis of MG1655 cells at various phases of growth up to stationary phase. Cells were grown anaerobically in M9 media containing glucose and fumarate. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli
