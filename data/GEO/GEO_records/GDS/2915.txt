TITLE
Aging liver

SUMMARY
Analysis of livers of Fisher F344/N males at 32, 58, and 84 weeks of age. Results provide insight into age-associated changes in the capacity of the liver to metabolize xenobiotic compounds.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
