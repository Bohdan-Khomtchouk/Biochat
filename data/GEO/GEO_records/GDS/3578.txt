TITLE
Beta-catenin depletion effect on multiple myeloma cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of multiple myeloma MM1.S cells depleted for beta-catenin. Beta-catenin is an effector of the Wnt signaling pathway. Results provide insight into the role of Wnt/beta-catenin signaling in the progression of multiple myeloma.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
