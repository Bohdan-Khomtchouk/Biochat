TITLE
Caspase-8 defieciency effect on intestinal epithelial cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of intestinal epithelial cells from animals with an intestinal epithelium specific deletion of caspase-8, a cysteine protease involved in regulating cellular apoptosis. Results provide insight into the role of caspase-8 in the intestinal epithelium.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
