TITLE
Neuron-specific T-box transcription factor deficiency effect on embryonic day 16.5 forebrains

SUMMARY
Analysis of E16.5 forebrains from neuron-specific T-box transcription factor (Tbr1)-/- embryos. Results provide insight the molecular mechanisms underlying regulation of brain development by TBR1.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
