TITLE
Dukes B colon cancer recurrence

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of previously resected frozen primary tumors from patients with Dukes' stage B colon cancer that recurred in 5 years. Results identify potential prognostic markers for tumor relapse.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL2006 : Human 19K oligo array

CITATIONS
Bandrés E, Malumbres R, Cubedo E, Honorato B et al. A gene signature of 8 genes could identify the risk of recurrence and progression in Dukes' B colon cancer patients. Oncol Rep 2007 May;17(5):1089-94. PMID:  17390049
