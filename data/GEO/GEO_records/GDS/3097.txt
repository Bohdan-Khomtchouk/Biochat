TITLE
Inflammatory breast cancer: tumor

SUMMARY
Analysis of tumor epithelia from patients with inflammatory breast cancer (IBC) and invasive non-IBC. This is part of a study that also examines the expression profiles of stromata surrounding tumors. Results provide insight into the contribution of the tumor component to the pathogenesis of IBC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
