TITLE
Cystic fibrosis: trachea

SUMMARY
Analysis of trachea from newborn pigs with cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator (CFTR) mutation; CFTR-/- pigs spontaneously develop hallmark features of CF lung disease. Results provide insight into Cystic fibrosis lung disease pathogenesis.

ORGANISM
