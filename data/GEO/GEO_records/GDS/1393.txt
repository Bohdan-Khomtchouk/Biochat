TITLE
Type 1 diabetes mellitus model of erectile dysfunction

SUMMARY
Analysis of F344 penile cavernosal tissue after 10 weeks of streptozotocin (STZ)-induced Type 1 diabetes. Results provide insight into molecular events associated with pathophysiology of diabetes-induced erectile dysfunction (ED).

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
