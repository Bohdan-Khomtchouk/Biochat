TITLE
Pseudomonas aeruginosa non-mucoid and mucoid strains

SUMMARY
Analysis of a non-mucoid Pseudomonas aeruginosa strain and a mucoid strain from a cystic fibrosis (CF) patient. P. aeruginosa is an opportunistic pathogen associated with chronic pulmonary infections in CF patients. Non-mucoidity is associated with early stages of CF, mucoidity with late stages.

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa
