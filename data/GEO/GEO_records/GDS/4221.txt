TITLE
AIDS-related lymphomas

SUMMARY
Analysis of tumors from HIV+ male patients with AIDS-related lymphomas (ARL). HIV+ individuals have an increased risk of developing an aggressive B-cell Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma relative to HIV- persons. Results identify a signature for ARL subtypes and provide insight into pathogenetic events in ARL.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
