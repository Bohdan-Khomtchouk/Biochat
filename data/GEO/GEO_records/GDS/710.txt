TITLE
Hippocampus ischemic tolerance time course

SUMMARY
Comparison of temporal expression patterns in hippocampi of hypoxia tolerant and sensitive animals.  Microdissected hippocampus CA1 regions analyzed 0, 1, 3, 12, 24, and 48 hours after ischemia operation.  Results offer insight into cell death in hypoxia.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL85 : [RG_U34A] Affymetrix Rat Genome U34 Array
