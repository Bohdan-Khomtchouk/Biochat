TITLE
Endothelial cell microenvironment effect (cardiovascular array)

SUMMARY
Comparison of high endothelial venules endothelial cells (HEVEC) freshly isolated from tonsils with dedifferentiated HEVEC cultured ex vivo for 48 hours identifies gene expression controlled by tissue microenvironment.
