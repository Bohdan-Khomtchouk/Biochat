TITLE
Macrophage response to hypoxia

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of cultured macrophages subjected to hypoxia for 24 hours. Macrophages were derived from the mononuclear cells of 4 healthy donors.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
