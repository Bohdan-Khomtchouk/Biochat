TITLE
Tibolone hormone effect on postmenopausal endometrium

SUMMARY
Comparison of endometria after short-term treatment with tibolone, estradiol (E2), or E2 + medroxyprogesterone acetate (E2 + MPA) in healthy postmenopausal women undergoing hysterectomy. A synthetic steroid, Tibolone is used as an alternative for estrogen or estrogen/progesterone hormone therapy.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
