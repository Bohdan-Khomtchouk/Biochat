TITLE
Glycosylphosphatidylinositol-specific phospholipase D overexpression effect on hepatoma cells: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of hepatoma HepG2 cells 6 and 12 hours following transduction with a recombinant adenovirus expressing glycosylphosphatidylinositol-specific phospholipase D (GPI-PLD). Results provide insight into the role of GPI-PLD in hepatic lipid metabolism.
