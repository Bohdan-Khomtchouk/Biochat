TITLE
Idiopathic myelofibrosis: hematopoietic CD34+ stem cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of pooled hematopoietic CD34+ stem cells from peripheral blood of patients with idiopathic myelofibrosis (IM), a rare chronic myeloproliferative disorder. Results provide insight into the molecular pathogenesis of IM.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL96 : [HG-U133A] Affymetrix Human Genome U133A Array
