TITLE
Bronchial epithelial cell line response to various airway pathogens

SUMMARY
Analysis of bronchial epithelial BEAS-2B cells exposed to UV-inactivated Staphylococcus aureus, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, or respiratory syncytial virus. Results provide insight into the common and specific molecular responses of bronchial epithelial cells to various airway pathogens.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
