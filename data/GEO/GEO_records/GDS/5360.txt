TITLE
Casein kinase 1 α deficiency effect on leukemia stem cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of leukemia stem cells (LSC) knocked down for casein kinase 1 α (Csnk1a1). Csnk1a1, a serine-threonine kinase, is critical for acute myeloid leukemia (AML) cell survival in vivo. Results provide insight into the role of Csnk1a1 in AML.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
