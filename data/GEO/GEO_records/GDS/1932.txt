TITLE
Retinoblastoma-like p107 knockout effect on neurospheres (MG-430B)

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of neurospheres cultured from the brain of embryonic day 13.5 p107 knockout embryos. p107 knockout animals exhibit enhanced stem cell self-renewal and have an increased number of stem cells.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
