TITLE
Rosiglitazone effect on kidneys

SUMMARY
Analysis of kidneys from lean and obese animals fed a diet supplemented with rosiglitazone for 12 weeks. Rosiglitazone is a thiazolidinedione drug used to treat diabetes. Results provide insight into the mechanism of action of rosiglitazone.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL1355 : [Rat230_2] Affymetrix Rat Genome 230 2.0 Array
