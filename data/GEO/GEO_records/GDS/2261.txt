TITLE
Liver response to various toxins

SUMMARY
Analysis of livers of animals 48 hours after exposure to the toxin alpha-naphthylisothiocyanate (ANIT), dimethylnitrosamine (DMN), or n-methylformamide (NMF). Results provide insight into the mechanism of toxicity of each compound.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL1355 : [Rat230_2] Affymetrix Rat Genome 230 2.0 Array
