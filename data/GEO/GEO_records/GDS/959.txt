TITLE
Asthma and interleukin 13 (MOE430B)

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of lung tissue from interleukin 13 (IL-13) knockout mice treated with house dust mite allergen. IL-13 mediates the development of asthma by causing hyperreactivity of the airway and mucus overproduction in the lung.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL340 : [MOE430B] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430B Array
