TITLE
Male contraceptive agent Gamendazole effect on the testis: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of testes of males treated with gamendazole for up to 24 hours. Gamendazole is a nonhormonal, nonsteroidal, oral indazole carboxylic acid contraceptive agent for males. The gamendazole analog lonidamine was also examined. Results provide insight into the mechanism of action of gamendazole.

ORGANISM
