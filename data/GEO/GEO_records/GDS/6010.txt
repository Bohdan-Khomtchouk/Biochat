TITLE
Influenza virus H5N1 infection of U251 astrocyte cell line: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of U251 astrocyte cells infected with the influenza H5N1 virus for up to 24 hours. Results provide insight into the immune response of astrocytes to H5N1 infection.
