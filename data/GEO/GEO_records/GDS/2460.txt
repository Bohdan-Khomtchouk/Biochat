TITLE
Autoimmune model: CD4+ T cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of CD4+ T cells from sanroque mutants that produce antinuclear autoantibodies. sanroque mutants exhibit a phenotype resembling the autoimmune disease systemic lupus erythematosus. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of the autoimmunity in the sanroque strain.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
