TITLE
Alveoli destruction time course

SUMMARY
Temporal study of lung alveoli destruction induced by caloric restriction. Alveoli number and surface area are linked to oxygen consumption, and calorie restriction lowers oxygen consumption.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL81 : [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
