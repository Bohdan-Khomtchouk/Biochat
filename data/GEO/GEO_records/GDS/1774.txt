TITLE
Chronic hepatitis C infection response to Agaricus blazei medicinal extract: peripheral blood cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood cells from chronic hepatitis patients following oral administration of Agaricus blazei Murill (AbM) extract 3 times daily for 1 week. AbM extracts are used as a non-prescription remedy for cancer and infections, including hepatitis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL3307 : ABI Human Genome Survey Microarray v2.0
