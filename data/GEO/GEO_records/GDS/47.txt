TITLE
Granulosa cell tumorigenesis  (Mu11K-A)

SUMMARY
Comparison of global changes in ovarian transcriptomes uniquely associated with either granulosa cell tumors or luteomas, and identification of new markers for this tumor phenotype. Luteinizing hormone hypersecreting mice studied.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL75 : [Mu11KsubA] Affymetrix Murine 11K SubA Array
