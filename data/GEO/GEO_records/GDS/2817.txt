TITLE
Nephronophthisis model

SUMMARY
Analysis of kidneys of animals lacking Glis2. The GLIS2 gene, which encodes a Kruppel-like zinc finger transcription factor, is found in a region of chromosome 16 p linked to nephronophthisis (NPHP). Results provide insight into the role of Glis2 in the pathophysiology of NPHP.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL2897 : GE Healthcare/Amersham Biosciences CodeLink™ Mouse Whole Genome Bioarray

CITATIONS
Attanasio M, Uhlenhaut NH, Sousa VH, O'Toole JF et al. Loss of GLIS2 causes nephronophthisis in humans and mice by increased apoptosis and fibrosis. Nat Genet 2007 Aug;39(8):1018-24. PMID:  17618285
