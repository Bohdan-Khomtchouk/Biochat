TITLE
Dietary supplement Boswellia serrata effects on liver

SUMMARY
Hepatic gene expression analysis in Swiss-Webster mice fed diet supplemented with hexane or methanol extracts of Boswellia serrata for 21 days. Extracts from Boswellia serrata may have anti-inflammatory activity.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
