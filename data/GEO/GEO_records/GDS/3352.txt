TITLE
Juvenile hormone III effect on adult female insects

SUMMARY
Analysis of whole bodies of yellow white (y, w) adult female insects topically treated with exogenous juvenile hormone (JH) III. JH can modulate immunity in insects. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying JH regulation of innate immunity in insects.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

PLATFORM
GPL72 : [DrosGenome1] Affymetrix Drosophila Genome Array
