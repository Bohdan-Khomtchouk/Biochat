TITLE
Novobiocin treatment: dose response

SUMMARY
Analysis of LE234 acrD- cells treated with novobiocin at various concentrations up to 200 ug/ml for 5 minutes. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.
