TITLE
Tumor cell response to topoisomerase poison camptothecin

SUMMARY
HeLa tumor cells treated with 10 uM topoisomerase poison camptothecin (CPT) for 8 hours. This dose and time are sufficient to induce rapid apoptosis. Results identify potential targets for adjuvant cancer therapy.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
