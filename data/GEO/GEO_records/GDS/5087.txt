TITLE
Transcriptional regulator steroid receptor coactivator-2 deficiency effect on the heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of hearts of mutants lacking transcriptional regulator steroid receptor coactivator-2 (SRC-2). Results provide insight into the role of SRC-2 in cardiac function.

ORGANISM
