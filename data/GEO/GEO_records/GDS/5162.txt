TITLE
Gentamicin effect on fur-deficient cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of Î”fur and wildtype cells treated with the bacterial antibiotic gentamicin and harvested 1 hr post-treatment. Fur is the master regulator of iron metabolism in E. coli. Results provide insight into Fur's influence on the magnitude of stress responses triggered by bactericidal antibiotics.
