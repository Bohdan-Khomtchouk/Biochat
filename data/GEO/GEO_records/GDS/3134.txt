TITLE
Moderate physical exercise training effect on the heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of left heart ventricles of animals subjected to a moderate physical exercise regimen that induces cardioprotection. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of this cardioprotective effect.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL1355 : [Rat230_2] Affymetrix Rat Genome 230 2.0 Array
