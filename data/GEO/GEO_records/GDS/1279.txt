TITLE
Age effect on extraocular muscles

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of extraocular muscle (EOM) from animals 6, 18, and 30 months of age. Extensor digitorum longus (EDL) muscles also examined. Results provide insight into the effect of age on EOM.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL85 : [RG_U34A] Affymetrix Rat Genome U34 Array
