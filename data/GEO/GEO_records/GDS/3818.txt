TITLE
Atherosclerotic lesion regression: aorta

SUMMARY
Analysis of Apolipoprotein E-deficient C57BL/6 females fed a high cholesterol diet for 30 wks to induce advanced atherosclerosis, and then treated with adenovirus expressing human apoE3 to regress the atherosclerotic lesions. Results provide insight into molecular basis of atherosclerosis regression

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
