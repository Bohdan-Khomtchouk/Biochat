TITLE
Alcohol sensitive and tolerant populations

SUMMARY
Analysis of alcohol-sensitive Canton S animals immediately following ethanol exposure and a population of Canton S animals that developed tolerance 2 hours after ethanol exposure. Results provide insight into the underlying cellular and physiological mechanisms that respond to alcohol exposure.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster
