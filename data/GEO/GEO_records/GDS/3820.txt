TITLE
Autosomal dominant monocytopenia: polymorphonuclear leukocytes (STAT60)

SUMMARY
Analysis of polymorphonuclear cells from patients affected with autosomal dominant monocytopenia characterized by mycobacterial, fungal, and viral infections, and frequently myelodysplasia. RNA extracted using STAT60. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying this syndrome.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
