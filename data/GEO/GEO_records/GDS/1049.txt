TITLE
Terminal heart failure model

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of left ventricles of 7 week old double transgenics overexpressing human angiotensin and renin. Double transgenics exhibiting cardiac hypertrophy compared to those exhibiting terminal heart failure with cachexia. Double transgenics treated with losartan also examined.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL85 : [RG_U34A] Affymetrix Rat Genome U34 Array
