TITLE
Histone deacetylase inhibitor effect on endothelial cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of umbilical vein endothelial cells treated with 500 nM trichostatin A (TSA) for 24 hours. TSA is a histone deacetylase inhibitor. TSA treatment results in decreased endothelial nitric oxide synthase expression.
