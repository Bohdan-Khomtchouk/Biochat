TITLE
Dopamine β-hydroxylase knockout effect on embryonic heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of adrenergic-deficient (Dbh-/-) E10.5 hearts. Adrenergic hormones are essential for early heart development. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms that mediate adrenergic actions in early heart development.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL8321 : [Mouse430A_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430A 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
