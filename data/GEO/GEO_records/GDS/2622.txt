TITLE
Epidermal growth factor effect on mammary epithelial cell line: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of mammary epithelial MCF10A cells following treatment with epidermal growth factor for various time points up to 480 minutes. Results provide insight into the dynamics of growth factor signaling.

Homo sapiens
