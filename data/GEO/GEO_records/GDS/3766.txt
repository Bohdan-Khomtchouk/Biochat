TITLE
Inhibitor of nuclear factor kappa B kinase beta-subunit overactivity effect on skin cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of back skin from transgenic (Tg) animals with keratinocyte-specific IKKbeta overexpression. The Tg animals present an inflammatory skin phenotype with histological features characteristic of interface dermatitis. Results provide insight into the role of IKKbeta in skin physiopathology.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
