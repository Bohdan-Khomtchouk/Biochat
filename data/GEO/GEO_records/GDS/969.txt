TITLE
Bone marrow prolonged storage effects

SUMMARY
Analysis of impact of pre-analytical handling of bone marrow (BM). Time delays of 0, 24, or 48 hours between sample aspiration and RNA extraction examined. Storage of BM has dramatic effects on mRNA expression of individual transcripts.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
