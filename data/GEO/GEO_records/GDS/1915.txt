TITLE
Hepatocyte nuclear factor 4 alpha knockout effect on the small intestine

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of small intestines lacking hepatocyte nuclear factor 4 alpha (HNF4alpha). Cre/Lox system used to knock out Hnf4a in the small intestine. HNF4alpha is a member of the nuclear receptor family of ligand-dependent transcription factors.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
