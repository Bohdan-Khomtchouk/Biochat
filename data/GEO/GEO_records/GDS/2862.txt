TITLE
Hypothalamus during normal female sexual development

SUMMARY
Analysis of hypothalami from juvenile, early pubertal, and late pubertal female animals. Results provide insight into the role of the hypothalamus in controlling the onset of puberty.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
