TITLE
CD4+ T cell differentiation (HG-133B)

SUMMARY
Analysis of CD4+ T cell differentiation by expression profiling of subpopulations of CD4+ cells representing 5 successive stages of differentiation: intrathymic T progenitors, double positive thymocytes, single positive thymocytes, naïve T cells from cord blood, and naïve T cells from adult blood.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
