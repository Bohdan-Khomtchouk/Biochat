TITLE
Metastatic breast cancer model: premetastatic lung

SUMMARY
Analysis of premetastatic lung from BALB/c mice without any tumors (naïve) and from mice bearing nonmetastatic (67NR) or metastatic (4T1) breast carcinomas. Results provide insight into metastatic tumor-induced molecular changes in premetastatic lungs before the arrival of metastatic tumor cells.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
