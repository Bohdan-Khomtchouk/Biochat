TITLE
Norfloxacin treatment: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of C600 cells at various time points up to 30 minutes following treatment with 50 ug/ml norfloxacin.  Norfloxacin added to cells grown in LB. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli
