TITLE
Measles: peripheral blood mononuclear cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) from measles patients. PBMCs collected upon hospital admission, at discharge, and one month after discharge. Results provide insight into the immunological changes that occur during measles infections.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
