TITLE
Activin deficiency effect on ovarian granulosa cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of granulosa cells from animals deficient for all ovarian activins. Granulosa cells are associated with the developing oocyte. Results provide insight into the role of activins in ovarian growth, differentiation, and cancer.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
