TITLE
Sodium arsenate effect on Azucena and Bala varieties

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of 1-week-old roots from varieties Azucena and Bala following treatment with 1 ppm sodium arsenate. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of arsenate tolerance exhibited by the Bala variety.

ORGANISM
Oryza sativa

PLATFORM
GPL2025 : [Rice] Affymetrix Rice Genome Array
