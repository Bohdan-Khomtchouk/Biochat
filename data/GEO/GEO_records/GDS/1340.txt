TITLE
Exercise effect on aged muscle

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of vastus lateralis muscles from 6 healthy, elderly, sedentary males after 3 months of exercise at 80% of maximal capacity.  Results provide insight into the molecular changes induced by exercise in sedentary skeletal muscles.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
