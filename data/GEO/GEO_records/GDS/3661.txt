TITLE
Hypertensive heart failure model

SUMMARY
Analysis of heart from age-matched, spontaneously hypertensive animals with and without heart failure (HF). Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying the transition to hypertensive HF.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
