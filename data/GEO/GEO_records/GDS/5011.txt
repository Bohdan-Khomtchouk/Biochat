TITLE
Neonatal antibiotic effect on preterm neonate small intestine

SUMMARY
Analysis of small intestinal tissues of preterm piglets treated with broad-spectrum antibiotics twice daily from birth to tissue collection on day 5. Results provide insight into molecular impact of prophylactic antibiotics on the incidence and severity of necrotizing enterocolitis in preterm pigs.

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa
