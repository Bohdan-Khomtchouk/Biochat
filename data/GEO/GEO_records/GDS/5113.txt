TITLE
Transcription factor Grainyhead-like 2 depletion effect on lung epithelial cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of MLE-15 lung epithelial cells depleted for Grainyhead-like 2. Results provide insight into the role of Grainyhead-like 2 in the morphogenesis and differentiation of lung epithelial cells.
