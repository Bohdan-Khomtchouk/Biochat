TITLE
p38alpha deficiency effect on the fetal liver

SUMMARY
Analysis of embryonic day 13.5 and 15.5 fetal livers lacking p38alpha. p38alpha is a mitogen-activated kinase that controls inflammatory responses and cell proliferation. Results provide insight into the mechanisms underlying the enhanced proliferation of p38alpha-deficient hematopoietic cells.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
