TITLE
HIV viral infection time course

SUMMARY
Gene expression profile of proliferating normal peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMC) infected with HIV type 1 RF. Assessed at 0, 12, 24, 48, and 72 hours postinfection and compared with those of matched uninfected PBMC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL80 : [Hu6800] Affymetrix Human Full Length HuGeneFL Array
