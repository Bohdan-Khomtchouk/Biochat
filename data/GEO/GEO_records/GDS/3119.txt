TITLE
Ulcerative colitis

SUMMARY
Analysis of colonic mucosa samples with or without signs of inflammation from patients with ulcerative colitis (UC), a type of inflammatory bowel disease (IBD). Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms contributing to the development of inflammation in UC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
