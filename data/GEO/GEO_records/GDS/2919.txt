TITLE
Tremorogenic chemical harmane effect on astrocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of astrocytes treated with the tremorogenic chemical harmane. Increased blood harmane concentration is associated with an increased risk of essential tremor. Astrocyte dysfunction is associated with neurodegenerative diseases.
