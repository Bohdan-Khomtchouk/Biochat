TITLE
Coxsackievirus B3 pathogenesis (I)

SUMMARY
Temporal analaysis of an in vitro model of coxsackievirus B3 (CVB3) infection. HeLa cells infected with either CVB3 or control PBS and samples examined at 0, 0.5, 1, 3, 5, 7 and 9 hours following treatment.
