TITLE
Viral infection: time course

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of Oregon R males 24 and 48 hours after drosophila C virus infection. Results provide insight into the response of the innate immune system to viral infections.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

PLATFORM
