TITLE
Huntingtons disease and combination drug therapy

SUMMARY
Whole brain hemispheres from Huntingtons disease model R6/2 transgenic mice treated with creatine, tacrine and moclobemide from 5 weeks of age. Drug therapy was aimed at boosting neurotransmitter levels and improve cognitive functions.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
