TITLE
Vulvar intraepithelial neoplasia

SUMMARY
Analysis of vulvar intraepithelial neoplasia (VIN) lesions. VIN is a premalignant disorder caused by human papillomaviruses. Results provide insight into the pathogenesis of VIN.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
