TITLE
Dominant negative thyroid hormone receptor expression and PPARalpha activation effect on the heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of hearts of dominant negative thyroid hormone receptor (TR) transgenics after treatment with the peroxisome proliferators activated receptor alpha (PPARalpha) agonist WY14643. Results provide insight into the crosstalk between TR and PPARalpha in the heart.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
