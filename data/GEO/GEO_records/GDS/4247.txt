TITLE
Cystic fibrosis: trachea, bronchus and distal lung

SUMMARY
Analysis of trachea, bronchus and distal lung from newborn pigs with cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator (CFTR) gene mutations. Cystic fibrosis (CF) pigs develop lung disease. Results provide insight into CF lung disease pathogenesis.

ORGANISM
Sus scrofa
