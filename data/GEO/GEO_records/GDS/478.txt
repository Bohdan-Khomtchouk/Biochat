TITLE
Coxsackievirus B3 pathogenesis (II)

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of in vitro model of coxsackievirus B3 (CVB3) infection. HeLa cells infected with either CVB3, CVB3 and U0126 (blocks cytokines and metalloproteinases) or control PBS, and samples examined at 0, 0.5, 3 and 9 hours after treatment.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
