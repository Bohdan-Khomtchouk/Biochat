TITLE
Various brain tumors

SUMMARY
Analysis of 12 primary brain tumor biopsies with some variation in their histological diagnoses. These results, together with those obtained from miRNA profiling by real-time PCR, provide insight into the relationship between endogenous fluctuations in miRNA and mRNA expression levels.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
