TITLE
Benzalkonium chloride adapted E. coli K12

SUMMARY
Analysis of E. coli K12 strains adapted to grow in benzalkonium chloride (BC). BC is a commonly used disinfectant and preservative. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of this resistance.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli

PLATFORM
GPL189 : Sigma Genosys Panorama Escherichia coli Gene Array

CITATIONS
Bore E, Hébraud M, Chafsey I, Chambon C et al. Adapted tolerance to benzalkonium chloride in Escherichia coli K-12 studied by transcriptome and proteome analyses. Microbiology 2007 Apr;153(Pt 4):935-46. PMID:  17379704
