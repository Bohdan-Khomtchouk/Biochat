TITLE
Embryonic gonad development

SUMMARY
Analysis of male and female gonads at 11.5 to 18.5 days postcoitum (dpc). Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying the functional and structural changes in the developing male and female embryonic gonads.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
