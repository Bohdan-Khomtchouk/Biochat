TITLE
Cadmium effect on hepatocyte in vitro

SUMMARY
Analysis of primary cultured hepatocytes exposed to 4 uM cadmium for 3 hours. Cadmium is a potent carcinogen. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of cadmium toxicity.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
