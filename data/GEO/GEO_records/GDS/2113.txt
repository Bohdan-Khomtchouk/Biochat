TITLE
Pheochromocytomas of various genetic origins

SUMMARY
Analysis of 76 adrenal and extra-adrenal pheochromocytomas. These neural crest-derived tumors of uniform phenotype arise from inherited or sporadic mutations in at least six independent genes. Results provide insight into the molecular pathogenesis of pheochromocytomas.
