TITLE
Antiepileptic drugs levetiracetam and phenytoin effect on the brain

SUMMARY
Analysis of the brainstem, frontal cortex, and hippocampus of animals chronically treated for 90 days with levetiracetam or phenytoin. Levetiracetam inhibits epileptogenesis in the kindling model of epilepsy. Phenytoin's antiepileptic activity involves the inhibition of sodium channels.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
