TITLE
Heart regeneration in zebrafish

SUMMARY
Analysis of hearts of zebrafish 1 to 7 days following partial heart amputation. Unlike mammals, zebrafish hearts display an extensive regenerative capacity. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of this regenerative capacity.

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

PLATFORM
GPL1319 : [Zebrafish] Affymetrix Zebrafish Genome Array

CITATIONS
Sleep E, Boué S, Jopling C, Raya M et al. Transcriptomics approach to investigate zebrafish heart regeneration. J Cardiovasc Med (Hagerstown) 2010 May;11(5):369-80. PMID:  20179605
