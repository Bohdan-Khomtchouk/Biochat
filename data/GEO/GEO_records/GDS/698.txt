TITLE
Spermatogonial stem cell activity time course under various culture conditions (RAE230A)

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of stem cell activity examined in laminin binding testis germ cells cultured on either STO fibroblasts for 0 to 20 days or MSC-1 Sertoli cells for 20 days.  Results provide insight into maintenance of stem cell activity.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
