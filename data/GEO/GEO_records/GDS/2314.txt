TITLE
Antenatal steroid effect on the placenta

SUMMARY
Analysis of placentas of C57BL animals following antenatal steroid (AS) treatment using dexamethasone. AS therapy is used to promote fetal lung maturation. Results provide insight into the risks associated with AS therapy.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
