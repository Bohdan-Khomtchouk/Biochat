TITLE
Vitamin E deficiency effect on distal colon

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of distal colons from 8 Hooded Lister males fed a vitamin E deficient diet for 12 weeks. Vitamin E deficiency is associated with inflammatory bowel disease and colon cancer. Results provide insight into the contribution of vitamin E deficiency to colon pathologies.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
