TITLE
Neuroblastoma differentiation induced by retinoic acid: time course

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of neuroblastoma Neuro2a cell differentiation induced by retinoic acid. Gene expression examined at several time points up to 24 hours following treatment with 20 uM all-trans-retinoic acid. Provides insight into the role of mKIAA genes in the early stages of neurite outgrowth.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
