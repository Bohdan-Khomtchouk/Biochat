TITLE
Diet study: yeast effect time course

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of gene expression in response to nutrition factors. Yeast-fed and yeast-restricted adults sampled at 12 hourly intervals. Genes and networks regulating insulin ligand and receptor activity characterized.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster
