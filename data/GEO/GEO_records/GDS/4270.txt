TITLE
Intravenous corticosteroid therapy effect on children with severe ulcerative colitis: peripheral blood

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood from pediatric patients with severe ulcerative colitis (UC) on day 3 of intravenous corticosteroid (IVCS) therapy. Individual response to IVCS is variable. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying response to IVCS in pediatric UC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
