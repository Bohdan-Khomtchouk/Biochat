TITLE
Inflammatory dilated cardiomyopathy

SUMMARY
Analysis of parvovirus B19 positive endomyocardial biopsy (EMB) specimens from patients with inflammatory dilated cardiomyopathy (DCMi). Results report on a distinctive cardiac expression pattern in DCMi thereby providing insight into the pathogenesis of this DCM subtype.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
