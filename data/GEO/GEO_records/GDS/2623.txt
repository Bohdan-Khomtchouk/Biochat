TITLE
Epidermal growth factor effect on cervical carcinoma cell line: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of cervical carcinoma HeLa cells following treatment with epidermal growth factor for various time points up to 480 minutes. Results provide insight into the dynamics of growth factor signaling.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
