TITLE
Pituitary adenoma subtypes

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of 4 pituitary adenoma subtypes. Growth hormone (GH), prolactin (PRL), and adrenocorticotrophin (ACTH) secreting pituitary adenomas, and non-functioning pituitary adenoma examined. 5 specimens pooled and examined for each subtype. Results provide insight into tumor pathogenesis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
