TITLE
Multiple sclerosis: peripheral blood mononuclear cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) from multiple sclerosis (MS) patients. MS is an inflammatory disease of the central nervous system (CNS). Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying MS pathogenesis.
