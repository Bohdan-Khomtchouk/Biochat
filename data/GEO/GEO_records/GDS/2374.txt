TITLE
Glucose-dependent insulinotropic peptide-dependent adrenal hyperplasia

SUMMARY
Analysis of hyperplastic adrenal glands from patients with glucose-dependent insulinotropic peptide (GIP)-dependent Cushing's Syndrome (CS) or ACTH-dependent CS. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of the ectopic adrenal expression of GIPR in GIP-dependent CS.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
