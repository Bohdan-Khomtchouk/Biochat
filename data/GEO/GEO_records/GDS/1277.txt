TITLE
Obliterative bronchiolitis and  tracheal allograft

SUMMARY
Analysis of tracheal grafts from BALB/c donors transplanted into MHC incompatible C57BL/6 recipients. Allografts examined up to 25 days after transplantation. Provides insight into pathogensis of allograft rejection and associated obliterative bronchiolitis development.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
