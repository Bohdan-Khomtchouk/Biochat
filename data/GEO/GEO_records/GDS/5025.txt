TITLE
Age and gender effect on brain

SUMMARY
Analysis of brain from young (7.5-8.5 months) and old (31-36 months) wildtype males and females. Brains are sexually dimorphic in adults. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms associated with brain aging across gender.

ORGANISM
Danio rerio

PLATFORM
