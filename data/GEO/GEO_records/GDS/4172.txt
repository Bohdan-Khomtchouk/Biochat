TITLE
Effect of protease-resistant (PML-RAR)α on U937 cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of U937 cells transfected with GFP-(PML-RAR)α (PR) or GFP-PR2VR (cleavage-resistant mutant) and GFP-sorted at 6 and 9 hrs. Neutrophil elastase (NE) cleaves PR, the fusion protein that initiates acute promyelocytic leukemia. Results provide insight into leukemogenicity of NE-resistant PR.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
Uy GL, Lane AA, Welch JS, Grieselhuber NR et al. A protease-resistant PML-RAR{alpha} has increased leukemogenic potential in a murine model of acute promyelocytic leukemia. Blood 2010 Nov 4;116(18):3604-10. PMID:  20647568
