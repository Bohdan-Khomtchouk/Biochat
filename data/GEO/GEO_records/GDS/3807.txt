TITLE
Sebacic acid supplemented diet effect on db/db liver

SUMMARY
Analysis of livers of db/db animals fed a diet supplemented with sebacic acid (SA) for 6 weeks. SA supplementation improves fasting plasma glucose, HbA1c and glucose tolerance in this model of type 2 diabetes. Results provide insight into the mechanism of action of SA.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL6885 : Illumina MouseRef-8 v2.0 expression beadchip
