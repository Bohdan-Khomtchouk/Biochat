TITLE
Primary central nervous system lymphoma patients: brain tumor

SUMMARY
Analysis of brain tumor tissue from primary central nervous system lymphoma (PCNSL) patients. PCNSL is an extranodal form of non-Hodgkin lymphoma that arises in the craniospinal axis. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying survival in patients with PCNSL.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
