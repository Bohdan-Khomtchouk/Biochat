TITLE
Transcriptional profiling of tobacco smokers versus controls

SUMMARY
Analysis of lymphoblast cell lines derived from six subjects with active nicotine dependence. Depression susceptibility, nicotine dependence, alcohol dependence, and cannabis dependence have correlated genetic and environmental diatheses. Results provide insight into complex behaviour disorders.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
