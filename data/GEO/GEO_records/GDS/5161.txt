TITLE
Bcl6 deficiency effect on regulatory T cell

SUMMARY
Analysis of CD4+CD25+Foxp3+ regulatory T cells from mutants deficient for the transcriptional repressor Bcl6. Results provide insight into the role of Bcl6 in regulatory T cell function.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
