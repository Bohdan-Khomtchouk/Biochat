TITLE
Ligand screen in B cells: CGS-21680 hydrochloride (adenosine)

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of effects induced by 30 mM CGS-21680 hydrochloride (adenosine) in male c57BL/6 B cells. Cells incubated in culture with ligand and samples removed at several time points up to 4 hours. Spleen from male c57BL/6 used as reference.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL260 : Caltech 16K cDNA Mouse Array
