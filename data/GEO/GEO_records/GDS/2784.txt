TITLE
Survey of various adult tissues

SUMMARY
Analysis of eight distinct adult tissues in a model organism. Two larval tissues and whole organism also examined. Results provide insight into the degree of tissue specific gene expression at the adult stage.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

PLATFORM
GPL1322 : [Drosophila_2] Affymetrix Drosophila Genome 2.0 Array
