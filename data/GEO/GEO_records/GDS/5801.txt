TITLE
Protein kinase C δ deficiency effect on breast cancer cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of basal-like breast cancer (BC) cell lines MDA-MB-468 and BT-549 treated with protein kinase C δ (PKCδ) siRNA to induce apoptosis. Results provide insight into candidate apoptosis regulators in BC cells.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
