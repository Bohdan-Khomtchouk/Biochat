TITLE
Transcription factor RUNX1 knockout

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of RUNX1 null mutant embryos at E8.5 and E12. RUNX1 is necessary for normal development of all hematopoietic lineages, and is implicated in acute leukemias.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
