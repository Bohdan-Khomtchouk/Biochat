TITLE
miR-365 expression effect on lung adenocarcinoma cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of NCI-H441 adenocarcinoma cells expressing hsa-miR-365-2, a precursor of miR-365. Results identify targets of miR-365 and the role of miR-365 in the pulmonary transcriptional program responsible for lung development and malignancy.
