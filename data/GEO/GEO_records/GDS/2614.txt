TITLE
miRNA-1-2 knockout effect on the heart

SUMMARY
Analysis of hearts from miRNA-1-2 knockout mutants at postnatal day 10. miRNA-1-2 is a microRNA specific for cardiac and skeletal muscle. Results provide insight into the role of miRNA-1-2 in cardiac development.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
