TITLE
Testicular seminoma progression

SUMMARY
Analysis of testicular seminoma tumors at various stages of progression (pT1, pT2, pT3). Results provide insight into the pathogenesis of testicular seminoma.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
