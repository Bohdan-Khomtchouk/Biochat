TITLE
Auxin effects on seedlings

SUMMARY
Analysis of effects of indole-3-acetic acid (IAA, auxin) treatment on 5 day old light-grown Columbia-0 seedlings. Auxins promote cell expansion.

Arabidopsis thaliana
