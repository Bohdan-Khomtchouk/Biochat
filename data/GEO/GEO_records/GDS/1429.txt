TITLE
Various anti-cancer drugs effect on liver: time course and dose-response

SUMMARY
Analysis of livers of Sprague-Dawley males treated with the anticancer drug carmustine, methotrexate, or thioguanine. Animals treated daily with various doses and livers examined at various time points up to 5 days. Results combined with clinical pathology assessments in a chemogenomics database.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL1820 : Rat Uniset 10K
