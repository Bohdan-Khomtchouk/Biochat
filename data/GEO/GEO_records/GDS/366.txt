TITLE
Necrotizing enterocolitis development

SUMMARY
Rodent model of necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC) used to study effect of formula feeding and hypoxia on NEC development. Newborn rats either breast fed, or formula-fed with hypoxic stress. Three pups sacrificed daily for 5 days. Terminal ileum examined.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
