TITLE
Idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura

SUMMARY
Idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura (ITP) is an autoimmune hematologic disorder. CD3+ cells were studied in duplicate from healthy controls, patients with active ITP and patients with ITP in remission.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL91 : [HG_U95A] Affymetrix Human Genome U95A Array
