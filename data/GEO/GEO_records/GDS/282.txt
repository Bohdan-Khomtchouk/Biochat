TITLE
Hypo-osmotic shock, ppt1 mutant

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of wild type and ppt1 mutant strains after hypo-osmotic shock. Cultures grown with 1M sorbitol for ~20 hours, then cells resuspended in YPD after which samples collected at several time points up to 1 hour.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae
