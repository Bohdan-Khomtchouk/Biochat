TITLE
KIN10 kinase transcriptional induction effect on mesophyll cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of mesophyll protoplasts following transcriptional induction of the KIN10 (At3g01090) kinase. Results provide insight into the function of KIN10.

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana

PLATFORM
GPL198 : [ATH1-121501] Affymetrix Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array

CITATIONS
