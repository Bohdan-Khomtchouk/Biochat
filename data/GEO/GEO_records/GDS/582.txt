TITLE
Aging and oxidative stress in fly heads

SUMMARY
Analysis of effect of aging and oxidative stress in male w1118 fly heads. Aging examined using young (3 day) and adult (47 day) flies. Oxidative stress examined using sucrose-fed and 15 mM paraquat-fed flies.

ORGANISM
Drosophila melanogaster

PLATFORM
GPL72 : [DrosGenome1] Affymetrix Drosophila Genome Array
