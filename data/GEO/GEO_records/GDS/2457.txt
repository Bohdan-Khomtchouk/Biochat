TITLE
Hypoxia effect on the adrenal gland

SUMMARY
Analysis of adrenal glands from postnatal day 7 animals exposed to hypoxia from birth. Results provide insight into the role of the adrenal gland in mediating the increase in plasma corticosterone and steroidogenesis observed in animals exposed to hypoxia from birth.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
