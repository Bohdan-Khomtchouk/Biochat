TITLE
Pre-B cell colony enhancing factor depletion effect on pulmonary microvascular endothelial cell

Analysis of pulmonary microvascular endothelial cells depleted for pre-B cell colony enhancing factor (PBEF). Results provide insight into the role of PBEF in the endothelium.
