TITLE
Age effect on laryngeal muscle

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of laryngeal posterior cricoarytenoid muscle from animals 6, 18, and 30 months of age. Results provide insight into the effect of age and sarcopenia on non-locomotive muscles.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
