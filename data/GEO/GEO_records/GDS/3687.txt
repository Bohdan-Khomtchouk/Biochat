TITLE
Streptozotocin-induced diabetes during pregnancy effect on the neonatal lung

SUMMARY
Analysis of lung from 14 day old Sprague-Dawley neonates born to a parent with streptozotocin-induced diabetes. Maternal diabetes is associated with fetal and neonatal adverse effects including pulmonary disturbances.
