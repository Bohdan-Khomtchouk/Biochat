TITLE
Lineage-specific regulatory network specified by PAL-1

SUMMARY
Various C blastomere mutant embryos analyzed to deconvolve C blastomere lineage-specific expression patterns specified by the PAL-1 homeodomain protein. Embryonic tissue examined at various time points from the 4-cell stage (0 minutes) to 186 minutes.

ORGANISM
Caenorhabditis elegans

PLATFORM
GPL200 : [Celegans] Affymetrix C. elegans Genome Array
