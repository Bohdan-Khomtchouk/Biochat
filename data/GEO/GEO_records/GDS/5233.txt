TITLE
Bisphenol A effect on the fetal urogenital sinus: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of urogenital sinuses (UGS) of male fetuses exposed to bisphenol A. BPA is a ubiquitous, estrogenic chemical used in the production of polycarbonate plastics.   UGS examined at embryonic days 17 and 18, and postnatal day 0. Results provide insight into the molecular effects of BPA on UGS.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
