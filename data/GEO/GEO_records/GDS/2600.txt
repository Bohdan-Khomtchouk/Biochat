TITLE
Stationary phase: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of stationary phase MG1655 cells at OD600 of 2.4, 2.8, and 3.1.  Cells at OD 3.1 in LB media examined at various time points up to 60 minutes. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli
