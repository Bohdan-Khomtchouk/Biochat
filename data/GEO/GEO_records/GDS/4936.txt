TITLE
Nucleoside analog ARC effect on breast cancer cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of MCF-7 breast cancer cells treated with the nucleoside analog ARC (NSC 188491). ARC inhibits transcription and selectively kills cancer cells.  It has the ability to perturb angiogenesis in vitro. Results provide further insight into the mechanism of action of ARC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array
