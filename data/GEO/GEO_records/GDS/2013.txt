TITLE
Lupus-prone BWF1 males and females: spleen (MG-U74C)

SUMMARY
Analysis of spleens from 4-month-old pre-lupus male and female BWF1 animals. The BWF1 strain is a model for systemic lupus erythematosus. BWF1 females are more prone to lupus than BWF1 males.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL83 : [MG_U74Cv2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74 Version 2 Array

CITATIONS
Gubbels Bupp MR, Jørgensen TN, Kotzin BL. Identification of candidate genes that influence sex hormone-dependent disease phenotypes in mouse lupus. Genes Immun 2008 Jan;9(1):47-56. PMID:  18046341
