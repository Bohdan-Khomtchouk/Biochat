TITLE
Retina response to acute visible light exposure

SUMMARY
Analysis of retinas from dark adapted animals following acute exposure to visible light to induce photoreceptor death. Results compared with those from retinas of prCAD mutants, a model for photoreceptor degeneration (GDS3186).

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
