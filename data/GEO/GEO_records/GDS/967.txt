TITLE
Glycine receptor beta subunit mutant model for hyperekplexia

SUMMARY
Analysis of spinal cords from glycine receptor beta subunit mutants with alleviating or severe spastic symptoms. Mutants at various ages up to 6 months examined. Results provide insight into the basis for tendency of hyperekplexia (startle disease) symptoms to alleviate with age.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
