TITLE
Ozone effect on Interleukin-10 deficient lung: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of lung from interleukin-10 (IL10)-/- mutant males exposed to 0.3 ppm ozone (O3) for up to 72hr. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying the protective role of IL10 in O3-induced pulmonary inflammation.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
