TITLE
Stage III serous ovarian adenocarcinomas

SUMMARY
Analysis of stage III ovarian serous papillary adenocarcinomas from 5-year survivors and deceased patients. Ovarian carcinoma is often not detected until an advanced stage.  Results provide insight into gene expression patterns associated with survival that could have prognostic relevance.
