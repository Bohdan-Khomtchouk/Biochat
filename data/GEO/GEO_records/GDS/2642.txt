TITLE
Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis

SUMMARY
Analysis of colonoscopic biopsies from patients with Crohn’s disease (CD) or ulcerative colitis (UC). CD and UC are inflammatory bowel diseases with variable, overlapping clinical features and complex pathophysiologies. Results provide insight into the pathogenesis of CD and UC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL8300 : [HG_U95Av2] Affymetrix Human Genome U95 Version 2 Array
