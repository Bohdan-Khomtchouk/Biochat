TITLE
IL-28B polymorphism effect on hepatitis C virus-related hepatocellular carcinoma: resected liver

SUMMARY
Analysis of resected liver from hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) patients with chronic hepatitis C (CHC) representing  interleukin-28B (IL-28B) SNP rs8099917 TT genotype and TG/GG genotype.  Results provide insight into the association between IL-28B genotype and the outcome of HCC patients.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
