TITLE
Non-small cell lung carcinoma in female nonsmokers

SUMMARY
Analysis of paired tumor and adjacent normal lung tissue specimens obtained from nonsmoking female non-small cell lung carcinoma (NSCLC) patients in Taiwan. Results provide insight into potential prognostic biomarkers and therapeutic targets for NSCLC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
