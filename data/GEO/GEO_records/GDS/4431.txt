TITLE
Autistic children and their father’s age: peripheral blood lymphocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of peripheral blood lymphocytes of autistic and non-autistic children. Correlation among gene expression changes, paternal age, and autism examined.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
GPL570 : [HG-U133_Plus_2] Affymetrix Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
