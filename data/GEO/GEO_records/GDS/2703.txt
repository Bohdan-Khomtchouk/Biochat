TITLE
Transcription factor Klf9 deficiency effect on the intestine

SUMMARY
Analysis of jejuna of Krüppel-like factor 9 (Klf9) null mutants. Klf9, a zinc-finger transcription factor, is involved in the control of cell proliferation, cell differentiation and cell fate. Results provide insight into role of Klf9 in intestine crypt-villus cell renewal and lineage determination

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array

CITATIONS
Simmen FA, Xiao R, Velarde MC, Nicholson RD et al. Dysregulation of intestinal crypt cell proliferation and villus cell migration in mice lacking Kruppel-like factor 9. Am J Physiol Gastrointest Liver Physiol 2007 Jun;292(6):G1757-69. PMID:  17379758
