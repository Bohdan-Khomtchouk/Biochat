TITLE
Medroxyprogesterone acetate effect on the pregnant cervix

SUMMARY
Analysis of embryonic day 15 (E15) pregnant cervices treated with medroxyprogesterone acetate (MPA). The use of progestational agents such as MPA reduces the incidence of pre-term birth. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying this effect.

ORGANISM
