TITLE
Isolates from burn wound patients

SUMMARY
Analysis of three different P. aeruginosa strains isolated from burn wound patients and the same strains using two infection models (a plant model and a murine tumor model). Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying P. aeruginosa infections.

ORGANISM
Pseudomonas aeruginosa

PLATFORM
GPL84 : [Pae_G1a] Affymetrix Pseudomonas aeruginosa Array

CITATIONS
Bielecki P, Puchałka J, Wos-Oxley ML, Loessner H et al. In-vivo expression profiling of Pseudomonas aeruginosa infections reveals niche-specific and strain-independent transcriptional programs. PLoS One 2011;6(9):e24235. PMID:  21931663
