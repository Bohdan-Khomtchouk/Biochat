TITLE
YAP transcriptional regulator depletion effect on endothelial cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of umbilical vein endothelial HUVEC cells depleted for the transcriptional regulator YAP. YAP binds to the SH3 domain of the transcriptional activator Yes. Results provide insight into the role of YAP in endothelial cell proliferation.
