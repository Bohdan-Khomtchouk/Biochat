TITLE
Large intestine lacking intestinal flora

SUMMARY
Analysis of large intestines of animals lacking intestinal flora. Results provide insight into the role of the intestinal flora in the development and function of the host immune system in the large intestine.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL81 : [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array
