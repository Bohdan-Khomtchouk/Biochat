TITLE
Anti-TB chemotherapy effect on peripheral blood mononuclear cells from active tuberculosis patients: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of PBMCs from patients with active pulmonary tuberculosis (PTB) up to 6 months after initiation of anti-tuberculosis chemotherapy treatment. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms associated with PTB.
