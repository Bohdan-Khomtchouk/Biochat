TITLE
RNA polymerase II mutants (I)

SUMMARY
Analysis of RNA polymerase II (RNAPII) RPB1 mutants that are sensitive to the nucleotide-depleting drug 6-azauracil (6AU). Results provide further insight into the mechanisms of RNAPII transcription.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae
