TITLE
Endothelin-1 effect on fibroblasts

SUMMARY
Cultured primary lung fibroblasts incubated 4 hours in the presence or absence of 100 nM endothelin-1 (ET-1). Results suggest that ET-1 is involved in matrix synthesis and connective tissue deposition during wound repair and in pulmonary fibrosis.
