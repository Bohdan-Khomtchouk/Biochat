TITLE
Age effect on central and peripheral nervous systems

SUMMARY
Comparison of gene expression in the oculomotor nucleus (OMN) to that in the spinal cord. Animals 6, 18, and 30 months of age examined. Provides insight into the mechanisms underlying the divergent effects of age on the central and peripheral nervous systems.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array

CITATIONS
