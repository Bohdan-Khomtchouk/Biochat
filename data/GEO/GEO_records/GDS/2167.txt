TITLE
Activin ligand effect on LbetaT2 gonadotrope cell line: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of LbetaT2 pituitary gonadotrope cells at various time points up to 6 hours following treatment with activin. Activin, a member of the follicle stimulating hormone (FSH) beta superfamily of ligands, stimulates the secretion FSH from the gonadotrope cells of the anterior pituitary.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
