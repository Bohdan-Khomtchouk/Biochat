TITLE
Tienilic acid effect on the liver: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of livers from animals 6 and 24 hours following treatment with tienilic acid. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of tienilic acid associated hepatotoxicity.

Rattus norvegicus
