TITLE
Cadmium effect on liver cell line: dose response

SUMMARY
Analysis of HepG2 liver cells treated with 2 or 10 uM cadmium. Cadmium is an environmental toxicant. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of cadmium toxicity.
