TITLE
Focal adhesion kinase deficient fibroblast response to endothelin-1

SUMMARY
Analysis of fibroblasts lacking focal adhesion kinase (FAK) following treatment with endothelin-1 (ET-1). FAK deficient fibroblasts isolated from mutant embryos. ET-1 is a profibrotic factor. Results provide insight into the role of FAK in fibrogenesis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array

CITATIONS
