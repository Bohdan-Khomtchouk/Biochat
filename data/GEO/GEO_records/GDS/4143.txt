TITLE
High fat diet effect on prostate: ventral prostate lobe

SUMMARY
Analysis of ventral prostate lobe from 8-week-old C57BL/6J males fed a high fat diet for 12 weeks. High fat diets are a risk factor for prostate cancer. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms by which high fat diet can promote carcinogenesis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
