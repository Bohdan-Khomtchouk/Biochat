TITLE
Steady-state temperature (y14)

SUMMARY
Samples collected from cells grown continuously at 15°C, 17°C, 21°C, 25°C, 29°C, 33°C and 36°C. Included as part of a detailed characterization of global expression programs triggered by stress.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae

PLATFORM
GPL54 : y14

CITATIONS
Gasch AP, Spellman PT, Kao CM, Carmel-Harel O et al. Genomic expression programs in the response of yeast cells to environmental changes. Mol Biol Cell 2000 Dec;11(12):4241-57. PMID:  11102521
