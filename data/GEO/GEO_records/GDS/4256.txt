TITLE
Caseous tuberculosis granulomas comparison with normal lung parenchyma

SUMMARY
Analysis of caseous pulmonary tuberculosis (TB) granulomas. TB progression involves caseous granuloma development that cavitates, releasing infectious Mycobacterium tuberculosis bacilli. Results provide insight into TB etiology and pathogen-mediated dysregulation of host lipid metabolism.

Homo sapiens
