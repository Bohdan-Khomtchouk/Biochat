TITLE
Polyamide-chlorambucil conjugate 1R-Chl effect on leukemia cell line

SUMMARY
Analysis of leukemia K562 cells treated with the polyamide-chlorambucil conjugate 1R-Chl. 1R-Chl down-regulates transcription of the human histone H4c gene and inhibits the growth of several cancer cell lines in vitro.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
