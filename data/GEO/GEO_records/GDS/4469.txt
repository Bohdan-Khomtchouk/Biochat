TITLE
Sporadic medulloblastomas

SUMMARY
Analysis of sporadic medulloblastomas (MB), tumor grade IV from pediatric patients. Medulloblastoma is a highly malignant brain tumor occurring predominantly in children. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying the pathogenesis of MB.
