TITLE
HPV16 integration and episomal loss effect on cervical keratinocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of high-risk human papillomavirus (HRHPV) HPV16-containing cervical keratinocyte W12 cells selected for integrated HPV16. Results show that HRHPV integration and episome loss are key events in cervical neoplastic progression.
