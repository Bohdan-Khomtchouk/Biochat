TITLE
CD8 T cell development (Mu11K-B)

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of naïve and memory CD8 T cells from female RAG1-deficient transgenics expressing B6.2.16 T cell receptors specific for the human Y antigen. Genes protecting against programmed cell death upregulated in memory CD8 T cells.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL76 : [Mu11KsubB] Affymetrix Murine 11K SubB Array

CITATIONS
Liu N, Phillips T, Zhang M, Wang Y et al. Serine protease inhibitor 2A is a protective factor for memory T cell development. Nat Immunol 2004 Sep;5(9):919-26. PMID:  15311278
