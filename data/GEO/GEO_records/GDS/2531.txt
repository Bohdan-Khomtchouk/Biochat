TITLE
Antipsychotic agents clozapine and haloperidol effects on the brain (II)

SUMMARY
Analysis of brains of animals treated for 12 weeks with the atypical antipsychotic clozapine or the typical antipsychotic haloperidol. Clozapine's use as an antipsychotic is limited by agranulocytosis. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of clozapine's therapeutic and toxic effects.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL339 : [MOE430A] Affymetrix Mouse Expression 430A Array
