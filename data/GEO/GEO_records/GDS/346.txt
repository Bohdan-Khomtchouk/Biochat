TITLE
Serotonergic hallucinogen effect in somatosensory cortex

SUMMARY
Effect of 5-HT2A seratonin receptor agonist (±)2,5-dimethoxy 4-iodoamphetamine (DOI) in somatosensory cortex. 129Sv mice injected with DOI (2 mg/kg or 10 mg/kg), 1 hour. Abnormal 5-HT2A receptor function implicated in depression and schizophrenia.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL226 : MS1: M1K array

CITATIONS
González-Maeso J, Yuen T, Ebersole BJ, Wurmbach E et al. Transcriptome fingerprints distinguish hallucinogenic and nonhallucinogenic 5-hydroxytryptamine 2A receptor agonist effects in mouse somatosensory cortex. J Neurosci 2003 Oct 1;23(26):8836-43. PMID:  14523084
