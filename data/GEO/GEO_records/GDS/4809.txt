TITLE
Gata5 deficiency effect on the lung

SUMMARY
Analysis of lungs of C57BL/6J animals deficient for Gata5, a transcription factor expressed in the lung. Gata5 deficiency causes airway constrictor hyperresponsiveness (AHR). Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms by which Gata5 deficiency leads to AHR.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
