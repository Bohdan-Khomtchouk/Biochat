TITLE
Androgen deprivation effect on CD4 T-cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of CD4 T cells from castrated animals. Testosterone levels had been observed to be inversely correlated to the strength of the immune response.  Results provide insight into the molecular basis of this observation.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
