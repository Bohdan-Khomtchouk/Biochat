TITLE
Adipocytes and preadipocytes from subcutaneous and visceral fat depots

SUMMARY
Analysis of adipocytes and stromovascular fraction containing preadipocytes taken from subcutaneous fat and intraabdominal fat. Individuals with peripheral subcutaneous obesity are at little risk of complications of obesity, while those with visceral obesity are prone to these complications.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL81 : [MG_U74Av2] Affymetrix Murine Genome U74A Version 2 Array

CITATIONS
