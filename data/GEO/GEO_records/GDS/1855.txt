TITLE
Four-amino analog of tetrahydrobiopterin effect on cardiac allografts

SUMMARY
Analysis of cardiac allografts from transplant recipients treated with four-amino analog of tetrahydrobiopterin (ABH4), a nitric oxide synthase inhibitor shown to prolong allograft survival. The effect of tetrahydrobiopterin (BH4), N6-(iminoethyl)-L-lysine (L-NIL), and cyclosporin A also examined.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
