TITLE
Ovarian cancer cell lines

SUMMARY
Analysis of 10 ovarian cancer cell lines: 4 ovarian serous adenocarcinomas, 1 paclitaxel/cisplatin resistant ovarian adenocarcinoma cell line derivative, 5 ovarian clear cell adenocarcinomas. Expression profile of each cell line compared to its sensitivity to paclitaxel and cisplatin.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
