TITLE
Various normal pure cell cultures

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of various pure normal epithelial, endothelial, fibroblast, stromal, and muscle cell cultures. Data provide baseline for understanding cellular mechanisms for maintenance of stable cell types and for studying pathways to terminally differentiated state.
