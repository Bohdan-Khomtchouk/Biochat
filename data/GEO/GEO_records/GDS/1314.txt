TITLE
Malignant melanoma cell lines

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of MelJUSO, A375, 607B, 518A2, and Skmel 28 malignant melanoma cell lines. Results correlated with regions of chromosomal breakpoints, and indicate an association between chromosomal breakpoints and altered gene expression.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens

PLATFORM
