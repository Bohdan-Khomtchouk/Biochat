TITLE
D-penicillamine-induced autoimmunity model of idiosyncratic drug reactions

SUMMARY
Analysis of Brown Norway males 6 hours after treatment with 150 mg/kg of D-penicillamine. Some rheumatoid arthritis patients treated with D-penicillamine develop idiosyncratic drug reactions (IDR). Results provide insight into mechanisms underlying IDR.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array

CITATIONS
Séguin B, Boutros PC, Li X, Okey AB et al. Gene expression profiling in a model of D-penicillamine-induced autoimmunity in the Brown Norway rat: predictive value of early signs of danger. Chem Res Toxicol 2005 Aug;18(8):1193-202. PMID:  16097792
