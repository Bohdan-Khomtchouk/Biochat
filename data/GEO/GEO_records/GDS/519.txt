TITLE
Astrocyte response to beta amyloid peptide 25-35

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of cortical type I astrocytes cultured on beta amyloid peptide 25-35 fragment (bAP(25-35)). Samples examined at 12 hours, 1, 3, and 5 days. Amyloid plaques of Alzheimer's disease are composed of beta amyloid.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
