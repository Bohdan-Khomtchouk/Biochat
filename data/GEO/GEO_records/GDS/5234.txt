TITLE
Dicer deficiency effect on preadipocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of preadipocytes inactived for Dicer in vitro. Dicer, an endonuclease of the RNase III family, is involved in the processing of microRNAs. Results provide insight into the role of Dicer in adipocytes.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus

PLATFORM
GPL1261 : [Mouse430_2] Affymetrix Mouse Genome 430 2.0 Array
