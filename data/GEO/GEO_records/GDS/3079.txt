TITLE
Cadmium effect on the testis: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of testes of animals treated with cadmium chloride for 8 or 20 hours. Cadmium is an environmental pollutant that adversely affects the male reproductive system. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of the toxic effect of cadmium on the male reproductive system.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
