TITLE
Early maturation-stage seed compartments

SUMMARY
Analysis of LCM-captured compartments from seeds containing early maturation-stage embryos. Whole seeds at early maturation-stage also examined. The various compartments play distinct roles in seed formation. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms involved in seed development.

ORGANISM
Glycine max
