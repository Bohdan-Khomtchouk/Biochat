TITLE
Polyunsaturated fatty acids effect on cardiomyocytes

SUMMARY
Analysis of primary cardiomyocyte cultures treated with eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA) or docosahexaenoic acid (DHA). EPA and DHA are n-3 polyunsaturated fatty acids (PUFAs). Results provide insight into the molecular basis of the cardioprotective effect of n-3 PUFAs.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
