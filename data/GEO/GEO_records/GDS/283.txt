TITLE
Stationary phase, ypl230w mutant

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of wild type and ypl230w mutant strains in stationary phase. Cultures were grown to OD600 of 0.27 (ypl230w) and 0.4 (wild-type), then samples collected at various time points up to 24 hours.

ORGANISM
Saccharomyces cerevisiae
