TITLE
Ketogenic diet effect on brain hippocampus

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of brain hippocampi of animals fed the ketogenic diet (KD).  KD is an anticonvulsant treatment used to manage medically intractable epilepsies.   Results provide insight into the anticonvulsant action of KD.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus

PLATFORM
GPL341 : [RAE230A] Affymetrix Rat Expression 230A Array
