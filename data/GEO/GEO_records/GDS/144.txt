TITLE
Cardiac hypertrophy, exercise-induced

SUMMARY
Effects of chronic conditioning by swimming which increases left ventricular mass and induces myocyte hypertrophy. 8 week old FVB mice swum in tanks 7 days a week for 4 weeks. Animals sacrificed at several time points up to 28 days.
