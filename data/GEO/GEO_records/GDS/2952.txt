TITLE
Rheumatoid arthritis response to anti-tumor necrosis factor treatment: whole blood

SUMMARY
Analysis of whole blood samples from rheumatoid arthritis (RA) patients following anti-tumor necrosis factor therapy. Results provide insight into the pathogenesis of RA and the molecular mechanisms associated with the response to anti-TNF treatment.

Homo sapiens
