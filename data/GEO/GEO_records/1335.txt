TITLE
Juvenile hormone III effect on anterior midgut: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of anterior midguts of males after topical application of juvenile hormone III (JHIII). Midguts examined 24 hours after phloem feeding, and at various time points up to 16 hours after treatment. JHIII is a sesquiterpenoid involved in the regulation of insect development and reproduction.

ORGANISM
Ips pini
