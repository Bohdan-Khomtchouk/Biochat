TITLE
Burkitt lymphoma subtypes: lymph-node biopsies

SUMMARY
Analysis of lymph-node biopsies representing the 3 clinical subsets of Burkitt lymphoma (BL): endemic, sporadic, and immunodeficiency-associated. Each BL subtype affects different populations and can present with different features. Results provide insight into the molecular basis of BL subtypes.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
