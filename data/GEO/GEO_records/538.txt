TITLE
DNA methylation and histone deacetylation in neural stem cells

SUMMARY
Examination of DNA methylation and histone deacetylation in neural stem cells. Neurospheres from bcl2 transgene E14.5 forebrains treated with 150 nM trichostatin A (histone deacetylase inhibitor) or 500 nM 5-aza-2'-deoxycytidine (demethylating agent).

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
