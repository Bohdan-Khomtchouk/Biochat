TITLE
Hypoxic-ischemic injury response to erythropoietin pretreatment

SUMMARY
Analysis of 7 day old forebrains pretreated with erythropoietin (EPO) and then subjected to hypoxic-ischemic injury (HI). HI induced by ligation of the common carotid artery and confinement in hypoxic chambers for 50 minutes. Results provide insight into the neuroprotective effect of EPO.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
