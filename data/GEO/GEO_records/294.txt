TITLE
Ligand screen in B cells: B-lymphocyte chemoattractant

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of effects induced by 0.2 uM B-lymphocyte chemoattractant in male c57BL/6 B cells. Cells incubated in culture with ligand and samples removed at several time points up to 4 hours. Spleen from male c57BL/6 used as reference.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
