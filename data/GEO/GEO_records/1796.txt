TITLE
Prostacylin receptor agonist and indomethacin effect on interleukin-1 beta activated synovial fibroblasts

SUMMARY
Analysis of interleukin-1 beta activated synovial fibroblasts following treatment with indomethacin, a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug, alone or in combination with iloprost, a prostacyclin (PGI) receptor agonist. Results provide insight into the role of PGI in rheumatoid arthritis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
