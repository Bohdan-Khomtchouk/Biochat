TITLE
Indole acetic acid treatment: dose response and  time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of 10-12 day Columbia-O seedlings treated with either 0.1 uM or 1.0 uM indole acetic acid (IAA) auxin for 1 and 3 hours.  Treated seedlings analyzed in reference to untreated control.  Part of a study assessing the performance of the ATH1 array.

ORGANISM
Arabidopsis thaliana
