TITLE
Primary effusion lymphomas and associated viral infections

SUMMARY
Analysis of Kaposi's sarcoma-associated herpesvirus (KSHV) positive mononuclear cells from 3 patients with primary effusion lymphoma (PEL), 9 KSHV positive PEL cell lines, and 3 KSHV negative cell lines from lymphomatous effusions.  Effect of concomittant Epstein-Barr virus infection also examined.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
