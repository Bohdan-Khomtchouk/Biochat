TITLE
Cytomegalovirus strain comparison

SUMMARY
Human foreskin fibroblasts (HFF) infected with cytomegalovirus strains fhCMV-H (rapid spread in vitro) and fhCMV-T (slow spread in vitro). UV-inactivated viruses also examined to control for response to coat glycoprotein B and active viral infection.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
