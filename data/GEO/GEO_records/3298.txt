TITLE
Peripheral blood monocyte response to Francisella tularensis infections

SUMMARY
Analysis of blood monocytes infected with Francisella tularensis tularensis Schu S4 (virulent) or subsp. novicida (less virulent). F. tularensis causes the disease tularemia. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms that enable Francisella to dampen or subvert the host immune response.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
