TITLE
Spinal cord injury effect on supraspinal tracts: time course (RG-U34B)

SUMMARY
Temporal analysis of Sprague-Dawley males subjected to moderate spinal cord injury (SCI) at vertebral T9. Raphe magnus and sensorimotor cortex examined at various time points up to 3 months following trauma. Analysis of areas indirectly affected by injury helps understanding the mechanisms of SCI.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
