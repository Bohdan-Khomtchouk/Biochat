TITLE
Colon epithelial biopsies of ulcerative colitis patients

SUMMARY
Analysis of inflamed and un-inflamed colon epithelial biopsies of 67 ulcerative colitis (UC) patients from different anatomical locations of the gastrointestinal (GI) tract. Results provide insight into the regional variation of gene expression in UC patients and the pathogenesis of UC.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
