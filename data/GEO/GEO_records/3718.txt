TITLE
Zinc finger Zbtb20 deficiency effect on the developing hippocampus

SUMMARY
Analysis of hippocampi of postnatal day 2 mutants lacking Zbtb20. Zbtb20 is a member of the BTB/POZ zinc finger family. Results provide insight into the role of Zbtb20 in hippocampal development.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
