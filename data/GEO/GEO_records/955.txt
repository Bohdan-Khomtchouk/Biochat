TITLE
Early-life kainite-induced seizure effect on brain hippocampus: time course

SUMMARY
Analysis of early-life kainite induced seizures by expression profiling of brain hippocampi from animals at postnatal day 15. Hippocampi examined at various time points up to 240 hours following kainite treatment. Results provide insight into molecular effects of early childhood convulsions.

ORGANISM
Rattus norvegicus
