TITLE
Capreomycin effect on Mycobacterium tuberculosis

SUMMARY
Analysis of Mycobacterium tuberculosis cultures after treatment with capreomycin. Capreomycin is a drug used to treat multi-drug resistant tuberculosis (TB), and possesses bactericidal activity against non-replicating TB bacilli. Results provide insight into the spectrum of action of capreomycin.

ORGANISM
Mycobacterium tuberculosis H37Rv
