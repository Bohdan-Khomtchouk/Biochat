TITLE
Cytokine treatment effect on liver

SUMMARY
Liver mRNA profiles following intraperitoneal cytokine injection. Strains interferon gamma -/-, albumin-cre(-) Socs3(w/fl) and albumin-cre(+) Socs3(-/fl) injected with either PBS, interferon gamma, or interleukin 6, and liver examined at 4 hours.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
