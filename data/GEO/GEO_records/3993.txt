TITLE
BKS db/db model of type 2 diabetes: sciatic nerve

SUMMARY
Analysis of sciatic nerve from type 2 diabetes (T2D) BKS db/db mice at 24 weeks of age. Diabetic neuropathy (DN) is one of the most common and debilitating complications of diabetes. Results provide insight into molecular mechanisms underlying the development and progression of DN.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
