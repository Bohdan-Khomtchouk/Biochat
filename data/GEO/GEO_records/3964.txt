TITLE
Xenotransplant melanoma metastasis model [Data Set A]

SUMMARY
Analysis of subcutaneous tumors or lung metastases from immunodeficient mice injected subcutaneously or intravenously with a poorly-metastatic A375 melanoma cell line (ATCC) or with highly-metastatic derivative cell lines. Results provide insight into the molecular mechanisms underlying metastasis.

ORGANISM
Homo sapiens
