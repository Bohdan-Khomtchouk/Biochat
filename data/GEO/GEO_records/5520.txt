TITLE
Rosiglitazone effect on adipose progenitor cells

SUMMARY
Analysis of adipose progenitor cells from animals fed a diet supplemented with rosiglitazone (ROS) for 2 months. ROS is a thiazolidinedione antidiabetic drug. Results provide insight into the effect of chronic ROS administration on the stem cell properties of adipose progenitor cells.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
