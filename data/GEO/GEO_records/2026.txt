TITLE
TSLC1/IGSF4 deficiency effect on testis

SUMMARY
Analysis of whole testes of TSLC1/IGSF4 deficient animals. The TSLC1/IGSF4 protein belongs to the immunoglobulin superfamily of cell adhesion molecules. Results provide insight into the role of TSLC1/IGSF4 in spermatogenesis.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
