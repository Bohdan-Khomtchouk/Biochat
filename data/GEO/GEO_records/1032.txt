TITLE
Coxsackievirus B3 infection effect on hearts: time course

SUMMARY
Expression profiling of hearts from 4 to 5 week old A/J males after infection with coxsackievirus B3 (CVB3).  Hearts examined at various time points up to 30 days postinfection.  CVB3 infection leads to cardiac inflammation and fibrosis within 9 days of infection.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
