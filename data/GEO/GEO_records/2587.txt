TITLE
Recovery from stationary phase: time course (V)

SUMMARY
Analysis of MG1655 cells at various time points up to 90 minutes following transfer from stationary phase culture into media with 10 mM sodium phosphate and 0.2% glucose to OD of 0.4. This is part of a study tracking transcriptional responses to over 30 chemical and physiological perturbations.

ORGANISM
Escherichia coli
