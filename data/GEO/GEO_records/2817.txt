TITLE
Nephronophthisis model

SUMMARY
Analysis of kidneys of animals lacking Glis2. The GLIS2 gene, which encodes a Kruppel-like zinc finger transcription factor, is found in a region of chromosome 16 p linked to nephronophthisis (NPHP). Results provide insight into the role of Glis2 in the pathophysiology of NPHP.

ORGANISM
Mus musculus
